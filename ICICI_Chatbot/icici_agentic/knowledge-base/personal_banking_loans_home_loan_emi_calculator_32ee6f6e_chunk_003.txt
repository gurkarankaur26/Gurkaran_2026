TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Home Loan - Features, Eligibility, Interest Rates, and Benefits

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Home Loans  
TAGS: Home Loan, EMI, Interest Rates, Loan Tenure, Tax Benefits, Balance Transfer, Top-up Loan, Home Loan Insurance  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Home Loans with a maximum online loan amount of ₹10 crore. The final loan amount sanctioned depends on factors such as the applicant’s income, age, and credit score.

**Interest and EMI Details:**  
- **Pre-EMI Interest:** This is the interest accrued on the disbursed loan amount before EMI payments begin. It is applicable during the construction phase or before the full loan amount is disbursed.  
- **EMI Calculation:** ICICI Bank provides a Home Loan EMI Calculator on its website, allowing customers to adjust loan amount, tenure, and interest rate to find an ideal repayment plan.  
- **Lowering EMI:** To reduce EMI, customers can increase the loan tenure, make part pre-payments, or transfer the loan to another lender offering a lower interest rate.  
- **Part Payments:** Making part payments reduces the outstanding principal, which lowers the interest payable. While the EMI amount remains the same, a larger portion goes towards principal repayment.  
- **Amortisation Schedule:** ICICI Bank provides an amortisation schedule detailing:  
  - Outstanding balance before each EMI  
  - Payable EMI amount  
  - EMI components towards principal and interest  
  - Outstanding loan amount after each EMI payment  

**Loan Tenure:**  
- Flexible repayment options with loan tenure up to 30 years to suit individual financial situations.

**Tax Benefits:**  
- Under the Income Tax Act, 1961, Indian nationals can claim tax relief on payments made towards Home Loan repayment, subject to amendments in the Act.

**Loan Features and Benefits:**  
- **Competitive Interest Rates:** Attractive and affordable interest rates to ensure cost-effectiveness.  
- **Quick Processing:** Seamless and efficient loan processing to minimize wait time and provide faster access to funds.  
- **Balance Transfer Facility:** Option to transfer existing Home Loans to ICICI Bank to avail better interest rates and terms.  
- **Simplified Documentation:** Quick and streamlined application process with minimal documentation.  
- **Top-up Loans:** Existing customers can avail top-up loans for purposes such as home renovation, education, or other financial needs.  
- **Home Loan Insurance:** Insurance coverage to protect borrowers and their families from unforeseen events, providing financial security.  
- **Customer Support:** Dedicated support to assist borrowers throughout the loan tenure.  

**EMI Date Change:**  
- Customers can request a change in the EMI payment date by submitting a written application or visiting the branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Maximum online Home Loan amount: ₹10 crore  
- Loan tenure: Up to 30 years  
- Pre-EMI interest applicable during construction or partial disbursement  
- Flexible EMI calculation and adjustment via online EMI calculator  
- Part pre-payments reduce principal and interest burden  
- Income Tax benefits available under Income Tax Act, 1961  
- Balance transfer and top-up loan facilities available  
- Home Loan Insurance offered for borrower protection  
- Quick processing and simplified documentation  
- EMI date can be changed on request
