TITLE: Foreign Exchange (Forex) FAQs  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Foreign Exchange, Forex Prepaid Card, Currency Notes  
TAGS: Forex, Foreign Exchange, Forex Prepaid Card, Currency Notes, Reload, Refund, RBI Guidelines, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
**Minimum Transaction Amounts:**  
- Buy: Minimum transaction amount is USD 500 or its equivalent.  
- For Forex Prepaid Card or Currency Notes, the minimum limit per product is USD 200 or its equivalent.  
- Reload: No minimum transaction amount restriction.  

**Order Placement and Delivery:**  
- Customers should place ‘buy orders’ at least 3 working days prior to the date of travel.  
- Forex Prepaid Card and currency will be delivered within 2 working days after contacting the customer.  
- Forex Prepaid Card will be loaded within 1 working day after receipt of documents at the branch.  
- For urgent requirements, customers can visit any ICICI Bank Forex Servicing branch.  

**How to Buy/Reload/Refund Forex via Digital Channels:**  
- iMobile App: Log in > Cards & Forex > Forex Prepaid Cards > Select card > Reload.  
- Net Banking: Log in > Cards & Loans > Forex & Prepaid Cards.  

**Refund Policy:**  
- As per RBI Master Direction (Other Remittance Facilities dated January 1, 2016), resident Indians can get a refund of unutilized foreign exchange balance only after 10 days from the date of last transaction.  
- The unutilized balance on Forex Prepaid Cards can be refunded immediately upon request, subject to regulatory retention conditions.  
- The card remains active after refund and can be reloaded and used until expiry.  

**Currencies Offered Online:**  
- Foreign Currency Notes: USD, EUR, GBP  
- ICICI Bank Forex Prepaid Card: USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD, CHF, JPY, AED, HKD, SGD, THB, SAR, SEK, ZAR, NZD  

**Additional Information:**  
- Forex Prepaid Card reloads are processed instantly on a best-effort basis; if system issues occur, reload will be completed within 1 working day.  
- Foreign exchange can be purchased up to 180 days in advance of the travel date, subject to document submission.  
- If a transaction is canceled by the customer, any differential loss due to forex rate fluctuations will be deducted from the refund amount; profits from rate changes will not be passed on.  

**Definitions:**  
- "Authorised Person" as per Foreign Exchange Management Act, 1999, means an authorised dealer, money changer, off-shore banking unit, or any other person authorised to deal in foreign exchange or foreign securities.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum buy amount: USD 500 (USD 200 for prepaid card/currency notes)  
- No minimum reload amount  
- Place buy order 3 working days before travel  
- Delivery: 2 working days; reload: 1 working day  
- Refund of unutilized forex balance allowed after 10 days from last transaction  
- Forex prepaid card remains active after refund and can be reloaded  
- Online currencies: Notes (USD, EUR, GBP); Prepaid Card (multiple currencies)  
- Forex can be bought 180 days in advance  
- Cancellation refunds adjusted for forex rate losses only
