Mutual Fund Schemes and Plans Offered via ICICI Bank

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Investment, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Hybrid Funds, Arbitrage, Banking & Financial Services, ETFs, Growth, IDCW, Direct Plan, Regular Plan  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides access to a wide range of mutual fund schemes from various fund houses, including Quant and SBI Mutual Funds. These schemes cover different market capitalizations and investment strategies, such as large cap, mid cap, small cap, hybrid, arbitrage, and sector-specific funds. Investors can choose between Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options, as well as Direct and Regular plans.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes Available:  

1. Quant Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- Quant Large and Mid Cap Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- Quant Large Cap Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- Quant Mid Cap Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- Quant Small Cap Fund (Growth and IDCW)  

2. SBI Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- SBI Arbitrage Opportunities Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- SBI Automotive Opportunities Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- SBI Balanced Advantage Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- SBI Banking & Financial Services Fund (Growth and IDCW)  
- SBI BSE Sensex ETF  
- SBI Conservative Hybrid Fund (Annual IDCW)  

Each scheme is available in Direct and Regular plans, allowing investors to choose based on their preference for cost and advisory services. The IDCW option provides periodic income distribution, while the Growth option reinvests earnings to grow the investment value.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual fund schemes cover various market segments: large cap, mid cap, small cap, hybrid, arbitrage, sector-specific, and ETFs.  
- Options available: Growth (capital appreciation) and IDCW (income distribution).  
- Plans available: Direct (lower expense ratio, no distributor commission) and Regular (with distributor commission).  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified investment options through ICICI Bank’s platform.  
- Enables systematic investment and portfolio diversification.  
- Fund codes are provided for easy identification and transaction processing.
