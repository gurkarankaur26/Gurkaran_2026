SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a wide range of mutual fund investment options in partnership with various fund houses. These funds cater to different investment needs and risk profiles, including growth and income distribution cum capital withdrawal (IDCW) options. The mutual funds are available in both Direct and Regular plans, providing flexibility for investors.

Key mutual fund categories and schemes include:

1. **Baroda BNP Paribas Funds**  
   - Manufacturing Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Midcap Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Multi Asset Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Multi Cap Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Small Cap Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Value Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  

2. **Canara Robeco Funds**  
   - Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  
   - Consumer Trends Fund (Direct & Regular, Growth & IDCW)  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., Baroda BNP Paribas Manufacturing Fund - Dir - Growth: 152697). Investors can choose between growth options, where returns are reinvested, or IDCW options, where periodic income is distributed.

**Investment Process:**  
- Select the preferred mutual fund scheme and plan (Direct or Regular).  
- Choose the option: Growth or IDCW.  
- Complete the KYC and investment formalities through ICICI Bank’s platform.  
- Monitor investments and opt for systematic investment plans (SIP) or lump sum investments as per convenience.

**Benefits:**  
- Wide variety of schemes to suit different risk appetites and financial goals.  
- Flexibility to choose between growth and income distribution options.  
- Access to both direct and regular plans, allowing cost optimization.  
- Professional fund management by reputed fund houses.  
- Convenient investment through ICICI Bank’s digital and branch channels.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual funds available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth and IDCW (income distribution).  
- Multiple fund categories: Manufacturing, Midcap, Multi Asset, Multi Cap, Small Cap, Value, Balanced Advantage, Consumer Trends.  
- Unique scheme codes for easy identification.  
- Investment requires KYC compliance.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity and hybrid fund exposure.  
- Benefits include professional management, flexibility, and convenience.
