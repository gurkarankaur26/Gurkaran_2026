CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers comprehensive support for home buyers through its extensive database of approved residential projects across key cities in India. Customers can explore these projects to find their dream home and apply for home loans with ease.

**Home Loan Eligibility:**  
- Eligibility is calculated based on factors such as monthly income, fixed monthly obligations, current age, and retirement age.  
- Including the income of co-applicants can enhance the loan eligibility.  
- ICICI Bank provides an online Home Loan Eligibility Calculator to help customers estimate the loan amount they can borrow.

**EMI Calculator:**  
- The bank offers an online Home Loan EMI Calculator where customers can input the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure to calculate monthly EMI payments.

**Loan Application and Disbursement:**  
- Customers can apply for home loans online, subject to document submission and applicable terms.  
- The sanction process is quick, followed by prompt disbursement of the loan amount.  
- Loan amounts offered can be higher based on the applicant’s income.

**Home Buyers Guide:**  
- A dedicated resource designed for first-time home buyers.  
- Includes infographics, blogs, and practical calculators to simplify the home buying process.  
- Covers topics such as selecting a home, funding options, transferring existing home loans, and availing top-up loans on existing home loans.

**Property Area Definitions:**  
- **Carpet Area:** The actual usable area inside the apartment/building excluding walls.  
- **Built-up Area:** Carpet area plus the area of walls and balconies.  
- **Super Built-up Area:** Built-up area plus common spaces like lobby, lifts, stairs, garden, and swimming pool.

**Valuation Process:**  
- Evaluates the market value of the property considering demand and supply, property type, construction quality, and location.
