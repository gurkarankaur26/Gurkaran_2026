TITLE: ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Claim Process and Requirements  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Insurance Claims  
TAGS: Claim Process, Claim Form, Documentation, Claim Rejection, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance  

CONTENT:  
Claims should be reported as soon as possible after the event to avoid delays and the need for additional documentation. A claim form is a mandatory document required to process the claim. It can be downloaded from the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance website or collected from the nearest branch.

The insurer may request additional documents based on the initial documents submitted and the claim assessor’s review. Additional documents may be required due to factors such as:  
- Cause of death  
- Duration of the policy  
- Involvement in medical treatment  
- Legal and regulatory requirements  

Submitting all required records and documents completely and accurately ensures faster processing of the claim. Missing or incomplete documents may cause delays or rejection of the claim.

Claims can be rejected under certain circumstances, which are typically related to incomplete documentation, non-fulfillment of policy terms, or discrepancies in the claim details.

KEY POINTS:  
- Report claims as soon as possible after the event.  
- Claim form is mandatory and available online or at branches.  
- Additional documents may be requested based on cause of death, policy duration, medical treatment, and legal requirements.  
- Complete and accurate documentation speeds up claim processing.  
- Missing or incorrect documents can lead to delays or claim rejection.
