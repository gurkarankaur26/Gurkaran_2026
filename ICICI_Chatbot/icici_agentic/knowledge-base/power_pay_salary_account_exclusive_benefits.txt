The Power Pay Salary Account by ICICI Bank offers a range of exclusive benefits tailored for salaried individuals. This account requires no minimum balance, making it convenient and accessible. It comes with built-in Demat and Trading accounts, enabling seamless investment and trading activities. Account holders enjoy unlimited ATM transactions at no cost, enhancing ease of access to funds. Additional benefits include air and personal accidental insurance coverage, providing financial security. The account supports smart investments, helping customers grow their wealth digitally.
