TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Fastest to Cross 1 Million Milestone  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, Credit Card, Rewards, Video KYC, Contactless Payment, Digital Banking  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card, launched in 2018, has become the fastest credit card in India to cross the milestone of 1 million cards issued, achieving about 1.4 million cards in less than 20 months. This co-branded credit card, powered by Visa, is available to any registered Amazon India customer, including those who are not ICICI Bank customers.

Key features and benefits of the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card include:  
- Always-on unlimited rewards credited directly to the Amazon Pay balance.  
- Instant issuance of the credit card for select customers in less than 60 seconds.  
- Contactless payment feature for safe and convenient transactions.  
- Video KYC facility introduced in June 2020, enabling fully digital and contactless application process for new-to-bank customers across India.  

The card aims to provide a seamless and rewarding experience by combining the strengths of Amazon Pay’s digital ecosystem and ICICI Bank’s banking expertise. The video KYC facility allows customers to apply for the card remotely, enhancing convenience especially during the pandemic.

KEY POINTS:  
- Over 1.4 million Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit cards issued since 2018.  
- Available to all registered Amazon India customers, regardless of ICICI Bank relationship.  
- Unlimited, always-on rewards credited directly to Amazon Pay balance.  
- Instant card issuance in under 60 seconds for select customers.  
- Contactless payment enabled for secure transactions.  
- Video KYC facility for fully digital, contactless application process introduced in June 2020.  
- Powered by Visa network.
