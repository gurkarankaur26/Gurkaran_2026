TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Education Loan – Features, Eligibility, Interest Rates, Fees, and EMI Calculator

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Education Loan

TAGS:  
Education Loan, EMI Calculator, Interest Rates, Loan Tenure, Processing Fees, Loan Eligibility, Loan Repayment

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans designed to help students finance their higher education expenses such as tuition fees, accommodation, travel, books, and other related costs. The loan amount, interest rate, repayment tenure, and applicable charges vary depending on the loan structure chosen by the borrower.

**Key Features:**  
- Loan tenure up to 15 years including moratorium period.  
- Loan amount and interest rate vary based on the course, institution, and borrower profile.  
- Loans can be unsecured or secured (e.g., FD-backed).  
- Transparent disclosure of processing fees, GST, and administrative charges during application.  
- Tools such as the Education Loan EMI Calculator and Interest Calculator are available to help students plan their finances.

**Eligibility:**  
- Students pursuing higher education in India or abroad.  
- Eligibility depends on the course, institution, and borrower’s credit profile.

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- Interest rates depend on the loan amount, tenure, and security offered.  
- Processing fees and other administrative charges are disclosed upfront during the loan application process.  
- GST is applicable on processing fees and other charges.

**EMI and Repayment:**  
- The Education Loan EMI Calculator helps estimate monthly EMI, total interest payable, and overall borrowing cost based on loan amount, interest rate, and repayment tenure.  
- Students are advised to use the EMI calculator to compare different loan options and plan repayment carefully.  
- Repayment tenure can extend up to 15 years, including the moratorium period.  
- The total interest payable depends on the loan amount, interest rate, tenure, and repayment structure.  
- Borrowers should review the total borrowing cost, including interest and fees, before committing to the loan.

**Benefits:**  
- Helps students manage education expenses without immediate financial burden.  
- Transparent fee structure and easy access to calculators for informed decision-making.  
- Flexible repayment options tailored to borrower’s financial capacity.

**Process:**  
1. Estimate education expenses including tuition, accommodation, travel, and other costs.  
2. Use ICICI Bank’s Education Loan EMI and Interest calculators to understand monthly obligations and total cost.  
3. Review loan amount, interest rate, tenure, and applicable fees.  
4. Apply for the loan with required documentation.  
5. Upon sanction, plan repayment using the EMI calculator to ensure affordability.

KEY POINTS:  
- Maximum loan tenure: Up to 15 years including moratorium.  
- Use EMI and Interest calculators to estimate monthly payments and total interest.  
- Transparent disclosure of processing fees, GST, and administrative charges.  
- Loan can be unsecured or secured (FD-backed).  
- Eligibility depends on course, institution, and borrower profile.  
- Repayment planning is crucial to manage loan discipline and cost.  
- Education Loan covers tuition fees, accommodation, travel, books, and other education-related expenses.
