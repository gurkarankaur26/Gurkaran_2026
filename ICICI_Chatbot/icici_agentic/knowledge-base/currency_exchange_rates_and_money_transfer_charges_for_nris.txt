KEY POINTS:  
- Money2India offers low-cost, fast money transfers with real-time tracking for NRIs.  
- Exchange rates vary by country and are updated regularly; rates shown are as of July 01, 2026.  
- Service charges differ by country and transfer amount; some transfers have zero fees above certain thresholds.  
- For USA transfers, a fee of USD 4 applies for amounts below USD 1,000; transfers of USD 1,000 and above have zero fees.  
- UAE transfers incur a fixed charge of 12 AED.  
- Europe transfers attract a service charge of 0.5% of the remitting amount.  
- Transfers from Canada, Singapore, and the UK have zero service charges.  
- Customers should verify the latest exchange rates and fees on the Money2India platform before initiating transfers.
