TITLE: iMobile Features for ICICI Bank Savings Account Holders  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mobile Banking, Savings Account, Digital Payments  
TAGS: iMobile, ICICI Bank, UPI, Mobile Banking App, Savings Account, Online Payments, Fund Transfer, Utility Bill Payment, Mutual Funds, Insurance, Fixed Deposits  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s iMobile app is a comprehensive mobile banking application designed to serve both ICICI Bank customers and customers of other banks. It combines UPI-enabled payment features with traditional banking services, allowing users to manage multiple bank accounts and perform a variety of financial transactions from a single app.

Key Features of iMobile:  
- Interoperability: Customers of any bank can link their accounts and use the app for online payments and fund transfers.  
- UPI Integration: Users can scan QR codes, transfer money to any bank account or UPI ID, and use a single UPI ID to operate multiple bank accounts.  
- Account Opening: New customers can instantly open an ICICI Bank Savings Account through the app.  
- Utility Services: The app supports utility bill payments, mobile recharges, travel ticket bookings, and investments in mutual funds, insurance, and fixed deposits.  

Activation Process for Existing ICICI Bank Savings Account Holders:  
1. Download the iMobile app from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.  
2. Select “I already have an account.”  
3. Tap “Activate Now.”  
4. Verify your registered mobile number via SMS verification.  
5. Choose a login method: Fingerprint, 4-digit PIN, or Internet Banking User ID.  
6. Submit and authenticate using your Debit Card or Internet Banking credentials.  
7. Receive confirmation SMS on your registered mobile number.  

Note: If your mobile number is not registered with ICICI Bank, you can register it at the nearest ICICI Bank ATM or branch.  

Security:  
The iMobile app uses advanced encryption technologies to ensure safe and secure transactions.
