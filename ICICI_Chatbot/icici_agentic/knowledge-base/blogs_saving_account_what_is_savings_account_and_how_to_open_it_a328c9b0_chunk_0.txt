CONTENT:  
To register for ICICI Bank online banking, you need to fill out a form with your account number and personal details. After registration, the bank will generate a username and password, which you can use to access your account online.

If you are planning to open a new savings account, it is important to compare savings accounts from different banks. Consider factors such as the bank’s reputation, customer service quality, interest rates offered, minimum balance requirements, and the types of accounts available. Visiting the official websites of multiple top banks in India can help you make an informed decision.

KEY POINTS:  
- Online banking registration requires filling a form with account number and personal details.  
- Username and password are provided by the bank after registration.  
- When opening a new savings account, compare:  
  - Bank reputation  
  - Customer service  
  - Interest rates  
  - Minimum balance requirements  
  - Types of accounts offered  
- Use official bank websites for accurate and updated information.
