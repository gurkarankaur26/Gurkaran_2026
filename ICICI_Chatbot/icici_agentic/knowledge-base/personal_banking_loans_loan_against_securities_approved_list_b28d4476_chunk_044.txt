CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of SBI Mutual Fund schemes along with their respective scheme codes and types. The schemes include various fund categories such as Multi Asset Allocation, Multicap, Nifty Index Funds, Smallcap Funds, PSU Funds, and Quant Funds. Each scheme is available in different options like Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg) plans, and payout options such as Growth, Monthly IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), Quarterly IDCW, and Annual IDCW.
