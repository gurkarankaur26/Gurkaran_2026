TITLE: ICICI Bank Current Account - Features, Eligibility, Documents, and Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Accounts  
TAGS: Current Account, Business Banking, Account Opening, Eligibility, Fees, Interest Rates, Overdraft, Documentation  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Current Accounts designed primarily for businesses, companies, partnerships, and individuals who require higher transaction limits and frequent banking operations. These accounts provide flexibility in deposits and withdrawals, overdraft facilities, and are tailored to optimize business banking needs.

**Eligibility and Documentation:**  
To open a Current Account with ICICI Bank, the following documents must be submitted:  
- Proof of identity of the individual opening the account (PAN Card, Voter ID, Passport, or Driving Licence)  
- Proof of address for communication (telephone bill, electricity bill, etc.)  
- A cheque from an existing Savings Account  
- Recent colour photograph(s) of the applicant(s)  
- For companies and trusts: Certificate of Incorporation and Memorandum of Association  
- For companies: Official address for correspondence  
- For partnership firms: Partnership Agreement, identity proof, and residence proof of all partners  

After submission, a bank representative will complete the account opening formalities.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- No upper limit on deposits: You can deposit ₹20 lakh, ₹40 lakh, ₹50 lakh, or more based on your business requirements.  
- Large cash deposits are subject to tax reporting norms and may attract TDS or income tax implications as per the Income Tax Act, 2025. Proper documentation is advised for high-value deposits.  
- Overdraft facility available to meet short-term business funding needs.  
- Higher transaction limits suitable for business operations.  
- Quarterly Average Balance (QAB) requirement is ₹50,000. Non-maintenance of QAB attracts charges as per ICICI Bank’s schedule of charges.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- Maintaining a minimum Quarterly Average Balance of ₹50,000 is mandatory.  
- Charges apply if the QAB is not maintained; refer to ICICI Bank’s schedule of charges for detailed fee structure.  

**Process to Open Account:**  
1. Submit the required documents as per your business type.  
2. A bank representative will assist in completing the formalities.  
3. Upon verification, the Current Account will be opened and activated.  
4. Account details will be provided to the account holder.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum Quarterly Average Balance: ₹50,000  
- No maximum deposit limit; deposits of ₹20 lakh or more allowed  
- Overdraft facility available  
- Required documents vary by entity type (individual, company, partnership)  
- Large deposits subject to tax and regulatory compliance  
- Charges applicable for non-maintenance of QAB
