CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers competitive Two-Wheeler Loan (Bike Loan) interest rates and flexible repayment options to help customers finance their new bike or scooter. The interest rates vary based on loan amount, tenure, and borrower creditworthiness. The bank provides both fixed interest rate options and convenient loan repayment plans.

**Interest Rates:**  
- Interest rates generally range from 10.25%* to 26.10%* per annum.  
- For the quarter ended March 2026, the Bike Loan interest rate per annum ranged between 12.00% (minimum) and 15.00% (maximum), with a mean of 20.00%.  
- Rates are subject to change based on various factors and terms and conditions.

**Additional Information:**  
- Part payment charges are waived if the loan has been active for more than 24 months.  
- Penal charges apply for late payments and dishonoured payment instruments.  
- Loan cancellation charges vary depending on the timing of cancellation and whether the loan was digitally processed.  
- Physical copies of statements and schedules incur nominal fees; digital copies are free.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Two-Wheeler Loan interest rates range from 10.25%* to 26.10%* per annum, subject to borrower profile and loan details.  
- Loan processing fee can be up to 4% of the loan amount.  
- Part payment charges are nil after 24 months of loan seasoning; otherwise, 3% applies.  
- Late payment penal interest is 5% per annum plus GST on overdue EMIs.  
- Dishonour of payment instruments attracts a ₹500 + GST penalty per instance.  
- Loan cancellation charges are nil within the cooling-off period for digital loans; ₹2,500 + GST applies if cancelled after cooling-off but before first EMI.  
- Physical document requests attract nominal fees; digital requests are free.
