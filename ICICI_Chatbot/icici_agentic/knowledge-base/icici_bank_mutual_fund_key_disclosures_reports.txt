The commission rates vary by fund type and are subject to change at the discretion of ICICI Bank and the respective AMCs.

**Important Notes:**  
- ICICI Bank is an AMFI-registered mutual fund distributor.  
- The commission rates are indicative and may change without prior notice.  
- From 1 October 2013, ICICI Bank has waived transaction charges on all mutual fund purchases, both online and offline. Investors receive mutual fund units equivalent to the full subscription amount without any deduction for transaction charges.  
- Mutual fund investments are subject to market risks. Investors should carefully read the offer documents, including the Scheme Information Document (SID), Statement of Additional Information (SAI), and Key Information Memorandum (KIM), before investing.  
- ICICI Bank acts only as a distributor and is not responsible for any losses arising from investments in third-party mutual fund products. The investment contract is directly between the investor and the AMC.  
- Investors are advised to seek professional advice (tax, legal, financial, accounting) before making investment decisions.

- Commission rates vary by mutual fund scheme type, with maximum first-year trail commissions up to 2.07% for equity funds.
