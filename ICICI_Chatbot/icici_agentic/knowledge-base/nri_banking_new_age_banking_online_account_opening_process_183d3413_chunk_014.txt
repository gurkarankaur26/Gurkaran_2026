TITLE:  
ICICI Bank NRI Account Services and Mandate Holder Facility

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
NRI Banking, Account Management, Debit Card, Mandate Holder

TAGS:  
NRI Account, Debit Card, Mandate Holder, Cheque Book, ATM Card, Account Operation, India Branches, Overseas Branches

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a wide range of services for Non-Resident Indian (NRI) account holders, including access to a vast network of branches in India and select overseas locations. NRI customers can operate their accounts conveniently through these branches and via digital banking channels.

**Branches in India:**  
ICICI Bank has over 3500 branches across India where NRI customers can operate their accounts.

**Overseas Branches:**  
NRI customers can also operate their accounts from ICICI Bank branches located overseas (specific locations not listed here). Most transactions can be performed at these branches, though some transactions may be restricted.

**Debit/ATM Card:**  
- NRI account holders receive a debit cum ATM card linked to their account.  
- This card can be used for withdrawing funds, shopping, and authenticating transactions via Internet or Phone banking.  
- Debit cards linked to NRE accounts can be used at merchant establishments overseas and on e-commerce portals.  
- Debit cards linked to NRO accounts can only be used at merchant establishments in India and are not valid for e-commerce portals.

**Mandate Holder Facility:**  
- A mandate holder is a person authorized to operate your NRI account in India on your behalf.  
- The mandate holder can perform transactions such as withdrawals, deposits, and other banking operations.  
- ICICI Bank provides a cheque book and ATM card to the mandate holder to facilitate account operations.  
- It is recommended to appoint a family member in India as a mandate holder to ease account management.

**How to Register a Mandate Holder:**  
1. Submit the 'Add the Mandate' form, signed by both the account holder and the mandate holder.  
2. The mandate holder must visit an ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the form along with required documents.  
3. All documents must be self-attested by the respective account holders.  

**Cheque Book:**  
- A cheque book is provided when opening a new NRI account.  
- Requests for new cheque books can be made via Net Banking (Login -> Service requests -> Cheque Book request), by calling 24-hour customer care, or by visiting the nearest branch.  
- Charges applicable for ordering a new cheque book are available on the ICICI Bank website.  
- For resident accounts redesignated as NRO accounts, old cheque books remain valid.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank has 3500+ branches in India and select overseas branches for NRI account operations.  
- Debit cards linked to NRE accounts are usable overseas and on e-commerce portals; NRO account debit cards are limited to India and cannot be used on e-commerce portals.  
- Mandate holders can operate NRI accounts in India and are provided with cheque books and ATM cards.  
- Mandate holder registration requires submission of a signed form and documents at an ICICI Bank branch.  
- Cheque books can be requested online, via phone, or at branches; old cheque books remain valid for accounts redesignated as NRO.
