CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Sundaram Mutual Fund schemes available for investment, including their plan types, options, and unique identification numbers (Scheme Codes). The schemes cover various fund categories such as Equity Savings, Financial Services Opportunities, Flexicap, Focused, Large and Mid Cap, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Multi Asset Allocation, Multi Cap, and Small Cap funds. Each scheme is available in different plans (Direct and Regular) and options (Growth and IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), with some offering Half Yearly IDCW options.

Key Schemes and Details:  
- Sundaram Equity Savings Fund: Regular Plan, Half Yearly IDCW option (Scheme Code: 149675)  
- Sundaram Financial Services Opportunities Fund: Available in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 119597, 119596, 109059, 109058)  
- Sundaram Flexicap Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 150571, 150572, 150568, 150570)  
- Sundaram Focused Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and Half Yearly IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 149533, 149534, 149532, 149531)  
- Sundaram Large and Mid Cap Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 119566, 119565, 105001, 105000)  
- Sundaram Large Cap Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 148507, 148509, 148504, 148506)  
- Sundaram Mid Cap Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 119581, 119582, 101539, 101538)  
- Sundaram Multi Asset Allocation Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 152314, 152312, 152311, 152313)  
- Sundaram Multi Cap Fund: Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and Half Yearly IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 149669, 149668, 149667, 149666)  
- Sundaram Small Cap Fund: Direct Plan, Growth and IDCW options (Scheme Codes: 119589, 119588)  

These schemes provide investors with diversified options across market capitalizations and investment styles, catering to different risk appetites and income preferences. The IDCW options allow periodic income distribution, while Growth options reinvest earnings to enhance capital appreciation.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Sundaram Mutual Fund schemes available across various categories.  
- Plans offered: Direct and Regular.  
- Options available: Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), including Half Yearly IDCW in select schemes.  
- Scheme Codes provided for easy identification and investment.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity exposure and income options.
