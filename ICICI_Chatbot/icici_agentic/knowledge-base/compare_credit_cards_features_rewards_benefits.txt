CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Compare Credit Cards tool that enables customers to evaluate multiple credit cards based on their features and benefits. This tool helps users select the most suitable credit card according to their spending habits and preferences.  

How to Use the Compare Credit Cards Tool:  
1. Visit the Compare Credit Cards page on the ICICI Bank website.  
2. Select the credit cards you want to compare.  
3. Review the cards based on key parameters such as:  
   - Best usage categories (e.g., shopping, travel, dining)  
   - Reward points and redemption options  
   - Benefits and offers associated with each card  
4. Check additional details including how to maximize benefits, important card information, and application requirements.  

This comparison feature assists customers in making an informed decision by highlighting the differences and advantages of each credit card option.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Tool to compare multiple ICICI Bank credit cards side-by-side.  
- Comparison based on usage categories, reward points, benefits, and redemption options.  
- Helps identify the best card suited to individual spending patterns.  
- Provides additional information on maximizing benefits and application criteria.
