When planning to buy a home, understanding your eligibility for a home loan is crucial. Several factors influence your home loan eligibility, and ICICI Bank offers tools and insights to help you navigate this process efficiently.

Key factors affecting home loan eligibility include:  
- **Monthly Income:** Your income level directly impacts the loan amount you can avail.  
- **Age:** Lenders consider your age to determine the loan tenure and repayment capacity.  
- **Existing Financial Obligations:** Current loans or EMIs reduce your eligibility as they affect your repayment capacity.  
- **Loan Tenure:** The duration for which you want to take the loan influences the EMI and eligibility.
