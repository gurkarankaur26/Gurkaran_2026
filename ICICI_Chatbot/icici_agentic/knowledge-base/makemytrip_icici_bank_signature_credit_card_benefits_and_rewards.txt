TITLE: MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Signature Credit Card - Benefits and Rewards  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Rewards, Joining Fee, Lounge Access, MakeMyTrip, Travel Benefits  

CONTENT:  
The MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Signature Credit Card offers a range of benefits and rewards tailored for frequent travelers and MakeMyTrip users.  

Key features include:  
- Joining Fee: Rs. 2,500 + GST  
- On joining, receive Rs. 1,500 My Cash plus a MakeMyTrip holiday voucher worth Rs. 2,500  
- Complimentary MMTBLACK Exclusive membership  
- Complimentary international and domestic airport lounge access  
- Complimentary domestic railway lounge access  
- Extended validity of 1.5 years on Joining Benefit My Cash  

Note: Joining benefits are not applicable for cards with no joining fee and no annual fee.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Joining Fee: Rs. 2,500 + GST  
- Joining Benefits: Rs. 1,500 My Cash + Rs. 2,500 MakeMyTrip holiday voucher  
- Complimentary MMTBLACK Exclusive membership  
- Complimentary lounge access (international/domestic airports and domestic railways)  
- Extended validity of 1.5 years on Joining Benefit My Cash  
- Joining benefits excluded for no joining fee/annual fee cards
