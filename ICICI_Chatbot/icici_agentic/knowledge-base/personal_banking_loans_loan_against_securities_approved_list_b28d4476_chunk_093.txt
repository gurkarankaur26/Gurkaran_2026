UTI Liquid Fund and UTI Low Duration Fund - Overview and Details

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:

TAGS:  
UTI Liquid Fund, UTI Low Duration Fund, Mutual Funds, Dividend Plans, Growth Plans, Investment Options, IDCW, Direct Plan, Regular Plan

CONTENT:  
This document provides detailed information on various plans under the UTI Liquid Fund and UTI Low Duration Fund schemes. These funds offer multiple investment options including Dividend (IDCW) payout frequencies and Growth plans, available in both Regular and Direct plans.

**UTI Liquid Fund**  
- Available Plans:  
  - Regular Plan and Direct Plan  
  - Dividend Options: Monthly IDCW, Daily IDCW, Fortnightly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Half-Yearly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Flexible IDCW, Annual IDCW  
  - Growth Option available  
- Status: Some plans are discontinued (e.g., Monthly IDCW - Discontinued, Periodic IDCW - Discontinued)  
- Plan Codes: Examples include 102008 (Discontinued Monthly IDCW), 133973 (Regular Annual IDCW), 120304 (Direct Growth), 140517 (Regular Flexi IDCW)  

**UTI Low Duration Fund**  
- Available Plans:  
  - Direct Plan and Regular Plan  
  - Dividend Options: Annual IDCW, Daily IDCW, Fortnightly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Flexible IDCW, Half-Yearly IDCW  
  - Growth Option available  
  - Bonus Plan available in Regular Plan  
- Status: Some plans are discontinued (e.g., Annual IDCW - Discontinued, Bonus Plan - Discontinued)  
- Plan Codes: Examples include 141958 (Direct Annual IDCW), 120735 (Direct Growth), 102545 (Regular Bonus Plan)  

**Key Features:**  
- Multiple IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) payout frequencies to suit investor preferences  
- Availability of both Regular and Direct plans, allowing investors to choose based on their preference for distributor involvement  
- Growth options for capital appreciation without periodic income distribution  
- Some plans have been discontinued and are no longer available for investment  

KEY POINTS:  
- UTI Liquid Fund and UTI Low Duration Fund offer diversified investment options with various IDCW frequencies and growth plans.  
- Plans are available in both Regular and Direct formats.  
- Investors can select IDCW payout frequency as per their income needs: daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or annual.  
- Growth plans do not pay dividends but reinvest earnings to increase NAV.  
- Some plans have been discontinued; investors should verify current availability before investing.  
- Bonus plans are available under UTI Low Duration Fund Regular Plan but may be discontinued for some variants.  
- Each plan has a unique code for identification.
