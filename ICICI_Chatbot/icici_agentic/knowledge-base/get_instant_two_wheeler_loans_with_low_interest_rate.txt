CONTENT:  
Two-wheelers have become a popular mode of transport in both urban and rural India due to their affordability, ease of maneuvering, and convenience. To support this demand, banks and lenders offer two-wheeler loans at attractive interest rates, making it easier for customers to purchase bikes and scooters.

**Loan Amount:**  
- Loans are available up to 100% of the on-road price of the two-wheeler.  
- Earlier, loans were typically offered for 70%-80% of the vehicle cost.  
- Borrowers should carefully select the loan amount as higher loan amounts lead to higher interest payments.

**Interest Rates:**  
- Interest rates on two-wheeler loans generally range from 10% to 26%.  
- The final interest rate depends on factors such as the borrower’s relationship with the lender, loan amount, tenure, and CIBIL score.  
- It is advisable to choose reputed lenders offering competitive interest rates.

**Additional Charges:**  
- Apart from interest, borrowers may have to pay:  
  - Loan processing charges  
  - Documentation charges  
  - Registration certificate charges  
  - CIBIL report charges  
- These charges vary between lenders.

**Eligibility and Documentation:**  
- Eligibility criteria and required documents vary by lender.  
- Common documents required include:  
  - Identity proof  
  - Address proof  
  - Income proof  
  - Vehicle registration documents  
- Borrowers should verify eligibility and documentation requirements before applying to ensure faster loan approval.

**Application Process:**  
- Applications can be submitted by visiting the nearest branch of the lender.  
- Some lenders offer additional convenience such as applying via SMS or online platforms.  
- Many banks provide online tools like two-wheeler loan interest rate calculators to help borrowers estimate interest rates and EMIs for better transparency.

**Benefits:**  
- Easy access to funds for purchasing two-wheelers.  
- Competitive interest rates and flexible loan amounts.  
- Availability of pre-approved loan schemes for existing customers.  
- Online tools for better financial planning.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount up to 100% of on-road price.  
- Interest rates range from 10% to 26%, based on multiple factors.  
- Additional charges include processing, documentation, registration, and CIBIL fees.  
- Required documents: identity, address, income proof, vehicle registration.  
- Application via branch visit, SMS, or online.  
- Use online EMI calculators for transparency.  
- Pre-approved loans available for existing customers.
