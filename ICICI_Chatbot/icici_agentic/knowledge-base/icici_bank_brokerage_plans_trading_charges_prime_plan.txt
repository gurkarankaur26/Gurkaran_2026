ICICI Bank offers the Prime Plan under its ICICIdirect platform, designed to provide customers with lower brokerage charges and enhanced trading benefits. The Prime Plan features lifetime validity and is tailored for active traders seeking cost-effective and efficient trading solutions.

Key features of the Prime Plan include:  
- Brokerage rates as low as 0.07% on trades, enabling significant cost savings.  
- Payouts on selling shares are processed within 5 minutes, ensuring quick access to funds.  
- Access to exclusive, handpicked research reports to assist in informed trading decisions.  
- Specially reduced interest rates on Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL) and Margin Trading Funding (MTF), facilitating easier margin trading at lower costs.  
- One Click Equity facility that allows seamless investing, monitoring, and exiting of equity portfolios with a single click, enhancing user convenience and trading efficiency.

This plan is ideal for investors looking for a comprehensive trading experience with lower brokerage fees, faster payouts, and value-added research and margin trading benefits.
