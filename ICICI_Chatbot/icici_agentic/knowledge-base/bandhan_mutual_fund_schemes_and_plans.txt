TITLE: Bandhan Mutual Fund Schemes and Plans  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment Products  
TAGS: Bandhan Mutual Fund, Investment Plans, Growth Option, Dividend Option, Direct Plan, Regular Plan, IDCW, SIP, Mutual Fund Codes  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Bandhan Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include various plans under Bandhan Banking and PSU Fund, Bandhan Bond Fund, Bandhan Corporate Bond Fund, and Bandhan CRISIL IBX Gilt April 2028 Index Fund. Each scheme is available in different options such as Direct Plan and Regular Plan, with choices between Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options including Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Annual, Daily, and Fortnightly IDCW.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Plans:  
1. Bandhan Banking and PSU Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW (Monthly, Quarterly, Annual, Daily, Fortnightly)  

2. Bandhan Bond Fund  
   - Income Plan, Medium Term Plan, Short Term Plan  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Growth option only  

3. Bandhan Corporate Bond Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW (Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Annual, Periodic)  

4. Bandhan CRISIL IBX Gilt April 2028 Index Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 121279 for Bandhan Banking and PSU Fund - Dir - Growth). Investors can choose the plan and option that best suits their investment goals and income preferences.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Bandhan Mutual Fund schemes are available through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes offer Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment options include Growth and various IDCW frequencies (Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Annual, Daily, Fortnightly).  
- Suitable for investors seeking equity, bond, and gilt index fund exposure.  
- Each scheme and plan has a unique identification code for easy reference.  
- IDCW options provide periodic income distribution, while Growth options reinvest earnings.
