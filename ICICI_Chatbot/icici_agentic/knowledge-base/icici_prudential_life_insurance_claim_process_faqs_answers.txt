CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Claim Process provides a clear guide on how to report and file claims for life insurance policies.  

**How to Report a Claim:**  
- Online: Visit the claims section of ICICI Prudential Life website at www.iciciprulife.com and fill the online claim intimation form.  
- Phone: Call the 24x7 Claim Care number 1800 2660 (within India) between 10:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m., Monday to Saturday (except national holidays). Overseas customers can call +91 80693 85555.  
- Branch: Visit the nearest ICICI Prudential Life Insurance branch.  
- Email: Send claim intimation to claimsupport@iciciprulife.com.  
- SMS: Send SMS to 56767 from your registered mobile number.  

Note: A claim is formally registered only after receiving a written claim intimation at the Branch or Claims Cell.  

**Documents Required to File a Claim:**  
The documents vary depending on the claim type (death, maturity, or health). Generally, the mandatory documents include:  
- Duly filled and signed Claimant Statement Form  
- Recent photograph of the claimant  
- Copy of Death Certificate of the Life Assured (issued by government authority)  
- Claimant’s photo identity proof  
- Claimant’s address proof (any one of: Aadhar Card, Valid Passport, Driver's License, Voter ID)  
- PAN card or Form 60 of the claimant  
- Copy of cancelled cheque, bank statement, or bank passbook with printed account number and claimant’s name  
- Duly filled and signed Payout Mandate Form with claimant’s bank account details  

For a detailed list of documents, refer to the Claim Documents Checklist available on the ICICI Prudential Life website.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Claim reporting can be done online, via phone, email, SMS, or by visiting a branch.  
- 24x7 Claim Care number: 1800 2660 (India), +91 80693 85555 (overseas).  
- Written claim intimation is mandatory for formal registration.  
- Mandatory documents include claimant statement, death certificate, identity and address proofs, PAN/Form 60, bank details, and photograph.  
- Detailed document checklist is available on the official website.
