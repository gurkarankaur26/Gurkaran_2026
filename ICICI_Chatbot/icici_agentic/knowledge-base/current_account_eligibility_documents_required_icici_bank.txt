CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides detailed guidelines on the eligibility and documentation required to open a Current Account for various business entities including Individuals (for business purposes), Sole Proprietorship Firms, Hindu Undivided Families (HUF), Partnership Firms, and Private/Public Limited Companies.

**Documents Required for Current Account Opening**

1. **For Sole Proprietorship Firm:**  
   - Two government-issued documents in the name of the sole proprietorship firm confirming name and address (registration/license documents).  
   - Latest passport-size colour photograph of the sole proprietor.  
   - PAN Card of the sole proprietor.  
   - Proof of identity and proof of address of the sole proprietor.  
   - If Power of Attorney (POA) is granted for account operations, photograph, identity, and address proof of the POA holder.  
   - Account opening cheque from an existing Current Account.

2. **For Hindu Undivided Family (HUF):**  
   - Latest passport-size colour photograph of the karta/authorized signatories.  
   - Identity and address proof of the karta/authorized signatories.  
   - Current landline/mobile number and e-mail ID.  
   - Permanent Account Number (PAN) in the name of the HUF.  
   - HUF Letter (available as a PDF document).  
   - Account opening cheque from an existing Current Account or Karta’s Savings Account.

3. **For Partnership Firm:**  
   - Latest passport-size colour photographs of authorized signatories.  
   - Identity and address proof of authorized signatories.  
   - List of beneficial owners holding more than 10% in the firm (on letterhead).  
   - Identity and address proof of such beneficial owners.  
   - Proof of address of the entity.  
   - Permanent Account Number (PAN) of the entity.  
   - Current landline/mobile number and e-mail ID of the entity.  
   - Partnership deed.  
   - Partnership letter.  
   - Account opening cheque from an existing Current Account.  
   - **Note:** In case of change in partner(s), complete KYC documents need to be submitted.

4. **For Private/Public Limited Company (including One Person Company - OPC):**  
   - Certificate of incorporation.  
   - Memorandum of Association (MoA) and Articles of Association (AoA).  
   - ICICI Bank Board Resolution for opening the account.  
   - (Further documents typically required but not fully listed in the source.)

**Additional Information:**  
- All documents must be self-attested.  
- Proof of identity and address documents should be selected from the bank’s prescribed list of acceptable documents.  
- Account opening cheque should be from an existing current account to facilitate the process.

KEY POINTS:  
- Different business entities require specific sets of documents for current account opening.  
- PAN card and proof of identity/address are mandatory for all types of accounts.  
- Partnership firms must disclose beneficial owners holding more than 10%.  
- KYC compliance is mandatory and must be updated in case of changes in partners or authorized signatories.  
- Account opening cheque from an existing current account is required for verification.  
- HUF accounts require a specific HUF letter and PAN in the name of the HUF.
