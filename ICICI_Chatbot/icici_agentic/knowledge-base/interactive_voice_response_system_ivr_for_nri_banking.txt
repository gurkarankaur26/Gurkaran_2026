TITLE: Interactive Voice Response System (IVR) for NRI Banking  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: NRI Banking, Customer Service  
TAGS: IVR, NRI Banking, Customer Support, Account Services, Money Transfer, Loans, Cards  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers an Interactive Voice Response (IVR) system tailored for NRI customers to access a wide range of banking services conveniently and efficiently. The IVR system provides 24x7 automated assistance for various banking needs, enabling NRIs to manage their accounts and transactions without visiting a branch.

Key services accessible via the IVR system include:  
- Account Information: Check balances and recent transactions for NRE/NRO accounts.  
- Fixed Deposits: Information on Fixed Deposit schemes offering up to 7.2% returns.  
- Money Transfer: Access to Money2India, a cost-effective online money transfer service for sending money to India.  
- Loans and Cards: Digital sanctioning of loans and credit cards in 5 simple steps.  
- Investments: Information on Mutual Funds (SIP) and Portfolio Investment Scheme for NRIs to purchase and sell shares.  
- Payments: Scan and Pay services for seamless payments.  
- Digital Banking: Full control over bank accounts through digital platforms.  
- Business Banking: Services including cash management, bulk payments via I-multipay, merchant services with instant settlements, and trade finance for export-import transactions.  
- Customer Support: Assistance with queries related to accounts, cards, loans, and other banking products.

The IVR system is designed to provide quick, reliable, and secure access to banking services, ensuring NRIs can manage their finances anytime, anywhere.
