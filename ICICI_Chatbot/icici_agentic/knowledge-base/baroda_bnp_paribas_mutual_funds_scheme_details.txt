CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Baroda BNP Paribas mutual fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include various fund categories such as Arbitrage, Balanced Advantage, Flexi Cap, Health and Wellness, India Consumption, Large & Mid Cap, and Large Cap funds. Each scheme is available in different plans and options, including Direct and Regular plans, with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options offered monthly or quarterly.

Key schemes and their options include:  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Arbitrage Fund (Regular Plan) with Monthly and Quarterly IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Flexi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Health and Wellness Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas India Consumption Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Large & Mid Cap Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Baroda BNP Paribas Large Cap Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 150254 for Baroda BNP Paribas Arbitrage Fund - Reg - Mthly IDCW). Investors can choose between Growth options, which reinvest earnings, or IDCW options, which distribute income periodically.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund categories available under Baroda BNP Paribas Mutual Funds.  
- Options include Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment options: Growth and IDCW (monthly or quarterly).  
- Unique scheme codes for easy identification and transaction.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity and arbitrage fund options.  
- IDCW options provide periodic income distribution; Growth options reinvest earnings.
