TITLE: HSBC Mutual Funds - Scheme List and Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: HSBC, Mutual Funds, Investment, Growth, IDCW, Fund Codes  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of HSBC Mutual Fund schemes available for investment, including their plan types (Direct/Regular), options (Growth/IDCW), and unique scheme codes. These funds cover various categories such as Consumption, Financial Services, Flexi Cap, Focused, India Export Opportunities, Large & Mid Cap, Large Cap, Midcap, Multi Asset Allocation, and Multi Cap funds.

Each scheme is identified by:  
- Fund Name  
- Plan Type: Direct (Dir) or Regular (Reg)  
- Option: Growth or IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal)  
- Scheme Code (a unique numeric identifier)

KEY POINTS:  
- HSBC offers a wide range of mutual fund schemes catering to different investment needs and risk profiles.  
- Plans are available in Direct and Regular options, with Direct plans generally having lower expense ratios.  
- Options include Growth (reinvestment of earnings) and IDCW (periodic income distribution).  
- Scheme codes are essential for transactions and identification of specific funds.  
- Fund categories include sector-specific funds (e.g., Financial Services, Consumption), market cap-based funds (Large Cap, Midcap, Large & Mid Cap), and diversified funds (Flexi Cap, Multi Cap, Multi Asset Allocation).  
- Investors can choose schemes based on their investment goals, risk appetite, and preference for income or growth.
