TITLE: ICICI Bank Coral Credit Card – Complimentary Movie Tickets Offer and Eligibility  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: ICICI Bank, Coral Credit Card, Complimentary Tickets, BookMyShow, Eligibility, Spend Criteria, Add-on Card  

CONTENT:  
Starting April 1, 2026, ICICI Bank Coral Credit Cardholders can avail complimentary movie/event tickets on BookMyShow by meeting specific spend criteria. To be eligible for the complimentary tickets offer in a calendar quarter, cardholders must spend ₹25,000 or more on their Coral Credit Card during the preceding spends quarter.  

**Spends and Offer Eligibility Period:**  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Dec 26, 2025 and Mar 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets between Apr 1, 2026 and June 30, 2026.  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Mar 26, 2026 and June 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets between July 1, 2026 and Sep 30, 2026.  

**Additional Details:**  
- Customers holding dual network credit cards (Mastercard & Amex or Visa & RuPay) can avail one complimentary movie offer per month, per variant.  
- Any additional bookings beyond the complimentary benefit will be charged as per rates set by the service provider.  
- Add-on/Plus card members must individually meet the ₹25,000 spend requirement in the preceding quarter to be eligible for the offer in the subsequent quarter.  

For booking movie tickets and detailed terms and conditions, customers are advised to refer to the official ICICI Bank website.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility for complimentary movie tickets requires ₹25,000 spend in the preceding quarter.  
- Complimentary tickets valid only in the following calendar quarter after meeting spend criteria.  
- Dual network cardholders can avail one complimentary ticket per month, per variant.  
- Add-on cardholders must meet spend criteria individually.  
- Additional tickets beyond complimentary ones are chargeable.
