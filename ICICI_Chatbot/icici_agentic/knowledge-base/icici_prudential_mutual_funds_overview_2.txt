TITLE: ICICI Prudential Mutual Funds Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: ICICI Prudential, Mutual Funds, Equity Funds, Growth, IDCW, Investment, Fund Options  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential offers a wide range of mutual fund schemes catering to different investment needs and preferences. These funds are available in both Direct and Regular plans, with options for Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) payout modes. The schemes cover various sectors and strategies including equity savings, ESG exclusionary strategy, exports and services, flexicap, FMCG, focused equity, housing opportunities, India opportunities, and infrastructure.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- ICICI Prudential Equity Savings Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & Quarterly IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential ESG Exclusionary Strategy Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Exports and Services Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Flexicap Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential FMCG Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Focused Equity Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Housing Opportunities Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential India Opportunities Fund (Direct & Regular; Growth & IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Infrastructure Fund (Direct; Growth & IDCW)  

Each fund has a unique scheme code for identification and investment purposes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund options available under ICICI Prudential Mutual Funds.  
- Plans available in Direct and Regular modes.  
- Investment options include Growth and IDCW payout modes.  
- Sector-specific funds such as FMCG, Infrastructure, Housing, and ESG strategies.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity and thematic investment opportunities.  
- Scheme codes provided for easy reference and transaction.
