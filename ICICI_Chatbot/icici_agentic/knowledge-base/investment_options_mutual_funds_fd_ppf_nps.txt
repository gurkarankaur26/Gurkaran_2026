CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of flexible investment options designed to help customers meet both short-term and long-term financial goals. Starting early with these plans can help unlock better future returns. The key investment products include Mutual Funds, Fixed Deposits (FD), Public Provident Fund (PPF), and National Pension System (NPS).

**Mutual Funds**  
- Diversify your portfolio with various mutual fund schemes.  
- Suitable for different risk appetites and investment horizons.  
- Professional fund management to optimize returns.

**Fixed Deposits (FD)**  
- Secure investment with guaranteed returns.  
- Flexible tenure options ranging from 7 days to 10 years.  
- Interest rates vary based on tenure and prevailing market conditions.  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with applicable penalties.  
- Interest payout options: monthly, quarterly, annually, or on maturity.

**Public Provident Fund (PPF)**  
- Long-term savings scheme with tax benefits under Section 80C.  
- Tenure of 15 years, extendable in blocks of 5 years.  
- Interest rate is compounded annually and is subject to government revision quarterly.  
- Minimum annual contribution: ₹500; maximum: ₹1.5 lakh.  
- Loan and partial withdrawal facilities available after certain years.

**National Pension System (NPS)**  
- Voluntary retirement savings scheme.  
- Contributions eligible for tax benefits under Section 80CCD.  
- Flexible investment options in equity, corporate bonds, government securities, and alternative assets.  
- Regular contributions help build a retirement corpus.  
- Partial withdrawals allowed under specific conditions.

**Benefits of Investing with ICICI Bank**  
- Wide range of investment products to suit different financial goals.  
- Expert advisory and portfolio management services.  
- Convenient online and branch banking facilities.  
- Transparent fee structures and competitive interest rates.  
- Early investment advantage to maximize returns.
