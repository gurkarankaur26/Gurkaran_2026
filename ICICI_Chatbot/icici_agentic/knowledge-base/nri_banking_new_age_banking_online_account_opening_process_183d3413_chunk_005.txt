TITLE:  
ICICI Bank NRI Services: Address & Signature Update, E-Statement Registration, and Fund Repatriation

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY:  
NRI Banking Services, Account Management, Fund Repatriation  

TAGS:  
NRI Banking, Address Change, Signature Update, E-Statement, Fund Repatriation, Net Banking, Customer Care  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides convenient services for NRIs to update their address, signature, email ID, register for e-statements, and repatriate funds. These services can be accessed online, by post, or by visiting any ICICI Bank branch in India.

**Address and Signature Update:**  
- NRIs can update their address or signature by submitting the relevant request forms along with supporting documents.  
- For signature update, download the 'Signature Change' request form, complete it, and attach proof of changed signature.  
- Documents can be posted to the local ICICI Bank P.O. Box in countries including USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE to save on courier charges.  
- Alternatively, documents can be submitted at any ICICI Bank branch in India.

**Email ID Update:**  
- NRIs can update their email ID by logging into their Net Banking account and placing the request online.

**E-Statement Registration:**  
- NRIs can register for e-statements online via Internet Banking.  
- Alternatively, download the 'E-Statement Registration' form and mail it to the above India address or submit it at any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- Posting documents to local P.O. Boxes in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE is also accepted.

**Fund Repatriation:**  
- NRIs can repatriate funds through two modes: Demand Draft or Wire Transfer.  
- Repatriation requests for fixed deposits can be placed online via the "Service Request" option in Net Banking.  
- Alternatively, download the 'Outward Repatriation of Funds' request form and submit it as per instructions.

**Customer Support:**  
- For any clarifications or assistance, NRIs can contact ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care or use the "Email us" option on the official website www.icicibank.in/nri.

KEY POINTS:  
- Address and signature updates can be done by post (local P.O. Box in select countries) or at any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- Signature update requires a completed form and proof of changed signature.  
- Email ID updates and e-statement registrations are available online via Net Banking.  
- Fund repatriation can be done via Demand Draft or Wire Transfer; requests can be placed online or by submitting a form.  
- 24/7 Customer Care support is available for all NRI banking queries.
