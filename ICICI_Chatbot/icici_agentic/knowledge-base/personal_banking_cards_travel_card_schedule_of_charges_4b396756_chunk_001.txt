Forex Prepaid Card Fees & Charges - ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Forex Prepaid Card offers transparent fee structures for various transactions including issuance, ATM withdrawals, reloads, and cross-currency transactions. Below is a detailed schedule of charges and important guidelines to help you manage your card usage efficiently.

**Fee Structure:**

| Charge Type                          | Details                                                                                  |
|------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Transaction Fee (POS/Online)        | NIL                                                                                      |
| Cross Currency Fee                  | 3.5% + GST (applicable on all transaction types where billing currency differs from card currency) |
| Cross Currency Transaction          | A transaction where the billing currency differs from the card currency                   |
| Wallet to Wallet Transfer Fee       | 2.5% of transfer amount (charged to the destination wallet)                              |
| Cash Advance / Cash@POS Transactions Fee | 0.5% of transaction amount (withdraw cash by swiping card at merchant outlets)          |
| Lost Card Replacement Fee           | USD 20.00 (applicable for international locations)                                      |
| Fee for Uncapped ATM Withdrawals    | 1% of withdrawal amount                                                                  |
| Taxes                              | All fees and charges are exclusive of taxes. GST at 18% effective from March 1, 2020      |

**Daily ATM Withdrawal Limits and Fees by Currency:**

| Currency | Daily ATM Withdrawal Limit | ATM Withdrawal Fee |
|----------|----------------------------|--------------------|
| USD      | USD 2,000                  | USD 2.00           |
| EUR      | EUR 1,500                  | EUR 1.50           |
| GBP      | GBP 1,000                  | GBP 1.50           |
| AUD      | AUD 2,000                  | AUD 2.00           |
| CAD      | CAD 2,000                  | CAD 2.00           |

**Important Notes:**

- Cash withdrawal limits may vary depending on the ATM abroad and acquiring bank limits, which may be lower than ICICI Bank’s limits.
- Some international banks may levy additional surcharges on ATM transactions. Check ATM screen information for details.
- Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) may involve additional third-party currency conversion charges. DCC allows customers to choose the currency for payment at POS, online, or ATM.
- ICICI Bank does not benefit from DCC charges except for currency conversion from billing currency to active wallet currency.
- Customers should carefully review and confirm DCC charges before proceeding with transactions.
- To avoid extra charges, it is recommended to:
  - Choose to pay in local currency during POS or online transactions.
  - When withdrawing cash at ATMs, select “Accept without currency conversion” to avoid DCC charges.

KEY POINTS:  
- No transaction fee on POS/online payments.  
- Cross currency fee is 3.5% + GST on all transactions involving currency conversion.  
- Wallet to wallet transfers incur 2.5% fee on the receiving wallet.  
- Cash withdrawals at merchants cost 0.5% of the transaction amount.  
- Lost card replacement internationally costs USD 20.  
- ATM withdrawal fees vary by currency, with daily limits specified.  
- DCC transactions may incur additional charges; paying in local currency is advised to save costs.  
- GST of 18% applies on all fees and charges.
