**Fixed Deposit (FD)**  
- A simple investment product offering safety, liquidity, flexibility, and assured returns.  
- Multiple FD variants available catering to a wide range of customers.  
- Can be opened via iMobile, Net Banking, ATM, and other channels.  
- Minimum deposit amount: ₹10,000.  
- FD tenure starts from 7 days.  
- Loan facility available up to 90% of the FD amount.

**Recurring Deposit (RD)**  
- Helps save consistently and conveniently for future benefits.  
- Enables tracking of recurring transactions to plan and monitor long-term investment goals.  
- Loan facility available against the RD account.

**iWish (Goal-Based Savings Product)**  
- Available to ICICI Bank savings account holders.  
- Allows creation of specific financial goals and flexible deposits of any amount at any time.  
- Offers assured returns.  
- Goal amount range: ₹5,000 to ₹5,00,000.  
- Tenure range: 6 months to 5 years.
