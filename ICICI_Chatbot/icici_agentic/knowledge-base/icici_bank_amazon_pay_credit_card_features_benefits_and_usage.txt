TITLE: ICICI Bank Amazon Pay Credit Card - Features, Benefits, and Usage  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards / Digital Payments  
TAGS: ICICI Bank, Amazon Pay, Credit Card, Rewards, Digital Payments, Visa, Cashback, Eligibility, Fees  

CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Amazon Pay Credit Card offers customers the ability to earn rewards on purchases made across Amazon.in and with Amazon Pay partner merchants. Cardholders can use their reward earnings to purchase from over 16 crore items on Amazon.in or for transactions such as flight tickets, hotel bookings, food delivery, movie tickets, and more through Amazon Pay.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- Earn rewards on every purchase made on Amazon.in and with Amazon Pay partner merchants.  
- Rewards can be redeemed for a wide range of products and services available on Amazon.in.  
- Amazon Pay integration allows for convenient, one-click digital payments, enhancing the checkout experience.  
- Supports cash-load feature to facilitate digital transactions for customers who prefer cash payments, promoting a less-cash society.  

**About ICICI Bank:**  
ICICI Bank Ltd is a leading private sector bank in India with consolidated total assets of ₹15,73,812 crore as of March 31, 2021. The bank operates across 15 countries and has subsidiaries in insurance, asset management, securities brokerage, and private equity.  

**About Amazon Pay:**  
Amazon Pay is a digital payment solution that enables seamless and secure payments on and off Amazon.in. It supports the Government of India’s vision of promoting electronic payments and reducing cash transactions.  

**About Visa Inc.:**  
Visa Inc. is a global leader in digital payments, operating VisaNet, a secure and reliable payment network capable of processing over 65,000 transaction messages per second. Visa focuses on innovation to support the growth of digital commerce worldwide.
