Changes in India’s sovereign rating and other risks detailed in the bank’s filings with the United States Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) may also affect future outcomes.

The bank does not undertake any obligation to update these statements to reflect events or circumstances occurring after the date of release. For a comprehensive description of risks, refer to ICICI Bank’s filings with the SEC, available at www.sec.gov.
