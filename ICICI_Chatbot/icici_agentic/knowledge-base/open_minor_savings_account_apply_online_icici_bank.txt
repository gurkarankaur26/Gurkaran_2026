CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Minor Savings Account designed specifically for children and teenagers. This account can be operated in three ways: guardian-operated, self-operated with guardian consent, or independently operated once the minor attains a certain age.

**Account Types:**  
1. **Guardian-operated Account:**  
   - The account is operated by a parent or legal guardian on behalf of the minor.  
   - Suitable for minors below 10 years of age.  

2. **Self-operated Account with Guardian Consent:**  
   - Minors aged 10 years and above can operate the account with the consent of their guardian.  
   - Allows the minor to learn banking and manage finances under supervision.  

3. **Independent Operation:**  
   - Once the minor turns 18, the account can be converted to a regular savings account and operated independently.  

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- Competitive interest rates on savings.  
- Easy online application process.  
- Access to debit cards (subject to eligibility and guardian consent).  
- Internet and mobile banking facilities for easy account management.  
- Nomination facility available.  
- Helps inculcate financial discipline and savings habits from a young age.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Minors below 18 years of age.  
- Guardian must be a parent or legal guardian for guardian-operated accounts.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- No minimum balance requirement for minor savings accounts.  
- Standard charges applicable for cheque books, debit cards, and other services as per ICICI Bank’s schedule of charges.  

**Interest Rates:**  
- Interest is calculated on the daily closing balance and credited quarterly.  
- Rates are competitive and subject to change as per RBI guidelines.  

**Process to Open Account:**  
- Apply online through the ICICI Bank website or visit the nearest branch.  
- Submit KYC documents of the minor and guardian (proof of identity, address, and relationship).  
- Guardian consent required for self-operated accounts.  
- Account activation and welcome kit provided upon successful verification.  

**FAQs:**  
- *Can a minor operate the account independently?*  
  Yes, minors aged 10 and above can operate the account with guardian consent. Full independent operation is allowed after turning 18.  

- *Is there a minimum balance requirement?*  
  No, there is no minimum balance requirement for minor savings accounts.  

- *Can the minor get a debit card?*  
  Yes, debit cards can be issued with guardian consent and subject to bank policies.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minor Savings Account can be guardian-operated, self-operated with consent, or independently operated after 18.  
- No minimum balance requirement.  
- Competitive interest rates with quarterly interest credit.  
- Online application available.  
- Debit card and internet banking facilities available.  
- Helps minors learn financial management early.
