TITLE:  
Nippon India Mutual Funds - Fund Options and Plans

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Nippon India, Investment Plans, IDCW, Growth, Duration Funds, Interest Payouts

CONTENT:  
This document provides a detailed list of Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include various fund options categorized by duration, plan type (Direct or Regular), and dividend payout frequency (Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Annually, Daily, Weekly). The schemes are primarily focused on Long Duration Funds, Overnight Funds, Short Duration Funds, and Ultra Short Duration Funds.

Key Fund Options:  
1. Nippon India Nivesh Lakshya Long Duration Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend payout options: Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Half-Yearly IDCW, Annual IDCW, Growth option  
   - Scheme codes range from 143702 to 143712  

2. Nippon India Overnight Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend payout options: Daily IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Growth option  
   - Scheme codes range from 145810 to 145833  

3. Nippon India Short Duration Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend payout options: Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Growth option  
   - Scheme codes range from 101665 to 125269  

4. Nippon India Ultra Short Duration Fund  
   - Available in Direct plan  
   - Dividend payout option: Daily IDCW  
   - Scheme code 143500  

Note: IDCW stands for Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal, indicating dividend payout options.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes are available through ICICI Bank for different investment horizons and dividend preferences.  
- Investors can choose between Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend payout frequencies include Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, and Annually, along with Growth options where dividends are reinvested.  
- Scheme codes are provided for easy identification and transaction purposes.  
- These funds cater to investors looking for long duration, overnight, short duration, and ultra-short duration investment options.
