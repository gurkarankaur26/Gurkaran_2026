TITLE: Comparison of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, and iWish Flexible RD  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Products, Deposits, Investment Options  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, iWish, Tax Saving FD, Premature Withdrawal, Interest Rates, Penalties, Investment Options  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers three primary deposit investment products: Fixed Deposit (FD), Recurring Deposit (RD), and iWish Flexible RD. Each product caters to different investment needs and preferences.

**Fixed Deposit (FD):**  
- Investment: One-time lump sum deposit.  
- Deposit Amount: Multiples of 100 INR.  
- Deposit Date: Once at the start of the investment.  
- Number of Deposits: Not applicable (single deposit).  
- Premature Withdrawal: Available with or without premature withdrawal facility. Premature closure may attract penalties and adjusted interest rates.  
- Tax Benefits: Tax Saving FD available with a lock-in period of 5 years, eligible for tax benefits under Section 80C. Interest rates on tax-saving FDs are higher than regular FDs.  

**Recurring Deposit (RD):**  
- Investment: Fixed monthly instalments.  
- Deposit Amount: Multiples of 100 INR.  
- Deposit Date: A fixed date every month.  
- Number of Deposits: One per month.  
- Penalty for Missing Deposits: Yes, Rs. 12 penalty for every Rs. 1000 on the interest rate.  
- Premature Withdrawal: Allowed with penalty and interest adjustment.  
- Tax Benefits: No tax benefits on RD.  

**iWish Flexible RD:**  
- Investment: Flexible monthly contributions; any amount and any number of times per month.  
- Deposit Date: Any date you wish.  
- Penalty for Missing Deposits: No penalties; you can skip deposits anytime.  
- Premature Withdrawal: Allowed with penalty and interest adjustment.  
- Tax Benefits: Tax saving benefits available in iWish.  

**Choosing the Right Product:**  
- If you have a lump sum amount to invest immediately, choose an FD.  
- For tax-saving on FD, opt for Tax Saving FD with a 5-year lock-in.  
- If you can only invest small fixed amounts monthly, RD is suitable.  
- If you want flexibility in monthly contributions or may skip some months, iWish Flexible RD is ideal.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FD is a one-time lump sum investment; RD and iWish involve monthly deposits.  
- FD offers tax-saving options under Section 80C with a 5-year lock-in.  
- RD penalizes missed monthly instalments; iWish does not.  
- Premature withdrawal is allowed in all products but may attract penalties and interest adjustments.  
- iWish offers flexible monthly contributions with no penalties for missed deposits.  
- Choose the product based on your investment amount, frequency, and tax-saving needs.
