TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card Benefits, Offers, and Card Management  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Cards, Offers, Fuel Surcharge Waiver, BookMyShow, Travel Insurance, Card Renewal, Card Replacement  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of credit cards with various benefits including fuel surcharge waivers, accelerated reward points on fuel spends, cashback at partner fuel stations, complimentary movie ticket offers, travel insurance, and easy card management services.

**Fuel Credit Cards:**  
- ICICI Bank fuel credit cards such as the HPCL Super Saver Credit Card provide benefits like fuel surcharge waiver, accelerated reward points on fuel spends, and cashback at partner fuel stations.

**BookMyShow Offers:**  
- To avail BookMyShow offers, select your movie, seats, location, and showtime, proceed to payment, accept terms and conditions, open the Offers/Apply Promo Codes section, choose Credit Cards, search for ICICI, select the applicable offer (e.g., “Up to ₹500 off on 2nd ticket” or “25% off up to ₹100 on 2nd ticket”), enter your complete Credit Card number, and complete the payment.  
- Complimentary movie ticket value is capped as per the specific offer and card variant. Limits vary based on the ICICI Bank Credit Card type.  
- Offers are available all days, subject to terms and daily quota availability.  
- If a transaction fails, wait 20 minutes and retry. If the message states “Stock for the day is exhausted,” the offer inventory is depleted.  
- In case of booking failure but charge deduction, BookMyShow will initiate a reversal request the same day. Contact Customer Care if reversal is delayed.

**Travel Insurance:**  
- Some ICICI Bank Credit Cards offer complimentary travel insurance, available only on select card variants.  
- Eligibility, coverage limits, and claim procedures are subject to the card’s terms and conditions.  
- Detailed policy coverage and claim procedures are available on the respective credit card product page.

**Card Dispatch and Delivery:**  
- Customers receive SMS and email alerts with courier name and airway bill number when the card is dispatched.  
- The first delivery attempt is made within 5 working days from the date of request.

**Card Renewal:**  
- Renewal Credit Cards are typically dispatched about 10 days before the current card’s expiry date.  
- The existing card remains valid until the last day of the month printed on the card.  
- If the renewal card is not received, customers can raise a non-receipt request via Net Banking or by contacting Customer Care.

**Card Replacement:**  
- Replacement Credit Cards can be requested by calling Customer Care from the registered mobile number.  
- Alternatively, re-dispatch requests can be made through Net Banking under Cards > Credit Cards > Manage Your Credit Card > View All > Replacement or renewal card not received.

KEY POINTS:  
- Fuel credit cards offer surcharge waivers, reward points, and cashback on fuel spends.  
- BookMyShow offers include up to ₹500 off on the 2nd ticket or 25% off up to ₹100 on the 2nd ticket, subject to card variant and daily quota.  
- Complimentary travel insurance is available on select credit cards with specific eligibility and coverage.  
- Card dispatch notifications include courier details via SMS and email.  
- Renewal cards are sent 10 days before expiry; existing cards remain valid until expiry month-end.  
- Card replacement and re-dispatch can be requested via Net Banking or Customer Care.  
- Transaction failures on offers have specific retry and reversal procedures.
