ICICI Bank Ltd (BSE: ICICIBANK, NSE: ICICIBANK, NYSE: IBN) is a leading private sector bank in India. As of December 31, 2020, the bank’s consolidated total assets stood at ₹15,19,353 crore. ICICI Bank operates across 15 countries, including India, and has a diversified presence through its subsidiaries in insurance, asset management, securities brokerage, and private equity.

The bank’s subsidiaries include some of India’s leading private sector companies in insurance, asset management, and securities brokerage, as well as one of the country’s largest private equity firms.

ICICI Bank is committed to transparency and compliance with applicable securities laws.
