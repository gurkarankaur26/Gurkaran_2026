TITLE: Nippon India Mutual Fund Schemes  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Nippon India, Investment, Growth, Dividend, Index Fund, Large Cap, Mid Cap  

CONTENT:  
This document lists various Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include different fund types such as Flexi Cap Fund, Focused Fund, Growth Mid Cap Fund, Index Fund (Nifty 50 Plan), Innovation Fund, and Large Cap Fund. Each scheme is available in different plans and options including Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg) plans, with options for Growth, Dividend (IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), and Bonus.

Key schemes and their options include:  
- Nippon India Flexi Cap Fund: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Nippon India Focused Fund: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Nippon India Growth Mid Cap Fund: Available in Direct, Regular, and IP (Investment Plan) with Bonus, Growth, and IDCW options.  
- Nippon India Index Fund - Nifty 50 Plan: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth, Bonus, and IDCW options (Annual, Half Yearly, Quarterly).  
- Nippon India Innovation Fund: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Nippon India Large Cap Fund: Available in Direct plan with Bonus, Growth, and IDCW options.  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction processing.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes are available through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes are offered in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment options include Growth, Dividend (IDCW), and Bonus.  
- Dividend payout frequencies include Annual, Half Yearly, and Quarterly for some schemes.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each plan and option.  
- Suitable for investors looking for diversified equity mutual fund options including flexi cap, focused, mid cap, index, innovation, and large cap funds.
