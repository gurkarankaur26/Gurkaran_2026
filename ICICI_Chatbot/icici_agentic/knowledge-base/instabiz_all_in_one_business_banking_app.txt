CONTENT:  
InstaBIZ is ICICI Bank’s all-in-one business banking app designed to help businesses manage their finances anytime and anywhere. The app offers a comprehensive suite of digital banking services tailored for business needs, enabling quick payments, instant account access, and seamless banking operations directly from a mobile device.

Key features of the InstaBIZ app include:  
- **Payment Solutions:** Make quick and secure payments to vendors, employees, and other business partners.  
- **Trade Solutions:** Access trade finance services to support import/export and other trade-related transactions.  
- **Merchant Solutions:** Manage merchant payments and collections efficiently through integrated digital tools.

The app empowers businesses to streamline their financial operations, reduce dependency on physical branches, and enhance overall banking convenience.

KEY POINTS:  
- InstaBIZ is a mobile app for business banking by ICICI Bank.  
- Enables quick payments and instant access to business accounts.  
- Provides trade finance solutions for import/export businesses.  
- Offers merchant payment and collection management tools.  
- Designed for anytime, anywhere business financial management.
