TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Features, Benefits, and Application  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, Credit Card, Rewards, No Annual Fee, Forex Markup, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card is designed to offer customers unlimited, never-expiring rewards on everyday purchases, with a special focus on Amazon transactions. Cardholders can earn up to 5% rewards on Amazon purchases, which are seamlessly credited to their Amazon Pay balance, enhancing the shopping experience on the platform.

Key Features:  
- No Joining Fee  
- No Annual Fee  
- Low Forex Markup of 1.99%  

Benefits:  
- Earn unlimited rewards on all purchases  
- Up to 5% rewards on Amazon purchases credited directly to Amazon Pay balance  
- Rewards never expire, allowing flexible redemption  
- Low foreign exchange markup fee, making it cost-effective for international transactions  

Eligibility and Application:  
- Available to eligible Indian residents (specific eligibility criteria can be checked on the ICICI Bank website)  
- Application can be completed online through the ICICI Bank official portal  

Fees and Charges:  
- No joining fee  
- No annual fee  
- Forex markup fee at 1.99% on international transactions  

Process to Apply:  
- Visit the ICICI Bank official website  
- Navigate to the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card page  
- Fill in the online application form with required personal and financial details  
- Submit documents as per the instructions  
- Await approval and card dispatch  

FAQs:  
Q: Are there any fees associated with the card?  
A: No joining or annual fees apply. A forex markup fee of 1.99% is charged on international transactions.  

Q: How are rewards credited?  
A: Rewards earned on Amazon purchases are credited directly to the Amazon Pay balance.  

Q: Do rewards expire?  
A: No, the rewards earned on this card never expire.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Unlimited, never-expiring rewards on all purchases  
- Up to 5% rewards on Amazon purchases credited to Amazon Pay balance  
- No joining or annual fees  
- Low forex markup fee of 1.99%  
- Online application available  
- Suitable for frequent Amazon shoppers and international travelers
