TITLE: Overview of Loan Market Growth and Collateralisation Options  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans and Credit  
TAGS: Loans, Collateral, Credit Access, Household Income, Government Policies, Capital  

CONTENT:  
The loan market has experienced significant growth in recent years, driven by multiple factors such as rising household incomes, increased access to credit, and favourable government policies. Customers are increasingly seeking ways to collateralise their assets to access quick capital efficiently. Collateralised loans provide borrowers with the opportunity to leverage their assets to obtain funds at competitive interest rates and flexible terms. This trend reflects the evolving financial landscape where credit availability is expanding, enabling more individuals and businesses to meet their financial needs promptly.

KEY POINTS:  
- Significant growth in the loan market in recent years.  
- Key drivers: rising household income, increased credit access, favourable government policies.  
- Collateralisation of assets is a common method to access quick capital.  
- Collateralised loans offer competitive interest rates and flexible terms.  
- Growing credit availability supports diverse financial needs of customers.
