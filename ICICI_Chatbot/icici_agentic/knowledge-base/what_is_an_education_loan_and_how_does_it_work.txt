TITLE: What is an Education Loan, and How Does it Work?  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Education Loan  
TAGS: Education Loan, Student Loan, Loan Eligibility, Loan Benefits, Loan Process, Interest Rates, Repayment, Collateral, Tax Benefits  

CONTENT:  
An education loan is a financial product designed to help students fund their education in India or abroad. ICICI Bank offers education loans with competitive interest rates and flexible terms to make quality education affordable.  

**Key Features of ICICI Bank Education Loans:**  
- Unlimited Income Tax benefits under Section 80(E) on the full interest paid.  
- Collateral-free loans up to Rs 4 lakh for select institutes and courses.  

**Loan Amount and Coverage:**  
- Loan amounts range from a minimum of Rs 1 lakh to Rs 1 crore.  
- Up to Rs 50 lakh for education in India.  
- Up to Rs 1 crore for education abroad.  
- Loans available for both undergraduate and postgraduate courses.  
- In certain cases, 100% financing is available.  
- Covers tuition fees, library fees, hostel fees, laboratory fees, cost of books, laptops, travel expenses, and other related costs.  

**Repayment Terms:**  
- Flexible repayment options with tenure up to 12 years after course completion.  
- Repayment typically starts after a moratorium period of 6 months to 1 year post course completion.  
- Repayment tenure can be extended from 5 to 7 years in some cases.  

**Eligibility and Documentation:**  
- Applicant must provide:  
  - Marksheet and passing certificates of 10th and 12th exams.  
  - Admission letter from the college/university.  
  - Fee structure of the course.  
  - KYC documents of applicant and co-applicant.  
  - Income proof (in some cases).  
- Parents and siblings can be co-applicants.  

**Collateral and Guarantor Requirements:**  
- Loans up to Rs 4 lakh: No collateral required.  
- Loans from Rs 4 lakh to Rs 7.5 lakh: Third-party guarantor required.  
- Loans above Rs 7.5 lakh: Collateral mandatory.  
- For loans to study abroad, insurance is mandatory.  

**Loan Disbursement:**  
- The loan amount is directly paid to the educational institution at the start of the semester.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Education loans help cover all education-related expenses including tuition, travel, and equipment.  
- Tax benefits under Section 80(E) apply on the interest paid.  
- Flexible repayment tenure up to 12 years post course completion.  
- Collateral-free loans available up to Rs 4 lakh; collateral required for higher amounts.  
- Co-applicants allowed to improve eligibility.  
- Insurance mandatory for loans for studying abroad.
