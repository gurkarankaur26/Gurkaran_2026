ICICI Bank Current Account Opening and Business Banking Information

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

Business Banking, Current Accounts, Account Opening

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of current accounts tailored to various business types including private/public limited companies, LLPs, partnerships, proprietorships, trusts, societies, exporters, government-tender entities, startups, and MSMEs. The bank provides specialized current account variants such as GEM, RERA, and escrow accounts to meet sector-specific requirements.

**Eligibility:**  
- Private/public limited companies  
- LLPs  
- Partnerships  
- Proprietorships  
- Trusts and societies (registered)  
- Exporters and importers  
- Government-tender entities  
- Startups and MSMEs  

**Documentation Requirements:**  
- KYC documents of promoters/directors (PAN, passport, Aadhaar)  
- Proof of business address  
- GST registration certificate (if applicable)  
- Board resolution or authorization letter  
- Bankers’ reference  
- Recent photographs  
- Proprietorships require proprietor’s personal KYC, proof of business address, firm registration (if any), and business proofs such as invoices or trade licenses  
- Exporters/importers must provide Import Export Code (IEC), customs registration, past export documents or foreign inward remittance proofs, and trade-specific licenses  
- FATCA/CRS self-declarations for global tax compliance where applicable  
- Alternate business proofs accepted for GST-exempt or new businesses without GST  

**Account Opening Process:**  
- Digital onboarding available for many business customers, subject to regulatory and customer-type conditions  
- Digital onboarding includes e-KYC, scanned document uploads, and Relationship Manager (RM) assistance  
- Most documentation and verification take place offline at the branch or through the RM  
- Account activation typically takes a few business days depending on document completeness and regulatory checks  

**Features and Benefits:**  
- Multiple current accounts allowed for companies and firms to segregate cash flows (e.g., payroll, collections, project accounts)  
- Integrated solutions for startups and MSMEs including payroll, collections, and merchant onboarding  
- Consolidated reporting and reconciliation support by Business Banking team  
- Product variants matched to business scale and turnover with fixed minimum turnover/eligibility criteria applied  
- Guidance provided by ICICI Bank RM on the best current account type based on legal form and transaction profile  

KEY POINTS:  
- Comprehensive current account options for diverse business types  
- Simplified documentation for proprietorships, startups, and MSMEs  
- Digital onboarding reduces paperwork and speeds up account activation  
- Exporters/importers have specialized documentation and trade finance support  
- GST registration required if applicable; alternatives accepted for GST-exempt businesses  
- FATCA/CRS compliance mandatory for applicable entities  
- Multiple current accounts allowed for better cash flow management  
- Account activation within a few business days after document submission  
- Dedicated RM support for product selection and account management
