TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Mutual Fund Investment Options and Details

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Investment Options, Fund Codes, Growth, IDCW, Direct Plan, Regular Plan

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of mutual fund schemes available through ICICI Bank, including fund names, plan types, dividend options, and unique identification codes. The funds cover various asset classes such as multi-asset allocation, multicap, natural resources, index funds, small cap, value funds, and overseas equity funds.

Each fund is available in different plan types:  
- Direct Plan (Dir)  
- Regular Plan (Reg)  

Dividend options include:  
- Growth (Gth or Growth)  
- IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal)

The document lists the fund names along with their corresponding unique codes, which are essential for investment and transaction purposes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual funds are categorized by fund house (e.g., DSP) and fund type (e.g., Multi Asset Allocation, Multicap, Small Cap).  
- Each fund has a unique code for identification during transactions.  
- Plans are available in Direct and Regular options.  
- Dividend options include Growth and IDCW.  
- Investors can choose funds based on their investment goals and risk appetite.  
- The list includes both domestic and overseas equity funds.  
- Important for investors to use the correct fund code when investing or redeeming units.
