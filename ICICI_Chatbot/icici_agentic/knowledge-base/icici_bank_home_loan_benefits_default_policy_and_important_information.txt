CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Home Loans with various benefits that can help reduce the overall cost of borrowing. In case of default in Home Loan EMI payments, the following applies:

- Borrowers may incur penalty charges for missed EMI payments.  
- Defaulting on EMI payments can negatively impact the borrower’s credit score.  
- The bank generally provides a grace period to allow borrowers to make payments without penalty.  
- For borrowers facing genuine financial difficulties, ICICI Bank offers options for loan restructuring to ease repayment.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Penalty charges apply on defaulted EMI payments.  
- Default affects credit score adversely.  
- Grace period is usually provided before penalties are applied.  
- Loan restructuring options are available for genuine financial hardship cases.
