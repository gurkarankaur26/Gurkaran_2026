CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a variety of mutual fund investment options in partnership with Kotak Mutual Fund. These funds cater to different investment needs including equity, debt, hybrid, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance), infrastructure, and sector-specific funds. Investors can choose between Direct and Regular plans, and between Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes available:  
- Kotak Debt Hybrid Fund (Regular, Monthly IDCW)  
- Kotak Equity Savings Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and Monthly IDCW)  
- Kotak ESG Exclusionary Strategy Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Flexicap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Focused Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Infrastructure & Economic Reform Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Large & Midcap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Large Cap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Manufacture in India Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak Midcap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Kotak MNC Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  

Each fund is identified by a unique code for easy reference and investment processing.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund categories: Equity, Debt, Hybrid, ESG, Infrastructure, Sector-specific  
- Investment plans: Direct and Regular  
- Payout options: Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal)  
- Monthly IDCW available for select funds  
- Unique fund codes for each scheme variant  
- Suitable for investors with varying risk appetites and investment horizons
