TITLE: Education Loan Details and Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Education Loan  
TAGS: Education Loan, Interest Rate, Repayment Terms, Loan Coverage, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
An Education Loan from ICICI Bank is designed to support students in financing their higher education by covering tuition fees, living expenses, and other related costs. This loan acts as a financial bridge, enabling students to invest in their future and pursue their academic goals without immediate financial burden.

Key features of the Education Loan include competitive interest rates, flexible repayment terms, and comprehensive coverage of educational expenses. Borrowers can choose repayment plans that suit their financial situation, often starting repayment after the completion of the course or after a grace period.

Eligibility criteria typically include admission to a recognized educational institution and satisfactory credit assessment. The loan amount and tenure depend on the course, institution, and the applicant’s financial background.

For detailed information on interest rates, repayment schedules, and expense coverage, customers are encouraged to consult the official ICICI Bank resources or contact the bank directly.

KEY POINTS:  
- Education Loan supports tuition fees, living expenses, and other education-related costs  
- Competitive interest rates applicable (specific rates to be confirmed with the bank)  
- Flexible repayment terms, often starting post-course completion  
- Eligibility requires admission to a recognized institution and credit assessment  
- Loan amount and tenure vary based on course and applicant profile  
- Helps students invest in their future by easing financial constraints during education
