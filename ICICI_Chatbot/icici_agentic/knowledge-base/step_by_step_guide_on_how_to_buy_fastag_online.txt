CONTENT:  
FASTag is a prepaid RFID tag mandated by the Ministry of Road Transport & Highways for electronic toll collection on national highways in India. It enables automatic deduction of toll charges, reducing wait times at toll plazas, avoiding cash handling, and ensuring smooth traffic flow.

**How to Buy FASTag Online from ICICI Bank:**  
1. **Via iMobile App (for ICICI Bank customers):**  
   - Open the iMobile app  
   - Go to ‘Bill Payments’  
   - Select ‘ICICI FASTag’  
   - Click ‘Buy new’ and enter vehicle and personal details  
   - Confirm details and proceed with payment  

2. **Via ICICI Internet Banking (for ICICI Bank customers):**  
   - Log in to ICICI Internet Banking at www.icici.bank.in  
   - Visit icici.bank.in/fastag  
   - Click on ‘New Customer’  
   - Fill in the FASTag application form with required details  
   - Make payment online  
   - FASTag will be delivered via courier after successful payment  

3. **Via WhatsApp Banking:**  
   - Save ICICI Bank’s WhatsApp number: 8640086400  
   - Follow instructions to apply for FASTag  

4. **Via Pockets Wallet App by ICICI Bank:**  
   - Open the Pockets app  
   - Tap on ‘FASTag’  
   - Enter vehicle and personal details  
   - Confirm and complete the purchase  

**Documents Required:**  
- Documents must be in the vehicle owner’s name.  
- If the owner is not present, the driver can submit their photo ID proof.  
- Documents are verified by the service provider before activation.  

**Activation and Recharge:**  
- FASTag is activated once the documents are verified and payment is processed.  
- You must open a FASTag account and load funds to pay toll charges.  
- Recharge options include UPI, Internet Banking, and other digital payment platforms.  
- Once activated, FASTag can be recharged online without submitting documents again.  

**Charges:**  
- A one-time processing fee and refundable security deposit may apply depending on the vehicle type.  
- Exact charges vary; check at the time of application.  

**Benefits of FASTag:**  
- Faster toll plaza transit with dedicated FASTag lanes  
- Cashless toll payments  
- Reduced waiting time and traffic congestion  
- Convenient online recharge and management
