ICICI Bank Home Loan - Features, Application Process, and Important Information

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers home loans that can be applied for online via www.icicibank.com or by visiting the nearest ICICI Bank branch. The loan disbursement and repayment process includes several key features and important considerations:

1. **Home Loan Application:**  
   - Apply online or at any ICICI Bank branch.  
   - Loan approval is subject to document submission and applicable terms and conditions.  
   - Loan applications are accepted at the sole discretion of ICICI Bank.

2. **EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment):**  
   - EMI is a fixed monthly payment that includes both principal and interest.  
   - It is paid on a specific date every month over the loan tenure.  
   - Under the Pre-Equated Monthly Instalment (Pre-EMI) option, borrowers pay only the interest on the disbursed loan amount during the construction phase.  
   - Actual EMI payments (principal + interest) start after possession of the house.

3. **Property Registration:**  
   - Registration involves stamping and payment of registration charges for the sale deed or agreement for sale.  
   - The property must be recorded at the sub-registrar’s office of the concerned jurisdiction.

4. **Capital Gains Tax Exemption:**  
   - Capital gains are exempt if the customer purchases a new flat within two years of selling the original flat and invests the entire capital gain amount into the new property.  
   - This is subject to the provisions of the Income Tax Act, 1961.  
   - Customers are advised to consult their Chartered Accountant for detailed tax implications.

5. **Interest Rates and Terms:**  
   - Interest rates applicable are floating rates.  
   - Terms and conditions apply as per the loan agreement and bank policies.

6. **Important Disclaimers:**  
   - The information provided on third-party websites linked to ICICI Bank is for general information only and does not constitute investment advice.  
   - ICICI Bank is not responsible for the accuracy or completeness of information on third-party sites.  
   - Customers should perform due diligence before finalizing any property transactions.

KEY POINTS:  
- Home loan applications can be made online or at branches.  
- Pre-EMI option allows interest-only payments during construction.  
- EMI includes principal and interest, paid monthly after possession.  
- Property registration requires stamping and recording at sub-registrar office.  
- Capital gains tax exemption available if reinvested in new property within two years.  
- Interest rates are floating and subject to terms and conditions.  
- Customers should consult tax professionals for capital gains implications.  
- ICICI Bank disclaims liability for third-party website information.
