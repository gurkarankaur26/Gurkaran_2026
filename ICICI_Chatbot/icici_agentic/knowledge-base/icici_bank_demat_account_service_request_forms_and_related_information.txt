TITLE: ICICI Bank Demat Account Service Request Forms and Related Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Demat Account Services  
TAGS: Demat Account, Service Request Forms, KYC, Fees, Nomination, Delivery Instructions, Account Modification  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides a comprehensive set of service request forms for Demat account holders to facilitate various transactions and account management activities. Customers can download these forms and submit them at any demat-enabled ICICI Bank branch along with the required KYC documents.

**Key Services and Forms Available:**  
- Demat Account Opening Form  
- CDSL Pledge and Unpledge Request Forms  
- Rights and Obligations Document for Demat Account  
- Dematerialisation and Rematerialisation of Shares Forms  
- Repurchase or Redemption of Units Form  
- Application for Reissue of TIFD or DIS Booklet (without requisition slip)  
- Account Freezing and Defreezing Forms  
- Demat or Trading Account Closure Form  
- E-instruction Facility Forms  
- Demat Account Details Modification/Updation Forms  
- CERSAI / KRA KYC Forms  
- Nomination Facility Forms or Declaration for No Nominee  
- Correspondence Address Change Form  
- Transmission Related Documents  
- Additional Documents if Nomination is Not Done  
- Net Banking, Phone Banking Access, Mobile Alerts, and E-statement Forms  
- Pledge and Hypothecation Forms  
- FATCA or CRS Declaration and Annexures  
- Delivery Instruction Slip Annexures including:  
  - FORM 12: Delivery Instructions by Client  
  - FORM 13: Delivery Instructions by Power of Attorney Holder on Behalf of Clients  
  - FORM 15: Inter-Depository Delivery Instructions  
  - FORM 16: Inter-Depository Delivery Instructions by POA Holder  
  - NSDL/CDSL Annexure for Off-Market Sale Consideration Payment Details  
  - Annexure for Processing Off-Market Transfer Instructions in Shares of Private Limited Companies  
- Declaration Form for Separate Mobile Number and Email ID  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- There is no fee for opening a Demat Account with ICICI Bank.  
- Nominal fees apply based on the services availed. Customers should refer to the official service charges schedule for detailed information.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Demat account opening is free of charge.  
- Multiple service request forms are available for various Demat account operations.  
- All forms require submission at demat-enabled ICICI Bank branches with KYC documents.  
- Nomination facility and address change can be managed through specific forms.  
- Delivery instruction slips and off-market transfer annexures are provided for share transactions.  
- FATCA/CRS declarations are part of compliance documentation.  
- Customers can update mobile number and email ID separately via a declaration form.
