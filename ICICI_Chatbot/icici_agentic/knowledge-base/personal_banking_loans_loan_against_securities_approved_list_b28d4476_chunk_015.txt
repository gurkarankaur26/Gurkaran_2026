TITLE:  
Mutual Fund Schemes Offered via ICICI Bank

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Investment, Equity Funds, Hybrid Funds, Arbitrage Funds, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a variety of mutual fund schemes from different fund houses, including DSP and Edelweiss, across multiple categories such as equity, hybrid, arbitrage, and balanced advantage funds. These schemes are available in different plans (Direct and Regular) and options (Growth, IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Bonus).

Key mutual fund schemes listed include:

- DSP World Gold Mining Overseas Equity Omni FoF (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- Edelweiss Aggressive Hybrid Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options, including Plan B)  
- Edelweiss Arbitrage Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW, and Bonus options)  
- Edelweiss Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW options)  
- Edelweiss Flexi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- Edelweiss Focused Equity Fund and Edelweiss Focused Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 119278 for DSP World Gold Mining Overseas Equity Omni FoF - Dir - IDCW).

Investors can choose schemes based on their investment goals, risk appetite, and preference for dividend payout or growth options.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual fund schemes available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth, IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, and Bonus.  
- Schemes cover various categories: Equity, Hybrid, Arbitrage, Balanced Advantage, Flexi Cap, and Focused funds.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for easy identification and transaction.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified investment options through ICICI Bank.
