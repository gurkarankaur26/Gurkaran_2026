The Rubyx Debit Card from ICICI Bank is designed to provide superior value and exclusive benefits to cardholders. Named after the precious gemstone Ruby, this debit card offers a range of rewards and privileges tailored for lifestyle and entertainment needs.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- **Reward Points:** Earn reward points on your spends which can be redeemed for various offers and gifts.  
- **Entertainment Benefits:** Enjoy discounts on movie tickets and other entertainment-related expenses.  
- **Airport Lounge Access:** Complimentary access to select airport lounges based on your spending, enhancing your travel experience.  

The Rubyx Debit Card is ideal for customers looking for a debit card that combines everyday banking with lifestyle privileges.

- Earn reward points on transactions.  
- Discounts on movie tickets.  
- Complimentary airport lounge access based on spends.
