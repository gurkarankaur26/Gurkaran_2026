TITLE: ICICI Bank Student Education Loan  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Education Loan  
TAGS: Education Loan, Student Loan, Interest Rate, Eligibility, Documents, Fees, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Student Education Loans to support students pursuing professional education in both domestic and international institutes. The loan is designed to cover tuition fees, living expenses, and other education-related costs.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Indian residents applying for higher education in recognized institutions.  
- Parents, co-borrowers, or guarantors may be required to provide income proof.  

**Interest Rate:**  
- Starting at 10.50% per annum.  

**Loan Application Process:**  
Applicants need to submit the following documents:  
- Income Proof: Salary slips or income tax returns of co-borrower or guarantor.  
- Bank Statements: Recent bank statements for financial evaluation.  
- Loan Application Form: Complete and duly filled.  
- Photographs: Recent passport-sized photographs.  
- Collateral Documents: If applicable, documents related to collateral security.  

**Benefits:**  
- Loan available for studies in India and abroad.  
- Competitive interest rates starting at 10.50% p.a.  
- Flexible repayment options.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Education loan for domestic and international studies.  
- Interest rate starting at 10.50% p.a.  
- Requires income proof, bank statements, application form, photographs, and collateral documents if applicable.  
- Supports tuition fees and other education-related expenses.
