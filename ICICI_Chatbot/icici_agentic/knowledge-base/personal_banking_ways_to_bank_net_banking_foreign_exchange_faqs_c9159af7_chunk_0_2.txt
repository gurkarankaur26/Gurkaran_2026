TITLE:  
Forex Prepaid Card Services and Refund Policy

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Forex Services, Prepaid Cards, Customer Service

TAGS:  
Forex Prepaid Card, Refund Policy, Forex Services, Eligibility, Customer Care, Branch Visit, Close Relatives, Travel Forex

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Forex Prepaid Cards that can be linked either in the capacity of ‘Self’ or for close relatives (Mother, Father, Spouse, Son, and Daughter). The Forex Prepaid Card service is currently available only in select cities: Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Baroda, Gurugram, Noida, and Chandigarh.

Refunds for Forex Prepaid Cards linked as ‘Self’ can be processed online. However, if the card is linked in the capacity of a close relative and not linked to any account under ‘Self’, the customer must visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch to request a refund of the balance on the card.

When requesting Forex for close relatives, the requester must select the relationship status as ‘Close Relative’ in the online form and provide the personal and travel details of the traveler. Both the person requesting the Forex and the traveler must sign the form jointly and be physically present at the time of Forex delivery.

Refunds are processed within 7-10 working days after the request is made. For any assistance, customers are advised to contact ICICI Bank Customer Care.

KEY POINTS:  
- Forex Prepaid Cards can be linked for ‘Self’ or close relatives (Mother, Father, Spouse, Son, Daughter).  
- Service available only in Bengaluru, Chennai, Delhi, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune, Ahmedabad, Baroda, Gurugram, Noida, and Chandigarh.  
- Refunds for ‘Self’ linked cards can be done online.  
- Refunds for cards linked to close relatives require branch visit.  
- Requesting Forex for close relatives requires joint signatures and physical presence at delivery.  
- Refunds processed within 7-10 working days.  
- Customer Care support available for assistance.
