CONTENT:  
A Savings Account with ICICI Bank offers a secure place to keep your money while earning interest over time. It provides easy access to funds for managing expenses or achieving short-term financial goals. The account helps inculcate consistent savings and budgeting habits by allowing you to track transactions and balances conveniently.

**Documents Required to Open a Savings Account:**  
| Category                         | Documents Accepted                  | Conditions                          |  
|---------------------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|  
| Proof of Identity and Address    | Documents containing both identity and address details (e.g., Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID) | Must contain both identity and address details |  
| Proof of Communication Address   | Any valid document showing communication address (if different from address proof) | - |  

**How to Apply for a Savings Account Online:**  
1. Visit the ICICI Bank Savings Account section and click on ‘Open Account’.  
2. Enter your details such as Name, PAN, Pincode, and Mobile Number.  
3. You will receive an OTP on your mobile number; enter it to verify.  
4. Agree to the disclaimer and click on ‘Apply Now’.  
5. An ICICI Bank representative will contact you via phone, email, SMS, or WhatsApp to assist with the account opening process.  

**How to Apply Offline:**  
- Visit any ICICI Bank branch.  
- Fill out the application form and submit the required documents.  
- Your account will be opened after verification.  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Savings Account:**  
- Earn interest on your savings.  
- Easy and flexible access to funds.  
- Helps in budgeting and managing expenses.  
- Convenient online and offline account opening options.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Requires valid proof of identity and address for account opening.  
- Online application involves OTP verification and assistance from bank representatives.  
- Offline application available at any ICICI Bank branch.  
- Savings Account helps earn interest and manage finances effectively.
