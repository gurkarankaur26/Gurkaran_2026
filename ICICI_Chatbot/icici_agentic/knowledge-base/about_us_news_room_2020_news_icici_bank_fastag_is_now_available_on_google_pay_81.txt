ICICI Bank FASTag - Seamless Toll Payments and Recharge Options

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers FASTag, a contactless toll payment solution that enables vehicle owners to make seamless toll transactions without stopping at toll plazas. The FASTag can be purchased and recharged conveniently through multiple digital channels provided by ICICI Bank.

Key features and services include:  
- Purchase of FASTag via ICICI Bank’s digital platforms such as internet banking, iMobile app, InstaBIZ app, Pockets app, or by visiting the nearest ICICI Bank branch.  
- Recharge of FASTag using internet banking, UPI (including Google Pay), and NEFT platforms, allowing for seamless and contactless top-ups.  
- Non-ICICI Bank customers can also purchase FASTag through the Pockets app or by visiting the dedicated FASTag webpage.  
- The initiative supports contactless toll payments, enhancing urban mobility and reducing the need for physical visits to kiosks or banks, especially beneficial during the pandemic.  
- ICICI Bank’s FASTag is interoperable and accepted widely across toll plazas, ensuring a smooth and stress-free commute.

For more information and updates, customers can visit the official ICICI Bank website.

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag enables contactless toll payments for vehicles.  
- Purchase options: ICICI Bank internet banking, iMobile, InstaBIZ, Pockets app, bank branches.  
- Recharge options: Internet banking, UPI (Google Pay), NEFT.  
- Non-ICICI Bank customers can buy FASTag via Pockets app or ICICI Bank FASTag webpage.  
- Supports seamless, stress-free toll transactions and urban mobility.  
- Eliminates the need to visit toll plazas or bank branches physically.  
- Robust acceptance infrastructure and interoperability across toll plazas.
