TITLE: ICICI Bank Mutual Fund Investment Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, HDFC Funds, Investment Options, Fund Codes, Growth, IDCW, Equity, Hybrid, Infrastructure  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of HDFC mutual fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. Each fund is identified by its name, plan type (Direct or Regular), option (Growth or IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), and a unique fund code. The funds cover various categories including equity, hybrid, infrastructure, and innovation funds.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes include:  
- HDFC Diversified Equity All Cap Active Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Dividend Yield Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Equity Savings Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Flexi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Focused Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Hybrid Debt Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and Monthly/Quarterly IDCW options)  
- HDFC Hybrid Equity Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Infrastructure Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Innovation Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Large and Mid Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans, Growth and IDCW options)  
- HDFC Large Cap Fund (Direct plan, Growth option)  

Each fund is associated with a unique code for easy identification and transaction processing.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual funds are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth (reinvestment of earnings) and IDCW (payout of earnings).  
- Fund categories include Equity, Hybrid, Infrastructure, and Innovation.  
- Unique fund codes are provided for each scheme and option.  
- Monthly and Quarterly IDCW options are available for certain hybrid debt funds.  
- Investors can choose funds based on their investment goals and risk appetite.
