TITLE: Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund Schemes Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Debt Funds, Corporate Bond Funds, Credit Risk Funds, Index Funds, Investment Options, Aditya Birla Sun Life  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a structured overview of various Aditya Birla Sun Life mutual fund schemes available for investment. These schemes include different categories such as Banking & PSU Debt Funds, Corporate Bond Funds, Credit Risk Funds, and CRISIL-indexed Debt Funds. Each scheme is available in multiple plans including Direct and Regular options, with growth and income distribution cum capital withdrawal (IDCW) variants offered monthly, quarterly, or as bonuses.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Variants:  

1. **Banking & PSU Debt Fund**  
   - Retired (Ret) Plan with Monthly IDCW  
   - Retired Plan with Quarterly IDCW  

2. **Corporate Bond Fund**  
   - Direct Plan: Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW  
   - Regular Plan: Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW  

3. **Credit Risk Fund**  
   - Direct Plan: Bonus, Growth, IDCW  
   - Regular Plan: Growth, IDCW  

4. **CRISIL-IBX Financial Services Debt Index Funds**  
   - 3 to 6 Months Debt Index Fund (Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW)  
   - 9 to 12 Months Debt Index Fund (Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW)  

5. **CRISIL IBX 60:40 SDL plus AAA PSU - Apr 2027 Index Fund**  
   - Direct and Regular Plans, Growth and IDCW  

6. **Dynamic Bond Fund**  
   - Direct Plan: Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW  
   - Regular Plan: IDCW  
   - Retirement Plan: DAP (Dividend Adjustment Plan)  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction purposes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple investment options available under Aditya Birla Sun Life mutual funds through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes include debt-oriented funds focusing on banking, PSU, corporate bonds, credit risk, and CRISIL-indexed debt instruments.  
- Plans available in Direct and Regular options with Growth and IDCW variants.  
- IDCW payouts can be monthly, quarterly, or as bonuses depending on the scheme.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each variant to facilitate transactions and tracking.  
- Suitable for investors seeking fixed income, credit risk exposure, or index-linked debt investments.
