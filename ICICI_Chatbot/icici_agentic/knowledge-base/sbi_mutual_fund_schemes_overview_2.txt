TITLE: SBI Mutual Fund Schemes Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment  
TAGS: SBI Mutual Fund, Equity Savings Fund, ESG Fund, Flexicap Fund, Focused Fund, Healthcare Fund, Innovative Fund, Large & Midcap Fund, Large Cap Fund, Midcap Fund, MNC Fund, Investment Options  

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various SBI Mutual Fund schemes, including their types, plan options, and dividend payout frequencies. The schemes cover a wide range of investment strategies such as equity savings, ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) exclusionary strategies, flexicap, focused, healthcare, innovative opportunities, large & midcap, large cap, midcap, and MNC funds. Each scheme is available in Direct and Regular plans with options for Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) payouts, either monthly, quarterly, or annual, depending on the scheme.

Key SBI Mutual Fund Schemes:

1. **SBI Equity Savings Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW  
   - Suitable for investors seeking a mix of equity, debt, and arbitrage opportunities with regular income.

2. **SBI ESG Exclusionary Strategy Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Focuses on companies with strong ESG practices, excluding those involved in harmful activities.

3. **SBI Flexicap Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Invests across large-cap, mid-cap, and small-cap stocks offering flexibility in market capitalization.

4. **SBI Focused Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Concentrated portfolio investing in a limited number of stocks for potentially higher returns.

5. **SBI Healthcare Opportunities Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Targets investments in healthcare and pharmaceutical sectors.

6. **SBI Innovative Opportunities Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Invests in companies with innovative business models and growth potential.

7. **SBI Large & Midcap Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Balanced investment in large and mid-cap stocks.

8. **SBI Large Cap Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Focuses on large-cap stocks with stable growth.

9. **SBI Midcap Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct and Regular  
   - Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Invests primarily in mid-cap stocks with higher growth potential.

10. **SBI MNC Fund**  
    - Plans: Direct and Regular  
    - Options: Growth, IDCW  
    - Invests in multinational companies with global presence.

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 134641 for SBI Equity Savings Fund - Direct - Monthly IDCW). Investors can choose between Growth options, where returns are reinvested, or IDCW options, where income is distributed periodically.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple SBI Mutual Fund schemes available catering to different investment needs.  
- Plans offered in Direct and Regular options.  
- Dividend payout options include Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, and Annual IDCW depending on the scheme.  
- Schemes cover diverse sectors and investment strategies including ESG, healthcare, large & midcap, and innovative opportunities.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for easy reference.  
- Suitable for investors seeking regular income or capital appreciation through equity and hybrid funds.
