CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include various fund categories such as Multi Asset Allocation, Multi-Cap, Nifty 50 Index, Pharma & Healthcare, PSU Equity, Quant, Regular Savings, Small Cap, Special Opportunities, and Transportation and Logistics funds. Each scheme is available in different plan options such as Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg), and payout options like Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

Key schemes include:  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Multi Asset Allocation Fund (Reg - IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Multi-Cap Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty 50 Index Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Pharma & Healthcare Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life PSU Equity Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Quant Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Regular Savings Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and Monthly IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Small Cap Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Special Opportunities Fund (Dir and Reg - Growth)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Transportation and Logistics Fund (Dir - Growth and IDCW)  
- Aditya Birla Sun Life Nifty Bank ETF  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 151309 for Multi Asset Allocation Fund - Reg - Growth). Investors can choose between growth options, where returns are reinvested, or IDCW options, where income is distributed periodically.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund categories available catering to different investment objectives and risk profiles.  
- Options to invest via Direct or Regular plans.  
- Choice of Growth or IDCW payout options.  
- Unique scheme codes provided for easy identification and transaction.  
- Includes equity, index, sectoral, and thematic funds.  
- ETFs available for passive investment strategies.
