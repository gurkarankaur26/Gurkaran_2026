CONTENT:  
A Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) is a disciplined investment approach that allows investors to invest a fixed amount regularly in mutual funds. SIPs leverage the power of compounding, where returns generated are reinvested to generate further returns, leading to significant wealth accumulation over time.

**Key Features and Benefits of SIP:**  
- **Compounding Effect:** Regular contributions over an extended period help grow both the initial investment and the returns generated, resulting in higher overall returns.  
- **Flexibility:** Investors can start, pause, increase, or decrease their SIP investments as per their changing financial goals and circumstances.  
- **Diversification:** Mutual funds invest in a diversified portfolio of assets such as stocks, bonds, and other instruments, which helps spread risk and reduce vulnerability to market volatility.  
- **Rupee Cost Averaging:** SIPs help mitigate market volatility by purchasing more units when prices are low and fewer units when prices are high.

**Example of SIP Investment:**  
If you invest Rs. 10,000 monthly in a mutual fund with fluctuating Net Asset Values (NAV), the number of units allocated each month varies based on the NAV, as shown below:

| Month | Investment Amount | NAV (Rs.) | Units Allocated | Total Units in Portfolio |  
|-------|-------------------|-----------|-----------------|--------------------------|  
| 1     | Rs. 10,000        | 80        | 125             | 125                      |  
| 2     | Rs. 10,000        | 85        | 118             | 243                      |  
| 3     | Rs. 10,000        | 75        | 133             | 376                      |  
| 4     | Rs. 10,000        | 90        | 111             | 487                      |  

This fluctuation in units purchased helps reduce the impact of market volatility on the overall investment.

**How to Start an SIP with ICICI Bank:**  
1. Assess your risk tolerance and financial goals to select the appropriate mutual fund.  
2. Choose a mutual fund based on your risk profile and past performance reviews.  
3. Complete the application process, link your Demat account, and complete KYC formalities.  
4. Start investing online via the ICICI Bank iMobile App or internet banking.  

**Using the SIP Calculator:**  
ICICI Bank provides an online SIP calculator to estimate returns on your investments. By entering the investment amount, duration, and expected annual return rate, you can view your total investment, estimated returns, and projected future wealth. This tool helps in making informed financial decisions.

**When to Start Investing in SIP:**  
- **In Your 20s or 30s:** Starting early maximizes the benefits of compounding, allowing you to accumulate substantial wealth over time.  
- **In Your 40s or 50s:** SIPs can help secure a comfortable retirement or meet other financial goals, though the compounding period is shorter.  
- **Prerequisite:** Ensure a steady income source before starting SIP to maintain regular contributions.
