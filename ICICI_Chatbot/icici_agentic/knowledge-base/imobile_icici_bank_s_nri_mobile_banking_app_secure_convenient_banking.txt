CONTENT:  
iMobile is ICICI Bank’s dedicated mobile banking app designed for NRI customers, offering secure and convenient banking services. The app enables users to perform a wide range of banking transactions on the go with enhanced security features.

Key Features and Requirements:  
- **SIM Binding:** Registration on iMobile requires the SIM card linked to your ICICI Bank account. This ensures secure access and prevents unauthorized use.  
- **Active Mobile Number:** The registered SIM must be active and capable of sending SMS for verification and transaction purposes.  
- **Secure Login Options:** Users can log in using MPIN, Internet Banking ID, or biometric authentication such as fingerprint recognition, ensuring multiple layers of security.  
- **Operating System Compatibility:** iMobile supports devices running Android 9 or higher and iOS 17 or higher.  
- **Single Device Access:** The app can be accessed on only one device per user to maintain account security.  
- **No Charges from ICICI Bank:** The app is free to download and use. However, standard operator data and SMS charges may apply as per your mobile service provider.  

Benefits:  
- Convenient access to banking services anytime, anywhere.  
- Enhanced security with SIM binding and multiple login options.  
- Seamless integration with ICICI Bank’s NRI banking services.  
- User-friendly interface compatible with latest mobile OS versions.  

KEY POINTS:
