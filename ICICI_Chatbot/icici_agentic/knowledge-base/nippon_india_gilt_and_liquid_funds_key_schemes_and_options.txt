TITLE: Nippon India Gilt and Liquid Funds - Key Schemes and Options  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Nippon India, Gilt Fund, Liquid Fund, Low Duration Fund, Investment Options, Dividend, Growth, Payout  

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Nippon India mutual fund schemes available for investment, focusing on Gilt Funds, Liquid Funds, and Low Duration Funds. These funds offer multiple plan options such as Direct and Regular plans, with different dividend payout frequencies and growth options.

**Nippon India Gilt Fund**  
- Available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Monthly IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), Growth, and Payout plans.  
- Suitable for investors seeking exposure to government securities with relatively lower risk.  

**Nippon India Liquid Fund**  
- Available in Direct, Regular, and Retail plans.  
- Dividend options include Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Quarterly IDCW.  
- Growth option also available.  
- Ideal for investors looking for high liquidity and low risk with short-term investment horizons.  

**Nippon India Low Duration Fund**  
- Available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend options include Bonus, Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, and Growth.  
- Designed for investors seeking low duration debt instruments with moderate risk and better returns than liquid funds.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple investment options available: Direct, Regular, and Retail plans.  
- Dividend payout frequencies: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Bonus, and Growth options.  
- Suitable for different risk profiles and investment horizons: Gilt Funds for government securities, Liquid Funds for high liquidity, and Low Duration Funds for moderate risk and returns.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code (e.g., Nippon India Gilt Fund - Dir - Mthly IDCW: 118670).  
- Investors can choose payout or reinvestment options based on their income needs.
