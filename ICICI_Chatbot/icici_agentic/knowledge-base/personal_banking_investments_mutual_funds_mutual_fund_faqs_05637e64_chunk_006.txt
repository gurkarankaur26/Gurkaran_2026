ICICI Bank Mutual Funds - Investment, SIP, Redemption, and Withdrawal Details

CONTENT:  
Mutual funds are a smart way to grow your money over the long term by offering diversification and professional management. While mutual funds are market-linked and subject to fluctuations, diversification helps reduce volatility compared to investing directly in individual stocks. The risk level varies depending on the type of fund selected.

**Investment and Payment Process:**  
- Payments for mutual fund investments are securely made through your ICICI Bank savings account using Net Banking or iMobile.  
- Once payment is authorized, units are allotted based on the applicable Net Asset Value (NAV) and cut-off timing.  
- Additional purchases can be made in the same scheme using the same process.

**Systematic Investment Plan (SIP):**  
- SIP instalments are automatically debited from your registered bank account on the chosen SIP date once the mandate is set up.  
- Most Asset Management Companies (AMCs) allow SIP dates such as 1st, 7th, 10th, 15th, 25th, etc.  
- The minimum SIP amount usually starts at ₹100 to ₹500 depending on the fund, making SIPs flexible and convenient for different investor segments.  
- SIP dates can be checked before placing the order.

**Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) and Income Distribution:**  
- Certain debt and hybrid funds offer Systematic Withdrawal Plans (SWPs) that allow investors to receive a fixed monthly amount.  
- Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options may provide periodic payouts based on fund performance and distribution policies.

**Redemption Process:**  
- Open-ended mutual fund units can be redeemed on any business day.  
- Redemption orders are processed based on the applicable NAV and credited to your registered bank account within the standard settlement cycle.  
- Redemption proceeds are credited only to the registered bank account mapped to your folio as per guidelines. You may update your bank mandate through MF Central (www.mfcentral.com) before placing a redemption request.  
- The amount is credited directly via NEFT/IMPS, ensuring safe and seamless settlement.

**Cut-off Timings and Settlement Cycle:**  
- Redemption cut-off time for Liquid & Overnight funds: 2:55 PM on business days.  
- Redemption cut-off time for all other schemes (except Liquid & Overnight funds): 2:55 PM on business days.  
- Cut-off times may vary for other schemes like International funds.  
- Orders placed before the cut-off time are processed on the same day’s NAV; orders placed after the cut-off are processed on the next business day.

**Settlement Timelines:**  
- Liquid funds: Redemption proceeds credited within T+1 working day.  
- Equity and hybrid funds: Redemption proceeds credited within T+2 or T+3 working days depending on the scheme category.  
- Standard settlement cycle is T+3 working days if redemption is done before the cut-off time and T+4 days if done after the cut-off time.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual funds offer diversification and professional management with varying risk based on fund type.  
- Payments and SIP instalments are securely processed via ICICI Bank Net Banking or iMobile.  
- SIP minimum amount ranges from ₹100 to ₹500; multiple SIP dates available.  
- SWPs and IDCW options provide periodic income from certain debt and hybrid funds.  
- Redemption is allowed any business day with proceeds credited to registered bank accounts only.  
- Redemption cut-off time is 2:55 PM for most schemes; orders before cut-off processed same day NAV.  
- Settlement cycle: T+1 day for liquid funds, T+2/T+3 days for equity/hybrid funds.  
- Bank mandate updates for redemption can be done via MF Central (www.mfcentral.com).
