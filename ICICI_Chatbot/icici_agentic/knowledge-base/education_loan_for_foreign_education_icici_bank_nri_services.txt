CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans specifically designed for foreign education to help students and NRIs invest in their future. The loan facility provides financial support with flexible repayment options and tax benefits.

Loan Amount:  
- Secured loan up to ₹3 crore  
- Unsecured loan up to ₹1 crore  

Interest Rates:  
- Secured loan: Starts at 3.25%* + Repo Rate  
- Unsecured loan: Starts at 5%* + Repo Rate  

Processing Fees:  
- Starts at 0.5% (plus 18% GST) or ₹30,000 (plus 18% GST), whichever is lower  

Repayment Tenure:  
- Up to 15 years, including the course period  

Benefits:  
- High loan amount to cover extensive education expenses abroad  
- Flexible repayment tenure to ease financial burden  
- Tax benefits under prevailing income tax laws  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount: Secured up to ₹3 crore, Unsecured up to ₹1 crore  
- Interest rates linked to Repo Rate with starting points at 3.25%* (secured) and 5%* (unsecured)  
- Processing fee: 0.5% or ₹30,000 plus GST, whichever is lower  
- Repayment tenure up to 15 years including course duration  
- Tax benefits available on the loan
