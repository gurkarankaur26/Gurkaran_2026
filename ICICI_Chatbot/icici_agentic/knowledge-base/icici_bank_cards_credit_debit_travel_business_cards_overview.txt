ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of cards designed to meet diverse banking needs including personal, travel, and business requirements. The card portfolio includes credit cards, debit cards, travel cards, and specialized business and corporate cards.

**Types of Cards and Features:**  
- **Credit Cards:** Provide rewards, cashback, and global acceptance for personal and business use.  
- **Debit Cards:** Linked to savings or current accounts, offering easy access to funds with secure transactions.  
- **Travel Cards:** Prepaid cards designed for international travel, offering currency flexibility and safety.  
- **Business & Corporate Cards:** Tailored for enterprises to manage expenses efficiently with features such as:  
  - Expense control  
  - Global reach for payments  
  - Reward points and cashback offers  
  - Easy integration with business finance systems  

**Benefits:**  
- Streamlined expense management for businesses  
- Access to global payment networks  
- Attractive rewards and cashback programs  
- Enhanced control over corporate spending
