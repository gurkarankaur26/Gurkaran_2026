TITLE: Bill Payment and Recharge on iMobile App  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Digital Banking, Payments  
TAGS: Bill Payment, Recharge, iMobile App, Digital Payments, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s iMobile app offers an all-new Bill Payment and Recharge section designed to provide customers with a seamless and convenient way to manage their payments and recharges digitally. The feature allows users to pay utility bills, recharge mobile phones, DTH, and other services instantly through the app without the need for cash or physical visits.

Key features include:  
- Instant bill payments for utilities such as electricity, water, gas, and broadband.  
- Mobile and DTH recharge options with multiple payment methods.  
- Secure and easy-to-use interface integrated within the iMobile app.  
- Access to transaction history and payment reminders to avoid missed payments.  
- Support for multiple billers and service providers across India.  

Benefits:  
- Convenience of managing all bill payments and recharges in one place.  
- Time-saving with instant processing and confirmation of payments.  
- Enhanced security with ICICI Bank’s digital banking safeguards.  
- 24/7 availability allowing payments anytime, anywhere.  

Eligibility:  
- Available to all ICICI Bank customers with an active iMobile app account.  
- Requires registration and login to the iMobile app using valid ICICI Bank credentials.  

Fees and Charges:  
- Bill payments and recharges through the iMobile app are generally free of charge.  
- Standard data charges may apply as per the user’s mobile network provider.  

Process to Use:  
1. Download and install the ICICI Bank iMobile app from the official app store.  
2. Log in using your ICICI Bank customer ID and password or biometric authentication.  
3. Navigate to the Bill Payment and Recharge section.  
4. Select the type of bill or recharge service you want to pay.  
5. Enter the required details such as account number, mobile number, or subscriber ID.  
6. Choose the payment method (savings account, credit card, debit card, or UPI).  
7. Confirm the payment and receive instant confirmation and receipt.  

FAQs:  
Q: Can I schedule bill payments in advance?  
A: Yes, the iMobile app allows scheduling of bill payments to avoid late fees.  

Q: Are there any limits on the amount I can pay?  
A: Payment limits are subject to your account type and bank’s transaction policies.  

Q: Is the payment process secure?  
A: Yes, ICICI Bank uses advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your transactions.  

KEY POINTS:  
- iMobile app provides a dedicated Bill Payment and Recharge section.  
- Supports multiple billers and recharge services across India.  
- Instant, secure, and convenient digital payments.  
- Available 24/7 for ICICI Bank customers.  
- No additional fees for payments via the app.  
- Supports scheduling and payment reminders.
