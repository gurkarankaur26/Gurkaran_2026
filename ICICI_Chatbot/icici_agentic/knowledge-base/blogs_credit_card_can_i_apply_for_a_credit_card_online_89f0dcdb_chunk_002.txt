CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a variety of credit cards tailored to different financial needs, including Coral Credit Card, Rubyx Credit Card, Sapphiro Credit Card, Rupay Credit Card, Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card, travel and fuel credit cards, and secured credit cards. Customers can conveniently apply for these credit cards online through ICICI Bank’s user-friendly web portal.

**Application Process:**  
- The online application process is simple and can be completed from home in a few steps.  
- Applicants need to submit required documents such as PAN card, address proof, income proof, and identity documents. Additional documents may be requested by the bank as per requirements.  
- After submission, ICICI Bank verifies the details and processes the application.  
- Applicants can track their credit card application status online for updates.

**Documents Required:**  
- PAN Card  
- Address Proof  
- Income Proof  
- Identity Proof  
- Additional documents as requested by the bank

**Types of Credit Cards Offered:**  
- Coral Credit Card  
- Rubyx Credit Card  
- Sapphiro Credit Card  
- Rupay Credit Card  
- Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card  
- Travel Credit Cards  
- Fuel Credit Cards  
- Secured Credit Cards

**Benefits:**  
- Easy and convenient online application process  
- Wide range of credit card options to suit different financial needs  
- Ability to track application status online

KEY POINTS:  
- Online application available for multiple types of credit cards  
- Required documents include PAN, address proof, income proof, and identity proof  
- Application verification and processing done by ICICI Bank
