TITLE: Senior Citizen Savings Bank Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Savings Account  
TAGS: Senior Citizen, Savings Account, Fixed Deposit, SCSS, Interest Rates, Banking Benefits  

CONTENT:  
The Senior Citizen Savings Bank Account by ICICI Bank is designed specifically for senior citizens, providing convenience, safety, and higher returns to support their financial well-being. The account offers several benefits including monthly payout fixed deposits, digital banking convenience, and no service charges.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- **Monthly Payout Fixed Deposit (FD):** Senior citizens can opt for fixed deposits with monthly interest payouts, ensuring a steady income stream.  
- **Higher Returns:** The account offers higher interest rates on Fixed Deposits and Senior Citizen Savings Scheme (SCSS) compared to regular accounts.  
- **Digital Convenience:** Access banking services anytime through 24/7 phone banking and digital platforms, ensuring ease of transactions and account management.  
- **No Service Charges:** The account comes with NIL service charges, making it cost-effective for senior citizens.  

Eligibility:  
- Designed exclusively for senior citizens (typically aged 60 years and above).  

Interest Rates and Fees:  
- Higher interest rates applicable on Fixed Deposits and SCSS for senior citizens (specific rates vary and are subject to change as per bank policy).  
- No service charges applicable on the Senior Citizen Savings Bank Account.  

Process to Open Account:  
- Visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch or apply online through the official website.  
- Submit valid identity proof, address proof, and age proof documents.  
- Complete the KYC process as per bank guidelines.  

FAQs:  
- **Can I open this account if I am below 60 years?**  
  No, this account is specifically for senior citizens aged 60 years and above.  
- **Are there any charges for maintaining the account?**  
  No, the account has NIL service charges.  
- **How can I avail monthly payout FD?**  
  You can opt for monthly payout FD at the time of deposit or through your relationship manager.  
- **Is digital banking available for this account?**  
  Yes, 24/7 phone banking and digital banking services are available for account holders.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Senior Citizen Savings Bank Account tailored for individuals aged 60 and above.  
- Offers monthly payout fixed deposits for regular income.  
- Higher interest rates on FD and SCSS compared to regular accounts.  
- No service charges on the account.  
- 24/7 phone banking and digital banking facilities for convenience.
