KEY POINTS:  
- Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes offer multiple investment options including Growth, Dividend (IDCW), and Bonus plans.  
- Schemes are available in both Direct and Regular plans, with Direct plans generally having lower expense ratios.  
- Fund categories include Large Cap, Multi Asset, Multi Cap, and various Index Funds tracking Nifty indices.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for easy tracking and investment.  
- Dividend options (IDCW) provide periodic income distribution to investors.  
- Bonus plans reinvest dividends into additional units, increasing the investment corpus.
