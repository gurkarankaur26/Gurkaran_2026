TITLE: Fixed Deposit FAQs  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Deposits / Fixed Deposits  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, FD, Interest Rates, Tenure, Senior Citizens, Cumulative FD, Non-Cumulative FD, Tax Saving FD, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
A Fixed Deposit (FD) with ICICI Bank is a secure investment where you deposit a lump sum amount for a fixed tenure at a predetermined interest rate. The interest rate remains constant throughout the tenure, providing predictable and guaranteed returns.

**Key Features:**  
- Minimum deposit: ₹10,000  
- No predefined upper limit (subject to regulatory and internal policies)  
- Tenure options: 7 days to 10 years  
- Interest calculation:  
  - Compounded quarterly for cumulative FDs  
  - Paid out monthly or quarterly for non-cumulative FDs  
- Interest payout options: Monthly, Quarterly, or On Maturity (for cumulative FD)  
- Senior citizens (60 years and above) receive preferential higher interest rates (only for resident senior citizens)  
- Auto-renewal option available upon maturity  
- Maturity proceeds can be credited to linked Savings or Current Account  
- Maturity instructions can be modified anytime before maturity via Net Banking, iMobile app, or branch visit  

**Types of Fixed Deposits:**  
- **Cumulative FD:** Interest is compounded quarterly and paid at maturity, resulting in higher returns.  
- **Non-Cumulative FD:** Interest is paid out monthly or quarterly, suitable for those seeking regular income.  
- **Tax Saving FD:** Fixed deposit with a 5-year lock-in period, eligible for tax deduction under Section 80C, subject to applicable rules.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Resident individuals  
- Minors (through guardians)  
- Senior citizens  

**Interest Rates:**  
- Vary based on tenure and FD type  
- Preferential rates for senior citizens  
- Rates are updated periodically based on market conditions and internal policies  
- Latest rates can be viewed on ICICI Bank’s website, iMobile app, Net Banking, or at any branch  

**Process to Open FD:**  
- Deposit lump sum amount (minimum ₹10,000)  
- Choose tenure and interest payout option  
- Can be opened via branch, Net Banking, or iMobile app  

**Comparison with Recurring Deposit (RD):**  
- FD requires lump sum deposit upfront  
- RD involves monthly instalments for gradual savings  
- Both offer fixed interest rates, but FD generally offers higher returns due to upfront deposit  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum FD amount: ₹10,000  
- Tenure: 7 days to 10 years  
- Interest compounded quarterly for cumulative FD; paid monthly/quarterly for non-cumulative FD  
- Senior citizens get higher interest rates  
- Tax Saving FD has 5-year lock-in and qualifies for Section 80C benefits  
- Auto-renewal and flexible maturity instructions available  
- Eligible for resident individuals, minors (via guardians), and senior citizens  
- Interest rates updated periodically; check online or via app
