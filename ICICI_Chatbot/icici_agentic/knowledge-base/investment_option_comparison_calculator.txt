The Investment Option Comparison Calculator is a tool designed to help customers compare different investment options based on their investment capacity and financial goals. By inputting relevant details, users can evaluate and choose the most suitable investment plan that aligns with their objectives. This tool aids in making informed decisions by providing a clear comparison of potential returns and benefits of various investment products offered by ICICI Bank.

- Helps compare multiple investment options  
- Based on user’s investment capacity and goals  
- Facilitates informed decision-making  
- Supports financial planning and goal achievement
