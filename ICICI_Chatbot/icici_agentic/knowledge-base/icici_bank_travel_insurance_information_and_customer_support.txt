CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers travel insurance products underwritten by ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited. The insurance covers international travel and group travel under specific policies such as International Travel Insurance (ICITIOP02002V020102) and Voyager Travel Insurance Group Travel Insurance Policy (4110).  

ICICI Bank acts as a corporate agent for ICICI Lombard and facilitates enrolment in these insurance products. Enrolment is purely voluntary and not linked to any other banking facility.  

For detailed information on risk factors, terms, conditions, and exclusions, customers should carefully read the policy wording before purchasing.

- ICICI Bank Customer Support Email: customer.care@icicibank.com  
- ICICI Lombard Support Email: customersupport@icicilombard.com  
- Fax Number (ICICI Lombard): 022 61961323  

Customers are warned against spurious phone calls and fraudulent offers related to insurance policies. IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India) does not engage in selling policies, announcing bonuses, or investing premiums. Customers receiving suspicious calls are advised to lodge a police complaint.  

Grievances can also be registered with IRDAI at bimabharosa.irdai.gov.in/Home/Home.
