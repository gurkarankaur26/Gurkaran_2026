TITLE: Personal Loan for Salaried Individuals  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
A personal loan is an ideal financial solution for salaried individuals facing short-term funding needs such as marriage expenses, travel, medical emergencies, education, home renovation, and more. ICICI Bank offers personal loans with flexible terms and quick disbursal to meet these requirements efficiently.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Age: 20 to 58 years  
- Employment: Stable job with regular monthly income  
- Credit Score: Good credit history  
- Minimum salary requirement varies based on employer type, loan amount, tenure, and existing relationship with ICICI Bank  
- Required Documents:  
  - Proof of Identity: Aadhaar Card, Voter’s ID, Valid Passport, or Driving Licence  
  - Proof of Residence: Aadhaar Card, Recent Utility Bill, Valid Passport, or Leave and Licence Agreement  
  - Latest 3 months’ Salary Slips  
  - Latest 3 months’ Bank Statement (salary credited account)  
  - 2 Passport size photographs  

**Loan Features:**  
- Loan amount and eligibility subject to internal assessment and ICICI Bank policy terms  
- Flexible repayment tenure from 12 to 72 months  
- Loan usage permitted for personal needs such as weddings, medical emergencies, travel, education, home renovation, etc.  
- Loan not permitted for illegal activities, gambling/betting, or speculative investments (e.g., stock market)  

**Application Process:**  
- Online application via ICICI Bank website or iMobile app by filling basic details and uploading documents digitally  
- Offline application available at any ICICI Bank branch  
- Existing ICICI Bank customers may be eligible for Instant Personal Loans and can check offers via iMobile app or Net Banking  
- Upon approval, loan amount is disbursed directly to the borrower’s bank account quickly and securely  

**Benefits:**  
- Quick and hassle-free loan approval and disbursal  
- Affordable personal loan interest rates (contact ICICI Bank for current rates)  
- Flexible repayment options tailored to borrower’s convenience  
- Access to instant personal loan offers for existing customers  

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 20-58 with stable income and good credit score  
- Documents required: Identity proof, residence proof, salary slips, bank statements, photographs  
- Loan tenure: 12 to 72 months  
- Loan usage: Personal expenses excluding illegal or speculative activities  
- Application: Online via website/app or offline at branches  
- Instant loan offers for existing ICICI Bank customers  
- Fast disbursal directly to bank account  
- Affordable interest rates (contact bank for details)
