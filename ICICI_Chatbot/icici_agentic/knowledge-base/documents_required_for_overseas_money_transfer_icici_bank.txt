TITLE: Documents Required for Overseas Money Transfer | ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Overseas Money Transfer  
TAGS: KYC, Overseas Remittance, Proof of Identity, Proof of Address, Registration, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
Residents of India who do not hold an ICICI Bank account can initiate overseas money transfer transactions by visiting an ICICI Bank branch in India. To facilitate this, customers must complete a one-time registration process by submitting basic KYC (Know Your Customer) documents, which include proof of identity and proof of address. Once the registration is complete, customers can submit their transaction requests directly at the branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Non-account holders can transfer money overseas by visiting an ICICI Bank branch.  
- One-time registration is mandatory before initiating transactions.  
- Required documents for registration:  
  - Proof of Identity  
  - Proof of Address  
- After registration, transaction requests can be submitted at the branch.
