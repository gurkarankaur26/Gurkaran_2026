CONTENT:  
An NRI (Non-Resident Indian) is defined as an Indian citizen who resides outside India:  
- For employment, business, or vocation purposes  
- With an intention to stay outside India for an uncertain duration  
- Deputed outside India temporarily for employment  

A Person of Indian Origin (PIO) is a foreign citizen (excluding citizens of Bangladesh or Pakistan) who:  
- Has held an Indian passport at any time; or  
- Has parents or grandparents who were Indian citizens; or  
- Is a spouse (not a citizen of Bangladesh or Pakistan) of an Indian citizen or PIO as defined above.  

**Types of Accounts:**  
- NRE (Non-Resident External) Savings Account can be opened online.  

**Repatriation:**  
- Both principal and interest amounts in the NRE Savings Account are fully repatriable.  

**Joint Account:**  
- NRE Savings Account can be held jointly with other NRIs and Resident Indians (only relatives).  
- Mode of operation with Resident Indians is "Former or Survivor" (FRS), where only the primary applicant has transaction rights.  
- Resident joint applicants can be added as mandate holders.  

**Cash Withdrawal and Transaction Limits:**  
- Cash withdrawal limit: ₹25,000 per day  
- Transaction limit at merchant establishments: ₹50,000 per day (effective from 01 October 2011)  

**Debit Card:**  
- NRE Savings Account holders receive a Smart Shopper Silver Debit Card.  
- Joint account holders can get separate debit cards if the mode of operation is other than FRS or JTLY (Jointly).  
- Debit Card and PIN can be requested online via Net Banking "Service Request" or through 24-hour Customer Care.  
- Request forms can be downloaded and mailed to ICICI Bank’s Mumbai address or submitted at any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- NRIs in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can post documents to local P.O. Boxes to save courier charges.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- Debit Card annual charges, cash withdrawal, and balance enquiry charges at non-ICICI Bank ATMs apply (details available on the bank’s website).  

**Customer Support:**  
- Visa Global Customer Assistance Service is available for card-related support.  

KEY POINTS:  
- NRI definition and PIO eligibility criteria  
- NRE Savings Account is fully repatriable  
- Joint accounts allowed with NRIs and Resident Indian relatives (FRS mode)  
- Daily cash withdrawal limit: ₹25,000; transaction limit: ₹50,000  
- Smart Shopper Silver Debit Card issued to NRE account holders  
- Separate debit cards for joint holders under specific modes  
- Multiple channels to request debit cards and PINs  
- Local P.O. Box facility for NRIs in select countries to submit documents  
- Applicable fees for debit card and ATM usage at non-ICICI Bank ATMs
