SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of mutual fund schemes available through ICICI Bank, including various plans under Nippon India and SBI mutual funds. The schemes are categorized by fund house, plan type (Direct or Regular), and dividend options (Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Daily IDCW).  

Key mutual fund schemes include:  

1. **Nippon India Ultra Short Duration Fund**  
   - Plan Types: Direct and Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Daily IDCW  
   - Suitable for investors seeking low duration debt funds with regular income options.  

2. **SBI Banking and PSU Fund**  
   - Plan Types: Direct and Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Growth, Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Weekly IDCW  
   - Focuses on banking and public sector undertakings, suitable for moderate risk investors.  

3. **SBI Corporate Bond Fund**  
   - Plan Types: Direct and Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW  
   - Invests in corporate bonds, ideal for investors seeking stable income with moderate risk.  

4. **SBI CPSE Bond Plus SDL Sep 2026 50:50 Index Fund**  
   - Plan Types: Direct and Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Growth, IDCW  
   - Invests in CPSE bonds and State Development Loans, suitable for conservative investors.  

5. **SBI Credit Risk Fund**  
   - Plan Types: Direct  
   - Dividend Options: Daily IDCW, Growth, IDCW  
   - Targets credit risk bonds, suitable for investors willing to take higher risk for better returns.  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction. Investors can choose plans based on their investment horizon, risk appetite, and income requirements.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual fund schemes are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend options include Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, and Daily IDCW.  
- Fund categories include Ultra Short Duration, Banking & PSU, Corporate Bond, CPSE Bond, and Credit Risk funds.  
- Suitable for various investor profiles from conservative to moderate and high risk.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each plan and dividend option.
