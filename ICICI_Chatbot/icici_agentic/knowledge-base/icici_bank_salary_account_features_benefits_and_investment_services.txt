TITLE: ICICI Bank Salary Account – Features, Benefits, and Investment Services  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Accounts  
TAGS: Salary Account, Investment Services, Mutual Funds, Government Bonds, Demat Account, Online Account Opening, Interest Rates  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Salary Account offers a range of benefits designed to simplify salary management and provide investment opportunities for account holders. The account comes with competitive interest rates, especially favorable for existing account holders, making it an attractive option for employees and employers alike.

Key features include easy investment services where Salary Account holders can invest in Mutual Funds, Government Bonds, and purchase insurance policies directly through their account. Additionally, a Demat Account can be linked to the Salary Account, enabling seamless investments and trading activities.

Employers benefit from ICICI Bank’s online account opening facility, which allows them to open Salary Accounts for their employees quickly and conveniently with minimal documentation, eliminating the need to visit a branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Competitive interest rates, generally better for existing account holders  
- Investment options available: Mutual Funds, Government Bonds, Insurance policies  
- Ability to link a Demat Account for investments and trading  
- Online account opening facility for employers with minimal documentation  
- Simplifies salary management and investment processes for employees
