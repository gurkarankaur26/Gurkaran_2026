Gold Loans are a popular financing option for individuals needing quick funds for personal needs, business expansion, or emergencies. ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans up to 75% of the market value of the pledged gold, making it essential to understand eligibility criteria and how to calculate the loan amount.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Minimum age: 18 years  
- Applicable for both salaried and self-employed individuals  
- Stable income positively influences eligibility, though credit scores are not heavily considered  
- Gold purity accepted: 18 to 24 carats  
- Gold must be hallmarked or certified for purity  

**Loan Amount Calculation:**  
- Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio: Up to 75% of the current market value of gold  
- Market value is determined based on the prevailing gold price, which fluctuates regularly  
- Purity and weight of gold are critical; higher purity (closer to 24 carats) increases loan eligibility  
- Example: For gold valued at ₹1,00,000 with an LTV of 60%, the loan amount would be ₹60,000  

**Required Documents:**  
- Proof of identity (e.g., Aadhaar card, PAN card, Passport)  
- Proof of address (e.g., utility bills, rental agreement)  
- Proof of income (salary slips, bank statements)  
- Gold ownership proof and gold purity certificate or hallmark  

**Process to Calculate Eligibility:**  
1. Weigh your gold accurately using a gold scale.  
2. Verify the purity through hallmarking certificates or a professional jeweller.  
3. Check the current market price of gold.  
4. Calculate the maximum loan amount by applying the LTV ratio (up to 75%) to the market value of your gold.  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Gold Loan:**  
- Quick processing with minimal documentation  
- Competitive interest rates (specific rates vary; check with the bank)  
- Flexible repayment options  
- No prepayment penalty  
- Loan disbursal within hours after approval
