CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s FASTag offers a seamless and convenient way to pay toll charges electronically, eliminating the need for cash transactions at toll plazas. Key benefits include automatic deduction of toll charges, hassle-free travel without the need to carry cash or change, and multiple convenient recharge options.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
1. **Automatic Toll Payment:** Toll charges are automatically deducted from your FASTag account as you pass through toll plazas, ensuring a smooth and quick passage.  
2. **No Cash Hassles:** No need to carry cash or wait in queues for change at toll booths.  
3. **Convenient Recharge Options:** Recharge your FASTag account easily through ICICI Bank’s Retail Internet Banking (RIB), iMobile app, or UPI Mandate.  

**Standing Instructions (SI) for Auto Recharge:**  
- Customers can set up Standing Instructions via ICICI Bank Retail Net Banking to automatically recharge their FASTag account when the balance falls below a predefined threshold.  
- This eliminates the need to manually recharge the FASTag every time the balance is low, ensuring uninterrupted toll payments.  

**Auto Recharge through iMobile App:**  
- ICICI Bank provides an option to set auto recharge through the iMobile app, allowing customers to define recharge frequency and amount.  
- This feature helps avoid situations where insufficient funds in the FASTag wallet cause delays or double toll charges.  

**Auto Recharge via UPI Mandate:**  
- Customers can enable auto recharge using UPI Mandate by following these steps:  
  1. Login to ICICI Bank FASTag Customer Portal.  
  2. Navigate to ‘Payments’ → ‘Standing Instructions for UPI’.  
  3. Enter details to set up Standing Instructions through UPI.  
  4. Accept the authorization request in the linked UPI app on the same day.  
  5. Accept the pre-debit notification and enter PIN if required (PIN required only if recharge amount exceeds INR 15,000).  
  6. UPI Mandate will be triggered within 24 hours, and the recharge amount will be deducted automatically.  

- Once set, the recharge will happen automatically as per the defined frequency (e.g., daily), ensuring the FASTag wallet is always funded.  

**Important Notes:**  
- The auto recharge facility is linked to your bank account.  
- PIN entry is required only for recharge amounts exceeding INR 15,000.  
- The authorization and pre-debit acceptance are one-time processes for setting up the mandate.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag enables automatic toll payments without cash.  
- Standing Instructions via Retail Net Banking allow automatic recharge below threshold limits.  
- Auto recharge can be set up via iMobile app or UPI Mandate for hassle-free wallet top-ups.  
- UPI Mandate requires authorization and pre-debit acceptance; PIN needed only for amounts above INR 15,000.  
- Ensures uninterrupted travel and avoids double toll charges due to insufficient funds.
