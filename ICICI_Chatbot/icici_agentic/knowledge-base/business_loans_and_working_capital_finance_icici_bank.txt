ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of business loan products designed to support the growth and financial needs of businesses. These include Working Capital Loans and Term Loans with facilities available up to ₹75 Crores.  

Key features of ICICI Bank’s business loans include:  
- Loans can be availed by pledging an asset as collateral or security to meet working capital and long-term funding requirements.  
- Collateral-free loans are also available, especially for Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).  
- Government-supported loan schemes are offered to MSMEs to facilitate easier access to finance.  

These loans are structured to help businesses manage their cash flow, invest in growth opportunities, and meet operational expenses efficiently.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹75 Crores  
- Loan types: Working Capital Loan, Term Loan  
- Collateral: Loans available with or without collateral  
- Special focus: Government-supported loans for MSMEs  
- Purpose: To meet working capital and long-term funding needs
