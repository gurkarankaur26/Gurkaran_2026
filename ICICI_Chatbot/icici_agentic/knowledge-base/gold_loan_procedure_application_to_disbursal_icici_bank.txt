CONTENT:  
Gold Loans provide quick access to funds by pledging gold jewellery or ornaments as collateral. ICICI Bank offers a streamlined Gold Loan process with minimal documentation and competitive interest rates. This guide covers the entire procedure from application to disbursal.

**What is a Gold Loan?**  
A secured loan where gold jewellery or ornaments are pledged as collateral. The loan amount depends on the value of the gold.

**Key Benefits:**  
- Quick disbursal of funds  
- Attractive and competitive interest rates  
- Flexible repayment options: EMI, Bullet Payment, Partial Repayments  
- Minimal documentation required  

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Age: 18 to 85 years  
- Nationality: Indian citizens only  
- Gold Quality: 18 to 24 carats accepted  
- Eligible Applicants: Salaried individuals, self-employed, business owners, homemakers, senior citizens  

**Documents Required:**  
- Identity Proof: Passport, Voter ID, Aadhaar Card, Driving Licence  
- Address Proof: Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence, or NREGA Job Card  
- Additional: Proof of landholding if applying for an Agriculture Loan above ₹1 lakh  

**Application Process:**  
1. **Visit ICICI Bank Website or Branch:**  
   - Online: Access the Gold Loan section on the official ICICI Bank website  
   - In-Branch: Visit any ICICI Bank branch with gold jewellery and documents  

2. **Complete the Application Form:**  
   - Online: Fill in personal details (Full Name, Mobile Number, Address), agree to terms, and submit  
   - In-Branch: Fill the physical application form  

3. **Gold Evaluation:**  
   - Submit your gold for evaluation to determine loan amount based on purity and weight  

4. **Loan Approval and Disbursal:**  
   - After verification and evaluation, the loan amount is sanctioned and disbursed quickly  

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- Interest rates are competitive and vary based on loan amount and tenure (specific rates to be confirmed at application)  
- Minimal processing fees apply (details available at branch or website)  

**Repayment Options:**  
- EMI (Equated Monthly Installments)  
- Bullet Payment (lump sum repayment at end of tenure)  
- Partial Repayments allowed  

KEY POINTS:  
- Gold Loan is a secured loan against gold jewellery (18-24 carats)  
- Eligibility: Indian citizens aged 18-85, including salaried, self-employed, homemakers, senior citizens  
- Minimal documentation: ID and address proofs required  
- Quick and easy application online or at branch  
- Flexible repayment options with competitive interest rates  
- Additional documents needed for agriculture loans above ₹1 lakh  
- Loan amount based on gold evaluation  
- Fast disbursal after approval
