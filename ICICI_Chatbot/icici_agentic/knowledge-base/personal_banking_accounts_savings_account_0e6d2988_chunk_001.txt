TITLE:  
Conversion of ICICI Bank Savings Account to NRO Account

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Accounts / NRO Account Conversion  
TAGS: Savings Account, NRO Account, Resident Account, Non-Resident Indian, Account Conversion, FEMA Guidelines  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank allows customers to convert their Resident Savings Account into a Non-Resident Ordinary (NRO) Account through two modes:

1. **DIY (Do It Yourself) Mode:**  
   - Log into your Net Banking account.  
   - Navigate: Login > Customer Service > Service Request > Click on ‘Convert your SB account to NRO Account’.  

2. **Assisted Mode:**  
   - While filling in relationship details, mention the Resident Account number(s) to be converted to NRO Account(s).  
   - The Account number(s) will remain the same for convenience.  

**Important Information:**  
- If you hold both a Resident Account and a Non-Resident Account and your status is Non-Resident Indian (NRI), you must convert your Resident Account to an NRO Account as per FEMA guidelines.  
- You need to fill out the Resident-to-NRO conversion request form (available as a PDF, 132 KB).  
- After conversion, your Account Number remains unchanged. Funds from the Resident Savings Account will be automatically transferred to the NRO Savings Account.  
- If your Indian address has not changed, no proof of address submission is required.  
- If your Indian address has changed, you must submit proof of the changed address.  
- Submission of proof of your current overseas address is mandatory.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Two modes for conversion: DIY via Net Banking and Assisted Mode.  
- Account number remains the same post-conversion.  
- Mandatory submission of overseas address proof.  
- Proof of Indian address required only if changed.  
- Compliance with FEMA guidelines is necessary for NRIs holding both Resident and Non-Resident accounts.  
- Resident-to-NRO conversion request form must be submitted.
