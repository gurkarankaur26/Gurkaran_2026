TITLE: ICICI Bank Plus Card - Add-on Credit Card for Your Family  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Add-on Credit Card, Supplementary Card, Family Credit Card, Reward Points, Credit Limit Sharing  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Plus Card is an add-on or supplementary credit card issued against a primary ICICI Bank credit card. It allows family members to share the primary card’s credit limit while maintaining individual spending freedom. You can apply for up to 3 Plus Cards, enabling your loved ones to enjoy the benefits and rewards associated with the primary credit card.

Key features of the ICICI Bank Plus Card include:  
- Shared credit limit with the primary cardholder  
- Earn reward points on spends made using the Plus Card  
- Access to exclusive offers such as BookMyShow discounts and deals  

The Plus Card is designed to provide convenience and flexibility for families, allowing multiple users to benefit from a single credit line while enjoying individual control over their expenses.

KEY POINTS:  
- Add-on credit card linked to a primary ICICI Bank credit card  
- Up to 3 Plus Cards can be issued per primary card  
- Shared credit limit across primary and Plus Cards  
- Earn reward points on all transactions made using the Plus Card  
- Access to exclusive offers, including BookMyShow discounts  
- Enables family members to have individual spending freedom under one credit line
