CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential offers a variety of mutual fund schemes with multiple dividend payout options and growth plans. These schemes cater to different investment needs and preferences, including daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly, and quarterly dividend options (IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal). The funds are available in both Direct and Regular plans.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:  

1. ICICI Prudential Savings Fund  
   - Dividend Options: Daily IDCW, Fortnightly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Other IDCW  
   - Growth Option available  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  

2. ICICI Prudential Short Term Fund  
   - Dividend Option: IDCW  
   - Growth Option available  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  

3. ICICI Prudential Ultra Short Term Fund  
   - Dividend Options: Daily IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW  
   - Growth Option available  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  

4. Invesco India Corporate Bond Fund  
   - Dividend Option: Annual IDCW  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple dividend payout frequencies: Daily, Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly, Quarterly, Annual  
- Both Direct and Regular plans available for most funds  
- Growth option available for all major funds  
- Suitable for investors seeking regular income or capital appreciation  
- Fund codes are assigned for each plan and option for easy identification
