TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Mutual Fund Investment Options - Axis Mutual Funds Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Axis Mutual Funds, Liquid Funds, Money Market Funds, Overnight Funds, Short Duration Funds, IDCW, Growth, Investment Codes

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Axis Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include Liquid Funds, Money Market Funds, Overnight Funds, and Short Duration Funds with different plan options such as Direct (Dir), Regular (Reg), and Retirement (Ret) plans. Each scheme offers multiple dividend payout options including Monthly IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), Weekly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Annual IDCW, Daily IDCW, and Growth options.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:

1. **Axis Liquid Fund**  
   - Plans: Regular, Retirement  
   - Dividend Options: Monthly IDCW, Weekly IDCW  
   - Suitable for investors seeking liquidity with regular income.

2. **Axis Money Market Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct, Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Annual IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Daily IDCW, Growth  
   - Ideal for short-term investments with moderate returns and liquidity.

3. **Axis Overnight Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct, Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Growth  
   - Suitable for ultra-short-term investments with daily liquidity.

4. **Axis Short Duration Fund**  
   - Plans: Direct, Regular, Retirement  
   - Dividend Options: Monthly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Growth  
   - Designed for investors looking for short-term debt instruments with moderate risk.

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 112714 for Axis Liquid Fund - Ret - Mthly IDCW). Investors can choose the plan and dividend option based on their investment horizon and income requirements.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Axis Mutual Fund schemes are available via ICICI Bank for diversified investment.  
- Plans include Direct, Regular, and Retirement options catering to different investor preferences.  
- Dividend payout options vary: Monthly, Weekly, Quarterly, Annual, Daily IDCW, and Growth.  
- Suitable for investors seeking liquidity, short-term income, or growth through debt and liquid funds.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for easy reference and transaction.  
- No specific fees, eligibility criteria, or interest rates are provided in this document; investors should refer to the respective scheme documents for detailed terms.
