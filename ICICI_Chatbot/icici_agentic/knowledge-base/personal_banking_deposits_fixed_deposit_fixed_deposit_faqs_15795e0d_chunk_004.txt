TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit (FD) - Features, Benefits, Interest Rates, and FAQs

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Fixed Deposit, Investment, Banking Products

TAGS:  
Fixed Deposit, FD Calculator, Interest Rates, Tax Saver FD, Loan Facility, FD Benefits, Premature Withdrawal, FD Laddering, Deposit Insurance

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits (FDs) offer guaranteed returns at fixed interest rates with flexible tenures ranging from 7 days to 10 years. Customers can open and manage FDs digitally via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app and Net Banking.

**Interest Rates and Calculation:**  
- Interest rates vary based on tenure and customer category and are subject to change.  
- Interest can be credited monthly or quarterly to the linked Savings or Current Account.  
- Interest is calculated at the contracted rate; monthly payouts are at a discounted rate.  
- The FD Calculator on the ICICI Bank website provides indicative maturity amounts based on user inputs but does not include tax deductions or TDS.  
- Tax Saver FDs with a tenure of 5 years are available and can be estimated using the FD Calculator.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits:**  
- Guaranteed fixed returns, unaffected by market fluctuations.  
- Flexible tenure options from 7 days up to 10 years.  
- Monthly or quarterly interest payout options for regular income.  
- Loan or overdraft facility against FD.  
- Auto-renewal or auto-closure options upon maturity.  
- Digital opening and management through iMobile app and Net Banking.  
- Deposits insured up to ₹5 lakh per depositor per bank under DICGC (including principal and interest).  
- Suitable for low-risk or conservative investors.  
- FD laddering allows creating multiple smaller-tenure FDs to maintain liquidity and earn better interest than Savings Accounts.

**Premature Withdrawal and Closure:**  
- Callable FDs can be closed before maturity but are subject to penalties and revised interest rates as per bank policy.  
- Short-tenure and laddered FDs provide liquidity and flexibility.  
- Upon maturity, funds are credited to the linked account if auto-closure is selected; otherwise, the FD renews automatically for the same tenure if auto-renewal is chosen.  
- Interest payout failures due to incorrect or inactive account details are reprocessed after correction. Account details can be updated via Net Banking, iMobile app, or branch.

**Process to Manage FDs:**  
- Open and manage FDs digitally via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app or Net Banking.  
- Update payout account details online or at any branch.  
- Withdraw or renew FDs online or by visiting a branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Tenure: 7 days to 10 years  
- Interest payout: Monthly or quarterly  
- Tax Saver FD tenure: 5 years  
- Deposit insurance: Up to ₹5 lakh per depositor under DICGC  
- Premature withdrawal: Allowed with penalties and revised interest rates  
- Digital management: Available via iMobile app and Net Banking  
- Loan facility: Available against FD  
- Auto-renewal and auto-closure options available  
- FD laddering helps maintain liquidity and better returns than Savings Account  
- FD Calculator provides indicative returns excluding tax deductions
