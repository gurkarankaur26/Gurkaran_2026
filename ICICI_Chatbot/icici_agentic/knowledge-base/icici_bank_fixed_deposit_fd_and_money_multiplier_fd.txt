CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Fixed Deposits (FD) and Money Multiplier FD products designed to help customers grow their savings with attractive interest rates and flexible tenures. These products combine convenience, liquidity, and high returns, suitable for various financial goals such as education, vacations, or retirement planning.

**Money Multiplier FD**  
- Combines the liquidity of a Savings Account with the high returns of a Fixed Deposit.  
- Automatically linked to your Savings Account for added convenience.  

**Eligibility Criteria for FD and Money Multiplier FD**  
- Age Requirement: Applicants must be 18 years or older.  
- Eligible Applicants: Resident Indians, Overseas Citizens of India (OCI), Persons of Indian Origin (PIO), and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs).  
- Eligible Entities: Partnership Firms, Limited Companies, Sole Proprietorship Firms, Families, Hindu Undivided Families (HUFs), Trust Accounts.  

**How to Open an FD with ICICI Bank**  
1. Use the iMobile app or Internet Banking to access your account.  
2. Navigate to the Fixed Deposit (FD) section.  
3. Enter the amount you want to invest.  
4. Choose a tenure that fits your financial objectives (short-term to long-term options available).  
5. Select whether you want to receive returns regularly or on maturity.  
6. Review the details and confirm. No documentation is required, and the FD is opened instantaneously.  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank FD**  
- High interest rates compared to regular savings accounts.  
- Flexible tenure options to suit different financial goals.  
- Easy online management and auto-renewal options.  
- Suitable for long-term financial planning.  
- Instant account opening with no paperwork.
