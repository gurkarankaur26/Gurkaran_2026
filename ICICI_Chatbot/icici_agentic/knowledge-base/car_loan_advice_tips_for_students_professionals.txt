TITLE: Car Loan Advice & Tips for Students & Professionals  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans / Car Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Student Loan, Professional Loan, Interest Rates, Eligibility, Fees, Loan Process, Benefits  

CONTENT:  
This document provides comprehensive advice and tips for students and professionals seeking car loans from ICICI Bank. It covers eligibility criteria, interest rates, fees, application process, benefits, and frequently asked questions to help borrowers make informed decisions.

Eligibility:  
- Students and professionals aged 21 to 60 years can apply.  
- Minimum income criteria apply for salaried and self-employed individuals.  
- Valid identity proof, address proof, and income documents are required.  

Interest Rates:  
- Competitive interest rates starting from 7.50% per annum (subject to change based on credit profile).  
- Rates vary depending on loan amount, tenure, and applicant profile.  

Loan Amount & Tenure:  
- Loan amount up to 90% of the on-road price of the vehicle.  
- Tenure ranges from 12 months to 84 months (1 to 7 years).  

Fees and Charges:  
- Processing fee applicable as per the loan amount (typically up to 1% of the loan amount).  
- Prepayment charges may apply if the loan is closed before the tenure ends.  
- Late payment fees applicable on overdue EMIs.  

Application Process:  
1. Submit the online application form with required documents.  
2. Verification of documents and credit assessment by ICICI Bank.  
3. Loan approval and sanction communicated to the applicant.  
4. Disbursement of loan amount directly to the vehicle dealer or applicant’s account.  

Benefits:  
- Quick and hassle-free loan approval process.  
- Flexible repayment options with EMI calculators available online.  
- Option to top-up loan or balance transfer from other lenders.  
- Dedicated customer support for loan servicing and queries.  

FAQs:  
Q: Can students apply for a car loan without income proof?  
A: Students may need a co-applicant with a stable income to qualify.  

Q: What documents are required for self-employed professionals?  
A: Income tax returns, bank statements, business proof, identity and address proof.  

Q: Is there a penalty for prepayment?  
A: Prepayment charges may apply depending on the loan terms.  

Q: How long does the loan approval take?  
A: Typically 2-5 working days after document submission and verification.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility: Age 21-60, income proof required  
- Interest rates from 7.50% p.a.  
- Loan up to 90% of vehicle price  
- Tenure: 1 to 7 years  
- Processing fee up to 1% of loan amount  
- Quick approval and disbursement  
- Flexible repayment options  
- Prepayment and late payment charges applicable
