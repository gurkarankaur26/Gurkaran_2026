CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Personal Loans with flexible eligibility criteria and transparent terms to suit both salaried and self-employed individuals. The Personal Loan Eligibility Calculator helps customers estimate the maximum loan amount they can avail based on income, repayment capacity, credit history, and existing liabilities.

**Key Features:**  
- Loan amount up to ₹50 lakh  
- Repayment tenure up to 72 months  
- Zero foreclosure charges after payment of 24 EMIs  
- No collateral required  
- 100% digital application process  
- Stable and predictable interest rates for consistent monthly payments  
- No hidden charges; all fees are disclosed upfront  

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Age: 20 to 58 years for Indian citizens  
- Minimum age: 20 years for salaried individuals, 23 years for self-employed  
- Stable income source and satisfactory credit profile required  
- Minimum salary requirement varies based on employer type and relationship with ICICI Bank  
- Self-employed individuals can apply under business instalment loans  
- Eligibility depends on monthly income, existing financial obligations, repayment capacity, credit score, employment profile, and income stability  
- Final eligibility is subject to internal assessment, verification, and credit evaluation  

**Required Documents:**  
_For Salaried Applicants:_  
- Proof of identity and residence (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card)  
- Latest 3 months’ salary slips  
- Latest 3 months’ bank statements showing salary credits  

_For Self-Employed Applicants:_  
- Proof of identity and residence  
- Income proof (audited financials for last 2 years)  
- Latest 6 months’ bank statements  
- Office address proof  
- Proof of residence or office ownership  
- Proof of continuity of business  

**Application Process:**  
- Use the Personal Loan Eligibility Calculator online by entering monthly income, current EMIs, and desired loan tenure to get an estimate of eligible loan amount  
- Submit required documents for verification  
- Undergo internal assessment and credit evaluation  
- Upon approval, complete the digital application process  

KEY POINTS:  
- Maximum loan amount: ₹50 lakh  
- Tenure: Up to 72 months  
- Zero foreclosure charges after 24 EMIs  
- No collateral required  
- Eligibility based on age, income, credit profile, employment type  
- Separate documentation for salaried and self-employed  
- Transparent fees and stable interest rates  
- Digital application and eligibility check available
