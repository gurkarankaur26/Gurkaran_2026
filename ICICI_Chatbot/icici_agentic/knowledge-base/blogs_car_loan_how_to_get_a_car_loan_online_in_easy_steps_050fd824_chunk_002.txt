ICICI Bank Car Loan - Direct Dealer Disbursement Process and Application Guide

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a streamlined and flexible Car Loan process with direct dealer disbursement, enabling customers to purchase vehicles easily and efficiently. The process is designed to be digital, minimal in documentation, and customer-friendly, with features such as eKYC and customizable loan terms.

**Car Loan Direct Dealer Disbursement Process:**  
1. **Select the Car and Dealer:**  
   Customers can choose any authorised dealer and car of their preference. ICICI Bank’s eligible customers have the flexibility to purchase from any authorised dealer across India.

2. **Customise Your Loan:**  
   Tailor the loan amount and tenure to suit your budget and financial needs. ICICI Bank offers flexibility in adjusting loan terms.

3. **eSign the Agreement:**  
   Complete the loan formalities by digitally signing the agreement, ensuring a smooth and quick process.

4. **Disbursement to Dealer’s Account:**  
   After signing, the loan amount is directly disbursed to the dealer’s account, facilitating easy purchase completion.

Post application, customers can download their delivery order and present it to the dealer to take delivery of their new car.

**eKYC for Car Loan:**  
ICICI Bank uses an eKYC process that allows customers to enter their current address for car registration, even if it differs from their permanent address. Verification is done via Aadhaar OTP, reducing physical documentation and speeding up processing. This enables faster car registration and a hassle-free experience.

**Key Features of ICICI Bank Car Loan:**  
- **Minimal Documentation:** Only essential documents are required, reducing paperwork.  
- **Loan Amount:** Up to 100% of the on-road price of the car can be financed.  
- **Digital Application:** Entire process including application, document upload, and loan tracking is digital, minimizing branch visits.

**Steps to Apply for a Car Loan:**  
1. **Enter Necessary Information:**  
   Provide loan amount, tenure, PAN, Aadhaar details, and basic employment/financial information.

2. **Customise Your Loan:**  
   Select car and dealer details, then adjust loan amount and tenure based on eligibility.

3. **eSign and Submit:**  
   Digitally sign the loan agreement to complete the application.

4. **Loan Disbursement:**  
   Loan amount is disbursed directly to the dealer’s account.

**Benefits:**  
- Flexibility to buy from any authorised dealer across India.  
- Up to 100% financing of on-road price.  
- Quick and hassle-free digital process with eKYC.  
- Minimal documentation and branch visits.  
- Direct disbursement to dealer ensures smooth purchase.
