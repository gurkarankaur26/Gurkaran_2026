TITLE: Home Loans for Self-Employed Individuals  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Home Loans  
TAGS: Home Loan, Self-Employed, Eligibility, Loan Application, Co-Applicant  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers home loans tailored for self-employed individuals, including both professionals and non-professionals. Even without extensive income proof, self-employed applicants can secure home loan approvals through flexible eligibility criteria and minimal documentation requirements. Applicants have the option to add a co-applicant, such as a spouse or immediate family member, to enhance their loan eligibility. ICICI Bank provides comprehensive assistance throughout the loan application process to facilitate quick approval and help applicants achieve their dream of homeownership.

KEY POINTS:  
- Home loans available for self-employed professionals and non-professionals.  
- Flexible eligibility criteria and minimal documentation for self-employed applicants.  
- Option to add a co-applicant (spouse or immediate family member) to increase eligibility.  
- Complete assistance provided during the application process for faster approval.
