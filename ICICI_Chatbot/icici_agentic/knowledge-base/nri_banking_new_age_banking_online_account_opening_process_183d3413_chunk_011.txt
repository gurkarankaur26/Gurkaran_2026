TITLE:  
ICICI Bank NRE/NRO Account Fund Transfer Options and Do Not Call (DNC) Registration

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Banking Services, NRE/NRO Accounts, Fund Transfer, Customer Communication

TAGS:  
ICICI Bank, NRE Account, NRO Account, Fund Transfer, Wire Transfer, Money2India, Exchange Houses, Foreign Currency Cheques, INR Cheques, Do Not Call Registry

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides multiple options for transferring funds to your NRE (Non-Resident External) or NRO (Non-Resident Ordinary) savings accounts. These include both online and offline modes, catering to convenience and large value transfers.

**Fund Transfer Options:**

1. **Online Modes:**  
   - **Money2India:**  
     An internet-based money transfer tracking service that offers easy and convenient fund transfers to ICICI Bank NRE/NRO accounts.  
     - Requires a one-time instant online registration.  
     - Available only in Canada, Hong Kong, Singapore, Sweden, Switzerland, UK, USA, and UAE.  
     - For registration and details, visit www.money2india.com.

2. **Offline Modes:**  
   - **Wire Transfer:**  
     - Suitable for large value transfers.  
     - Available for most countries.  
     - To initiate, visit your local bank abroad and provide wire transfer instructions to transfer funds to your ICICI Bank NRE/NRO account.

   - **Exchange House Transfers:**  
     - ICICI Bank has tie-ups with 24 key Exchange Houses and Correspondent Banks in Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC) countries.  
     - GCC countries include Bahrain, Kuwait, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Oman, and United Arab Emirates.  
     - Facilities include Insta-Transfers, I-Express, Speed Remittance, and INR Demand Draft (DD) services.

   - **Foreign Currency Cheques:**  
     - Cheques drawn on foreign banks in foreign currency can be deposited into the beneficiary’s NRE Savings Account.

   - **INR Cheques:**  
     - INR cheques can be drawn from other NRE Savings Accounts in favor of your ICICI Bank NRE savings account.  
     - INR cheques can also be drawn from any account in favor of your ICICI Bank NRO savings account.

**Important Notes:**  
- Availability of transfer modes may vary depending on your country of residence.  
- Wire transfers and exchange house transfers are reliable options for large and urgent remittances.

**Do Not Call (DNC) Registry:**  
- Customers can register under the DNC to avoid promotional calls.  
- Important bank communications such as account statements and revision of charges will continue even if registered under DNC.  
- To register, provide your name, country, telephone numbers (format: ISD - Area code and number for landline; ISD - Number for mobile), and email ID.  
- Registration requests can be sent via email to donotcallregistry@icicibank.com.  
- Processing time for DNC registration is at least 15 days from receipt of details.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund transfer options available for ICICI Bank NRE/NRO accounts: online (Money2India) and offline (Wire Transfer, Exchange Houses, Foreign Currency Cheques, INR Cheques).  
- Money2India service requires one-time registration and is available in select countries.  
- Wire transfers are suitable for large value transfers and require visiting your local bank abroad.  
- Exchange house transfers are available in GCC countries with multiple remittance options.  
- Foreign currency and INR cheques can be deposited into respective NRE/NRO accounts.  
- DNC registration helps avoid promotional calls but does not stop essential bank communications.  
- DNC registration requires submission of personal and contact details and takes at least 15 days to process.
