CONTENT:  
Obtaining a credit card as a self-employed professional can be challenging due to irregular income patterns and unique financial documentation requirements. Unlike salaried individuals, self-employed applicants must provide additional proof of income stability to qualify for credit card approval. ICICI Bank recognises these challenges and has streamlined the application process to better serve self-employed customers.

**Eligibility and Documentation:**  
- Self-employed professionals must provide income verification documents such as:  
  - Income Tax Returns (ITR)  
  - Bank Statements  
  - Profit and Loss Statements  
- Identity proof documents required include:  
  - Aadhaar Card  
  - Driving Licence  
  - Other government-issued identification  
- It is advisable to consult an accountant to verify income documents before applying.  

**Creditworthiness:**  
- A strong credit score is essential for approval.  
- Factors influencing creditworthiness include:  
  - On-time payment history  
  - Low debt-to-income ratio  
- Maintaining a good credit history improves chances of approval and access to better terms and benefits.  

**Application Tips:**  
- Research eligibility criteria and required documents thoroughly before applying.  
- Demonstrate financial responsibility by showing savings and timely payments.  
- Avoid multiple rejected applications as they can negatively impact your credit score.  
- Prepare all necessary documents in advance to ensure a smooth application process.  

ICICI Bank’s tailored approach helps self-employed professionals navigate the credit card application process with greater ease, offering a smoother experience and clearer guidelines for income verification and eligibility.

KEY POINTS:  
- Self-employed applicants must provide ITR, bank statements, or profit and loss statements as income proof.  
- Identity proof such as Aadhaar or Driving Licence is mandatory.  
- Strong credit score and good credit history are critical for approval.  
- Consulting an accountant for income verification is recommended.  
- Research and preparation reduce chances of rejection and credit score damage.  
- ICICI Bank offers a streamlined credit card application process for self-employed professionals.
