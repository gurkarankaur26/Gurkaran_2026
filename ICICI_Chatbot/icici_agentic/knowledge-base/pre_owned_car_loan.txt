TITLE: Pre-owned Car Loan  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Used Car Loan, Pre-owned Car Loan, Interest Rates, Loan Tenure, Application Process  
CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Pre-owned Car Loan specifically designed for purchasing used vehicles. This loan differs from a normal Car Loan, which is intended for brand-new cars. The key differences include interest rates and loan tenures, with Pre-owned Car Loans typically having slightly higher interest rates compared to loans for new cars.  

To apply, customers can fill out an online application form to receive a customised Car Loan quote instantly. This streamlined process helps borrowers understand their loan options and terms quickly.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Pre-owned Car Loan is for purchasing used cars.  
- Normal Car Loan is for brand-new vehicles.  
- Interest rates on Pre-owned Car Loans are usually slightly higher than those for new car loans.  
- Loan tenures may vary between Pre-owned and new car loans.  
- Online application available for instant customised quotes.
