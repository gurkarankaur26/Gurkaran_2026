CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of banking services tailored for personal, business, corporate, and NRI customers. The bank provides various loan products including personal loans, home loans, vehicle loans, education loans, and gold loans with competitive interest rates. Customers can select loan types based on their requirements and check applicable interest rates and eligibility criteria.

Gold loans are available where customers can pledge gold ornaments of specified carat and weight to avail loans. The loan amount is determined based on the weight and purity of the gold. The bank provides flexible options to increase or decrease the number of ornaments pledged.

ICICI Bank ensures transparent processes for loan application, approval, and disbursal. Customers can access detailed information about interest rates, fees, and eligibility on the official website or by contacting the bank directly.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan types: Personal, Home, Vehicle, Education, Gold Loans  
- Gold loan eligibility based on ornament weight and carat  
- Competitive interest rates (specific rates vary and are available on the website)  
- Flexible loan amount based on gold pledged  
- Transparent loan application and approval process  
- Services available for Personal, Business, Corporate, and NRI customers
