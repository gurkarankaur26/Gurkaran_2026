TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Salary Account – Features, Eligibility, Benefits, and Comparison with Savings Account

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Bank Accounts

TAGS:  
Salary Account, Savings Account, Account Benefits, Eligibility, Fees, Transaction Limits, ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Salary Accounts designed specifically for salaried individuals receiving regular income from their employers. These accounts come with several benefits over regular Savings Accounts, including higher transaction limits, waived account maintenance fees, and reduced transaction charges.

**Eligibility for Organisation Partnership:**  
- Organisations must employ at least 20 employees.  
- Average salary criteria: Rs 20,000 for rural areas and Rs 25,000 for urban areas.

**Documents Required to Open a Salary Account:**  
- Valid ID proof such as PAN Card, Passport with photo and signature, Voter ID Card, Aadhaar Card, or Driver’s Licence.  
- Employment details and salary slips as proof of employment.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Salary Account:**  
- Higher transaction limits compared to Savings Accounts, allowing larger ATM withdrawals and debit card transactions.  
- No account maintenance fees, saving customers from monthly or annual charges.  
- Reduced fees for NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS transactions, making fund transfers more affordable.  
- Access to additional features such as free and often unlimited chequebooks.  

**Comparison Between Savings Account and Salary Account:**  

| Feature                     | Savings Account                          | Salary Account                          |
|-----------------------------|----------------------------------------|---------------------------------------|
| Purpose                     | To save money and earn interest        | For salaried individuals receiving salary |
| Transaction Limits          | Lower                                  | Higher                                |
| Account Maintenance Fees    | Usually applicable                      | Waived                                |
| Chequebooks                 | Limited free chequebooks                | Free and often unlimited chequebooks  |
| Documentation              | ID proof, address proof                 | ID proof, address proof, employment proof, salary slips |

**Additional Information:**  
- Converting an existing ICICI Bank Savings Account to a Salary Account allows customers to enjoy the benefits of higher transaction limits, waived fees, and reduced transaction charges.  
- Both account types serve different financial needs; salaried individuals are encouraged to opt for Salary Accounts to maximize benefits.

KEY POINTS:  
- Organisations must have at least 20 employees with minimum average salaries (Rs 20,000 rural, Rs 25,000 urban) to partner with ICICI Bank.  
- Salary Account holders enjoy no account maintenance fees and higher transaction limits.  
- Reduced transaction fees for NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS for Salary Account holders.  
- Free and often unlimited chequebooks for Salary Account holders.  
- Required documents include valid ID proof and employment proof with salary slips.  
- Salary Accounts are ideal for salaried individuals; Savings Accounts are for general savings with interest benefits.  
- Conversion from Savings to Salary Account is beneficial for salaried customers.
