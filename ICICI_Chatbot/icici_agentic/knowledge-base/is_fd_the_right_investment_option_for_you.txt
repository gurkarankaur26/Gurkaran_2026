CONTENT:  
Fixed Deposit (FD) is a reliable and safe investment option offered by ICICI Bank, allowing investors to deposit a specific sum for a fixed tenure to meet long-term financial goals. FDs provide assured and steady returns, unaffected by market fluctuations, making them ideal for capital preservation and predictable growth.

Key features of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits include:  
- Tenure flexibility: FDs can be opened for periods ranging from 7 days to 10 years, enabling investors to align deposits with their financial objectives.  
- Easy online opening: FDs can be created online in minutes with minimal documentation, providing convenience and time savings.  
- Callable Fixed Deposits: These allow quick access to funds without breaking the FD or compromising returns, useful in emergencies.  
- Overdraft facility: Investors can avail a bank overdraft against their FD as collateral, providing liquidity without liquidating the deposit.
