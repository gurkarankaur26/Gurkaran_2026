CONTENT:  
When choosing investment products, Fixed Deposits (FDs) and Recurring Deposits (RDs) are popular options due to their risk-free nature and fixed returns. Both products are not affected by market fluctuations, making them suitable for conservative investors. ICICI Bank also offers a flexible recurring deposit called iWish, which provides additional convenience.

**What is a Fixed Deposit (FD)?**  
- Deposit a fixed amount for a specific tenure ranging from 7 days to 10 years.  
- Interest is credited monthly, quarterly, or reinvested in the principal.  
- Interest rates remain fixed for the entire tenure.  

**What is a Recurring Deposit (RD)?**  
- Deposit a fixed amount at regular intervals, usually monthly.  
- Interest is earned monthly and compounded quarterly.  
- Interest rates remain fixed for the entire tenure.  

**What is the iWish Flexible Recurring Deposit?**  
- Allows investing any amount at any time during the tenure, unlike fixed monthly deposits in regular RD.  
- Available to all ICICI Bank Internet Banking customers at no extra cost.  
- Offers the same interest rates as regular RD.  

**Benefits of FD, RD, and iWish Flexible RD:**  
- Higher returns compared to regular savings accounts.  
- Secured investment with guaranteed returns.  
- Flexible tenure and investment amount options.  
- Can be used as collateral for credit cards or loans.  
- Helps inculcate the habit of regular investing.

KEY POINTS:  
- FD and RD offer fixed returns and are risk-free investment options.  
- iWish flexible RD allows flexible deposit amounts and timing.  
- Interest rates vary between 2.50% to 5.50% depending on the product and tenure.  
- Minimum deposit amounts differ: FD (₹10,000), RD (₹500), iWish (₹50).  
- Tenure options vary: FD (7 days to 10 years), RD (6 months to 10 years), iWish (6 months to 5 years).
