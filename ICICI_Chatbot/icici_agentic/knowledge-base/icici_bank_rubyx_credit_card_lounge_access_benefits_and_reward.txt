CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Rubyx Credit Card offers exclusive benefits including complimentary movie/event tickets via BookMyShow and lounge access. To avail the complimentary tickets offer starting April 1, 2026, cardholders must spend ₹25,000 or more on their Rubyx Credit Card within the preceding quarter. This spend qualifies the customer for the complimentary ticket offer in the following calendar quarter.

**Spends and Offer Eligibility Period:**  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Dec 26, 2025 and Mar 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie/event tickets from Apr 1, 2026 to June 30, 2026.  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Mar 26, 2026 and June 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie/event tickets from July 1, 2026 to Sep 30, 2026.

Customers holding dual network cards (Mastercard & Amex or Visa & RuPay) can avail one complimentary movie offer per month, per card variant. Any additional bookings beyond the complimentary benefit will be charged as per the rates set by the service provider.

Add-on or Plus card members must individually meet the ₹25,000 spend requirement in the preceding quarter to be eligible for the complimentary ticket offer in the subsequent quarter.

For booking movie tickets and detailed terms and conditions, customers are advised to refer to the official ICICI Bank website.

KEY POINTS:  
- Complimentary movie/event tickets via BookMyShow available on Rubyx Credit Card.  
- Eligibility requires ₹25,000 minimum spend in the preceding quarter.  
- Spend periods and corresponding offer periods are clearly defined.  
- Dual network cardholders can avail one complimentary ticket per month, per variant.  
- Add-on/Plus cardholders must meet spend criteria individually.  
- Additional bookings beyond complimentary tickets are chargeable.
