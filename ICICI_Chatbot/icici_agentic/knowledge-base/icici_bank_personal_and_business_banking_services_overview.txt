CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of personal and business banking products and services designed to meet diverse financial needs.  

**Personal Banking Services:**  
- **Savings Account:** Open a savings account for seamless payments and easy money management.  
- **Fixed Deposits (FDs):** Secure your future with stable returns through fixed deposits tailored to your financial goals.  
- **Payments:** Use scan and pay options for instant, wallet-free shopping and transactions.  
- **Credit Cards:** Explore various credit card options to support your financial journey with benefits and rewards.  
- **Home Loans:** Avail home loans through a simple 5-step process to help you realize your dream home.  
- **Mutual Funds (SIP):** Invest in thousands of mutual fund schemes via Systematic Investment Plans (SIP) to grow your wealth.  
- **Life Insurance:** Choose affordable and flexible term insurance plans to protect your family’s future.  
- **Special Services:** Access exclusive personal banking offers and explore over 2,500 investment schemes.  

**NRI Banking:**  
- Attractive interest rates on FCNR deposits to help NRIs grow their savings.  
- Comprehensive money transfer, loans, and card services tailored for NRI customers.  

**Business Banking Services:**  
- **Business Accounts:** Customized accounts designed to suit various business needs.  
- **Cash Management and Merchant Services:** Efficient bulk payment solutions through ICICI Bank E-Collection and I-multipay.  
- **Trade Services:** 24/7 banking support for export-import transactions.  
- **Business Loans:** Financing solutions to support business growth.  
- **Global Market Services:** Access to capital market and securities market services, including custodial services.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Savings accounts enable seamless payments.  
- Fixed deposits offer stable returns for financial goals.  
- Instant payments via scan and pay technology.  
- Credit cards with various benefits to support financial needs.  
- Home loans processed in 5 simple steps.  
- Mutual funds available through SIP with thousands of schemes.  
- Affordable and flexible life insurance term plans.  
- NRI banking includes attractive FCNR deposit rates and tailored services.  
- Business banking includes customized accounts, bulk payment solutions, trade services, and business loans.  
- Capital market and custodial services available for business clients.
