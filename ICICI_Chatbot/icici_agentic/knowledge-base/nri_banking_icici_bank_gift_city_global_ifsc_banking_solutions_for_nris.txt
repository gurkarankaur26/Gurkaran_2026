CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers specialized banking solutions for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) through its Global IFSC Banking Unit (GIFT City). This platform enables NRIs to operate accounts and fixed deposits in multiple foreign currencies with attractive interest rates and flexible tenures.

Key Features:  
- Multi-currency accounts supporting 8 different currencies, allowing NRIs to hold and operate accounts in USD, GBP, EUR, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, and SGD.  
- Fixed Deposits with tenures starting from as short as 7 days, with the option of auto-renewal for convenience.  
- Competitive interest rates on deposits across all supported currencies, designed to maximize returns for NRIs.  
- Seamless international funds transfer with no inward remittance charges, facilitating easy movement of funds worldwide.  
- Ability to operate accounts and deposits entirely from abroad, providing global banking convenience.  

Eligibility:  
- Available exclusively to Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs).  
- Customers must comply with KYC and regulatory requirements as per RBI guidelines for NRI accounts.  

Interest Rates & Tenure:  
- Fixed Deposit tenures range from 7 days to longer periods as per customer preference.  
- Interest rates vary by currency and tenure, with competitive rates offered to enhance returns.  
- Interest is payable as per the terms of the deposit, with options for cumulative or periodic payouts.  

Fees & Charges:  
- No inward remittance charges on funds received from abroad.  
- Other banking fees applicable as per ICICI Bank’s standard tariff for NRI accounts.  

Process to Open Account:  
- Application can be initiated online or through ICICI Bank’s GIFT City branch.  
- Submission of required documents including proof of NRI status, identity, and address.  
- Account activation upon verification and compliance with regulatory norms.
