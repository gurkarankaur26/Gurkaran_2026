CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits (FDs) offer a secure and reliable investment option with assured returns and flexible tenure choices. Investors can select tenures ranging from as short as 7 days to as long as 10 years, aligning investments with their financial goals. FDs provide a fixed interest rate throughout the tenure, ensuring predictable income without exposure to market fluctuations.

Interest payouts can be customized to suit individual needs, with options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual, or annual interest payments. This flexibility makes FDs a suitable source of regular income, especially for retirees or those seeking steady cash flow.

ICICI Bank FDs also offer liquidity through premature withdrawal options, allowing investors to access funds before maturity if needed. Additionally, an overdraft facility against the FD value is available, providing financial support during emergencies without breaking the FD.

The convenience of online FD booking enables investors to create an FD account within 60 seconds from anywhere, eliminating paperwork and branch visits.

Investors should consider inflation and tax implications, as these factors can affect the real returns and purchasing power of FD earnings. Consulting a financial advisor is recommended to tailor FD investments according to individual risk tolerance and financial objectives.

KEY POINTS:  
- Tenure options: 7 days to 10 years  
- Interest payout frequency: Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annual, Annual  
- Fixed interest rate assured for the entire tenure  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with applicable terms  
- Overdraft facility available against FD value  
- Online FD booking available within 60 seconds  
- No exposure to market fluctuations  
- Suitable for steady income and financial planning  
- Inflation and taxes may impact real returns  
- Recommended to consult financial advisors for personalized planning
