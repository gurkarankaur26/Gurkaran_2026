ICICI Bank offers Recurring Deposit (RD) schemes for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) with competitive interest rates based on the tenure of the deposit. The interest rates are effective from 29 December 2025 and vary according to the deposit period.  

**Interest Rates for NRI Recurring Deposits:**  

| Tenure (Period)               | Rate of Interest (% p.a.) w.e.f 29 December 2025 |  
|------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|  
| 6 months                     | 4.50%                                           |  
| 9 months                     | 5.50%                                           |  
| 12 months                   | 6.25%                                           |  
| 15 months                   | 6.25%                                           |  
| 18 months                   | 6.30%                                           |  
| 21 months                   | 6.30%                                           |  
| 24 months                   | 6.30%                                           |  
| 27 months                   | 6.45%                                           |  
| 30 months                   | 6.45%                                           |  
| 33 months                   | 6.45%                                           |  
| 36 months                   | 6.45%                                           |  
| Above 3 years up to 5 years | 6.50%                                           |  
| Above 5 years up to 10 years| 6.50%                                           |  

**Premature Withdrawal Penalty Rates:**  

| Tenure of Deposit           | Less than ₹25 crore | ₹25 crore and above |  
|----------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|  
| Less than 1 year           | 0.50%               | 0.50%               |  
| 1 year & above but less than 5 years | 1.00%       | 1.00%               |  
| 5 years and above          | 1.00%               | 1.50%               |  

- Penalties and interest rates are subject to revision without prior notice.  
- For detailed terms and conditions, customers are advised to visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch.  

KEY POINTS:  
- NRI Recurring Deposit interest rates range from 4.50% p.a. (6 months) to 6.50% p.a. (above 3 years).  
- Interest rates increase with longer tenure, with highest rates for deposits above 3 years.  
- Premature withdrawal penalties vary based on deposit amount and tenure, ranging from 0.50% to 1.50%.  
- Rates and penalties are subject to change without prior notice.  
- For full terms and conditions, visit an ICICI Bank branch.
