ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to invest and manage Mutual Funds through its iMobile App. Investors can start investing with as little as ₹500 via Systematic Investment Plans (SIPs), making it accessible for beginners and young investors.

**Key Features and Processes on iMobile App:**

1. **Downloading CAGR Statement:**  
   - Open iMobile App and log in.  
   - Navigate to ‘Invest’ > ‘Mutual Funds’ > ‘CAGR Statement’.  
   - Enter required details and download the report.  
   The CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) helps track the growth of your investments over time.

2. **Updating Nominee Details:**  
   - Go to ‘Mutual Funds’ under ‘Invest’ tab.  
   - Select ‘Service Request’ > ‘Update Nominee Details’.  
   - Choose the folio, enter nominee’s name, relationship, and other details.  
   - Submit and confirm via OTP.

3. **Changing/Updating Email Address:**  
   - Access ‘Mutual Funds’ under ‘Invest’ tab.  
   - Select ‘Service Request’ > ‘Change/Update Email Address’.  
   - Select folios for which you want to update mobile number/email.  
   - Enter new email, verify through OTP, and confirm.

**Calculating Mutual Fund Returns:**

- **Lump Sum Investment Formula:**  
  Future Value (FV) = Present Value (PV) × (1 + r/100)^n  
  Where:  
  - PV = Initial investment amount  
  - r = Estimated rate of return (%)  
  - n = Investment duration (years)

- **Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) Formula:**  
  FV = P × [((1 + i)^n - 1) × (1 + i) / i]  
  Where:  
  - P = Monthly SIP amount  
  - i = Compounded rate of return (monthly)  
  - n = Number of months invested

These formulas incorporate compounding interest to estimate the future value of investments. ICICI Bank also provides mutual fund calculators to help investors estimate expected returns.

**Mutual Fund Investment Fees:**  
Investors should be aware of various fees associated with mutual funds, including expense ratios, entry and exit loads, and transaction charges. These fees vary by fund and impact overall returns.
