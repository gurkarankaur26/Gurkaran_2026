CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of DSP Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include various fund categories such as Equity Savings, Flexi Cap, Focused, Global Innovation Overseas Equity, Healthcare, India Tiger, Large & Mid Cap, Large Cap, Midcap, and Multi Asset Allocation funds. Each scheme is available in different plan types (Direct and Regular) and options (Growth and IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) with monthly or quarterly IDCW where applicable.

KEY POINTS:  
- DSP Mutual Funds are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) with monthly or quarterly IDCW payouts where applicable.  
- Each scheme has a unique fund code for identification.  
- These funds cover a wide range of investment strategies including equity savings, flexi cap, focused equity, global innovation, healthcare, sectoral (India Tiger), large & mid cap, large cap, midcap, and multi-asset allocation.  
- Investors can choose plans and options based on their investment goals and income preferences.
