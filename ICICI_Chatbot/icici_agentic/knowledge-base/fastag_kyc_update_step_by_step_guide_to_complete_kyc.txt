TITLE: FASTag KYC Update: Step-by-Step Guide to Complete KYC  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Payments / FASTag / KYC  
TAGS: FASTag, KYC, PPI Wallet, ICICI Bank, Customer Verification, Wallet Limits, Full KYC  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank issues FASTag wallets as Prepaid Payment Instruments (PPIs) with two types of KYC statuses: Small PPI and Full KYC PPI. Each has specific eligibility, limits, and benefits.

**Small PPI Wallet (Minimum KYC):**  
- Issued after verifying minimum customer details: mobile number (verified via OTP) and self-declaration of name and unique identity number from any officially valid document (OVD) such as:  
  - Aadhar Card  
  - PAN Card  
  - NREGA Job Card  
  - Driving License  
  - Passport  
- Wallet balance limit: ₹10,000 at any point in time.  
- Monthly loading limit: ₹10,000.  
- Annual loading limit: ₹1,20,000.  
- No cash withdrawal or fund transfer allowed from the wallet.  
- Closure proceeds are transferred back to the source account from which the wallet was loaded.  

**Full KYC Wallet:**  
- Requires submission of full KYC documents either at ICICI Bank branches or via online/video-based KYC verification (where available).  
- Documents required for individuals:  
  - Aadhar Card  
  - PAN Card  
  - NREGA Job Card  
  - Driving License  
  - Passport  
  - Application form  
- Documents required for corporate customers:  
  - GSTIN  
  - Incorporation certificate  
  - Partnership Deed  
  - UDYAM Certificate  
  - List of Partners/Directors  
  - Company PAN  
  - Photo ID and address proof of Authorized Signatory/Beneficial Owner  
  - Application form  
- Benefits of Full KYC:  
  - Increased wallet outstanding balance limit up to ₹1,00,000.  
  - Ability to load higher amounts subject to limits.  
- Closure proceeds are transferred back to the source account.  
- Branch officials verify documents and update KYC as per guidelines.  

**Process to Update FASTag KYC:**  
1. Visit ICICI Bank branch with required documents for Full KYC submission.  
2. Alternatively, complete video-based KYC verification online where available.  
3. After verification and approval, Full KYC benefits will be activated.  
4. Customers can check their FASTag KYC status by logging into the ICICI Bank FASTag portal and reviewing wallet or KYC details.  

**Customer Support:**  
- For assistance, customers may contact ICICI Bank FASTag customer support using their registered mobile number.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Small PPI wallet limits: ₹10,000 balance, ₹10,000 monthly load, ₹1,20,000 annual load.  
- Full KYC wallet limit: ₹1,00,000 outstanding balance.  
- No cash withdrawal or fund transfer allowed from wallet.  
- Closure proceeds transferred back to source account.  
- Full KYC can be completed at branch or via video KYC.  
- Required documents vary for individuals and corporates.  
- KYC status can be checked online via ICICI Bank FASTag portal.
