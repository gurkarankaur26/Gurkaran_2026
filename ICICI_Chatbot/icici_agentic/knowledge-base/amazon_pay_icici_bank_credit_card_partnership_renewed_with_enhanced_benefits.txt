TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card Partnership Renewed with Enhanced Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards, Co-branded Credit Cards, Travel & Shopping Rewards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Cashback, Forex Markup, Travel Benefits, No Annual Fee, Co-branded Card  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank and Amazon Pay have renewed their partnership for the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card, which is India’s most-adopted co-branded credit card with over 5 million customers since its launch in 2018. The card offers simple, transparent rewards and seamless convenience, making it a popular choice for digital payments.

Effective October 11, 2025, the card will feature a reduced forex markup on international transactions, lowering it from 3.5% to 1.99%. This enhancement makes the card more attractive for travel and international spending.

**Key Benefits:**  
- **Cashback on Amazon Shopping:**  
  - Prime members receive unlimited 5% cashback (excluding Gold Coins).  
  - Non-Prime members receive unlimited 3% cashback (excluding Gold Coins).  
- **Cashback on Travel Bookings via Amazon Pay (flights and hotels):**  
  - Prime members get 5% unlimited cashback.  
  - Non-Prime members get 3% unlimited cashback.  
- **Other Cashback:**  
  - 2% unlimited cashback on transactions in Amazon Pay categories (such as login and pay with Amazon).  
- **No Annual or Joining Fees:** The card has zero joining and annual fees.  
- **No-Cost EMI:** Always-on 3-month no-cost EMI on eligible Amazon.in purchases.  
- **Reduced Forex Markup:** 1.99% on international transactions (previously 3.5%).  

The card is positioned as both a rewarding everyday credit card and a travel companion, catering to digitally savvy users who seek flexible redemption options and rich rewards.

KEY POINTS:  
- Over 5 million customers use the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card.  
- No joining or annual fees.  
- Unlimited cashback: 5% for Prime members and 3% for Non-Prime members on Amazon shopping and travel bookings.  
- Reduced forex markup of 1.99% on international transactions.  
- Always-on 3-month no-cost EMI on eligible Amazon.in purchases.  
- Cashback also available on Amazon Pay category transactions (2%).  
- Partnership renewal effective from October 11, 2025.
