TITLE: How to Convert a Salary Account to a Savings Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Account Services  
TAGS: Salary Account, Savings Account, Account Conversion, ICICI Bank, Banking Process, Documentation, Customer Care  
CONTENT:  
Converting a Salary Account to a Savings Account at ICICI Bank involves a straightforward process when guided properly. A Salary Account is a specialized account provided by employers to facilitate salary deposits, typically featuring zero balance requirements and waived debit card charges. In contrast, a Savings Account is designed for a broader audience, offering interest on deposits, flexible transactions, and options for long-term savings.

**Key Steps to Convert Salary Account to Savings Account:**  
1. **Consult Your Employer:** Check with your company’s HR department to confirm if your organisation permits the conversion, as some companies have specific policies or bank partnerships.  
2. **Contact ICICI Bank:** Reach out to ICICI Bank via branch visit, Customer Care call, or mobile app to express your intention to convert your Salary Account to a Savings Account.  
3. **Submit Conversion Request:** Officially submit your request for conversion. Bank representatives will provide the necessary forms and guide you through the process.  
4. **Provide Required Documents:** Submit valid identity and address proofs such as Aadhaar Card, PAN Card or Passport, and utility bills or rental agreements for address verification.  
5. **Complete Formalities:** Fill out any additional forms or documents as requested by the bank.  
6. **Close Salary Account:** You may need to close your existing Salary Account to complete the conversion and activate your new Savings Account.

**Benefits of Savings Account over Salary Account:**  
- Earn interest on deposits.  
- Greater flexibility in transactions.  
- Suitable for long-term savings and financial planning.  

**Important Notes:**  
- Salary Accounts typically have zero balance requirements and waived debit card fees, which may differ in Savings Accounts.  
- Always verify with your employer and bank for any specific terms or conditions related to your account conversion.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Salary Account is employer-linked with zero balance and waived debit card charges.  
- Savings Account offers interest, transaction flexibility, and long-term saving options.  
- Conversion requires employer approval and bank communication.  
- Documents needed: Aadhaar, PAN/Passport, address proof.  
- Process includes submitting request, document verification, form filling, and closing Salary Account.  
- Contact ICICI Bank via branch, Customer Care, or mobile app for assistance.
