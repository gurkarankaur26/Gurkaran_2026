TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card Benefits and Features  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Rewards, Benefits, Fees, Lounge Access, Golf, Cashback, UPI, Customer Care  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of credit card benefits designed to enhance customer experience and provide value through rewards, cashback, and exclusive privileges. Key features include joining benefits, milestone rewards, fee reversals, complimentary lounge access, golf rounds, and special offers on Adani network spends. The credit cards are widely accepted both online and offline and can be easily linked with multiple UPI apps for seamless transactions.

**Joining Benefits:**  
- Welcome vouchers worth ₹9,000 and above (including Tata Cliq, Uber, EaseMyTrip, Tata Croma)  
- Fee reversal on annual spends of ₹10 Lakhs (applicable from 2nd year onwards)  

**Reward Points and Redemption:**  
- Earn 7 Adani Reward Points per ₹100 spent on Adani network (airports, Adani One app, electricity bills, etc.)  
- Earn 3 Adani Reward Points per ₹100 spent on Adani network (other categories)  
- Reward points can be redeemed against catalogues and cashback, with each point valued up to ₹1  

**Milestone Benefits:**  
- Spend ₹8 Lakhs and receive 2 EaseMyTrip vouchers, each worth ₹3,000  

**Fee Reversal:**  
- Fee reversal on spends of ₹6 Lakhs in the previous year  
- Pre-approved offers available for existing credit card customers with 0 joining fee and 0 annual fee  

**Complimentary Access and Offers:**  
- 4 complimentary Domestic Airport lounge visits per quarter  
- 2 complimentary International Airport lounge visits per year  
- 4 complimentary rounds of golf every month  
- Unlimited complimentary golf rounds/lessons every month (specific cards)  
- Up to ₹500 off on the second ticket on BookMyShow (twice a month)  

**UPI and Payment Features:**  
- Easy linking of credit card with multiple UPI apps  
- Earn cashback and rewards on UPI transactions  
- Load any amount from ₹500 to ₹10,000 on ICICI Bank Pockets app  
- Manage services via SMS or ICICI Bank Pockets app  
- Issued based on buyer’s KYC; beneficiary KYC not required  

**Customer Support:**  
- 24x7 dedicated customer care for credit card services  
- Safe and secure transactions  

KEY POINTS:  
- Joining benefits include welcome vouchers worth ₹9,000+ and fee reversal on high spends  
- Reward points earn rate: 7 points/₹100 on Adani network, 3 points/₹100 on other Adani spends  
- Milestone benefit: ₹8 Lakhs spend yields ₹6,000 worth EaseMyTrip vouchers  
- Complimentary lounge access: 4 domestic visits/quarter, 2 international visits/year  
- Complimentary golf rounds: 4 per month, with some cards offering unlimited rounds/lessons  
- Fee reversal available on spends of ₹6 Lakhs in previous year  
- Pre-approved offers with zero joining and annual fees for existing customers  
- Credit cards can be linked with multiple UPI apps for cashback and rewards  
- ICICI Bank Pockets app allows loading ₹500 to ₹10,000 without beneficiary KYC  
- 24x7 customer care support
