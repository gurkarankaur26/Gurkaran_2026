TITLE: Reward Points Policy Update for ICICI Bank Credit Cards  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards, Rewards  
TAGS: Reward Points, Utility Transactions, Government Transactions, Credit Card Benefits  

CONTENT:  
Starting 05 February 2024, ICICI Bank will update its reward points policy for credit card transactions. Rent pay and e-wallet loading transactions will no longer accrue any reward points. However, this change does not apply to the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card, which will continue to offer reward points on these transactions. Additionally, government transactions will now be included under utility payment transactions, allowing customers to earn reward points on these payments as per the existing utility transaction rewards structure. The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card will continue to follow its current reward points policy without these changes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Rent pay and e-wallet loading transactions will not earn reward points from 05 February 2024.  
- Government transactions will be classified under utility payment transactions starting 05 February 2024.  
- Reward points will continue to be earned on utility transactions as per card benefits.  
- Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card is exempt from these changes and will continue to earn reward points on rent pay, e-wallet loading, and other transactions as before.
