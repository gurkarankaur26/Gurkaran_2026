CONTENT:  
This document provides a structured overview of various mutual fund schemes available through ICICI Bank, focusing on key fund options from Aditya Birla Sun Life and Axis Mutual Funds. The funds include different categories such as overnight funds, savings funds, short-term funds, banking & PSU debt funds, and corporate bond funds. Each fund is available in multiple plans and options including Direct and Regular plans, with Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly).

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:

1. **Aditya Birla Sun Life Funds**  
   - **Overnight Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options (daily, weekly, monthly).  
   - **Savings Fund**: Available in Direct, Regular, and Retirement plans with Growth and IDCW options (daily, weekly).  
   - **Short Term Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options (quarterly).  
   - **Nifty SDL Plus PSU Bond Sep 2026 60:40 Index Fund**: Regular plan with IDCW option.

2. **Axis Mutual Funds**  
   - **Banking & PSU Debt Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options (daily, weekly, monthly).  
   - **Corporate Bond Fund**: Available in Direct plan with Growth and IDCW options.

**Common Features:**  
- Plans: Direct (investor directly invests) and Regular (through distributors).  
- Options: Growth (reinvestment of income) and IDCW (periodic income distribution).  
- IDCW Frequency: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly depending on the fund.  
- Fund Types: Overnight, Savings, Short Term, Debt, Corporate Bond, Index Funds.  

**Benefits:**  
- Diversified investment options catering to different risk profiles and investment horizons.  
- Flexibility in choosing income distribution frequency.  
- Access to reputed fund houses through ICICI Bank platform.  

**Note:** For detailed NAVs, minimum investment amounts, eligibility criteria, fees, and latest performance data, please refer to the respective fund fact sheets or ICICI Bank’s mutual fund investment portal.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple mutual fund schemes available from Aditya Birla Sun Life and Axis Mutual Funds.  
- Options include Growth and IDCW with various payout frequencies.  
- Plans available in Direct and Regular modes.  
- Fund categories include Overnight, Savings, Short Term, Banking & PSU Debt, Corporate Bond, and Index Funds.  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified debt and hybrid mutual fund options.
