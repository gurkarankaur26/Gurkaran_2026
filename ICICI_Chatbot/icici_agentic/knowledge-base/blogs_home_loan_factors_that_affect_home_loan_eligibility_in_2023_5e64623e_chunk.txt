Securing a home loan is a significant step towards owning your dream home. ICICI Bank offers a Home Loan Eligibility Calculator to help you preliminarily assess your eligibility and plan your finances effectively. This tool considers factors such as your credit score, income stability, debt management, and property criteria to provide an estimate of your loan eligibility.

To ensure a smooth home loan process, maintain a healthy credit score, demonstrate stable income, manage your debts responsibly, and select a property that meets the lender’s requirements. ICICI Bank provides a wide range of home loan products with competitive interest rates tailored to assist you in achieving your homeownership goals.

For a more accurate assessment and personalized guidance, it is recommended to consult with an ICICI Bank representative who can offer expert advice based on your specific financial situation and needs.

KEY POINTS:  
- Use the ICICI Bank Home Loan Eligibility Calculator for a preliminary assessment.  
- Eligibility depends on credit score, income stability, debt management, and property criteria.  
- Maintain a healthy credit score and stable income for better loan approval chances.  
- ICICI Bank offers competitive interest rates and diverse home loan options.  
- Personalized consultation with ICICI Bank representatives is advised for accurate guidance.
