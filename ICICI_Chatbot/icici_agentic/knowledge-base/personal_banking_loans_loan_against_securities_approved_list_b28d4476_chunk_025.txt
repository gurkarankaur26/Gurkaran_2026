ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund Schemes – Key Details and Codes

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Mutual Funds, Investment Products

TAGS:  
ICICI Prudential, Mutual Funds, Fund Codes, Growth Option, IDCW Option, Investment

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund schemes along with their respective scheme codes. The schemes are available in different options such as Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal). The list includes various fund categories such as Infrastructure Fund, Innovation Fund, Large & Mid Cap Fund, Large Cap Fund, Manufacturing Fund, MidCap Fund, MNC Fund, Multi-Asset Fund, Multicap Fund, and NASDAQ 100 Index Fund.

Each scheme is identified by a unique code which is essential for investment and transaction purposes. The schemes are offered in both Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg) plans, with options for Growth and IDCW payouts.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Prudential Infrastructure Fund, Innovation Fund, Large & Mid Cap Fund, Large Cap Fund, Manufacturing Fund, MidCap Fund, MNC Fund, Multi-Asset Fund, Multicap Fund, and NASDAQ 100 Index Fund are available.  
- Each fund has multiple options: Direct Plan Growth, Direct Plan IDCW, Regular Plan Growth, and Regular Plan IDCW.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each option to facilitate transactions.  
- Growth option: Earnings are reinvested in the fund, increasing the NAV.  
- IDCW option: Periodic income distribution to investors.  
- Direct plans have lower expense ratios compared to regular plans.  
- Suitable for investors looking for diversified equity and multi-asset investment options.
