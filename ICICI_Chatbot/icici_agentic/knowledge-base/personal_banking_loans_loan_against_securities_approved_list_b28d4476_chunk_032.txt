TITLE:  
Mutual Fund Schemes List

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Investment, Fund Schemes, Kotak Funds, LIC MF  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of mutual fund schemes available for investment, including various Kotak and LIC Mutual Fund schemes. Each scheme is identified by its name, plan type (Direct or Regular), option (Growth or IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), and a unique scheme code.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes include:

- Kotak MNC Fund (Regular - IDCW)  
- Kotak Multi Asset Allocation Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Multi Asset Omni FOF (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Multicap Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Nifty 50 ETF and Index Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Nifty Bank ETF  
- Kotak Nifty PSU Bank ETF  
- Kotak Pioneer Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Small Cap Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak Special Opportunities Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- Kotak US Specific Equity Passive FOF (Direct & Regular - Growth)  
- LIC MF Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth)  
- LIC MF Large & Mid Cap Fund (Direct & Regular - Growth & IDCW)  
- LIC MF Large Cap Fund (Direct - Growth)  

Each scheme offers different investment options and plans to suit various investor preferences, including growth options for capital appreciation and IDCW options for periodic income.

KEY POINTS:  
- Schemes are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each fund variant for easy identification.  
- The list includes equity, multi-asset, sectoral, and balanced advantage funds.  
- Kotak and LIC Mutual Funds are prominently featured.  
- ETFs (Exchange Traded Funds) like Kotak Nifty 50 ETF and Kotak Nifty Bank ETF are included.
