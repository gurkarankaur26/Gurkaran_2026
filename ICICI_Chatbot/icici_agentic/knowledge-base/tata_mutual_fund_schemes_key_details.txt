TITLE: Tata Mutual Fund Schemes - Key Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Tata Mutual Fund, Investment, Growth, IDCW, Banking  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a structured list of Tata Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes are categorized by plan type (Direct or Regular), option (Growth or IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), and include unique scheme codes for easy identification.

Key Tata Mutual Fund Schemes include:  
- Tata Aggressive Hybrid Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW options)  
- Tata Arbitrage Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, Monthly IDCW options)  
- Tata Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW options)  
- Tata Banking and Financial Services Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW options)  
- Tata Business Cycle Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW options)  
- Tata Digital India Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW options)  
- Tata Dividend Yield Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth, IDCW options)  
- Tata Ethical Fund (Direct plan; Growth, IDCW options)  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code, for example:  
- Tata Aggressive Hybrid Fund - Direct - Growth: 119053  
- Tata Arbitrage Fund - Regular - Growth: 145723  
- Tata Balanced Advantage Fund - Direct - Growth: 146010  
- Tata Banking and Financial Services Fund - Regular - IDCW: 135791  

Investors can choose between Direct plans (lower expense ratio, no distributor commission) and Regular plans (through distributors). Options include Growth (capital appreciation) and IDCW (periodic income distribution). Some schemes offer Monthly IDCW for regular income.

KEY POINTS:  
- Tata Mutual Fund schemes are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth, IDCW (dividend payout), and Monthly IDCW.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for transactions.  
- Direct plans have lower expense ratios compared to Regular plans.  
- IDCW options provide periodic income, suitable for income-focused investors.  
- Growth options are suitable for investors seeking capital appreciation.  
- Monthly IDCW options provide monthly income distributions.
