### Benefits of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits with Monthly Interest Payouts

- **Regular Income:** Provides a steady monthly income, ideal for retirees and individuals seeking dependable cash flow.
- **Safety:** FDs are a secure investment option with guaranteed returns.
- **Flexible Tenure:** Investment durations range from 3 months to 10 years, catering to different financial goals.
- **Higher Rates for Senior Citizens:** Senior citizens enjoy higher interest rates compared to general investors.
- **Wealth Management:** Suitable for wealth preservation and planned income generation.

### Important Notes

- Interest rates and monthly interest amounts are subject to change. It is advisable to verify the current rates with ICICI Bank before making any investment decisions.
- The monthly interest payout option helps in managing monthly expenses without breaking the principal amount.

KEY POINTS:  
- Fixed Deposit tenures available: 3 months, 1 year, 5 years, 10 years  
- Interest rates vary from 4.75% to 7.5% depending on tenure and investor category  
- Senior citizens receive higher interest rates (up to 7.5%)  
- Monthly interest payouts provide regular income, beneficial for retirees  
- Investment amounts range from ₹1 lakh to ₹1.9 crore in the provided data  
- Interest rates and payouts are indicative and may change over time
