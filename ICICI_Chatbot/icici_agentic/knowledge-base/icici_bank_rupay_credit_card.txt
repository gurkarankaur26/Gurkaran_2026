CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank RuPay Credit Card offers the convenience of making payments via UPI with the flexibility of a credit card. Cardholders can link their RuPay Credit Card to major UPI apps and pay seamlessly through merchant QR codes or in-app purchases. This card is built on India’s domestic RuPay network, ensuring wide acceptance across the country. Users earn reward points on eligible transactions, combining everyday payment convenience with added benefits.

Key features include:  
- Easy linking with UPI apps  
- Seamless payment through QR codes and in-app purchases  
- Pan-India acceptance on the RuPay network  
- Reward points on eligible transactions  

Apply online to enjoy the benefits of UPI payments using your ICICI Bank RuPay Credit Card.

KEY POINTS:  
- Card Type: RuPay Credit Card  
- Network: RuPay (India’s domestic card network)  
- Payment Mode: UPI payments via linked credit card  
- Acceptance: Pan-India  
- Benefits: Earn reward points on eligible transactions  
- Application: Available online
