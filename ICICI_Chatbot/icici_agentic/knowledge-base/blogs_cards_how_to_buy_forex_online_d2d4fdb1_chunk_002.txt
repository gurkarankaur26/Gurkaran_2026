ICICI Bank offers comprehensive online Forex services designed to provide travellers with a seamless currency exchange experience. Customers can purchase Forex online with ease, benefit from doorstep delivery, and enjoy convenient reload options for their Forex Cards. The platform supports multiple currencies and incorporates advanced security measures to ensure safe transactions.

Key considerations when buying Forex online through ICICI Bank include:

1. **Purposes and Limits:**  
   - Forex transactions are subject to regulatory limits. The maximum permissible limit is USD 2,50,000 (or equivalent) per financial year for various purposes such as travel, education, and medical treatment.  
   - Ensure your Forex transaction complies with these limits and is for an eligible purpose.

2. **Documentation Requirements:**  
   - Customers must provide necessary documentation before initiating Forex transactions.  
   - Required documents may include valid ID proof, proof of travel, employment, or education, depending on the purpose of the Forex purchase.

3. **Regulatory Guidelines:**  
   - Familiarize yourself with the regulatory guidelines governing Forex transactions, especially if your remittance exceeds USD 2,50,000.  
   - For detailed information or assistance, customers can contact any ICICI Bank Forex Servicing Branch.

4. **Goods and Services Tax (GST):**  
   - Be aware of the applicable GST slab on Forex transactions.  
   - Ensure compliance with GST regulations related to your Forex purchases.

5. **Branch Locator:**  
   - Use the ICICI Bank website or mobile app’s Branch Locator tool to find the nearest Forex Servicing Branch for assistance or document submission.

ICICI Bank’s user-friendly online Forex platform, combined with diverse currency options and secure transaction processes, enables travellers to manage their Forex needs confidently and conveniently, ensuring a stress-free travel experience.

- Forex purchase limit: USD 2,50,000 or equivalent per financial year for eligible purposes.  
- Required documentation includes ID proof, travel proof, employment or education proof as applicable.
