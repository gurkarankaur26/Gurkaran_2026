CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes available for investment, highlighting their key variants and dividend options. The schemes include Low Duration Fund, Money Market Fund, Nifty AAA CPSE Bond Plus SDL Index Fund, and Nivesh Lakshya Long Duration Fund. Each scheme is offered in different plans such as Regular and Direct, with multiple dividend payout options including Growth, Dividend (IDCW) on Monthly, Quarterly, Weekly, Daily, Half-Yearly, and Annual basis, as well as Bonus options.

Key Nippon India Mutual Fund Schemes and Variants:

1. Nippon India Low Duration Fund  
   - Plans: Regular, Ret (Retail)  
   - Dividend Options: Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Daily IDCW, Bonus, Growth  

2. Nippon India Money Market Fund  
   - Plans: Direct, Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Bonus, Growth  

3. Nippon India Nifty AAA CPSE Bond Plus SDL - Apr 2027 Maturity 60:40 Index Fund  
   - Plans: Direct, Regular  
   - Dividend Options: Growth, IDCW  

4. Nippon India Nivesh Lakshya Long Duration Fund  
   - Plans: Direct  
   - Dividend Options: Annual IDCW, Half-Yearly IDCW, IDCW, Growth  

Each variant is identified by a unique scheme code (e.g., 111749 for Low Duration Fund - Monthly IDCW Regular Plan). Investors can choose the plan and dividend option that best suits their investment goals and income preferences.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Nippon India Mutual Fund schemes are available with various dividend payout options.  
- Plans include Regular and Direct, catering to different investor preferences.  
- Dividend options include Growth, IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) on Monthly, Quarterly, Weekly, Daily, Half-Yearly, and Annual basis, and Bonus units.  
- Scheme codes are provided for precise identification of each variant.  
- Suitable for investors seeking low duration, money market, bond index, or long duration fund options.
