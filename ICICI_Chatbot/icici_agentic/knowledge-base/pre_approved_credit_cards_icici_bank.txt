CONTENT:  
A Pre-Approved ICICI Bank Credit Card provides eligible customers with instant credit card approval without the need for additional documentation or credit checks. This offer is based on the customer’s creditworthiness and banking history, ensuring a faster and easier approval process. Importantly, checking for a pre-approved offer does not impact your credit score unless you accept and activate the card.

**Benefits of Pre-Approved ICICI Bank Credit Cards:**  
- Attractive discounts on shopping, dining, movies, and more.  
- Complimentary access to airport lounges.  
- Fuel surcharge waivers and discounts on fuel purchases.  
- Earn ICICI Bank Reward Points on transactions.  
- 24x7 concierge services for assistance.  
- Advanced chip technology for enhanced security against fraud and unauthorized transactions.

**How to Redeem and Activate Your Pre-Approved Credit Card:**  
1. **Via iMobile App:**  
   - Log in to the ICICI Bank iMobile app.  
   - Tap on the ‘Offers’ section to view your pre-approved credit card offers.  
   - Select the desired card from the available options.  
   - Verify pre-filled details and fill in any additional required information.  
   - Accept the Terms and Conditions.  
   - Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.  
   - View your credit card details and start transacting online.

2. **Via Net Banking:**  
   - Log in to ICICI Bank Net Banking.  
   - Click on ‘Apply Online’ under the ‘Cards and Loans’ tab to view your offers.  
   - Select the desired credit card.  
   - Verify pre-filled details and provide any additional information.  
   - Accept the Terms and Conditions.  
   - Enter the OTP sent to your registered mobile number.  
   - View your credit card details and start using the card.

**Important Notes:**  
- The pre-approved credit card offer is personalized and available only to eligible customers.  
- No additional documentation or credit checks are required for approval.  
- Your credit score remains unaffected unless you accept and activate the card.
