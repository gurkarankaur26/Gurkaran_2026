TITLE: ICICI Bank Gold Loan – Features, Eligibility, Interest Rates, and Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Gold Loan, Loan-to-Value, Interest Rates, Loan Tenure, Prepayment, Gold Valuation, Loan Repayment  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans against gold ornaments, jewellery, and gold coins (up to 50 grams per borrower) as collateral. The loan amount can be up to 75% of the gold’s market value, in line with RBI’s Loan-to-Value (LTV) guidelines. The loan amount is credited directly to the borrower’s ICICI Bank savings account or transferred via NEFT to another bank account if the borrower does not hold an ICICI Bank account.

**Loan Disbursal and Valuation:**  
- Onboarded trained appraisers with relevant experience carry out the valuation using appropriate testing equipment.  
- Once gold is valued and documents are verified, the loan may be disbursed on the same day.  
- The gold is sealed in the borrower’s presence and stored securely in bank vaults or strong rooms.  

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- Interest rates start from approximately 8.75% per annum and vary based on loan amount, tenure, and product variant.  
- A nominal processing fee applies; the exact amount is communicated during application.  
- GST is applicable on processing and service charges as per government regulations.  
- Penal interest is charged on delayed repayments in addition to regular interest.  

**Loan Tenure and Repayment:**  
- Loan tenure ranges from 6 months to 36 months, depending on the product and borrower preference.  
- ICICI Bank allows full prepayment or foreclosure before maturity; applicable charges, if any, are specified in the loan agreement.  
- Partial payments can be made anytime; however, gold will be released only after full repayment.  
- Repayment modes include cheque, NEFT, online transfer, or via the iMobile app.  
- Delayed payments attract penal charges; continuous non-payment may lead to auction of pledged gold.  

**Gold Custody and Safety:**  
- Gold is fully insured against loss or damage while in custody.  
- The pledged gold packet cannot be opened until full repayment and loan closure.  
- Gold is returned in the same condition as when pledged.  
- ICICI Bank takes full responsibility for safe custody and handles any incidents as per internal policy.  

**Loan Closure and Auction:**  
- After clearing all outstanding dues (principal and interest), the gold loan account is closed, and the gold packet is released with proper acknowledgement.  
- Gold is realized on the same day once the account is closed.  
- Borrowers can reclaim gold by clearing dues before auction execution.  
- Surplus amount after deducting dues and auction costs is refunded to the borrower.  
- Nominees or legal heirs can claim gold after repayment by submitting required documents such as death certificate, ID proof, and KYC.  

**Account Management:**  
- Loan status and details can be checked via ICICI Bank Net Banking, iMobile app, or by visiting the home branch.  
- Updated documents can be submitted at ICICI Bank branches.  
- Gold loans are physical loans and cannot be transferred.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount up to 75% of gold’s market value as per RBI LTV guidelines.  
- Interest rates start from 8.75% p.a., varying by loan amount and tenure.  
- Loan tenure: 6 to 36 months.  
- Processing fee applicable; GST charged on fees.  
- Same-day loan disbursal after valuation and document verification.  
- Gold sealed and stored securely; insured against loss/damage.  
- Prepayment and foreclosure allowed; partial payments allowed but gold released only after full repayment.  
- Penal interest on delayed payments; auction on continuous default.  
- Surplus refund after auction; nominee/legal heir can claim gold post repayment.  
- Repayment via cheque, NEFT, online transfer, or iMobile app.  
- Loan cannot be transferred; physical gold loan only.
