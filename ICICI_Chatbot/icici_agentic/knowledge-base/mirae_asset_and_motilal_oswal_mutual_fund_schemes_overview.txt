TITLE: Mirae Asset and Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund Schemes Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Mirae Asset, Motilal Oswal, Investment, IDCW, Growth, Liquid Funds, Duration Funds  

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various mutual fund schemes offered by Mirae Asset and Motilal Oswal, available through ICICI Bank. The schemes include different fund types such as Low Duration Fund, Money Market Fund, Overnight Fund, Short Duration Fund, Ultra Short Duration Fund, and Liquid Fund. Each scheme is available in different plans and options including Direct and Regular plans, with options for Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) frequencies such as Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Fortnightly.

**Mirae Asset Mutual Fund Schemes:**  
- **Low Duration Fund:** Available in Direct and Regular plans with options for Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, and Growth.  
- **Money Market Fund:** Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **Overnight Fund:** Available in Direct and Regular plans with Daily IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Weekly IDCW, and Growth options.  
- **Short Duration Fund:** Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **Ultra Short Duration Fund:** Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  

**Motilal Oswal Mutual Fund Schemes:**  
- **Liquid Fund:** Available in Direct plan with Daily IDCW and Fortnightly IDCW options.  

These funds cater to different investment horizons and income preferences, providing flexibility in terms of dividend payout frequency and growth options.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple mutual fund schemes from Mirae Asset and Motilal Oswal are available via ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes include Low Duration, Money Market, Overnight, Short Duration, Ultra Short Duration, and Liquid Funds.  
- Plans offered: Direct and Regular.  
- Options available: Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).  
- IDCW payout frequencies include Daily, Weekly, Fortnightly, Monthly, and Quarterly depending on the scheme.  
- Suitable for investors seeking varying durations and income preferences.
