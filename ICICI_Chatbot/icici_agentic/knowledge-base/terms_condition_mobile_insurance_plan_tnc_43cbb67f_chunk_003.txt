CONTENT:  
ICICI Lombard offers device insurance coverage with options for cashless repair or reimbursement depending on the serviceability of the insured location.

**Claim Process:**  
- If the insured device is not operational or the IMEI number is not clearly visible on the screen, ICICI Lombard will arrange for pickup of the device and process the claim on a cashless basis. The device will be repaired and returned to the customer.  
- If the insured location is not serviceable for cashless repair, the insured can get the device repaired at an Authorized Service Center and claim reimbursement.  
- In cases where the device repair cost exceeds the liability amount covered by ICICI Lombard, the customer must pay the balance amount in case of cashless repair. For reimbursement claims, only the liability amount will be reimbursed.

**Grievance Redressal:**  
ICICI Lombard is committed to fulfilling its promises and offers details of its insurance partners to ensure quality service and easy access to information.
