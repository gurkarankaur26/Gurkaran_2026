**Important Customer Advisory:**  
- ICICI Bank will never ask for confidential or sensitive information such as Card Number, CVV, OTP, etc., via phone calls, SMS, or emails.  
- Customers are advised not to open or click on any unauthorized emails claiming to be from ICICI Bank. Such emails should be reported immediately.  
- To report suspicious emails or communications, customers can use the official reporting channels provided by ICICI Bank.

**Fraud Prevention:**  
- ICICI Bank provides a list of official phone numbers from which it contacts customers. Customers should save these numbers to avoid missing legitimate calls and to protect themselves from fraudsters.  
- Always verify the authenticity of calls and messages claiming to be from ICICI Bank.

**Grievance Redressal:**  
- If the resolution provided by the standard service channels is unsatisfactory, customers can escalate their queries or complaints through ICICI Bank’s grievance redressal process.  
- Details of the grievance redressal policy are available for customers seeking further assistance.
