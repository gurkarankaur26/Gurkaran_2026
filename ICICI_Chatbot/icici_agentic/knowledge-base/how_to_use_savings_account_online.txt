TITLE: How to Use Savings Account Online  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Savings Account, Online Banking  
TAGS: Savings Account, Online Account Opening, Internet Banking, Mobile Banking, Fund Transfer, Account Types  

CONTENT:  
A savings account is a safe investment option to park idle funds and earn interest. ICICI Bank offers the facility to open a savings account online, eliminating the need to visit a branch.  

**How to Open a Savings Account Online:**  
1. Visit the official ICICI Bank website.  
2. Select the new account opening option and fill out the application form.  
3. Submit required documents such as ID proof and address proof.  
4. Choose from various types of savings accounts tailored for men, women, kids, and senior citizens, each offering specific benefits.  
5. After submission, the bank will create your account and provide a unique account number. Account details will be sent via email.  

**Registering for Internet Banking:**  
- After account opening, register for internet banking on the ICICI Bank website.  
- Fill in your account number and personal details.  
- Select a username and password.  
- Upon confirmation, log in using your credentials to access online banking services.  

**Using Your Online Savings Account:**  
- Perform various online transactions such as IMPS, RTGS, NEFT for fund transfers.  
- Pay online bills, open fixed deposits, and apply for loans through the online portal.  
- ICICI Bank also offers a mobile banking app for convenient banking on-the-go with similar features as the online portal.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Online savings account opening available via ICICI Bank website.  
- Required documents: ID proof, address proof.  
- Multiple account types available for different customer segments (men, women, kids, senior citizens).  
- Internet banking registration mandatory to access online services.  
- Online transactions supported: IMPS, RTGS, NEFT, bill payments, fixed deposits, loan applications.  
- Mobile banking app available for convenient access.
