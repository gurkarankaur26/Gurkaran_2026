TITLE: How to Open a Current Account: Step-by-Step Guide  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Business Banking, Current Account  
TAGS: Current Account, Business Banking, Account Opening, ICICI Bank, Overdraft, Eligibility, Fees  

CONTENT:  
A Current Account is designed for businesses that require frequent and high-volume transactions. Unlike savings accounts, Current Accounts do not earn interest on the balance but provide higher transaction flexibility, multiple daily transactions, and access to overdraft facilities. This account type is ideal for entrepreneurs, traders, firms, and companies managing day-to-day business payments efficiently.

**Key Features of ICICI Bank Current Account:**  
- Quarterly Average Balance (QAB) requirement: ₹50,000  
- Access to ICICI Bank InstaBIZ app and Corporate Net Banking  
- Comprehensive suite of business banking solutions  
- Higher limits on number and value of transactions  
- Overdraft facility availability  

**Eligibility:**  
- Business entities including proprietorships, partnerships, companies, and firms  
- Required documents include identity proof (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card), address proof, and business proofs such as company registration documents  

**Account Opening Process:**  

*Online Application:*  
1. Visit ICICI Bank website: www.icicibank.com  
2. Navigate to Business > Accounts > Current Account  
3. Click ‘Apply’ and fill out the online application form with your details  
4. Agree to the disclaimer and submit the form  
5. An ICICI Bank representative will contact you to assist in completing the application  
6. Keep necessary documents ready for verification (identity proof, address proof, business proofs)  
7. The online form initiates the process; final verification and account activation happen offline  

*Offline Application (Branch Visit):*  
1. Visit any ICICI Bank Branch  
2. Consult with the bank representative to select the appropriate Current Account type  
3. Fill out the Current Account application form with required details  
4. Submit copies of necessary documents (PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, proof of address, company registration, etc.)  
5. The bank reviews the application and verifies documents  
6. Upon successful verification, the Current Account is opened  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Current Account:**  
- Facilitates smooth business transactions with no restrictions on the number of transactions  
- Access to overdraft facilities to manage cash flow  
- Integration with InstaBIZ app and Corporate Net Banking for convenient account management  
- Dedicated customer support for business banking needs  

KEY POINTS:  
- Current Account QAB: ₹50,000  
- No interest on balance but high transaction flexibility  
- Suitable for businesses requiring frequent transactions  
- Online and offline account opening options available  
- Required documents: PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, address proof, business registration documents  
- Access to overdraft and digital banking platforms (InstaBIZ, Corporate Net Banking)  
- Dedicated customer assistance during account opening
