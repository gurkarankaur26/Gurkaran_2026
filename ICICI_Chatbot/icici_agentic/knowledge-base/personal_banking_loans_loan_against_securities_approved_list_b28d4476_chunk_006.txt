TITLE:  
Axis and Bandhan Mutual Fund Schemes Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Axis Mutual Fund, Bandhan Mutual Fund, Investment Schemes, Growth, IDCW, Direct Plan, Regular Plan  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of mutual fund schemes offered by Axis Mutual Fund and Bandhan Mutual Fund, including scheme names, plan types, and unique identification numbers (folio or scheme codes). The schemes are categorized by fund type, plan type (Direct or Regular), and dividend options (Growth or IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

**Axis Mutual Fund Schemes:**  
- Momentum Fund - Reg - IDCW  
- Multi Asset Allocation Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and Monthly IDCW options  
- Multicap Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Nifty 100 Index Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- NIFTY 50 ETF  
- Small Cap Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Value Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  

**Bandhan Mutual Fund Schemes:**  
- Aggressive Hybrid Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  
- Arbitrage Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Annual and Monthly IDCW and Growth options  
- Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct and Regular Plans) with Growth and IDCW options  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction processing.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual fund schemes are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), with IDCW further divided into Monthly and Annual payouts.  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each fund variant to facilitate transactions.  
- The list includes equity funds (Multicap, Small Cap, Value), hybrid funds (Aggressive Hybrid, Balanced Advantage), arbitrage funds, and index funds (Nifty 100, Nifty 50 ETF).  
- Investors can choose schemes based on their investment goals, risk appetite, and preferred dividend payout frequency.
