TITLE: ICICI Bank FASTag - Application, Recharge, and Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Services  
TAGS: FASTag, Toll Payments, Recharge, Digital Payments, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
FASTag is an electronic toll collection system that allows you to pay toll charges directly from your prepaid or savings account linked to it, enabling you to drive through toll plazas without stopping for cash transactions.  

**Application Process:**  
- You can apply for an ICICI Bank FASTag both online and offline.  
- Submission of a valid photo ID proof is mandatory during the application.  

**Recharge:**  
- Once the FASTag balance is exhausted, it can be recharged easily online, similar to mobile phone recharges.  

**Benefits:**  
- Avoids time-consuming cash transactions at toll plazas.  
- Enables seamless and faster movement through toll gates.  
- Convenient online recharge facility.
