TITLE: ICICI Bank Digital Car Loan  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Digital Loan, eKYC, Loan Approval, Flexible Repayment  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a fully digital Car Loan process designed to provide customers with a fast, secure, and convenient way to finance their vehicle purchase. The application process is streamlined with minimal documentation and includes a secure eKYC verification to ensure data accuracy and safety.  

**Application Process:**  
- Customers need to provide additional details such as address and office information.  
- The application is submitted online for approval and processing.  
- A bank representative may contact the customer for further information to facilitate loan processing.  
- For customers with pending KYC, the Video KYC (VKYC) process must be completed before submission.  

**Key Benefits:**  
- **Fast and Instant Approval:** The digital process enables quick loan approvals and timely disbursement without long waiting periods.  
- **Minimal Documentation:** Most documents are collected digitally, reducing paperwork and saving time.  
- **Loan Flexibility:** Customers can customize loan amount, tenure, and EMI structure to suit their budget.  
- **Secure eKYC Process:** The use of OTP and document verification ensures a safe and reliable approval process.  

ICICI Bank’s digital-first approach makes availing a Car Loan easy, hassle-free, and efficient, helping customers drive their dream car with flexible and timely financial support.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Fully digital Car Loan application and approval process  
- Additional customer details required: address, office details  
- Video KYC mandatory for pending KYC customers  
- Fast approval and disbursement  
- Minimal documentation, mostly digital  
- Flexible loan amount, tenure, and EMI options  
- Secure eKYC with OTP and document verification
