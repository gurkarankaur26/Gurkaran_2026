TITLE: ICICI Bank Home Loan Customer Care and Loan Servicing Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Home Loans, Customer Support  
TAGS: Home Loan, Customer Care, Loan Servicing, Contact, Mobile Banking, Email Update  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides comprehensive customer care and support services for home loan customers. Customers receive loan updates on their registered e-mail ID and mobile number, ensuring timely communication regarding their loan status.

**Key Services and Benefits:**  
- Access your home loan details anytime through the ICICI Bank iMobile app. To download the app, SMS iMobile to 5676766.  
- Customers can update their registered e-mail ID and mobile number to receive loan-related updates and notifications.  
- Updates to contact details can be made via:  
  - Online through the ICICI Bank website  
  - Visiting any Loan Servicing Branch  
  - Calling the customer care number 1800 1080  
- For branch locations and to select a Loan Servicing Branch, customers can visit maps.icicibank.com/mobile and filter by ‘Services’.  

**Customer Support Contact:**  
- Toll-Free Number: 1800 1080  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan updates are sent to registered e-mail and mobile number.  
- iMobile app provides easy access to loan details; download by SMS to 5676766.  
- Contact details can be updated online, via branch visit, or by calling 1800 1080.  
- Loan servicing branches can be located online for in-person assistance.
