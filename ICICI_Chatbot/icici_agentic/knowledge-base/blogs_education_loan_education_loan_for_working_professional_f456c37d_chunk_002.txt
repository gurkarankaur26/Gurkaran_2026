TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Education Loan for Working Professionals

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:

TAGS:  
Education Loan, Working Professionals, Eligibility, Documents Required, Application Process, Loan Disbursement, Moratorium, Collateral

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans specifically designed for working professionals to help finance higher education without bearing the entire financial burden upfront. The loan provides flexible repayment options including EMIs and may include a moratorium period allowing repayment to start after course completion.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Applicant must be an Indian resident.  
- Applicant must hold a graduate degree or diploma.  
- Applicant should have a minimum of 2 years of work experience if applying for a professional education loan.

**Documents Required:**  
- Proof of Identity or Current Address (any one): Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, Driving Licence, etc.  
- Academic Documents: Marksheets, Degree Certificates, Entrance Exam Scores.  
- Admission Proof: Acceptance Letter or Confirmation of Admission.  
- For salaried employees: Salary slips, Bank statements.  
- For self-employed individuals: Income Tax Returns (ITRs), GST Returns, Bank statements.

**How to Apply:**  
1. **Check Eligibility:** Verify eligibility criteria and prepare all necessary documents. Applications can be submitted online via ICICI Bank Net Banking or the iMobile App.  
2. **Secure Admission:** Use the loan sanction letter to apply to universities. Inform the bank once admission is confirmed.  
3. **Submit Final Documents:** Provide admission proof and any additional financial documents required by the bank.  
4. **Loan Disbursement:** After verification, the loan amount is disbursed either directly to the university or credited to the applicant’s account as per bank guidelines.

**Important Considerations Before Applying:**  
- **Course Credibility:** Choose courses and institutions that add value to your career, as banks assess course credibility before loan approval.  
- **Repayment Capacity:** Calculate estimated EMIs and ensure they fit within your monthly income to avoid financial stress.  
- **Moratorium Period:** Check availability of a moratorium period which allows repayment to start after course completion, providing financial flexibility.  
- **Collateral Requirements:** Higher loan amounts may require collateral such as property, fixed deposits, or insurance policies.

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum 2 years work experience required for professional education loans.  
- Multiple document types required including identity, academic, admission, and financial proofs.  
- Application can be done online via Net Banking or iMobile App.  
- Loan disbursed directly to university or applicant’s account.  
- Moratorium period available for repayment post course completion.  
- Collateral may be required for higher loan amounts.  
- Course credibility and repayment capacity are key factors for loan approval.
