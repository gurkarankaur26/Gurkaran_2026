TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Car Loan Charges, Prepayment, and Penal Charges Policy

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Car Loans, Loan Charges, Prepayment, Penal Charges

TAGS:  
Car Loan, Prepayment Charges, Penal Charges, Loan Recovery, EMI, Loan Foreclosure, CERSAI Charges, RBI Guidelines

CONTENT:  
This document outlines the charges, prepayment policies, and penal charges applicable to ICICI Bank car loans as per the latest RBI guidelines effective from April 1, 2024.

**Charges on Car Loans:**  
- **Cash Transaction Charges (for repayment of EMI dues in cash at branches):** ₹100 + applicable GST  
- **Prepayment Charges (On Foreclosure):**  
  - Nil for loan seasoning above 24 months  
  - 2% plus GST for loan seasoning between 13 to 24 months  
  - 3% plus GST for loan seasoning up to 12 months  
  - Nil for eligible Micro and Small segment customers  
- **CERSAI Charges:** ₹100 + applicable GST  

**Loan Related Recovery Charges (If applicable):**  
- Charges incurred in SARFAESI proceedings: At actuals  
- Charges incurred in sending different notices: At actuals  
- Enforcement charges: At actuals  
- Non Maintenance of Mode of Payment charges (NMMP): ₹800 + applicable GST  
- Paper advertisement charges: At actuals  
- Repossession charges: At actuals  
- Security guard charges: At actuals  

**Part Prepayment Charges for Car Loans:**  
- **Eligibility:** No minimum seasoning required to initiate part payment  
- Part prepayment allowed for all customer profiles (Individual, Non-individual, Salaried, Self-employed, etc.)  
- **Part Prepayment Charges:**  
  - 3% plus GST for loans with seasoning up to 24 months  
  - Nil for loans seasoning above 24 months  
- **Other Terms & Conditions:**  
  - Minimum part prepayment amount will be equivalent to one Equated Monthly Instalment (EMI)  
  - No maximum part prepayment amount  
  - No maximum number of part prepayments  
  - No minimum gaps in part prepayments  

**RBI Guidelines on Penal Charges (Effective April 1, 2024):**  
- RBI Circular No. RBI/2023-24/53 dated 18 August 2023 mandates that penalty charges for non-compliance with material loan terms will be treated as penal charges, not penal interest.  
- Penal charges will not be capitalized; no further interest will be computed on such charges.  
- This applies to all fresh loan facilities executed from April 1, 2024. Existing loans will switch to the new regime by the next review/renewal date or by June 30, 2024, whichever is earlier.  

**Revised Penal Charges:**  
| Existing Penal Interest | Applicable Penal Charges |  
|------------------------|--------------------------|  
| 24% per annum + applicable GST | 5% per annum + applicable GST |  

*Note: Goods and Services Tax and other government taxes, levies, etc., will be charged over and above the mentioned penal charges as per prevailing rates.*

KEY POINTS:  
- Prepayment charges vary based on loan seasoning; nil after 24 months.  
- Part prepayment allowed anytime with minimum amount equal to one EMI.  
- Penal charges capped at 5% per annum plus GST, replacing earlier 24% penal interest.  
- Various recovery and enforcement charges apply at actuals or fixed rates plus GST.  
- RBI guidelines effective from April 1, 2024, ensure penal charges are not compounded.
