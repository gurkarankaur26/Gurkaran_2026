CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Two-Wheeler Loans to help customers finance the purchase of motorcycles, scooters, and other two-wheeler vehicles. The loan eligibility, interest rates, application process, and features are designed to provide flexible and affordable financing options for both salaried and self-employed individuals.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
| Criteria                  | Salaried Individuals                          | Self-Employed Individuals                      |  
|---------------------------|----------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|  
| Indian Citizenship & Age   | Must be an Indian citizen above 21 years     | Must be an Indian citizen above 21 years      |  
| Source of Income           | Stable employment with Salary Slips/Form 16  | Stable income through business with ITR docs  |  
| Credit Score              | Good Credit Score                             | Good Credit Score                              |  
| Required Documents         | Proof of identity, address, income (Salary Slips/Form 16) | Proof of identity, address, income (Income Tax Return documents) |  
| Additional Requirements    | Bank Statements, valid signatures, photographs | Proof of ownership of business, business stability |  
| Specific Requirements      | Proof of employment stability or experience  | Proof of business stability or proof of ownership |  

**Interest Rates:**  
- Interest rates range from 10.25%* to 26.00%* per annum.  
- Rates vary based on creditworthiness and financial profile.  
- Applicable for new motorcycles, scooters, and other two-wheelers.  

**Application Process:**  
- Apply online via the ICICI Bank website or visit any ICICI Bank branch.  
- Alternatively, apply through the dealership by selecting a vehicle and contacting ICICI Bank.  
- Provide necessary documents as per eligibility criteria (can be skipped for existing customers).  
- Pre-approved customers may avail Instant Two-Wheeler Loan approval within minutes.  
- Loan amount is disbursed directly to the customer’s bank account or paid to the dealer.  

**Loan Features & Benefits:**  
- Competitive and flexible interest rates tailored to customer profiles.  
- Quick and hassle-free application and approval process.  
- Suitable for both salaried and self-employed individuals.  
- Option to check loan affordability and EMI using the Two-Wheeler Loan EMI Calculator.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility requires Indian citizenship, minimum age 21, stable income, and good credit score.  
- Interest rates range from 10.25%* to 26.00%* per annum based on credit profile.  
- Documents required include identity proof, address proof, income proof, and additional documents based on employment type.  
- Application can be done online, at branches, or via dealerships.  
- Instant loan approval available for eligible pre-approved customers.  
- Loan disbursal directly to bank account or dealer.  
- EMI Calculator available to plan finances before applying.
