ICICI Bank offers an online Life Insurance Calculator to help customers estimate their life and term insurance premiums quickly and conveniently. This tool enables users to calculate the premium amount based on their personal details and coverage requirements, facilitating informed decision-making for life insurance purchases.

The calculator considers factors such as age, sum assured, policy term, and premium payment frequency to provide an accurate premium estimate. It supports instant calculation without the need for documentation or lengthy processes.

- Quick and easy premium estimation  
- Helps in planning adequate life insurance coverage  
- Enables comparison of different policy terms and premium options  
- Supports instant decision-making for term insurance purchase
