ICICI Bank Education Loan for MBBS – Eligibility, Interest Rates, Documents & Benefits

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers customised Education Loans specifically for MBBS aspirants to provide financial support for pursuing medical education in India. The loan caters to students seeking funding for recognised and accredited institutions.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- **Nationality:** Applicant must be an Indian citizen.  
- **Age:** Between 16 and 35 years.  
- **Academic Record:** Must demonstrate a strong academic track record.  
- **Qualification:** Completion of 10+2 (12th standard) or Diploma is mandatory.  
- **Course:** Loan is exclusively for MBBS degree courses.  
- **Institution:** Must be recognised and accredited by relevant regulatory bodies in India.  
- **Loan Amount:** Up to ₹1 crore for studies in India.  
- **Co-borrower:** Required; eligible co-borrowers include relatives such as father, mother, brother, sister, spouse, grandparents, parents-in-law, or maternal/paternal uncle.  
- **Security:** Collateral may be required depending on the loan amount. Acceptable collateral includes property (house/flat), Fixed Deposits, or existing ICICI Bank Home Loan for cross-collateralisation.

**Documents Required:**  
- Duly filled online application form  
- Passport-size photographs  
- Copy of 12th standard mark sheet or latest educational certificate  
- Admission letter with cost of education (if available)  
- PAN Card of applicant and guardian/parent  
- Residence proof (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or other valid IDs)  
- Income proof of parent/co-borrower/guardian: recent 3 months’ salary slips, Form 16, and updated Income Tax Returns (ITR) for the past 2 years  
- Additional documents may be requested as per ICICI Bank policy

**Interest Rate & Loan Details:**  
- Interest rates are linked to the Repo rate, ensuring transparency and alignment with market conditions.  
- **Loan Tenure:**  
  - Undergraduate courses: Up to 10-12 years  
  - Postgraduate courses: Up to 12-14 years  
- **Maximum Loan Amount:**  
  - Domestic courses: Up to ₹1 crore  
  - International courses: Up to ₹3 crore  
- **Collateral:** Required depending on loan amount; accepted forms include Fixed Deposits, Property, or cross-collateralisation with existing mortgage loans.  
- **Unsecured Loan:** Available up to ₹1 crore for both undergraduate and postgraduate courses at premier institutions.  
- **Moratorium Period:** Course duration plus an additional grace period of up to 12 months.  
- **Margin:** No margin required for premier institutions.

**Benefits:**  
- High loan amount support up to ₹1 crore for MBBS studies in India.  
- Flexible repayment tenure aligned with course duration and post-study grace period.  
- Competitive interest rates linked to Repo rate for transparency.  
- Option for unsecured loans at premier institutions.  
- Collateral options include property and fixed deposits, easing the security process.  
- Co-borrower flexibility with close relatives to support loan approval.
