TITLE: ICICI Bank Car Loan Eligibility and Credit Score Requirements  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Eligibility, Credit Score, ICICI Bank, Loan Application  
CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank requires a strong credit score for car loan eligibility. The preferred credit score is 700 or above. A credit score of 750 or higher reflects responsible credit behavior and strengthens your car loan application with ICICI Bank. Maintaining a good credit score improves your chances of loan approval and may result in better loan terms.  
KEY POINTS:  
- Preferred credit score for car loan eligibility: 700 or above  
- A score of 750 or higher indicates responsible credit behavior  
- Higher credit scores strengthen loan applications  
- Good credit scores can lead to better loan terms
