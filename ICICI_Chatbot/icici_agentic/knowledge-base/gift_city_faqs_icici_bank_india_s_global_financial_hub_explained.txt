CONTENT:  
Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) is India’s first smart city and International Financial Services Centre (IFSC), located in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. It is designed to position India as a global hub for finance and technology services, aiming to bring back financial activities previously conducted in international financial centres such as Singapore and Dubai.

GIFT City hosts a wide range of financial institutions including banks, insurance companies, fintech firms, capital markets, global treasury centres, and fund managers. Businesses operating here benefit from a special regulatory and operational environment.

**International Financial Services Centre (IFSC):**  
- A special financial zone where financial institutions offer international services in foreign currencies.  
- Facilitates international financial business within India, reducing the need for companies to operate abroad.  
- Transactions at GIFT IFSC are conducted in multiple foreign currencies. Payments in Indian Rupees (INR) are permitted only for administrative and statutory expenses.  

**Regulatory Authority:**  
- The International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA) is the unified regulator for GIFT IFSC.  
- Established by the Government of India, IFSCA oversees all financial services and businesses operating within the IFSC.  
- Provides a single-window regulatory framework, ensures compliance with global standards, and promotes financial growth and foreign investment.  

**Benefits of Banking at GIFT City with ICICI Bank:**  
- Proximity to Ahmedabad with excellent connectivity via airports, rail, and metro.  
- Modern plug-and-play infrastructure for efficient operations.  
- Operational efficiency for international transactions.  
- Lower processing costs.  
- Access to global financial services under a single regulatory framework.  

**Account Eligibility and Types:**  
- Foreign Currency Savings Account: Eligible for all individuals including Resident Indians, NRIs, and minors. These accounts accrue interest except for those denominated in HKD currency.  
- Current Accounts: Can be opened by Resident Indians, NRIs, including minors.  
- Joint Accounts: Both Current and Savings Accounts can be opened in joint names with modes of operation such as either or survivor, jointly, or anyone or survivor.  
- Nomination facility is available on Savings Accounts.

- GIFT City is India’s first IFSC and smart city, designed to attract global financial services.  
- IFSCA is the single unified regulator for all financial activities in GIFT IFSC.  
- Transactions are primarily in foreign currencies; INR is allowed only for administrative/statutory expenses.  
- Accounts available: Foreign Currency Savings and Current Accounts for Resident Indians, NRIs, and minors.  
- Joint accounts and nomination facilities are supported.  
- ICICI Bank offers operational efficiency, lower costs, and access to global financial services at GIFT City.
