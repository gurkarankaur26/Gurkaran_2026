TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Features, Rewards, and Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Rewards, Digital Payments, Cashback, Visa  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card is a co-branded credit card designed to enhance the digital payment experience for Indian consumers, especially those shopping on Amazon.in. The card is initially offered by invitation to select ICICI Bank customers who use the Amazon app. Invited customers can apply digitally and receive an instant ready-to-use digital card, enabling immediate online shopping without waiting for the physical card. The physical card is delivered within a few days.

**Reward Structure:**  
The card offers reward points based on spending categories as follows:

| Spending Category                                | Reward Points per ₹100 Spent | Cashback Equivalent |
|-------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------|---------------------|
| Amazon Prime customers on Amazon.in              | 50 points (5% earnings back) | 5%                  |
| Non-Prime customers on Amazon.in                  | 30 points (3% earnings back) | 3%                  |
| Spends on digital categories and Amazon Pay partner merchants | 20 points (2% earnings back) | 2%                  |
| Spends at any other merchant accepting Visa cards | 10 points (1% earnings back) | 1%                  |

Reward points are credited monthly after the billing cycle to the customer’s Amazon Pay balance under the ‘Gift and Credits’ section. These points can be redeemed to purchase from over 160 million items on Amazon.in, including mobiles, electronics, groceries, appliances, fashion, and bill payments. Additionally, rewards can be used with Amazon Pay partner merchants for flight tickets, hotel bookings, food delivery, movie tickets, and more.

**Additional Features and Innovations:**  
- Instant issuance of a digital credit card for immediate online transactions.  
- Physical card delivery within a few days.  
- Contactless card technology leveraging Host Card Emulation (HCE) for mobile payments at physical stores.  
- Exclusive spend campaigns and partnerships with iconic brands like Manchester United and Ferrari.  
- First in India to offer instant credit cards online for pre-approved customers.

KEY POINTS:  
- Invitation-based issuance initially for select ICICI Bank customers using Amazon.in.  
- Instant digital card issuance for immediate use.  
- Reward points range from 1% to 5% cashback equivalent depending on spending category.  
- Rewards credited monthly to Amazon Pay balance and redeemable across Amazon.in and partner merchants.  
- Contactless payments enabled via mobile app using HCE technology.  
- Physical card sent within a few days of application.  
- Part of ICICI Bank’s broader strategy to innovate and expand card offerings with exclusive benefits.
