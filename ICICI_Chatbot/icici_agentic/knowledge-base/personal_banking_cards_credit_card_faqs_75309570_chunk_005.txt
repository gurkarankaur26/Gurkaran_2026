TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Credit Card Rewards, Redemption, and Benefits

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Credit Cards, Rewards Program, Travel Benefits

TAGS:  
ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Rewards Points, Redemption, Airport Lounge Access, Priority Pass, Fees, Eligibility

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Credit Cards offer a comprehensive rewards program where cardholders can earn reward points on eligible spends. These points can be redeemed for a variety of products and services including travel bookings, statement credits, and other catalogue options available on the ICICI Bank Rewards portal.

**Reward Points Earning and Redemption:**  
- Reward points can be viewed and tracked via the iMobile app or Net Banking.  
- Minimum points required for redemption vary by product or service category and are displayed on the Rewards portal.  
- Reward points are not equivalent to currency; their redemption value varies across products and services.  
- Eligible spends on add-on cards also earn reward points, which accumulate under the primary cardholder’s account.  
- Accelerated rewards allow earning additional points on specific categories or partner merchants such as travel, hotels, and online shopping.  
- Reward points have a validity of 3 years from the date of issuance and expire if not redeemed within this period.  
- Certain transactions such as fuel purchases, cash withdrawals, EMI conversions, fee payments, and wallet loads generally do not earn reward points.  
- Unredeemed points are forfeited upon closure of the credit card account.  
- Redemption options vary by card type and rewards program; not all cards offer the same redemption features.  
- A Redemption Handling Fee of ₹99 plus applicable GST is charged for every redemption request and will appear on the monthly credit card statement.  
- Once a redemption request is placed, it cannot be changed even if the points required remain the same.

**Airport Lounge Access:**  
- Eligible ICICI Bank Credit Cards provide complimentary access to domestic and international airport lounges.  
- Benefits include comfortable seating, refreshments, Wi-Fi, and other services to enhance the travel experience.  
- Travel-focused credit cards such as Sapphiro, Emeralde, Emeralde Private Metal, and select co-branded cards offer premium perks including airport lounge access, travel rewards, concierge services, and partner privileges.  
- The Priority Pass provided with these cards is pre-activated and can be used directly for accessing international airport lounges without any additional activation steps.

KEY POINTS:  
- Reward points validity: 3 years  
- Redemption Handling Fee: ₹99 + GST per redemption  
- Reward points not earned on fuel, cash withdrawals, EMI conversions, fee payments, wallet loads  
- Add-on card spends earn points credited to primary cardholder  
- Accelerated rewards on select categories and partner merchants  
- Unredeemed points forfeited on account closure  
- Airport lounge access included with select premium cards  
- Priority Pass is pre-activated for international lounge access  
- Redemption options and benefits vary by card type
