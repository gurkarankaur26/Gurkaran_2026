**Taxation on NRI Accounts:**  
- No tax is applicable in India on funds lying in NRE (Non-Resident External) accounts.  
- Interest earned on NRO (Non-Resident Ordinary) accounts is taxable at 30% plus applicable cess and surcharge.  
- If interest exceeds Rs. 5 million during the financial year, an additional surcharge of 10% applies.  
- Tax withholding percentages may vary; customers should check the latest details.  
- Tax regulations in the country of residence may also apply.  

**Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA):**  
- DTAA helps avoid double taxation on income earned in India and the home country.  
- Under DTAA, income may be taxed at a lower or nil rate in the source country (India).  
- To avail DTAA benefits in India, the following documents must be furnished:  
  - Tax Residency Certificate (TRC)  
  - Form 10F  
  - Permanent Account Number (PAN)  
- If PAN is not furnished, concessional DTAA rates will not be applicable.  
- Income tax rates will be as per the DTAA agreement between India and the respective country.  

**Opening an NRI Account with ICICI Bank:**  
- NRI accounts can be opened by visiting ICICI Bank branches or representative offices in Singapore, United Kingdom, Bahrain, Hong Kong, or UAE.  
- Alternatively, accounts can be opened through an online application process.
