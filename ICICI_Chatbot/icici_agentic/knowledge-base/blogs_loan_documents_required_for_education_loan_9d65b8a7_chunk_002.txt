TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Education Loan – Eligibility, Documentation, Collateral, and Loan Details

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Education Loan

TAGS:  
Education Loan, Eligibility, Collateral, Documentation, Loan Amount, Interest Rate, Co-borrowers, Academic Record

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans for students aged between 16 and 65 years at the time of loan maturity (case-by-case basis). The loan is designed for pursuing graduation, postgraduate degrees, or professional education diplomas from recognized and accredited institutes in India or overseas.

**Eligibility:**  
- Age: 16 to 65 years at loan maturity  
- Academic Qualification: Completion of 10+2 or equivalent  
- Admission: Loan approval is based on pre-admission sanction, entrance exam scores, and confirmed admission to the course/institute.

**Loan Amount and Collateral:**  
- Education Loan up to ₹20 lakh can be availed without collateral, subject to certain conditions.  
- For higher loan amounts, collateral such as property, Fixed Deposits, or existing ICICI Bank Home Loans may be required.  
- Property documents are mandatory only if opting for a secured Education Loan where the property acts as collateral.  
- Pledging property can help avail a higher loan amount at a lower interest rate.

**Co-borrowers:**  
- Co-borrowers can include family members such as parents, siblings, or spouses.

**Documentation Required:**  
- Proof of Identity and Address  
- Academic Records (including gap certificate if there is a break in education)  
- Proof of Income:  
  - Salaried individuals: Salary slips and bank statements  
  - Self-employed individuals: Income Tax Returns (ITR), GST details, and proof of business  
- ITR documents are mandatory for self-employed applicants/co-applicants/guarantors and for salaried individuals if the loan amount exceeds a certain threshold.

**Additional Information:**  
- Maintaining a good academic record and selecting accredited institutes positively impact loan approval chances.  
- The loan approval process considers academic continuity and financial stability of the applicant and co-borrowers.

KEY POINTS:  
- Age eligibility: 16 to 65 years at loan maturity  
- Academic qualification: 10+2 or equivalent  
- Loan amount up to ₹20 lakh without collateral under certain conditions  
- Collateral options: Property, Fixed Deposits, existing ICICI Home Loans  
- Co-borrowers: Parents, siblings, spouses  
- Required documents: Identity, address, academic records, income proof, ITR for self-employed and high-value loans  
- Gap certificate required if education history has breaks  
- Loan approval based on pre-admission sanction, entrance exam scores, and confirmed admission  
- Good academic record and accredited institute selection improve loan approval chances
