Education loans from ICICI Bank provide financial support for higher studies in India and abroad, with loan amounts up to ₹3 crore. The bank offers tailored education loan products for domestic and international courses, including collateral-free loans for premium institutes and special provisions for government employees, CXOs, and professionals with at least 2 years of work experience. Specialized loan options are also available for studies in countries like Canada, the UK, and Germany.

**Documents Required for Education Loan Application:**  
Applicants and, in some cases, their guardians must submit the following documents to ensure a smooth loan approval process:  

1. **Identity Proof (any one):**  
   - Aadhaar Card  
   - Voter ID  
   - Driving Licence  

2. **Academic Documents:**  
   - Mark sheets  
   - Degree certificates  
   - Entrance exam scores  

3. **Admission Proof:**  
   - Offer/Acceptance letter from the educational institution  
   - Confirmation of admission  
   - I-20 form (for USA admissions) or equivalent admission confirmation documents  

4. **Collateral Documents (if applicable):**  
   - Ownership documents of the pledged property (house or flat)  
   - Fixed Deposit (FD) proof with ICICI Bank (if FD is used as collateral)  
   - Documentation related to existing ICICI Bank Home Loan for cross-collateralisation  

**Note:** ICICI Bank reserves the right to request additional documents if required.

**Eligibility:**  
- Applicant must be an Indian national.  
- For collateral-free loans, eligibility may depend on the institute and course.  
- Government employees, CXOs, and professionals with 2+ years of work experience have special provisions.  
- Loans without proof of income are possible for smaller loan amounts; however, proof of income or collateral/guarantor may be required for higher loan amounts.

**Loan Features and Benefits:**  
- Loan amounts up to ₹3 crore.  
- Flexible options for collateral-free loans for premium institutes.  
- Specialized loans for studies in Canada, UK, Germany, and other countries.  
- Support for both domestic and international education expenses.  
- Option to use fixed deposits or existing home loans as collateral.  

**Important Considerations:**  
- Missing or incorrect documents can delay or result in rejection of the loan application.  
- It is advisable to prepare all required documents before applying.  
- Check specific eligibility criteria and terms for your loan amount and course.
