CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans with competitive interest rates to support students pursuing higher education in India and abroad. The interest rates vary based on factors such as course type, institution, loan amount, repayment tenure, loan security, and applicant eligibility. Education Loans cover tuition fees and other eligible academic expenses with flexible repayment options and structured EMI plans. Customers can apply online with simple documentation and select repayment tenures based on their financial needs and repayment capacity.

**Interest Rates (As of March 2026):**  
- Overall Education Loan interest rates range from 9.00% to 13.00% per annum, with a mean rate of 10.54% per annum.  
- Secured Education Loans for select domestic institutes start as low as 8.50% per annum.  
- Unsecured Education Loans for studies abroad start at 10.25% per annum.  
- Rates depend on course, institution category, loan security, applicant profile, and co-applicant credit history.
