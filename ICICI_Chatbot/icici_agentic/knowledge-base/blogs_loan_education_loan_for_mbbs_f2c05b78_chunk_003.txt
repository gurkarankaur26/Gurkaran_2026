CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers tailored Education Loans to support students pursuing MBBS courses both in India and abroad. The loan amount can cover tuition fees, living expenses, and other major educational costs.

**Loan Amount:**  
- Up to ₹1 crore for studies in India  
- Up to ₹3 crore for international studies

**Eligibility:**  
- Must meet age and academic requirements  
- Confirmed admission in the MBBS course  
- Academic records and entrance exam scores required

**Margin Requirements:**  
- Other category institutions require a 15% margin, which can be met through Fixed Deposits (FDs), scholarships, or initial fees paid to the institution.

**Documents Required:**  
- Academic documents  
- Admission letter  
- Identity and address proof  
- Income proof (if applicable)  
- Other supporting documents as required

**Application Process:**  
- Apply online or visit any ICICI Bank branch for offline application  
- Complete the application form accurately with all required details  
- Submit the necessary documents  
- Bank reviews the application and documents  
- Upon approval, the loan amount is disbursed directly into the applicant’s account

**Repayment Options:**  
- Flexible repayment tenure up to 12 years  
- Repayment typically begins after course completion, starting from a few months to one year after finishing the course  
- No prepayment penalty on Education Loans for medical students

**Guarantor and Collateral:**  
- Depending on the loan amount, a guarantor may be required  
- Collateral may be needed in certain cases to expedite the loan process

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount up to ₹1 crore (India) and ₹3 crore (abroad) for MBBS studies  
- Covers tuition fees, living expenses, and other major costs  
- Flexible repayment tenure up to 12 years, starting after course completion  
- No prepayment penalty for medical education loans  
- Guarantor and collateral requirements depend on loan amount  
- Margin of 15% applicable for other category institutions, can be met through FDs, scholarships, or initial fees  
- Application can be done online or offline at ICICI Bank branches
