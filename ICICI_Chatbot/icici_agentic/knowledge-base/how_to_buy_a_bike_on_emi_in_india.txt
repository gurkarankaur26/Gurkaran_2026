**What is a Bike Loan/Two-Wheeler Loan?**  
A Bike Loan is a financial product that enables you to purchase a bike and repay the loan amount in Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs). The EMI is a fixed monthly payment that includes both principal and interest, remaining constant throughout the loan tenure.
