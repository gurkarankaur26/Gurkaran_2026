ICICI Bank offers a variety of Credit Cards designed to cater to different customer needs, including lifestyle and travel benefits. These credit cards come with multiple rewards programs and can be customized based on the applicant’s requirements. They are available to individuals with varied annual incomes, making them accessible to a broad range of customers.

It is important to use credit cards responsibly and ensure timely repayment of dues to avoid penalties and interest charges. Customers should opt for a credit card only if they are confident in their ability to repay the outstanding amounts without delays.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank Credit Cards provide lifestyle and travel benefits.  
- Available to individuals with varied annual incomes.  
- Cards can be customized as per applicant’s needs.  
- Responsible usage and timely repayment are essential to avoid fees and interest.  
- Rewards programs are included with most credit cards.
