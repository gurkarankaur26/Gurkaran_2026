CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card is a co-branded credit card issued by ICICI Bank in association with Amazon and Visa. It is an invite-only program with no joining or annual fees. The card allows users to earn Amazon Pay balance on every purchase made on the Amazon website, which is credited directly to the cardholder’s Amazon account within 2 working days after the billing cycle ends.

Key features and benefits include:  
- No joining fee or annual fee.  
- Earnings on purchases on Amazon credited as Amazon Pay balance.  
- Contactless card for convenient payments.  
- Physical card delivery within 7 working days after approval; virtual card details available immediately for online transactions.  
- PIN generation or change can be done via IVR (toll-free 1800 1080), iMobile Pay app, or Net Banking.  
- No add-on cards available.  
- Use the payment method 'Login and Pay with Amazon' for transactions on Amazon Pay partner websites.  
- Monthly statements reflect all earnings from purchases.  
- Customer care support available from 7:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. on 1800 1080 (from registered mobile number).  
- Sales executive will contact within 48 hours for document collection and e-KYC completion.  

Important notes:  
- Earnings are credited only to the card owner’s Amazon account; using someone else’s card will not add earnings to your account.  
- If the physical card is not received within 7 working days, customers should contact customer care.  
- Amazon Pay transaction history can be checked at https://www.amazon.in/gp/payment/statement under the entry "Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card: Earnings for last billing cycle."
