ICICI Bank’s 3-in-1 Account integrates Savings, Demat, and Trading accounts into a single seamless platform, enabling customers to manage their banking and investment needs efficiently. Customers benefit from expert advisory services, theme-based investment portfolios, and exclusive access to Initial Public Offerings (IPOs). This integrated account simplifies fund transfers between savings and trading accounts, facilitating smooth online trading and investment activities.

- Paperless and secure account opening process.
