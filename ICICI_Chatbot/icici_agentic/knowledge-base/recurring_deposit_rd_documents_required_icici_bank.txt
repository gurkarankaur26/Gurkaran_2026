CONTENT:  
To open a Recurring Deposit (RD) account with ICICI Bank, customers need to submit specific documents as part of the account opening process. These documents help verify the identity and address of the applicant and ensure compliance with banking regulations.

**Documents Required to Open an RD Account:**  
1. **Proof of Identity (any one of the following):**  
   - Passport  
   - Voter ID Card  
   - PAN Card  
   - Driving License  
   - Aadhaar Card  
   - Any other government-issued photo ID  

2. **Proof of Address (any one of the following):**  
   - Passport  
   - Voter ID Card  
   - Driving License  
   - Aadhaar Card  
   - Utility bills (electricity, water, gas) not older than 3 months  
   - Rent agreement  
   - Bank account statement or passbook with address  

3. **Photographs:**  
   - Recent passport-sized photographs as per bank’s specifications  

4. **PAN Card:**  
   - Mandatory for tax purposes and to comply with KYC norms  

5. **KYC Documents:**  
   - Complete KYC (Know Your Customer) documentation as per RBI guidelines  

**Additional Information:**  
- The RD account can be opened by individuals, joint account holders, minors (with guardian), and NRIs (subject to applicable regulations).  
- The minimum monthly deposit amount and tenure options vary and can be selected at the time of account opening.  
- Interest rates on RD are competitive and compounded quarterly.  
- Premature withdrawal of RD is subject to penalties as per bank policy.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Submit valid identity and address proof documents.  
- PAN card is mandatory for RD account opening.  
- Recent passport-sized photographs required.  
- KYC compliance is essential.  
- RD account available for individuals, joint holders, minors, and NRIs.  
- Interest compounded quarterly; premature withdrawal penalties apply.
