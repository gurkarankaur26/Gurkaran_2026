TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card - Features, Benefits, and Usage  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, Credit Card, Cashback, Rewards, ICICI Bank, Amazon Prime, Fuel Surcharge Waiver  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card offers a range of cashback benefits tailored for Amazon shoppers and Amazon Pay merchant websites. It is designed to maximize rewards for Amazon Prime members and non-Prime cardholders alike, with no limits on earnings or expiry on rewards.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- **Cashback Rates:**  
  - Amazon Prime members earn 5% cashback on purchases made on Amazon India.  
  - Non-Prime cardholders earn 3% cashback on all Amazon products except digital products and gift cards, which earn 2%.  
  - Earn 2% cashback on purchases made on 100+ Amazon Pay merchant websites.  
  - Earn 1% cashback on all other payments.  
- **Fuel Surcharge Waiver:** 1% surcharge waiver on all fuel purchases.  
- **Earnings and Redemption:**  
  - Cashback earnings have no upper limit and no expiry date.  
  - Earnings are credited as Amazon Pay balance to the Amazon Account linked during card application.  
  - Cashback is credited at the end of each billing cycle.  
  - No minimum redemption amount is required.  
  - Cashback on e-books, gift cards (physical and digital), Amazon Pay money loading and auto reloading, bill payments, mobile and DTH recharges is 2%, regardless of Prime status.  
- **Exclusions:** No rewards on gold, silver, precious metals, tax-related payments, EMI purchases on Amazon, rent transactions, and fuel purchases (except for the surcharge waiver).  

**Application Status Tracking:**  
- Customers can track their application status by calling ICICI Bank Customer Care at 1800 1080 from their registered mobile number.  

KEY POINTS:  
- 5% cashback for Amazon Prime members on Amazon India purchases.  
- 3% cashback for non-Prime members on Amazon India purchases (2% on digital products and gift cards).  
- 2% cashback on 100+ Amazon Pay merchant websites.  
- 1% cashback on all other payments.  
- 1% fuel surcharge waiver.  
- Cashback credited as Amazon Pay balance with no expiry or limits.  
- No rewards on precious metals, tax payments, EMI, rent, and fuel purchases (except surcharge waiver).  
- Application status can be checked via Customer Care at 1800 1080.
