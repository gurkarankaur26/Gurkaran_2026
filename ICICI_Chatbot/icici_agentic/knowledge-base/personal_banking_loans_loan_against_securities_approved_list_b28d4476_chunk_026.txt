ICICI Prudential Nifty Index Funds and ETFs Overview

ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential offers a comprehensive range of Nifty-based mutual funds and Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) designed to cater to different investor preferences. These funds track various Nifty indices such as Nifty 50, Nifty Next 50, Nifty Bank, Nifty IT, Nifty 100 Low Volatility 30, and Nifty 200 Momentum 30, providing diversified exposure to Indian equity markets.

Key Fund Variants:  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty 50 Index Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty Next 50 Index Fund**: Offers Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty Bank Index Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty IT Index Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty 100 Low Volatility 30 ETF FOF**: Fund of Funds tracking low volatility stocks in the Nifty 100 index, available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty 200 Momentum 30 Index Fund**: Focuses on momentum stocks in the Nifty 200 index, available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **ICICI Prudential Nifty Private Bank ETF**: Provides exposure to private sector banks.

Plan Types:  
- **Direct Plan**: For investors who invest directly without distributor involvement, offering lower expense ratios.  
- **Regular Plan**: For investors investing through distributors or advisors.

Payout Options:  
- **Growth Option**: Earnings are reinvested, leading to capital appreciation.  
- **IDCW Option**: Periodic income distribution to investors.

Benefits:  
- Diversified exposure to key Indian equity indices.  
- Multiple plan and payout options to suit investor needs.  
- Access to sector-specific ETFs like Nifty Bank and Nifty IT.  
- Low volatility and momentum-based funds for risk-adjusted returns.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Nifty index funds and ETFs available under ICICI Prudential.  
- Options include Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW payout modes.  
- Funds cover broad market indices (Nifty 50, Nifty Next 50), sectoral indices (Bank, IT), and strategy-based indices (Low Volatility, Momentum).  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity exposure with flexibility in investment and income options.
