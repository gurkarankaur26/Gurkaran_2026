TITLE: ICICI Bank Education Loan – Features, Eligibility, Interest Rates, and Application Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Education Loan  
TAGS: Education Loan, Student Loan, Interest Rates, Eligibility, Fees, Application Process, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans to help students finance their higher education in India and abroad. The loan covers tuition fees, living expenses, travel costs, and other education-related expenses, ensuring that students do not have to pay out of pocket.

**Key Features:**  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹1.5 crore depending on the course and institution  
- Covers tuition fees, accommodation, travel, books, and other education-related expenses  
- Flexible repayment options with moratorium period until course completion plus 6 months to 1 year  
- Competitive interest rates with options for fixed and floating rates  
- No collateral required for loans up to ₹7.5 lakh; collateral/security required for higher amounts  
- Quick and hassle-free loan processing with minimal documentation  

**Eligibility:**  
- Indian resident students admitted to recognized institutions in India or abroad  
- Admission to full-time courses such as graduation, post-graduation, professional courses, and vocational training  
- Co-applicant (parent/guardian) with a stable income source is required  

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- Interest rates vary based on loan amount, course, and repayment tenure; typically starting from competitive base rates  
- Processing fees applicable as per bank norms  
- Prepayment and foreclosure options available without penalty in most cases  

**Application Process:**  
1. Fill the Education Loan application form online or at the nearest ICICI Bank branch  
2. Submit required documents including admission proof, identity proof, address proof, income proof of co-applicant, and academic records  
3. Bank evaluates the application and conducts credit assessment  
4. Loan sanction and disbursal as per the course fee schedule  

**Benefits:**  
- Financial support for higher education without immediate financial burden  
- Flexible repayment options tailored to student needs  
- Access to expert guidance and support throughout the loan tenure  
- Helps build credit history for future financial needs  

**Important Notes:**  
- Borrowers should assess their repayment capacity before applying  
- Read all terms and conditions carefully before finalizing the loan  
- Timely repayment helps maintain a good credit score  

KEY POINTS:
