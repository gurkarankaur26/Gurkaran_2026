ICICI Bank offers a Gold Loan specifically designed for the agricultural sector, providing farmers with quick access to funds by leveraging their gold assets. This loan is ideal for various agricultural needs such as purchasing equipment, managing daily expenses, or investing in crop inputs. The loan process involves valuation and documentation, after which the loan amount is disbursed directly to the customer’s account. The bank’s tailored solutions aim to support farmers in achieving a successful harvest season by ensuring timely financial assistance with minimal hassle.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan Type: Gold Loan for Agriculture  
- Purpose: Purchase of agricultural equipment, daily expenses, crop inputs  
- Loan Disbursement: After valuation and documentation, funds are credited to the customer’s account  
- Benefits: Quick access to substantial funds, minimal documentation, tailored for farmers’ needs  
- Target Customers: Farmers and individuals involved in agriculture
