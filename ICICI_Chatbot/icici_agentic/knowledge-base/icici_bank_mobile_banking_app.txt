CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive mobile banking app designed to provide customers with convenient access to a wide range of banking services. The app supports payments through UPI and other digital banking transactions, enabling users to manage their finances anytime and anywhere.

The app is available for download on both Android and iOS platforms:  
- Android users can download the app from the Google Play Store.  
- iPhone users can download the app from the Apple App Store.

The app ensures secure, fast, and easy banking, including fund transfers, bill payments, account management, and more.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mobile banking app supports UPI payments and other banking services.  
- Available on Google Play Store for Android users.  
- Available on Apple App Store for iPhone users.  
- Enables 24/7 access to banking services.  
- Secure and user-friendly interface.
