SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Recurring Deposit (RD) accounts designed to help customers build a disciplined savings habit with regular monthly contributions. The RD is suitable for individuals looking to save smaller amounts regularly and earn competitive interest rates.

**Key Features:**  
- Minimum monthly deposit: ₹500  
- Subsequent deposits: Multiples of ₹100  
- Minimum tenure: 6 months (extendable in multiples of 3 months)  
- Maximum tenure: 10 years  
- Interest earned on RD is taxable as per applicable laws  
- Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) applies on interest earned  

**Interest and Taxation:**  
- Interest rates vary and can be checked using the RD Calculator on the ICICI Bank website for maturity amount estimation.  
- Interest earned is subject to income tax and TDS as per government regulations.  

**Benefits:**  
- Helps inculcate a disciplined savings habit with small, regular contributions  
- Convenient online account opening and management through ICICI Bank’s digital platforms including iMobile app  
- Nomination facility available to secure savings for nominees  
- Suitable for individuals with surplus funds opting for Fixed Deposits (FD) or smaller regular savings via RD  

**Customer Experiences:**  
- Customers appreciate the ease of opening and managing RD accounts online without visiting the branch.  
- The digital platform is user-friendly, allowing comparison of rates and selection of tenure.  
- RD is recommended for young professionals and busy individuals seeking hassle-free, disciplined savings with good returns.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum monthly deposit: ₹500; subsequent deposits in multiples of ₹100  
- Tenure: 6 months minimum, up to 10 years  
- Interest earned is taxable; TDS applicable  
- Online account opening and management available  
- Nomination facility provided  
- RD Calculator available for maturity estimation  
- Ideal for disciplined savings with small monthly contributions
