ICICI Bank offers Home Loans up to ₹10 crore* with flexible interest rate options including floating and fixed rates tailored to customer needs. The interest rates are linked to the repo rate (effective 5 December 2025: 5.25%) and vary based on credit bureau score, customer segment, and profile. The rates and offers are valid till 30th June 2026.

**Additional Information:**  
- Customers with a Pre-approved Home Loan can avail online sanctioning at an interest rate of 7.55%* p.a. (subject to terms and conditions and bureau score).  
- Processing fee: 0.5% of the loan amount plus applicable taxes.  
- Interest rates are subject to change based on repo rate fluctuations, credit score, customer segment, and profile.  
- The bank offers minimal documentation and quick processing to facilitate home ownership.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Home Loan amount up to ₹10 crore available.  
- Floating interest rates start at 7.50%* p.a. (pre-approved loans at 7.55%* p.a.).  
- Fixed interest rates available for tenures from 24 months to full loan term.  
- Interest rates linked to repo rate (5.25% effective 5 Dec 2025).  
- Processing fee: 0.5% of loan amount + taxes.  
- Interest rates vary by credit score, customer type (salaried/self-employed), and loan amount.  
- Validity of rates: Till 30th June 2026.  
- Online sanctioning available for pre-approved customers.
