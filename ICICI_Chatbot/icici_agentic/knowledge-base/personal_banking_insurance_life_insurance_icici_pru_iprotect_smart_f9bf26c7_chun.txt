CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers the iProtect Smart term insurance plan providing comprehensive life and health cover. The plan ensures financial security for your family in case of unfortunate events during the policy term.

**Key Features:**  
- **Life Cover:** Benefit payable on the death of the life assured during the policy term.  
- **Return of Premium:** 100% return of premiums paid under the base product (excluding extra premiums, rider premiums, and taxes).  
- **Terminal Illness Benefit:** If the life assured is diagnosed with a terminal illness expected to lead to death within 6 months, confirmed by two independent medical practitioners, the benefit is payable.  
- **Increase in Life Cover:** You can increase the sum assured at key life milestones without medical examination, provided no claim has been admitted and the policy is in force. This option is available only with the Regular premium payment option. The additional premium is payable for the increased sum assured for the remaining policy term.  
  - Marriage: Increase by 50% of original sum assured, up to ₹50,00,000  
  - Birth or legal adoption of 1st child: Increase by 25%, up to ₹25,00,000  
  - Birth or legal adoption of 2nd child: Increase by 25%, up to ₹25,00,000  
- **Tax Benefits:**  
  - Premiums paid under the policy qualify for tax benefits under Sections 80C and 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961.  
  - Maximum tax benefit of ₹54,600 (₹46,800 under 80C and ₹7,800 under 80D) calculated at the highest tax slab rate of 31.2% (including cess, excluding surcharge) for premiums of ₹1,50,000 (life insurance) and ₹25,000 (health insurance).  
  - Tax benefits are subject to prevailing tax laws and conditions.  
- **Claim Settlement Ratio:** For FY 2023-24, ICICI Prudential has a strong claim settlement ratio (details available on the ICICI Prudential website).  
- **Coverage:** Over 91.74 million lives covered across individual and group customers as per ICICI Prudential Annual Report 2025.

**Additional Information:**  
- The increase in sum assured applies only to the base life cover and not to any rider covers.  
- Goods and Services Tax (GST) and cess will be charged extra as per prevailing rates.  
- The terminal illness diagnosis must be confirmed by medical practitioners registered with the Indian Medical Association and approved by the company. The company reserves the right for independent assessment.

KEY POINTS:  
- Life cover payable on death during policy term.  
- 100% return of premiums paid under base product (excluding riders and taxes).  
- Terminal illness benefit available with strict medical confirmation.  
- Option to increase sum assured at marriage and childbirth/adoption milestones without medical exams.  
- Tax benefits under Sections 80C and 80D with maximum benefit of ₹54,600.  
- Claim settlement ratio available for FY 2023-24; strong track record.  
- Over 91.74 million lives covered as per latest annual report.
