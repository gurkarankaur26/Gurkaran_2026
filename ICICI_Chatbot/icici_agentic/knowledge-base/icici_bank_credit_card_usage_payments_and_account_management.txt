TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card - Usage, Payments, and Account Management  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Payments, Net Banking, iMobile App, Billing Cycle, Customer Care, Auto Debit, Refunds  

CONTENT:  
- **Credit Card Delivery & Activation:**  
  The first delivery attempt of your ICICI Bank Credit Card is typically made within 5 working days after the order is placed.  

- **Viewing Credit Card Details:**  
  You can view your Credit Card number by logging into the iMobile app or Net Banking and selecting your Credit Card details.  

- **Mobile Number Registration & Alerts:**  
  If you receive transaction alerts on your mobile, your number is registered with the Bank. To confirm your registered mobile number, call Customer Care from your registered mobile number.  

- **User ID and Password Management:**  
  - To get your User ID, call Customer Care and follow the self-banking option.  
  - To generate a password, use the “Get Password” link on the ICICI Bank login page and validate your registered mobile number.  
  - You can change your Net Banking User ID for credit cards once by logging into Net Banking > My Profile > Update user ID.  

- **Bill Payment Registration:**  
  Register your card as a biller via Net Banking: Payments & Transfer > Pay Bills > Bill Payment > Credit Card > Select “ICICI Bank Credit Card” and provide the 16-digit card number to complete registration.  

- **Managing Card Usage:**  
  Enable or disable Online, International, and Tap & Pay transactions via:  
  - iMobile Pay > Cards & Forex > Choose your Credit Card > Manage Card > Enable Online/International/Tap & Pay  
  - Or Net Banking > Cards > Credit Cards > Manage your Card > View All > Manage Credit Card > Manage Credit Card Usage.  

- **Security & OTPs:**  
  OTPs for transactions are sent only to your registered mobile number. Ensure your mobile number is updated with the Bank for security purposes.  

- **Payment Methods:**  
  You can pay your Credit Card bill through:  
  - iMobile App  
  - Net Banking  
  - UPI  
  - NEFT  
  - Auto-debit setup  
  - Cheque or cash payment at select ICICI Bank ATMs that accept Credit Card payments.  

- **Late Payment Charges:**  
  If cheque payments are not received and cleared by the due date, late payment charges and interest will be applied on the outstanding amount as per the Credit Card Terms & Conditions.  

- **Excess Payment Handling:**  
  Any excess amount paid becomes a credit balance on your Credit Card account and will be automatically adjusted against future transactions or outstanding dues.  

- **Refunds and Chargebacks:**  
  Merchant refunds, reversals, chargebacks, or permanent fraud credits posted to your card are treated as payments and adjusted against your outstanding balance.  

- **Billing Cycle Change:**  
  You may request a change in your billing cycle once every six months, subject to availability on your specific Credit Card and Bank approval.  

- **Charges for Bill Payment:**  
  Bill payment facility through Net Banking is free of cost.  

- **Payment Allocation Order:**  
  Payments are allocated in the following order: income and charges, then EMI principal, cash principal, and retail principal, as per the Bank’s payment application logic.  

- **Payment Reflection Time:**  
  Allow up to 24 hours for payments to reflect in your Credit Card account. If the payment does not appear after this period, contact Customer Care with payment reference details.  

- **Auto Debit Setup:**  
  You can set up Auto Debit through the iMobile app by selecting Cards & Forex > [Auto Debit setup options].  

- **Customer Support:**  
  For queries related to excess payments or other issues, contact Customer Care or email customer.care@icicibank.com from your registered email with the last 4 digits of your card.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Credit Card delivery within 5 working days.  
- View card details via iMobile app or Net Banking.  
- OTPs sent only to registered mobile number.  
- Multiple payment options including iMobile, Net Banking, UPI, NEFT, auto-debit, cheque, and cash at select ATMs.  
- Late payment charges apply if payments are delayed.  
- Excess payments automatically adjusted to future dues.  
- Billing cycle change allowed once every six months, subject to approval.  
- Bill payment via Net Banking is free.  
- Payments allocated in a specific order (income/charges, EMI principal, cash principal, retail principal).  
- Payment reflection time up to 24 hours.  
- Auto Debit can be set up via iMobile app.
