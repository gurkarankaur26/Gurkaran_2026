CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of investment and insurance products designed to meet diverse financial needs, along with convenient digital banking facilities.

**Mutual Funds**  
- Invest anytime, anywhere using iMobile app and Internet Banking.  
- Choose from mutual fund schemes offered by leading fund houses.  
- Use the ‘Import Fund’ feature to consolidate all your mutual fund folios held elsewhere.  
- Access capital gain/loss statements under the “Reports” section in iMobile.  
- Perform non-financial transactions such as updating email address, mobile number, nominee name, and folio consolidation via iMobile.

**National Pension System (NPS)**  
- Market-linked retirement plan offering multiple tax benefits.  
- Portability and flexibility across retail, corporate, multiple fund managers, and asset allocation options.  
- Tax exemptions:  
  - Up to ₹2 lakh tax exemption.  
  - Additional tax benefit under Section 80CCD(1B) of up to ₹50,000 over and above Section 80CCD(1) deduction of ₹1.5 lakh.  
  - Corporate model benefits: up to 10% of basic pay in old tax regime and up to 14% in new tax regime.  
- On maturity, up to 60% of the corpus can be withdrawn as a tax-free lump sum.

**Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)**  
- Accidental insurance scheme providing life cover.  
- Coverage includes ₹2 lakh for death, ₹1 lakh for partial disability, and ₹2 lakh for permanent disability.  
- Annual premium: ₹20.  
- Eligibility: Customers aged between 18 and 70 years.  
- Application available online.

**Digital Banking Facilities**  
- Mobile Banking: Download and activate the iMobile app for seamless banking.  
- Net Banking: Call Customer Care to authenticate and generate User ID and password instantly online.  

**Special Offers**  
- ICICI Bank customers get access to exclusive offers and discounts across multiple sectors and top brands throughout the year.

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual Funds investment available via iMobile and Internet Banking with folio consolidation and reporting features.  
- NPS offers significant tax benefits with flexible investment and withdrawal options.  
- PMSBY provides affordable accidental insurance cover with ₹2 lakh life cover at ₹20 annual premium.  
- Digital banking through iMobile app and Net Banking ensures convenient access to banking services.  
- Exclusive offers available for ICICI Bank customers on various brands and sectors.
