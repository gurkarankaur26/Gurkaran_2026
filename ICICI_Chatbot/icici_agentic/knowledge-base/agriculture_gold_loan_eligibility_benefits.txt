Agriculture is a vital sector, especially in developing countries, where many depend on farming for their livelihood. Farmers often face challenges such as unpredictable weather, fluctuating market prices, and limited access to credit. To address these financial needs, ICICI Bank offers Agriculture Gold Loans, enabling farmers to leverage their gold jewelry as collateral to obtain funds quickly and conveniently.

ICICI Bank’s Agriculture Gold Loan provides financial support up to ₹2 crore, making it suitable for various agricultural requirements such as purchasing seeds, fertilizers, equipment, or managing daily farming expenses. The loan tenure options are flexible, with choices of 6 months, 12 months, and 36 months, allowing farmers to select a repayment period that best fits their financial planning.

Key features and benefits of ICICI Bank Agriculture Gold Loan include:  
- Loan amount up to ₹2 crore based on gold valuation  
- Competitive interest rates (specific rates to be checked with the bank at the time of application)  
- Flexible repayment tenures: 6, 12, and 36 months  
- Over 6,000 branches enabled for Gold Loan services across India, ensuring easy accessibility  
- Dedicated Gold Loan teams providing personalized assistance for a smooth loan process  
- Quick and straightforward loan approval process to ensure timely availability of funds
