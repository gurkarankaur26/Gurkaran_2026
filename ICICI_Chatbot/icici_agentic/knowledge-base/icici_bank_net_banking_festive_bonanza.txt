CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank presents the Net Banking Festive Bonanza, an exclusive offer running from October to November to reward both new and existing users of ICICI Bank Internet Banking. This initiative encourages customers to use Internet Banking for their transactions and get rewarded with gift vouchers and other benefits.

**For New Users:**  
- Make your first shopping transaction of Rs 500 or more through ICICI Bank Internet Banking.  
- Be among the first 2,000 customers each day to qualify and receive an Amazon gift voucher worth Rs 250.

**For Existing Users:**  
- Carry out 2 transactions totaling Rs 2,000 or more.  
- Be among the first 1,000 users daily to receive an Amazon gift voucher worth Rs 500.

**For Seasoned Users:**  
- Complete three or more transactions during the month totaling Rs 1 lakh or more.  
- The top 2 customers with the highest number and value of transactions will receive a Cleartrip voucher worth Rs 25,000 each.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Internet Banking:**  
- Access to over 4 lakh merchant apps and websites for shopping.  
- Easy login using Mobile Number, OTP, and ATM PIN.  
- EMI payments available on spends of Rs 25,000 and above.  
- Exclusive offers on top brands for Internet Banking users.  
- Secure and convenient platform for bill payments, fund transfers, and online purchases.
