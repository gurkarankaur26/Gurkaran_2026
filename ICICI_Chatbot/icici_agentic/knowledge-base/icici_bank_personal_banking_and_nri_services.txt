CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of personal banking products and services tailored to meet diverse financial needs. Key offerings include salary accounts, various loan products, credit cards, NRI banking solutions, forex prepaid cards, remittance services, and specialized loan facilities against shares and mutual funds. The bank also provides premium account options with exclusive benefits and family banking features.

**Salary Account**  
- Designed for salaried individuals with benefits such as minimum balance waiver for the primary account holder and up to five family members.  
- Complimentary insurance cover linked to salary credits.  
- Waiver of charges for online fund transfers via NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS.  

**Loan Products**  
- Home Loan, Personal Loan, Auto Loan, and Education Loan available with personalized assessment and solutions.  

**Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Cards**  
- Offers include airport lounge access on spends of Rs. 10,000 per quarter.  

**Investment and Wealth Management**  
- Personalized financial profile assessment.  
- Portfolio rebalancing across debt and equity based on risk appetite.  
- Realigned investment strategies to meet financial goals.  
- Suite of investment offerings with share account benefits and privacy.  

**Family Banking**  
- Extend salary account benefits to up to five family members.  
- Family banking enrollment available via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app or branch visit.  
- Select Banking allows benefits extension to up to nine family members, including domestic lounge access on debit cards and preferential offerings on Demat and loans.  

**NRI Accounts and Services**  
- Convert Resident Savings Account to NRO Account for managing income earned in India.  
- Open NRE Account to enjoy tax-free interest and full repatriation of funds.  
- Manage NRI accounts digitally from anywhere globally.  
- Products include NRE/NRO/3-in-1 Accounts, Fixed Deposits, Foreign Currency Deposits, Investments, and Loans.  

**Remittances**  
- Transfer money abroad or to India using ICICI Bank Wire transfer, SmartWire, Money2India, and Money2World platforms.  
- Secure, fast remittances with competitive exchange rates.  

**Forex Prepaid Card**  
- Range of forex prepaid cards available for international travel.  

**Loan Against Shares**  
- Loan up to 50% of share value, with a limit of Rs. 20 Lakhs.  
- Interest charged only on the amount used.  

**Loan Against Mutual Funds**  
- Loan up to Rs. 20 Lakhs on Equity Funds.  
- Loan up to Rs. 1 crore on Debt Funds.  

**Power Pay Account**  
- Titanium/Platinum Debit Card with unlimited ATM access.  
- Access to 3-in-1 Salary, Demat & Trading account with first-year AMC waiver.  
- Extend salary account benefits to up to five family members.  
- Waiver of charges on online transactions via NEFT/RTGS/IMPS.  
- Complimentary air and personal accident insurance with no hidden clauses.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Salary account benefits extend to family members with minimum balance waiver and insurance cover.  
- Multiple loan products with personalized financial assessment.  
- NRI banking services include easy account conversion, tax benefits, and global digital management.  
- Remittance services offer secure and competitive international money transfers.  
- Loan against shares and mutual funds available without selling assets, with specific loan limits and interest on utilized amounts only.  
- Power Pay Account offers premium debit cards, unlimited ATM access, and insurance benefits.  
- Family banking allows benefit extension to multiple family members with added privileges like lounge access.
