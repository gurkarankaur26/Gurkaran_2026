TITLE: Foreign Exchange Rates and Services  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking - Foreign Exchange  
TAGS: Forex, Currency Conversion, Telegraphic Transfer, Foreign Currency, Exchange Rates  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides foreign exchange services including currency conversion, forex card rates, and telegraphic transfers (TT). The bank offers competitive card rates for converting foreign currency to Indian Rupees (INR). The Malaysian Ringgit (MYR) currency availability is subject to market conditions and regulatory guidelines at the time of the transaction. Telegraphic Transfer (TT) services are available for both buying and selling foreign currency. Customers are advised to verify the date of the exchange rates displayed; if the date does not match the current date, they should contact their nearest ICICI Bank branch for updated rates. Forex card rates for Authorized Dealer Category II (ADII) and Full Fledged Money Changers (FFMC) for currency notes are also provided by the bank.

KEY POINTS:  
- Card rates apply for foreign currency conversion to INR.  
- MYR currency availability depends on market and regulatory conditions.  
- Telegraphic Transfer (TT) is offered for both buying and selling foreign currency.  
- Customers should verify the date of exchange rates; contact branch if outdated.  
- Forex card rates are available for ADII/FFMC for currency notes.
