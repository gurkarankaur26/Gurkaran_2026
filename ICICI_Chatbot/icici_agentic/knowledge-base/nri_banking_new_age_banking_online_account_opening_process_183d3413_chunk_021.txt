TITLE:  
ICICI Bank NRI Services and Domestic Debit Card Features

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY:  
NRI Banking, Debit Cards, Customer Support

TAGS:  
NRI Banking, Domestic Debit Card, ATM Withdrawals, Customer Care, Document Submission

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers convenient services for NRIs residing in the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE. NRIs can submit their documents by posting them to the local P.O. Box in their respective countries, helping save on courier charges. Alternatively, documents and registration forms can be submitted by visiting any ICICI Bank branch in India.

For any clarifications or additional information, customers can contact ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care or write through the "Email us" option available on the official website. Documents can also be sent directly to the NRI Services Centre in Mumbai or faxed, or submitted via the PO Box service.

Regarding debit cards, ICICI Bank provides both domestic debit cards and ATM cards with the following features:  
- Both cards allow cash withdrawals at ICICI Bank ATMs across India.  
- The domestic debit card additionally permits cash withdrawals at other banks’ VISA-enabled ATMs in India.  
- The domestic debit card can be used for purchase transactions at all VISA-enabled merchant locations within India.

KEY POINTS:  
- NRIs in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can submit documents via local P.O. Boxes to avoid courier charges.  
- Documents and registration forms can also be submitted at any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- 24-hour Customer Care support is available for queries.  
- Domestic debit card and ATM card allow cash withdrawals at ICICI Bank ATMs.  
- Domestic debit card also allows cash withdrawals at other banks’ VISA-enabled ATMs and purchases at VISA-enabled merchants in India.
