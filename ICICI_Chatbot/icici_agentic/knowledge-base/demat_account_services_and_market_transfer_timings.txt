TITLE: Demat Account Services and Market Transfer Timings  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Demat Account Services  
TAGS: Demat Account, Market Transfer, E-Instruction, Nominee, Client Master List, Account Update, ICICI Bank iMobile  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides various services related to Demat accounts, including market transfers, inter-depository transfers, account updates, and nominee additions. Customers can manage their Demat accounts conveniently through Net Banking and the ICICI Bank iMobile app.

**Market Transfer Timings:**  
- Monday to Friday: 6 p.m.*  
- Saturday: 2 p.m.*  

**Inter Depository Transfer Timings:**  
- Monday to Friday: 4 p.m.*  
- Saturday: 11 a.m.*  

*Note: For market transfers, e-Instructions must be submitted at least one hour prior to the NSDL pay-in deadline time. E-Instructions cannot be cancelled once submitted through Net Banking.

**Available Demat Account Services:**  
- Request closure of Demat account  
- Add a nominee to the Demat account  
- Update income range in the Demat account  
- Request Client Master List (CML) online or by post  
- Update mobile number, telephone number, and email ID  

**Access through ICICI Bank iMobile app:**  
All the above services can be accessed conveniently via the ICICI Bank iMobile app.

KEY POINTS:  
- Market transfer deadlines: Mon-Fri 6 p.m., Sat 2 p.m.  
- Inter Depository transfer deadlines: Mon-Fri 4 p.m., Sat 11 a.m.  
- E-Instructions must be submitted at least 1 hour before NSDL pay-in deadline  
- E-Instructions cannot be cancelled after submission via Net Banking  
- Services include Demat account closure, nominee addition, income update, CML request, and contact detail updates  
- Services accessible via ICICI Bank iMobile app
