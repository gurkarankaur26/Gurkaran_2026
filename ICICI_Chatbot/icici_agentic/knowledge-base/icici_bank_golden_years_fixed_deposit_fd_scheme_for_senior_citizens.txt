TITLE: ICICI Bank Golden Years Fixed Deposit (FD) Scheme for Senior Citizens  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Fixed Deposits, Senior Citizens Banking  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, Senior Citizens, Interest Rates, ICICI Bank, Savings, Investment  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank has introduced a special Fixed Deposit scheme named ‘ICICI Bank Golden Years FD’ exclusively for senior citizens. This scheme offers an attractive interest rate of 7.2% per annum for deposits up to ₹2 crore with a tenure ranging from more than 5 years to 10 years. The scheme is available for resident senior citizens and applies to both new fixed deposits and renewals of existing fixed deposits.

Key features of the ICICI Bank Golden Years FD scheme include:  
- Interest Rate: 7.2% per annum  
- Deposit Amount: Up to ₹2 crore  
- Tenure: More than 5 years (5 years 1 day) up to 10 years  
- Eligibility: Resident senior citizens  
- Availability Period: From 20 May 2020 to 30 September 2020  
- Interest rate benefit: 80 basis points higher than the rate applicable to the general public for the same deposit amount and tenure, and 30 basis points higher than the Bank’s previous senior citizen FD rates.  

Customers can open the FD conveniently through ICICI Bank’s Net Banking and mobile banking platforms or by visiting the nearest ICICI Bank branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Special FD scheme for senior citizens named ‘Golden Years FD’

- Tenure between 5 years 1 day and 10 years  
- Available from 20 May 2020 to 30 September 2020  
- Applicable for resident senior citizens only

- Can be opened online via Net Banking or iMobile app, or at branches
