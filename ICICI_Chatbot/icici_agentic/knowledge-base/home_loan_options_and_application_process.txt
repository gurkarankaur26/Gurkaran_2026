CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Home Loans with two types of interest rates: fixed and floating.  

- **Fixed Interest Rate Home Loan:**  
  The interest rate remains constant throughout the loan tenure, providing stability and predictable EMI payments. This option is suitable for borrowers who prefer consistent repayment amounts. Typically, fixed interest rates are higher than floating rates.  

- **Floating Interest Rate Home Loan:**  
  The interest rate varies based on market conditions and is linked to the lender’s base rate or the RBI’s Repo Rate. This means the EMI can increase or decrease during the loan tenure. This option is ideal for borrowers willing to accept fluctuations in repayments to potentially benefit from lower interest rates.  

**Home Loan Application Process:**  
- Check your eligibility before applying.  
- Decide on the type of property and the kind of Home Loan you want.  
- Select the loan amount and tenure.  
- Submit the required documents.  
- Use ICICI Bank’s Net Banking or iMobile app to manage and track your Home Loan application efficiently.  

**FAQs:**  
1. **Can I track the progress of my Home Loan application?**  
   Yes, you can track your application status via the iMobile app, Net Banking, or by contacting ICICI Bank Customer Care for real-time updates.  

2. **Are there any prepayment charges for a Home Loan?**  
   Prepayment charges depend on the loan type and interest rate. Home Loans with floating interest rates do not have any prepayment charges.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Fixed interest rate offers stable EMIs but usually at a higher rate.  
- Floating interest rate varies with market conditions, affecting EMI amounts.  
- Eligibility check and document submission are essential steps before applying.  
- Application progress can be tracked online or via customer care.  
- No prepayment charges on floating rate Home Loans.
