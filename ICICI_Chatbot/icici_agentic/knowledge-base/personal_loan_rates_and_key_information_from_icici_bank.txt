TITLE: Personal Loan Rates and Key Information from ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Interest Rates, Loan Charges, Eligibility, Loan Tenure, EMI, Prepayment, Processing Fee  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers personal loans with competitive interest rates and flexible terms to help customers manage financial emergencies and other personal expenses such as weddings, vacations, medical emergencies, and debt clearance. Personal loans cannot be used for speculative activities like investments or gambling.

**Interest Rates:**  
- Personal Loan: Starting from 10.85% p.a.  
- Flexicash (Overdraft facility for salary account holders): 12.35% to 14.10% p.a.*  

**Loan Amount:**  
- Minimum: Rs. 50,000  
- Maximum: Rs. 50 Lakhs  

**Loan Tenure:**  
- 12 months to 72 months  

**Eligibility:**  
- Minimum monthly salary: Rs. 30,000  

**Charges and Fees:**  
- Loan Processing / Origination Charges: Up to 2.00% of loan amount plus applicable taxes (Processing Fee can be up to 2.50% in some cases)  
- Prepayment Charges:  
  - 3% on principal outstanding amount if prepaid before 12 EMIs  
  - Nil after 12 EMIs paid  
  - For Business Loans (<= 50 Lakhs) booked under code ‘UP’, prepayment charges are NIL if the first EMI is paid (applicable only for Micro and Small enterprises, subject to URC submission; not applicable for Medium enterprises)  
- Penal Charges on Late Payment: 5% per annum on overdue EMI until repayment  
- Repayment Mode Swap Charges: ₹500 per transaction plus applicable taxes  
- Loan Cancellation Charges:  
  - NIL within Free Look / Cooling off period (15 days from disbursement); processing fee non-refundable  
  - Cancellation post cooling period and before 1st EMI: Proportionate APR with max cap of ₹3,000 (for Digital Lending)  
  - Cancellation Fee of ₹3,000 applicable for cancellations other than Digital Lending  
- EMI Bounce Charges: ₹500 per bounce plus applicable taxes  
- Non Maintenance of Mode of Payment (NMMP Charges): ₹800 plus applicable taxes/statutory levies, charged monthly if repayment mode is inactive or irregular  

**Important Notes:**  
- Personal loans are versatile and can be used for various personal financial needs but not for speculative activities.  
- The interest rates and charges may vary based on the loan type and borrower profile.  
- Prepayment benefits encourage early repayment after 12 EMIs without penalty.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal loan interest rates start at 10.85% p.a.  
- Loan amount ranges from Rs. 50,000 to Rs. 50 Lakhs  
- Tenure available from 12 to 72 months  
- Minimum salary eligibility: Rs. 30,000 per month  
- Processing fee up to 2.50% plus taxes  
- Prepayment charges waived after 12 EMIs  
- Late payment penalty: 5% p.a. on overdue EMI  
- EMI bounce charges: ₹500 plus taxes  
- Cancellation charges vary depending on timing and mode of loan application  
- Flexicash overdraft facility available for salary account holders at 12.35% to 14.10% p.a.
