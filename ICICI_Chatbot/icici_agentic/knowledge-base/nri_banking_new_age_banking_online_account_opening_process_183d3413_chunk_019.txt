CONTENT:  
**Repatriation of Funds from NRO Account:**  
- Proceeds from the sale of property can be repatriated from the NRO account subject to documentation and limits.  
- Required documents include:  
  - Property sale deed and purchase deed copies.  
  - If property was acquired as gift/inheritance, submit gift deed or Will/Succession certificate/Certificate of probate/Legal Heirship certificate.  
  - Documents in languages other than English or Hindi must be submitted with a notarized translated copy and vernacular declaration.  
- The repatriation amount should not exceed the sales proceeds and must be within USD 1,000,000 per financial year.

**PAN Requirements:**  
- From April 1, 2010, PAN (Permanent Account Number) is mandatory for NRIs to avail benefits under the Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA).  
- PAN submission is essential for:  
  - Lower tax deduction at source (TDS).  
  - Repatriation of funds from NRO accounts.  
  - Receiving TDS certificates and tax credit in the tax passbook.

**NRO Savings Account Opening:**  
- NRO Savings Account can be opened online.  
- After applying online, a customer service representative will contact the applicant within 15 minutes to assist with account opening.

**ATM/Debit Card Services:**  
- Request for ATM/Debit Card can be made online via the "Service Request" option in Net Banking.  
- Alternatively, card requests can be made by contacting ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care.  
- Request forms can be downloaded, filled, and mailed to:  
  ICICI Bank Limited,  
  RPC Mumbai,  
  5th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates,  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali,  
  Landmark: Next to Chandivali Studio, Opp MHADA,  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai - 400072, INDIA.  
- NRIs in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can post documents to local P.O. Boxes to save courier charges.  
- Card requests can also be submitted at any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- Applicable service charges will apply.

**ATM/Debit Card Deactivation (Hotlisting):**  
- Cardholders can request deactivation online via Net Banking "Service Request" option or by calling 24-hour Customer Care.  
- Requests can also be submitted at any ICICI Bank branch in India.

**Funds Transfer:**  
- Online funds transfer is available for:  
  - Other ICICI Bank accounts  
  - Non-ICICI Bank accounts  
  - Card-to-Card transfers  
- Payee registration is required before initiating transfers.

KEY POINTS:  
- Repatriation limit from NRO account: USD 1,000,000 per financial year.  
- Mandatory PAN for NRIs from April 1, 2010, for tax benefits and repatriation.  
- NRO Savings Account can be opened online with quick assistance.  
- ATM/Debit Card requests and deactivation available online, via customer care, by mail, or at branches.  
- NRIs in select countries can use local P.O. Boxes for document submission.  
- Funds transfer requires payee registration and can be done online.
