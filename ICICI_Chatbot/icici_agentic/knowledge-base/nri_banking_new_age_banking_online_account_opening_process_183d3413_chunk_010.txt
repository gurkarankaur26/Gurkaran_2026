TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Nomination Facility and Account Closure Process

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY:  
Banking Services, Account Management, Nomination, Account Closure

TAGS:  
Nomination, Account Closure, Nominee Update, Joint Account, Minor Nominee, Customer Care, NRI Services

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers an enhanced nomination facility allowing account holders to nominate up to four individuals for their accounts or lockers, with the total nomination amount not exceeding 100%. This facility applies to both existing and newly opened accounts, including joint accounts, where all account holders must submit the nomination update request.

**Nomination Facility Details:**  
- Up to 4 nominees can be assigned per account or locker.  
- Nomination can be made in favor of minors; however, a guardian must be appointed to receive funds on behalf of the minor until they reach majority.  
- Only individuals can be nominees; entities such as HUFs, partnership firms, body corporates, trusts, and societies are not eligible.  
- An account holder cannot be a nominee in the same account.  
- Mandate holders can be nominees.  
- Different nominees can be assigned for different accounts.  
- To add, delete, or update nominees, submit a ‘Nominee Updation Form’ signed by all account holders.  
- Nominee updation requests can be submitted through various modes (details available on the bank’s website or branch).

**Account Closure Process:**  
- To close an account, download and fill the 'Account Closure' request form and mail it to:  
  ICICI Bank Limited,  
  RPC Mumbai,  
  5th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates,  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali,  
  Landmark: Next to Chandivali Studio, Opp MHADA,  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai - 400072, INDIA.  
- If the account is inactive or dormant, activate it first by submitting the account activation request form.  
- NRIs residing in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can send documents to local P.O. Boxes to save courier charges.  
- Alternatively, visit any ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the closure request.  
- For branch locations, refer to the updated list on the ICICI Bank website.  
- Charges applicable as per the bank’s service charges.  

**Customer Support:**  
- For clarifications or more information, contact ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care or use the "Email us" option on the official website www.icicibank.in.  
- Customers can register under the Do Not Call (DNC) registry via the bank’s website.

KEY POINTS:  
- Nomination facility allows up to 4 nominees per account/locker.  
- Nominees must be individuals; minors can be nominated with a guardian appointed.  
- Joint account nomination updates require signatures of all account holders.  
- Account closure requires submission of a specific form; dormant accounts must be activated first.  
- NRIs have convenient options for submitting closure requests via local P.O. Boxes.  
- Customer support available 24/7 via phone and email.  
- Do Not Call registration available online.
