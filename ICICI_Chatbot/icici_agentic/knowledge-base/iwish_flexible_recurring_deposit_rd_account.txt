TITLE: iWish Flexible Recurring Deposit (RD) Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Savings & Deposits  
TAGS: Recurring Deposit, Flexible RD, Interest Rates, Senior Citizens, Premature Withdrawal, Savings Goals  

CONTENT:  
The iWish Flexible Recurring Deposit (RD) Account from ICICI Bank offers a convenient and flexible way to save money without the rigid conditions of a traditional RD. It allows you to save at your own pace and provides several benefits tailored to meet your financial goals.

Key Features:  
- No mandatory monthly funding: You can skip monthly payments without incurring any penalties.  
- Multiple accounts: You can open and fund several iWish flexible RD accounts simultaneously.  
- Easy funding: Deposit amounts can be transferred directly from your ICICI Savings Account via Internet Banking.  
- Maturity payout: The accumulated funds are transferred back to your Savings Account at the end of the maturity period.  
- Premature closure: You can close your iWish RD account before maturity in case of emergencies. Interest will be calculated based on the actual duration the funds were held, not the original tenure.  

Interest Rates:  
- Regular customers: 3.50% to 5.35% per annum, depending on the tenure chosen.  
- Senior citizens: 4.00% to 5.85% per annum, depending on the tenure.  

To calculate the exact interest rate, maturity amount, and savings required to achieve your goal, ICICI Bank provides an automated iWish RD calculator. This tool helps you plan your savings effectively.

Benefits:  
- Flexibility in monthly contributions without penalties.  
- Control over multiple savings goals with multiple accounts.  
- Convenient online funding and maturity payout.  
- Competitive interest rates with additional benefits for senior citizens.  
- Option for premature withdrawal with interest calculated on actual tenure.
