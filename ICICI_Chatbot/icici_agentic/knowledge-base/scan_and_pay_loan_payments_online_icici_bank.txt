TITLE: Scan and Pay Loan Payments Online - ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans, Digital Payments  
TAGS: Loan Payment, UPI, QR Code, Digital Banking, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to pay your loan outstanding amount using the Scan and Pay feature. Customers can scan the QR code provided by ICICI Bank through any UPI-enabled banking app or the BHIM app to make instant loan payments. This digital payment method simplifies the loan repayment process, making it quick and hassle-free without the need for cash or cheque deposits.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan payments can be made by scanning the ICICI Bank QR code via any UPI-enabled app or BHIM app.  
- Instant and secure payment method for loan outstanding amounts.  
- Eliminates the need for physical visits or cheque payments.  
- Available for all ICICI Bank loan customers.
