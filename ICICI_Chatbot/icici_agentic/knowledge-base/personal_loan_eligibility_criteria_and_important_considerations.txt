TITLE: Personal Loan Eligibility Criteria and Important Considerations  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Eligibility, Income, Work Experience, Liabilities, Interest Rates, Repayment  

CONTENT:  
When applying for a Personal Loan, banks consider several factors to determine your eligibility and offer favourable terms. Understanding these criteria can help you improve your chances of approval and negotiate better interest rates and repayment options.

**Occupation and Work Experience Requirements:**  
- **Salaried Professionals:** Minimum 2 years of total work experience with at least 1 year in the current company.  
- **Business Owners:** Minimum 5 years in the current business.  
- **Self-Employed Professionals:** Minimum 3 years of professional experience.  

**Current Liabilities:**  
Banks assess your existing financial obligations such as Home Loans, Education Loans, or other personal loans. They evaluate whether your income is sufficient to cover both your current liabilities and the new Personal Loan repayment.  

**Income and Credit Score:**  
A higher monthly income and a good credit score can help you negotiate for a lower interest rate and more flexible repayment terms.  

**Key Recommendations:**  
- Understand the eligibility criteria before applying.  
- Review your current liabilities and income to ensure you can manage additional loan repayments.  
- Use your financial strengths (income, credit score) to negotiate better loan terms.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum work experience varies by occupation: 2 years for salaried, 5 years for business owners, 3 years for self-employed.  
- Existing liabilities impact loan eligibility and repayment capacity.  
- Higher income and credit score can lead to favourable interest rates.  
- Knowing eligibility criteria helps in negotiating loan terms.
