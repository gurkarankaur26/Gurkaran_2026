SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Canara Robeco mutual fund schemes available for investment. The schemes include different fund categories such as Equity Hybrid, Flexi Cap, Focused, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Manufacturing, and Multi Asset Allocation funds. Each scheme is available in different plans and options, including Direct and Regular plans, with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- Canara Robeco Equity Hybrid Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and Monthly IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Flexi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Focused Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Large and Mid Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Large Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Manufacturing Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Mid Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Multi Asset Allocation Fund (Direct and Regular plans; Growth and IDCW options)  
- Canara Robeco Multi Cap Fund (Direct plan; IDCW option)  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., 118272 for Canara Robeco Equity Hybrid Fund - Direct - Growth). Investors can choose between Growth options, where returns are reinvested, or IDCW options, where periodic income distributions are made.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Canara Robeco mutual fund schemes are available for investment through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes are offered in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).  
- Fund categories cover Equity Hybrid, Flexi Cap, Focused, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Manufacturing, and Multi Asset Allocation.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for easy reference.  
- IDCW options provide periodic income distributions, suitable for investors seeking regular income.  
- Growth options are suitable for investors aiming for capital appreciation through reinvestment of returns.
