CONTENT:  
This document lists various Mirae Asset mutual fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes include different fund categories such as Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Multi Asset Allocation, Healthcare, Consumer, and ETFs. Each scheme is available in different plans: Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg), and in different options: Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

Key Mirae Asset Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- Mirae Asset Focused Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Great Consumer Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Healthcare Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Large & Midcap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Large Cap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Midcap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Multi Asset Allocation Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Multicap Fund (Direct and Regular, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Small Cap Fund (Direct, Growth and IDCW)  
- Mirae Asset Nifty 50 ETF  
- Mirae Asset Nifty Midcap 150 ETF  
- Mirae Asset NYSE FANG Plus ETF (Direct and Regular, Growth)  
- Mirae Asset S&P 500 TOP 50 ETF  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code (e.g., Mirae Asset Focused Fund - Dir - IDCW: 147205). Investors can choose the plan and option based on their investment goals and preferences.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Mirae Asset mutual fund schemes are available through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes are offered in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Options include Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).  
- Fund categories cover Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Multi Asset, Healthcare, Consumer, and ETFs.
