TITLE: ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit (FD) Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Deposits / Fixed Deposits  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, FD, Interest Rates, Premature Withdrawal, ICICI Bank, Banking Products  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit (FD) is a secure investment option offering attractive interest rates for a fixed tenure. Customers can open an FD by visiting any ICICI Bank branch or through their Relationship Manager. The FD tenure ranges from 7 days to 10 years, allowing flexibility based on the investor’s needs.

Interest rates vary depending on the tenure and the amount deposited. Senior citizens are eligible for higher interest rates compared to regular customers. Interest can be paid out monthly, quarterly, or at maturity, based on the customer’s preference.

Premature withdrawal of the FD is allowed but may attract a penalty. To avoid premature withdrawal penalties or for any assistance related to FD, customers are advised to contact their Relationship Manager or visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- FD tenure: 7 days to 10 years  
- Higher interest rates for senior citizens  
- Interest payout options: monthly, quarterly, or at maturity  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with penalty  
- For premature withdrawal or assistance, contact Relationship Manager or visit branch
