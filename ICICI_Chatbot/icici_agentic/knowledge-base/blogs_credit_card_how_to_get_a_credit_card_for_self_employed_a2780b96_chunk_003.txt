TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Credit Cards for Self-Employed Professionals

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Credit Cards

TAGS:  
Self-Employed, Credit Card Eligibility, Instant Platinum Credit Card, Fixed Deposit, Credit Score, Income Proof

CONTENT:  
Self-employed professionals can obtain ICICI Bank credit cards by following certain eligibility criteria and processes. To increase the chances of approval, applicants should maintain a strong credit score, file regular Income Tax Returns (ITRs), keep healthy bank balances, reduce existing liabilities, and submit accurate income documents.

For self-employed individuals who do not have income proof, ICICI Bank offers the Instant Platinum Credit Card. This card can be availed by opening a Fixed Deposit of Rs 20,000 for a minimum tenure of 180 days at any ICICI Bank branch.

KEY POINTS:  
- Maintain a strong credit score and file regular ITRs to improve credit card eligibility.  
- Keep healthy bank balances and reduce existing liabilities.  
- Submit accurate and complete income documents.  
- Self-employed without income proof can get the Instant Platinum Credit Card by opening a Fixed Deposit of Rs 20,000 for at least 180 days.  
- The Fixed Deposit must be opened at an ICICI Bank branch.
