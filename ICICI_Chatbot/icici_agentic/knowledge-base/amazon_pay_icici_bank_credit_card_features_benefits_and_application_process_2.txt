TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Features, Benefits, and Application Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Rewards, Digital Payments, Visa, No Annual Fee, Cashback  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card is one of the fastest-growing co-branded credit cards in India, having crossed two million issuances with over 80% of customers applying digitally. It offers a seamless, rewarding, and convenient payment experience, especially for Amazon users.

**Key Features:**  
- **No joining or annual fee**  
- **Reward Points:**  
  - Amazon Prime members earn 5% reward points on Amazon.in spends  
  - Non-Prime customers earn 3% reward points on Amazon.in spends  
  - 2% reward points on digital categories on Amazon.in such as bill payments, recharges, adding money to Amazon Pay balance, and movie bookings  
  - 2% reward points on spends at Amazon Pay merchants including Swiggy, BookMyShow, Yatra, and others  
  - 1% reward points on all other spends at any merchant location in India where Visa cards are accepted  
- **Reward Points Details:**  
  - One reward point equals one rupee  
  - No upper limit on reward points accumulation  
  - Reward points do not expire  
  - Points can be redeemed on over 16 crore products on Amazon.in and other merchants accepting Amazon Pay  
- **Additional Benefits:**  
  - Fuel surcharge waiver  
  - No cost EMI offers on a large selection of products  
- **Exclusions:** No reward points on fuel, EMI transactions, rent transactions, and gold purchases  
- **Usage:** The card is accepted at more than 4 million merchant locations across India  
- **Reward Credit:** Reward points are credited monthly after the billing cycle to the customer’s Amazon Pay balance  

**Application Process:**  
Customers can apply for the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card via the Amazon.in website or mobile app. The card issuance process is 100% digital, contactless, and paperless. A digital card is provided immediately upon approval, and the physical card is delivered by ICICI Bank within a few days.

KEY POINTS:  
- Fastest co-branded credit card in India to cross 2 million issuances  
- 100% digital application and issuance process  
- No joining or annual fees  
- High reward points for Amazon Prime members (5%) and other Amazon shoppers (3%)  
- Reward points on digital spends and Amazon Pay merchant spends (2%)  
- 1% reward points on all other spends via Visa acceptance  
- Reward points never expire and have no upper limit  
- Fuel surcharge waiver and no cost EMI offers available  
- Reward points credited monthly to Amazon Pay balance  
- Accepted at over 4 million merchant locations in India
