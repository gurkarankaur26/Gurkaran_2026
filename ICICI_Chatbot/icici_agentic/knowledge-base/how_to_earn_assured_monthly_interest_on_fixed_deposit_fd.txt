CONTENT:  
Fixed Deposits (FDs) are secure and reliable investment options offering assured returns through interest payments. By depositing a certain amount for a specific tenure, customers earn interest at predetermined rates, which can be compounded monthly, quarterly, or cumulatively. Opting for a monthly interest payout provides a steady recurring income stream.

**Key Features of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits:**  
- **Tenure:** Flexible tenure ranging from 7 days to 10 years.  
- **Interest Payout Options:** Monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or annual payouts.  
- **Interest Rates:** Vary based on tenure, deposit amount, and age of the investor. Higher principal and longer tenure generally yield higher returns.  
- **Premature Withdrawal:** Allowed but may attract penalties.  

**How to Open an FD with Monthly Interest Payout at ICICI Bank:**  
1. Log in to your ICICI Bank account via Internet Banking or iMobile app.  
2. Navigate to the Fixed Deposits section under 'Investments' or 'Savings'.  
3. Enter the investment amount and select the tenure as per your financial goals.  
4. Choose the interest payout frequency (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or annual).  
5. Review all details carefully before submitting the application.  

**Investment Strategies to Maximise Returns:**  
- **Compare Interest Rates:** Research and compare FD rates across banks to secure the best returns. Rates vary by tenure, deposit amount, and investor age.  
- **Diversify via Laddering:** Spread investments across multiple FDs with staggered maturities to balance liquidity and maximize returns.  
- **Choose Suitable Payout Frequency:** Align interest payout frequency with your cash flow needs.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FD tenure ranges from 7 days to 10 years.  
- Interest payout options include monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and annual.  
- Higher principal and longer tenure increase interest earnings.  
- Premature withdrawal is possible but may incur penalties.  
- Opening an FD can be completed online within 60 seconds via Internet Banking or iMobile app.  
- Diversification and laddering strategies help balance liquidity and returns.  
- Always compare interest rates before investing.
