TITLE: ICICI Bank Instant Platinum Credit Card  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Instant Platinum, Secured Credit Card, Fixed Deposit, Rewards, Contactless Payment, Fuel Savings, Dining Offers  

CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Instant Platinum Credit Card is designed for existing ICICI Bank customers who may not have a credit history or income proof. This secured credit card is backed by a Fixed Deposit (FD) with a minimum value of Rs 20,000, which must be at least 180 days old. The card is issued instantly over the counter at ICICI Bank branches against the FD.

Key features and benefits of the ICICI Bank Instant Platinum Credit Card include:  
- **No Joining or Annual Fee:** The card has zero joining and annual fees, making it cost-effective.  
- **Contactless Technology:** Enables quick and secure payments at retail outlets with built-in contactless payment technology.  
- **Reward Points:** Earn reward points on all card spends, which can be redeemed for cash or other exciting options.  
- **Dining Offers:** Access exclusive dining offers through the ICICI Bank Culinary Treats Program.  
- **Fuel Savings:** Avail a 1% waiver on fuel transactions ranging from ₹400 to ₹4,000 at HPCL fuel pumps, helping reduce daily commute costs.

**Eligibility and Application Process:**  
- The card is primarily for existing ICICI Bank customers with an FD of at least Rs 20,000 held for 180 days.  
- Income proof is generally required for credit card applications; however, existing customers or those with strong credit profiles may have simplified documentation requirements, subject to bank policies.  
- A good credit score improves the chances of approval, eligibility, and may help secure better credit limits.  
- To apply, visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch with your FD details. The card is issued instantly over the counter.

This card is suitable for individuals with limited income or no credit history who want to experience the benefits of a credit card, including convenience, security, and rewards.

KEY POINTS:

- 1% fuel transaction waiver on ₹400 to ₹4,000 at HPCL pumps
