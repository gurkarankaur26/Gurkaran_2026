TITLE: ICICI Bank Personal Loan Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Eligibility, Interest Rates, Fees, EMI, Salaried Employees, Private Company Employees  
CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Personal Loans tailored to salaried individuals, allowing them to select EMIs that comfortably fit their monthly budget. Private company employees are eligible to apply for a Personal Loan; however, loan disbursement is subject to the bank's internal eligibility assessment process.  

The pricing of Personal Loans depends on multiple factors such as the applicant's income, credit profile, and existing financial obligations. Applicable charges may include a processing fee and statutory taxes. There are no additional charges specifically for salaried employees.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal Loans available for salaried individuals including private company employees.  
- Loan disbursement subject to internal eligibility assessment.  
- EMI options designed to fit monthly budgets comfortably.  
- Pricing depends on income, credit profile, and existing obligations.  
- Applicable charges include processing fee and statutory taxes.  
- No extra charges specifically for salaried employees.
