CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers NRI Recurring Deposit (RD) accounts for Non-Resident Indians to help them save regularly and grow their wealth securely. The NRI RD allows customers to deposit a fixed amount every month and earn attractive interest rates over a flexible tenure.  

Key Features:  
- Minimum monthly deposit: ₹5,000  
- Flexible tenure options to suit your financial goals  
- Attractive interest rates applicable on NRE and NRO RD accounts  
- Easy online application process  

Eligibility:  
- Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) holding NRE or NRO accounts with ICICI Bank  

Interest Rates & Tenure:  
- Interest rates vary based on the tenure selected and the type of account (NRE or NRO)  
- Interest is compounded quarterly and credited to the account  
- Tenure options typically range from 6 months to 10 years (specific tenure options and rates to be confirmed at the time of application)  

Benefits:  
- Regular monthly savings discipline  
- Competitive interest rates higher than regular savings accounts  
- Flexible tenure to meet short-term or long-term financial goals  
- Interest earned on NRE RD is tax-free in India  
- Interest earned on NRO RD is subject to applicable taxes as per Indian tax laws  
- Safe and secure investment backed by ICICI Bank  

Process to Open NRI RD:  
1. Ensure you have an NRE or NRO account with ICICI Bank.  
2. Apply online through the ICICI Bank NRI portal or visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch.  
3. Choose the deposit amount (minimum ₹5,000) and tenure.  
4. Complete KYC and documentation as required.  
5. Start monthly deposits and track your RD account online.  

FAQs:  
Q: Can I open an NRI RD without an NRE/NRO account?  
A: No, an NRE or NRO account with ICICI Bank is mandatory to open an NRI RD.  

Q: Is premature withdrawal allowed?  
A: Premature withdrawal is subject to bank’s terms and may attract penalties.  

Q: How is interest paid?  
A: Interest is compounded quarterly and credited to your linked account.  

Q: Are the interest rates fixed?  
A: Interest rates are fixed for the tenure chosen at the time of opening the RD.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum monthly deposit: ₹5,000  
- Flexible tenure options (6 months to 10 years)  
- Attractive, fixed interest rates with quarterly compounding  
- NRE RD interest is tax-free; NRO RD interest is taxable  
- Must have an NRE or NRO account to open an RD  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with penalties  
- Online application and management available
