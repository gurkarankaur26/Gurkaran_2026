CONTENT:  
A Current Account with ICICI Bank is designed primarily for day-to-day business transactions such as making payments, receiving deposits, and managing cash flows. It is ideal for professionals, business owners, institutions, trusts, associations, and government departments who require frequent financial transactions.

Key features of the ICICI Bank Current Account include:  
- No interest is paid on the deposited amount, as the account is meant for transactional purposes rather than savings.  
- There is generally no upper limit on deposits; however, large transactions may be subject to specific bank guidelines, charges, or verification requirements.  
- The account provides financial flexibility with tools such as easy payments, quick overdrafts, and support for international transactions.  
- It simplifies business operations by offering all-inclusive, adaptable, and practical solutions tailored to everyday financial needs.  

Opening a Current Account with ICICI Bank empowers businesses and professionals to manage their finances smoothly and efficiently.

KEY POINTS:  
- Current Accounts do not earn interest.  
- Designed for frequent business transactions.  
- No upper limit on deposits, but large transactions may require verification and incur charges.  
- Offers overdraft facilities and international transaction support.  
- Suitable for professionals, businesses, institutions, trusts, associations, and government departments.
