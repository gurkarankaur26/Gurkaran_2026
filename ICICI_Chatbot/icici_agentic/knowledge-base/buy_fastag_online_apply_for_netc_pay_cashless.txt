CONTENT:  
The National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) programme, launched by NPCI, NHAI, and IHMCL, aims to automate electronic toll collection across India to reduce traffic congestion and create an efficient interoperable system covering all toll plazas. The Government of India has mandated electronic toll payments on all national highway toll plazas, with plans to extend this mandate to other toll plazas. Vehicles traveling through toll plazas must pay toll charges using FASTag.

FASTag is an RFID-enabled device that facilitates electronic toll payments directly from a prepaid account linked to the tag. The NETC programme is currently operational at over 650 toll plazas nationwide. FASTag enables cashless payments, saving time and fuel by eliminating the need to stop at toll booths. Additionally, many parking facilities across India are adopting FASTag for contactless parking payments.

ICICI Bank offers FASTag as part of the NETC initiative under the guidelines of NHAI and IHMCL. The ICICI Bank FASTag is easy to use and linked to a prepaid account from which toll charges are automatically deducted using RFID technology.
