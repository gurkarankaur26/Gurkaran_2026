TITLE:  
Motilal Oswal and Nippon India Mutual Funds Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Motilal Oswal, Nippon India, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Multi Cap, Index Funds, Hybrid Funds, Growth, IDCW, Investment  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a structured overview of various mutual fund schemes offered by Motilal Oswal and Nippon India, available through ICICI Bank. The schemes include different fund categories such as Large Cap, Mid Cap, Multi Cap, Index Funds, and Hybrid Funds with options for Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW).

Motilal Oswal Mutual Funds:  
- Large Cap Funds: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Mid Cap Funds: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Multi Cap Funds: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Nasdaq 100 ETF and FOF (Fund of Funds): Growth options available.  
- Nifty Index Funds: Including Nifty 500, Nifty India Defence, Nifty Microcap 250, Nifty Midcap 150, and Nifty Smallcap 250 Index Funds with Growth options.  
- S&P 500 Index Fund: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth option.  
- Small Cap Funds: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  

Nippon India Mutual Funds:  
- Aggressive Hybrid Fund: Available in Direct plan with Growth, IDCW, Monthly IDCW, and Quarterly IDCW options.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple fund categories to suit different investment goals: Large Cap, Mid Cap, Multi Cap, Small Cap, Index Funds, and Hybrid Funds.  
- Options available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) and Growth options provided for most funds.  
- Specific funds track major indices such as Nifty 500, Nifty Midcap 150, and S&P 500.  
- Hybrid funds like Nippon India Aggressive Hybrid Fund offer diversified investment with periodic income options (monthly, quarterly IDCW).  
- Suitable for investors seeking diversified equity and hybrid mutual fund investments through ICICI Bank.
