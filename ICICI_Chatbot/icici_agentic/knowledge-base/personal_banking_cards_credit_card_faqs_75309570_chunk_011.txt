CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of services and support for its Credit Card holders, including personalised concierge services, fee structures, cancellation procedures, and important terms and conditions.

**i-Assist Concierge Service:**  
- Available exclusively to select ICICI Bank Credit Card holders.  
- Provides personalised assistance for travel bookings, dining and event reservations, emergency help, medical support, and lifestyle requests.  
- Functions as a personal assistant through the Bank’s authorised partner, with services varying by Credit Card variant.  
- Available 24x7, 365 days a year.  
- Contact numbers:  
  - Toll-free from MTNL/BSNL lines: 1800 26 70731  
  - Standard call charges apply: 022 6787 2016  

**Credit Card Cancellation:**  
- Before cancellation, ensure all outstanding dues are cleared.  
- Redeem any accumulated reward points prior to cancellation as points will lapse after closure.  
- Cancellation can be requested by calling Customer Care at 1800 1080 from your registered mobile number.  
- Alternatively, cancellation can be done online via ICICI Bank’s website under Personal > Cards > Credit Cards > Manage Cards > Credit Card Cancellation.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- Rent Payment Fee: A fee of 1% + GST is charged on all rent payment transactions made using ICICI Bank Credit Cards.  
- This 1% rent payment fee is charged separately and is in addition to any platform or convenience fees imposed by the merchant.  
- IGST (Integrated Goods and Services Tax) is applicable on financial services such as interest, processing fees, and late payment charges. It appears as a tax component on eligible service charges in the Credit Card statement, as per government regulations.  

**Terms and Conditions:**  
- MITC (Most Important Terms and Conditions) outlines key terms for ICICI Bank Credit Cards.  
- Detailed Terms & Conditions and FAQs are available on the ICICI Bank website under the Credit Card product section.  
- An e-Welcome Kit is provided digitally, including the Membership Guide, Terms & Conditions, and MITC in downloadable PDF format for easy access.  

**Other Important Information:**  
- Email address updates can be done via the iMobile app (Cards & Forex > Select Credit Card > Manage) or Net Banking (Cards > Credit Cards > Manage Your Credit Card).  
- Credit Cards are delivered only to the registered mailing address or the nearest ICICI Bank Branch.  
- If unavailable at the registered address, card delivery can be authorised to a family member with a valid ID proof and an authorisation letter.  

KEY POINTS:  
- i-Assist concierge service offers 24x7 personalised support for select Credit Card holders.  
- Rent payment transactions incur a 1% + GST fee, separate from merchant fees.  
- IGST is applicable on financial service charges and appears on statements.  
- Credit Card cancellation requires clearing dues and redeeming reward points; can be done via phone or online.  
- MITC and detailed terms are accessible online; e-Welcome Kit is provided digitally.  
- Email updates and card delivery have specific processes to ensure security and convenience.
