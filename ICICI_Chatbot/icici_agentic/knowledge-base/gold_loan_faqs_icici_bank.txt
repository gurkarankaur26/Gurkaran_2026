CONTENT:  
A Gold Loan is a secured loan offered by ICICI Bank against the pledge of your gold ornaments. The loan amount depends on the purity and weight of the gold pledged. Once the loan is fully repaid, the pledged gold is returned in the same condition.

**Eligibility:**  
- Indian citizens aged 18 to 85 years  
- Salaried, self-employed, business owners, or agriculturalists  
- Must own gold ornaments with purity between 18 to 22 carats  
- Gold coins accepted up to 50 grams only at borrower level  
- Valid identity and address proof required (Aadhaar, Voter ID, Passport, Driving Licence)  
- Two passport-size photographs  
- For agricultural loans above ₹1 lakh, proof of landholding is required  
- PAN card mandatory for loan amounts above ₹5 lakh  
- Only resident Indians are eligible  

**Loan Amount:**  
- Minimum ₹3 lakh for new-to-bank customers in metro locations  
- Minimum ₹2 lakh in non-metro locations  
- Maximum up to ₹2 crore depending on customer profile  
- Loan amount is based on the prevailing gold rate, purity, and weight of the pledged gold  

**Application Process:**  
- Start application online via ICICI Bank website or iMobile app  
- Visit nearest branch for gold valuation and loan disbursement  
- Gold is securely stored by the bank until loan repayment  

**Repayment and Tenure:**  
- Multiple repayment options: NEFT, Cheque, RIB  
- Tenure limits and maximum Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio of 75% as per RBI guidelines  
- If gold value drops significantly, additional margin or repayment may be requested to maintain LTV  
- Loan can be repaid and consolidated subject to bank policies  
- Once previous loan is closed and gold released, reapplication with the same gold is allowed  

**Fees and Interest:**  
- Transparent fees and charges  
- Interest paid on Gold Loan is not tax-deductible as it is a secured personal loan  

**Additional Information:**  
- Gold Loan is issued in the name of a single applicant; nomination for claim purposes is allowed  
- By repaying ICICI Bank Gold Loan first, the released gold can be pledged with another bank  

**Benefits:**  
- Quick disbursal  
- Minimal documentation  
- Safety of pledged jewellery  
- Flexible repayment options  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan secured against gold ornaments (18-22 carats) and coins (up to 50g)  
- Eligibility: Indian residents aged 18-85, no income proof required  
- Loan amount: ₹2 lakh to ₹2 crore based on gold value and customer profile  
- Maximum LTV ratio: 75% as per RBI guidelines  
- Multiple repayment options and quick disbursal  
- PAN mandatory for loans above ₹5 lakh  
- Interest on Gold Loan is not tax-deductible  
- Reapplication allowed after full repayment and gold release
