TITLE: How to Convert Saving Account to Salary Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Account Services  
TAGS: Savings Account, Salary Account, Account Conversion, ICICI Bank, Eligibility, Documents, Fees, Benefits  

CONTENT:  
If you have a Savings Account and have recently started receiving a regular salary, converting your Savings Account to a Salary Account with ICICI Bank can offer several benefits such as higher transaction limits, waived minimum balance fees, and reduced fees for various banking services.

**Eligibility:**  
- You must be employed with a company that has a tie-up with ICICI Bank for Salary Accounts.  
- Your salary should meet the minimum criteria set by the bank (varies by employer tie-up).  

**Conversion Process:**  
1. **Check Eligibility:** Confirm if your employer has a tie-up with ICICI Bank for Salary Accounts.  
2. **Application Form:** Obtain the conversion form either by downloading it from ICICI Bank’s website or visiting the nearest branch.  
3. **Fill the Form:** Provide accurate details including your existing ICICI Bank Savings Account number, salary details, and employment information.  
4. **Submit Documents:** Submit the filled form along with necessary documents such as salary slips, employment ID card, and valid ID proof. Submission can be done at the branch or online if available.  
5. **HR Submission:** In many cases, you need to submit the form to your HR department who will coordinate with ICICI Bank for the conversion.  
6. **Online Conversion:** Eligible customers can also convert their accounts via ICICI Bank Net Banking or the iMobile app.  
7. **Special Cases:** If your employer does not have a tie-up with ICICI Bank, you can request your HR to contact ICICI Bank representatives. For special considerations, visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch or contact Customer Care.  

**Documents Required:**  
- Salary slips  
- Employment ID card  
- Valid ID proof (such as Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)  
- Filled application form  

**Benefits of Salary Account over Savings Account:**  
- No minimum balance requirement (waived maintenance fees)  
- Higher transaction limits  
- Reduced or waived fees on various banking services  
- Access to exclusive salary account offers and benefits  

KEY POINTS:  
- Conversion requires employer tie-up with ICICI Bank.  
- Application can be submitted via HR, branch, online banking, or mobile app.  
- Essential documents include salary slips, employment ID, and ID proof.  
- Salary Accounts offer waived minimum balance fees and higher transaction limits.  
- For employers without tie-ups, HR can coordinate with ICICI Bank for possible solutions.
