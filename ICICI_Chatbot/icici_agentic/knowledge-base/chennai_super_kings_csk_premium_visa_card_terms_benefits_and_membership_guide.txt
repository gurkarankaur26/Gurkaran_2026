CONTENT:  
The Chennai Super Kings (CSK) Premium Visa Card is a co-branded credit card offered by ICICI Bank, designed for cricket fans and cardholders seeking exclusive benefits related to the Chennai Super Kings franchise. This card provides a range of privileges including e-membership benefits, reward points, and special offers.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- Exclusive e-membership access to Chennai Super Kings events and merchandise.  
- Reward points on every purchase which can be redeemed for CSK merchandise and other rewards.  
- Special discounts and offers on ticket bookings and official CSK merchandise.  
- Contactless payment feature for quick and secure transactions.  
- Complimentary insurance coverage on card usage.  

Eligibility:  
- Applicant must be an Indian resident aged 18 years or above.  
- Must meet ICICI Bank’s standard credit card eligibility criteria including income and credit score requirements.  

Fees and Charges:  
- Annual Fee: As specified by ICICI Bank at the time of application (subject to change).  
- Interest Rate: Standard interest rates applicable on revolving credit balances as per ICICI Bank’s prevailing rates.  
- Late Payment Charges and other fees as per ICICI Bank’s credit card policy.  

Application Process:  
- Apply online through ICICI Bank’s official website or visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch.  
- Submit required KYC documents and income proof.  
- Upon approval, the card will be dispatched to the registered address.  

FAQs:  
Q: How can I redeem reward points earned on the CSK Premium Visa Card?  
A: Reward points can be redeemed through ICICI Bank’s rewards portal for CSK merchandise, vouchers, or other partner offers.  

Q: Is there any joining fee for the CSK Premium Visa Card?  
A: The joining fee and annual fee details are provided during the application process and may vary.  

Q: Can I convert my reward points into e-membership benefits?  
A: E-membership benefits are provided as part of the card privileges and may not require conversion of points.  

Q: What is the validity of the e-membership?  
A: The e-membership validity aligns with the card validity and is subject to renewal upon card renewal.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Co-branded credit card with exclusive CSK benefits  
- Reward points redeemable for merchandise and offers  
- Eligibility: Indian residents aged 18+ meeting ICICI Bank criteria  
- Annual fee and interest rates as per ICICI Bank policies  
- E-membership access included with card benefits  
- Application via online or branch with KYC and income proof
