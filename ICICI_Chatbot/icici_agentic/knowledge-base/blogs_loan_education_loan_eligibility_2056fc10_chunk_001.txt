CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Education Loans up to Rs 3 crore to support higher studies in India and abroad. The loan process is designed to be hassle-free with minimal paperwork, competitive interest rates, and quick approvals.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- **Nationality:** Indian  
- **Age:** Minimum 16 years; Maximum 65 years at loan maturity (case-to-case basis)  
- **Academic Record:** Proven good academic performance (minimum 50% marks acceptable with other criteria met)  
- **Qualification:** Completed 10+2 (12th Standard) or Diploma  
- **Course Pursuing:** Graduation, Postgraduate Degree, or PG Diploma in professional education  
- **University:** Recognized and accredited institutes in India and overseas  

**Loan Details:**  
- **Loan Amount:** Up to Rs 3 crore  
- **Co-borrowers Allowed:** Father, Mother, Brother, Sister, Spouse (Husband/Wife), Grandparents, Parents-in-law, Maternal/Paternal Uncle  
- **Security:** Property (house or flat), Fixed Deposit (FD), or existing ICICI Bank Home Loan for cross-collateral  

**Application and Documentation:**  
- Confirmed admission letter from the recognized institute  
- Proof of income of parents or co-borrowers to demonstrate repayment capability  
- Other mandatory documents as per bank’s requirements  

**Additional Information:**  
- No minimum salary requirement for parents applying as co-borrowers, but a stable financial background is necessary  
- Applicants can re-apply if initially rejected by addressing application drawbacks and submitting additional documents  
- Even with academic setbacks (minimum 50% marks), loans can be granted if other eligibility criteria are met and co-borrowers support the application  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Education Loan:**  
- High loan amount limit (up to Rs 3 crore)  
- Minimal paperwork and quick approval process  
- Competitive interest rates  
- Flexible co-borrower options  
- Security options including property and fixed deposits  

KEY POINTS:  
- Indian nationals aged 16-65 (at maturity) eligible  
- Minimum qualification: 10+2 or Diploma  
- Loan up to Rs 3 crore for recognized courses in India and abroad  
- Co-borrowers include close family members  
- Security can be property, FD, or home loan collateral  
- No minimum salary for co-borrowers but stable income proof required  
- Applicants with 50% marks can still qualify with other criteria met  
- Re-application allowed with improved documentation
