CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of Personal Loan products designed to meet various financial needs such as medical emergencies, weddings, holidays, and home renovations. These loans provide a convenient and hassle-free way to finance personal expenses with quick processing and flexible options.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Applicants must meet the bank’s specified eligibility requirements (typically related to age, income, employment status, and credit history).  
- Accurate and complete documentation is required to avoid delays or rejection.  

**Required Documents:**  
- Proof of identity  
- Proof of address  
- Income proof (salary slips, bank statements, or IT returns)  
- Other documents as specified by the bank  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Personal Loans:**  
- Quick and hassle-free loan approval and disbursal  
- Flexible loan amounts and tenure options  
- Competitive interest rates tailored to customer profiles  
- Suitable for a variety of personal financial needs  

**Application Process:**  
1. Check eligibility criteria before applying to avoid rejection and protect your credit score.  
2. Gather all necessary documents and ensure information is accurate and complete.  
3. Apply online or at an ICICI Bank branch.  
4. Upon approval, loan amount is disbursed quickly to the customer’s account.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal Loans can be used for emergencies, weddings, holidays, home renovations, and other personal expenses.  
- Eligibility and documentation are critical to ensure smooth processing.  
- Quick disbursal and flexible terms are key benefits.  
- Providing accurate information helps avoid delays and protects credit score.
