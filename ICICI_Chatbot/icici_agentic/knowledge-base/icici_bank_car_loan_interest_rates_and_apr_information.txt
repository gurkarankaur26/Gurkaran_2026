TITLE: ICICI Bank Car Loan Interest Rates and APR Information  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans / Car Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Interest Rates, APR, New Car Loan, Used Car Loan, Loan Fees  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers car loans with varying interest rates depending on whether the vehicle is new or used. The interest rates are structured as follows:

- New Car Loan:  
  - Minimum Interest Rate: 7.8%  
  - Maximum Interest Rate: 11.25%  
  - Mean Interest Rate: 8.8%  

- Used Car Loan:  
  - Minimum Interest Rate: 9.75%  
  - Maximum Interest Rate: 17.01%  
  - Mean Interest Rate: 13.03%  

The Annual Percentage Rate (APR) is used to compute the annualized credit cost, which includes both the interest rate and the loan origination fee. This provides a comprehensive measure of the cost of borrowing.

For customers interested in calculating the APR for their specific loan scenario, ICICI Bank provides an APR calculator tool.

KEY POINTS:  
- New car loan interest rates range from 7.8% to 11.25%, with an average of 8.8%.  
- Used car loan interest rates range from 9.75% to 17.01%, with an average of 13.03%.  
- APR includes interest rate plus loan origination fees, reflecting the true cost of the loan.  
- An APR calculator is available to help customers estimate their loan costs accurately.
