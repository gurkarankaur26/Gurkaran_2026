ICICI Bank offers Two-Wheeler Loans to eligible Indian nationals aged between 21 and 65 years. The loan is available to both salaried and self-employed individuals who meet the bank’s eligibility criteria, income assessment, and documentation requirements. This loan facility is designed to help customers purchase new two-wheelers with convenient repayment options and a streamlined application process.

Key features of the ICICI Bank Two-Wheeler Loan include:  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹30 lakh*  
- Interest rates starting at 10.25%*  
- Eligibility: Indian nationals aged 21 to 65 years, salaried and self-employed  
- Required documentation: Income proof, identity proof, address proof, and other documents as per bank’s policy  
- Application process: Online application with quick processing and approval  

Applicants should review the eligibility conditions, repayment terms, and required documents before applying. The loan offers flexible repayment options tailored to the borrower’s financial situation.
