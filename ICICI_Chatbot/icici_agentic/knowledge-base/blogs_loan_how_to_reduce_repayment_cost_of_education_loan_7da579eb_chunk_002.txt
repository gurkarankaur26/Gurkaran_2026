CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers education loans to support higher education for students, including provisions for interest subsidy under the Government of India’s scheme for Economically Weaker Section (EWS) borrowers. This subsidy is aimed at encouraging higher education by reducing the financial burden on eligible families.

**Interest Subsidy for EWS Category:**  
- The Ministry of Human Resource Development (HRD) has launched an interest subsidy scheme for education loans.  
- Eligibility: Borrowers whose household annual income is less than ₹4.5 lakh qualify for this subsidy.  
- The subsidy applies to loans availed specifically for education purposes.  
- The scheme benefits students, their spouses, children, and legal guardians who take the loan.

**Benefits of Education Loan:**  
- Enables funding for higher studies without immediate financial strain.  
- Interest subsidy reduces the effective cost of borrowing for eligible EWS category borrowers.  
- Helps build a bright career by making education affordable.

**Application Process:**  
- Education loans can be applied for online through ICICI Bank’s official portal.  
- Ensure eligibility criteria are met, especially income limits for subsidy.  
- Use the loan to cover tuition fees, living expenses, and other education-related costs.

KEY POINTS:  
- Interest subsidy available for EWS category borrowers with household income < ₹4.5 lakh.  
- Subsidy launched by HRD Ministry to encourage higher education.  
- Education loans cover students, spouses, children, and legal guardians.  
- Loans can be applied online via ICICI Bank.  
- Helps reduce repayment burden and supports career building.
