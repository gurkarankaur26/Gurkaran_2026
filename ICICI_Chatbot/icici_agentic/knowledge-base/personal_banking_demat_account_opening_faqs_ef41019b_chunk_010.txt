ICICI Bank Demat Account Services – Signature Change, Nomination, and Online Access

CONTENT:  
**Change of Signature in Demat Account**  
- To change the signature on your Demat Account, submit the 'Change of Signature' form at any ICICI Bank branch offering Demat services.  
- The new signature must be attested by the Branch Manager of the branch where your bank account linked to the Demat Account is held.  
- Mention the reason for the change of signature in the form.  
- After submission, the new signature will be updated in the records within 7 working days.  
- The 'Change of Signature' form can be downloaded from the ICICI Bank website.

**Nomination Facility in Demat Account**  
- Nomination can be made by individuals only; non-individuals such as societies, trusts, body corporates, partnership firms, Karta of Hindu Undivided Family, or holders of power of attorney cannot nominate.  
- A nominee can be a minor; in such cases, the guardian’s name and address must be provided.  
- Nomination can be made at the time of account opening or later by submitting a duly filled nomination form at any ICICI Bank Demat servicing branch.  
- Up to three nominees can be appointed per Demat Account.  
- If multiple nominees are appointed, the account holder must specify the percentage share for each nominee, totaling 100%.  
- If no percentage is specified, the default is equal distribution among nominees. Any odd lot will be transferred to the first nominee listed.  
- To opt out of nomination, submit the ‘Declaration Form for opting out of nomination’ at any ICICI Bank branch offering Demat services.

**Online Access to Demat Account**  
- Customers can access their Demat accounts 24/7 via the internet at www.icicibank.com.  
- Available online facilities include:  
  - Viewing transaction statements  
  - Viewing holdings  
  - Viewing account details  
  - Retrieving ISIN for securities  
  - Getting settlement details  
  - Requesting TIFD booklet or Client Master List (CML)  
  - e-Instructions facility to transfer securities  
- To use these online services, customers must register by submitting the prescribed registration form at any ICICI Bank branch offering Demat services.  
- The ICICI Bank Call Centre is also available to handle specific Demat account queries.

KEY POINTS:  
- Signature change requires submission of a form with attestation by Branch Manager; updated within 7 working days.  
- Nomination allowed only for individuals; up to 3 nominees with specified share percentages.  
- Nomination can be opted out by submitting a declaration form.  
- 24/7 online access to Demat account with multiple facilities available after registration.  
- Registration form for online access available at ICICI Bank Demat branches.
