TITLE: Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Savings Account  
TAGS: Basic Savings Account, Zero Balance, Debit Card, ATM Access, No Monthly Charges  

CONTENT:  
The Basic Savings Bank Deposit Account (BSBDA) from ICICI Bank is designed to encourage savings with no monthly maintenance charges. It offers essential banking facilities such as a debit card and access to ATMs across India. This account is ideal for individuals looking for a simple, no-frills savings account with transparent fees and easy access to funds.

Key features include:  
- No minimum balance requirement  
- No monthly charges  
- Free debit card issuance  
- Access to a wide network of ATMs across India  
- Net banking facility for easy account management  

This account is suitable for all individuals who want to start saving without worrying about maintaining a minimum balance or incurring hidden fees.

- No monthly maintenance charges

- ATM access across India  
- Net banking available for account management  
- Transparent fee structure
