CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of business and corporate banking services tailored to meet the needs of businesses of all sizes. The bank provides transparent service charges applicable to various business banking products and services including accounts, payments, loans, trade services, and cash management.

**Service Charges Overview:**  
- **Business Accounts:** Charges applicable on account maintenance, cheque books, demand drafts, and other transactional services.  
- **Payments and Collections:** Fees for bulk payments, electronic collections (I-multipay), and merchant services.  
- **Trade Services:** Service charges related to export-import transactions, including letter of credit issuance, bank guarantees, and foreign exchange transactions.  
- **Business Loans:** Processing fees, prepayment charges, and other applicable fees for various business loan products.  
- **Cash Management:** Charges for cash deposit, withdrawal, and reconciliation services.  
- **Capital Market Services:** Fees related to securities market transactions and custodial services.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- Transparent and competitive fee structure.  
- 24/7 banking support for trade and export-import transactions.  
- Bulk payment solutions to simplify payroll and vendor payments.  
- Dedicated services for capital market and securities transactions.  
- Customized loan products with clear fee disclosures.  

**Eligibility and Process:**  
- Business accounts and services are available to registered businesses, corporates, and SMEs.  
- Documentation requirements vary by product but generally include business registration proof, KYC documents, and financial statements.  
- Service charges are debited as per the agreed schedule and communicated upfront to customers.  

**Important Numbers and Fees:**  
- Specific fees vary by service and account type; customers are advised to refer to the detailed tariff schedule provided by ICICI Bank or contact their relationship manager.  
- Bulk payment and collection services (I-multipay) facilitate easy handling of large volume transactions with applicable charges based on transaction value and volume.  
- Trade finance charges include fees for letters of credit, bank guarantees, and foreign exchange services, calculated as a percentage of the transaction amount or as fixed fees.  

**FAQs:**  
- *How can I know the exact service charges applicable to my business account?*  
  Customers can request the detailed tariff schedule from their ICICI Bank relationship manager or visit the official website.  
- *Are there any charges for bulk payment services?*  
  Yes, charges apply based on transaction volume and value; detailed pricing is available on request.  
- *What are the fees for trade finance services?*  
  Fees vary depending on the type of service (LC issuance, guarantees, forex) and are communicated at the time of service initiation.  
- *Can service charges be waived or negotiated?*  
  Certain charges may be waived or negotiated based on the business relationship and transaction volumes; customers should discuss with their relationship manager.
