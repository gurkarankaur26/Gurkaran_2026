CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Demat accounts for holding securities in electronic form through NSDL and CDSL. The following are the detailed fees and charges applicable for ICICI Bank Demat accounts:

**Demat Account Charges and Fees (NSDL & CDSL):**

1. **Account Opening Charges:**  
   - No charges for opening a Demat account.

2. **Annual Maintenance Charges (AMC):**  
   - Rs. 300 per annum (exclusive of GST) for NSDL Demat accounts.  
   - Rs. 300 per annum (exclusive of GST) for CDSL Demat accounts.

3. **Transaction Charges:**  
   - Debit Transaction (Selling/Transfer of securities): Rs. 15 per transaction (exclusive of GST).  
   - Credit Transaction (Receiving securities): No charges.

4. **Pledge Creation/Closure:**  
   - Rs. 25 per pledge creation or closure (exclusive of GST).

5. **Dematerialization Charges:**  
   - Rs. 25 per certificate (exclusive of GST).

6. **Rematerialization Charges:**  
   - Rs. 25 per certificate (exclusive of GST).

7. **Other Charges:**  
   - Duplicate Demat statement: Rs. 50 per statement (exclusive of GST).  
   - Demat account closure: No charges.

**Eligibility:**  
- Individuals, HUFs, Companies, Trusts, and other entities can open a Demat account with ICICI Bank.

**Process to Open a Demat Account:**  
- Submit the completed account opening form along with KYC documents (PAN card, Aadhaar, address proof, photograph).  
- Complete in-person verification (IPV) as per regulatory guidelines.  
- Sign the agreement and submit the necessary documents.  
- Account activation typically occurs within 3-5 business days.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Demat Account:**  
- Safe and secure holding of securities in electronic form.  
- Easy transfer and settlement of securities.  
- Access to online trading and portfolio management.  
- Nomination facility available.  
- Consolidated account statement for all holdings.  
- Integration with ICICI Bank savings account for seamless fund transfer.

  No, ICICI Bank does not charge for opening a Demat account.  

- **What is the annual maintenance charge?**  
  Rs. 300 per annum (exclusive of GST).  

- **Are there charges for receiving securities?**  
  No, credit transactions are free of charge.  

- **How long does it take to open a Demat account?**  
  Typically 3-5 business days after submission of documents and IPV.  

- **Can NRIs open a Demat account?**  
  Yes, NRIs are eligible to open Demat accounts subject to applicable regulations.
