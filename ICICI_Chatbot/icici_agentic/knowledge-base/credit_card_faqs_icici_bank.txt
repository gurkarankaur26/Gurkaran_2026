CONTENT:  
A Credit Card is a payment instrument issued by ICICI Bank that allows customers to make purchases on credit within a pre-set limit. Customers can repay either the total outstanding amount or the minimum amount due on or before the due date every month.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Credit Cards:**  
- Convenient and secure payments  
- Interest-free credit period (typically 18-48 days depending on billing cycle and purchase date)  
- Rewards points and/or cashback  
- EMI payment options  
- Credit score building  
- Exclusive shopping offers  
- Concierge services  
- Fuel savings and surcharge waiver  
- Domestic and International airport lounge access  
- Partner merchant discounts  
- Zero liability on lost/stolen cards  
- Milestone benefits  

**Types of ICICI Bank Credit Cards:**  
- Lifestyle credit cards  
- Travel credit cards  
- Shopping credit cards

- Business credit cards  
- Student credit cards  
- Cards with airport lounge benefits  
- Entry-level cards  

**Contactless Credit Card:**  
Enables tap-and-pay transactions at NFC-enabled POS terminals. The card does not leave the customer’s hand during payment, reducing fraud risk. Low-value transactions can be performed without entering a PIN as per regulatory limits.

**Secured Credit Card:**  
Issued against a Fixed Deposit placed with the Bank. No lien or partial withdrawal should be on the Fixed Deposit at issuance. Suitable for customers with limited credit history or those wanting to build/repair credit score. Issued only to primary account holders.

**Key Terms:**  
- **Credit Limit:** Maximum amount you can spend on your card at any time, assigned based on eligibility.  
- **Billing Cycle:** Period between two statement dates, typically 28 to 31 days.  
- **Total Amount Due:** Full outstanding amount on your Credit Card for the billing cycle.  
- **Minimum Amount Due:** Minimum sum to be paid by due date to avoid late payment fees; interest applies on remaining balance.  
- **Due Date:** Last date to make payment to avoid late fees and maintain account health.  
- **Interest-Free Period:** Period from transaction date to statement due date when no interest is charged, provided previous balance is cleared in full.  
- **Reward Points:** Loyalty points earned on eligible transactions.  

**Usage:**  
You can use your ICICI Bank Credit Card for payments at merchant retail outlets or online. Transactions are added to your account and reflected in the monthly statement. You repay dues by the due date.
