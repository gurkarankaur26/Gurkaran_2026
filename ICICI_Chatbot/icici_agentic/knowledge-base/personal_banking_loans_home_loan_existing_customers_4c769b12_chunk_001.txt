TITLE:  
Home Loan Conversion Options for Existing ICICI Bank Customers

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Home Loans

TAGS:  
Home Loan, Mortgage Loan, Interest Rate Conversion, Loan Tenure, EMI Reduction, Conversion Fee, Loan Servicing

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers existing home loan customers the option to reduce their current mortgage loan interest rate through a conversion facility. This allows customers to either convert their existing margin to the prevailing margin or switch between floating and fixed interest rates.

**Conversion Facility Details:**  
- Customers can reduce their monthly EMI and/or loan tenure by opting for this conversion.  
- A conversion fee is applicable as follows:  
  - If prepayment charges apply on the loan, the fee is 0.5% of the outstanding loan amount plus applicable GST.  
  - If no prepayment charges apply, a flat fee of ₹1,000 plus applicable GST is charged.  
- For under-construction properties with partially disbursed loans, the conversion fee is calculated on the outstanding loan amount (original loan amount minus repaid amount).

**Process to Avail Conversion:**  
- Contact ICICI Bank Customer Care to inquire about the conversion facility, applicable rates, and schemes.  
- Alternatively, visit any Loan Servicing Branch with the following documents:  
  - Conversion Request Letter (available for download from ICICI Bank website)  
  - Payment for conversion fees via cheque or cash  
  - Proof of identity of applicant and co-applicant (if applicable)  
- Signatures of the applicant and co-applicant are required on all pages of the conversion document.

KEY POINTS:  
- Conversion allows switching between fixed and floating rates or changing the margin to the prevailing rate.  
- Conversion fee: 0.5% of outstanding loan + GST (if prepayment charges apply) or ₹1,000 + GST (if no prepayment charges).  
- Option to reduce EMI and/or loan tenure.  
- Required documents: Conversion request letter, payment for fees, identity proofs.  
- Signatures mandatory on all pages of the conversion document.  
- Contact Customer Care or visit Loan Servicing Branch to initiate the process.
