TITLE: Gold Loan Information and Assistance  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Gold Loan, Loan Eligibility, Interest Rates, Loan Process, Fees, Benefits, FAQs  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans to customers seeking quick and convenient financing against their gold jewelry. The loan is secured by pledging gold ornaments, providing easy access to funds without the need to sell the gold.

Eligibility:  
- Individuals who own gold jewelry and require funds.  
- Must provide valid identification and proof of ownership of gold.  

Loan Features and Benefits:  
- Quick processing and disbursal of loan amount.  
- Competitive interest rates based on the loan amount and tenure.  
- Flexible repayment options.  
- No requirement to sell gold; the gold remains pledged as security.  
- Loan amount sanctioned based on the purity and weight of the gold.  

Interest Rates and Fees:  
- Interest rates vary and are subject to change; customers should check the latest rates on the official website or by contacting the bank.  
- Processing fees and other charges may apply as per the bank’s policy.  

Loan Process:  
1. Submit gold ornaments for evaluation.  
2. Provide necessary KYC documents and proof of ownership.  
3. Bank assesses the gold and sanctions the loan amount.  
4. Loan amount is disbursed to the customer’s account.  
5. Repayment can be made as per agreed terms.  

FAQs:  
- Can I get a loan against any type of gold?  
  Yes, the loan is provided against gold jewelry of specified purity.  
- What happens if I do not repay the loan?  
  The bank has the right to auction the pledged gold to recover dues.  
- Is there any impact on credit score?  
  Timely repayment helps maintain a good credit score; defaults may affect credit history.  

Customer Consent:  
By availing the Gold Loan service, customers provide consent for communication and assistance related to the loan. This consent overrides any registration in Do Not Call (DNC) or National Do Not Call (NDNC) registries, if applicable.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Gold Loan secured by pledging gold jewelry.  
- Quick loan processing and disbursal.  
- Flexible repayment options.  
- Interest rates and fees applicable as per bank policy.  
- Customer consent for communication overrides DNC/NDNC registrations.
