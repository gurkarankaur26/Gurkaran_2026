TITLE: Loan Against Life Insurance Policy – Eligibility, Documents, Process, Benefits and FAQs  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Loan Against Life Insurance, Life Insurance Loan, Eligibility, Interest Rates, Documents, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
A Loan Against Life Insurance Policy allows policyholders to borrow money using the surrender value of their life insurance policy as collateral. This facility provides quick access to funds without liquidating the policy, making it useful during financial emergencies. Eligible policies include Endowment and ULIPs that have completed their lock-in period.

**How It Works:**  
- The insurer calculates the surrender value of the policy.  
- Loan amount sanctioned ranges between 50% and 90% of the surrender value:  
  - 50% for Equity ULIP  
  - 80% for Debt ULIP  
  - 90% for Endowment policies  
- The policy remains active, providing coverage and liquidity benefits while the loan is repaid with interest.  
- The loan is typically provided as an overdraft facility, so interest is charged only on the amount used and for the duration it is used.

**Benefits of Loan Against Life Insurance Policy with ICICI Bank:**  
- Quick approval process, faster than unsecured loans.  
- Lower interest rates due to secured nature of the loan.  
- Policy ownership and benefits continue during the loan tenure.  
- Overdraft facility ensures interest is charged only on utilized amount.

**Important Considerations:**  
- Loan amount is based on surrender value, not the total sum assured.  
- Surrender value is available only after paying premiums for at least three years.  
- Loan amounts typically range from ₹50,000 to ₹5 crore depending on the policy and lender.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- The life insurance policy must be approved by the lender.  
- Available to individuals and partnerships holding eligible policies.  
- The policy must have completed the lock-in period (usually 3 years).  

**Documents Required:**  
- Original life insurance policy document.  
- Identity proof (e.g., Aadhaar card, PAN card).  
- Address proof (e.g., utility bill, passport).  
- Recent passport-sized photographs.  
- Loan application form duly filled and signed.  

**Process to Apply:**  
1. Check eligibility of your life insurance policy for loan against it.  
2. Submit the required documents to ICICI Bank.  
3. Bank evaluates the surrender value and approves the loan amount.  
4. Loan is disbursed as an overdraft facility linked to your policy.  
5. Repay the loan with interest as per agreed terms.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan against life insurance uses surrender value as collateral.  
- Loan amount ranges from 50% to 90% of surrender value depending on policy type.  
- Only policies beyond lock-in period (3 years) are eligible.  
- Loan amount can be between ₹50,000 and ₹5 crore.  
- Interest charged only on utilized overdraft amount.  
- Policyholder retains ownership and benefits during loan tenure.  
- Quick approval and lower interest rates compared to unsecured loans.  
- Required documents include policy papers, ID, address proof, photos, and application form.
