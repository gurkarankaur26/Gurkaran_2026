ICICI Bank Personal Loan and Instant Overdraft Facility (FlexiCash)

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers an Instant Overdraft Facility called FlexiCash exclusively for its Salary Account holders. This facility serves as an alternative to Personal Loans and helps meet both planned and unplanned expenses. Interest is charged only on the utilized amount, and customers can clear outstanding dues at their convenience, paying interest monthly.

Additionally, ICICI Bank provides Personal Loans to both salaried individuals and self-employed professionals, including NRIs, with loan amounts up to Rs 10 lakh for NRIs and up to Rs 1 crore for self-employed professionals.

**Instant Overdraft Facility (FlexiCash):**  
- Available only to ICICI Bank Salary Account holders  
- Interest charged only on the amount utilized  
- Flexible repayment by clearing outstanding dues as per convenience  
- Suitable for planned or unplanned expenses  

**Personal Loan Eligibility and Details:**  

*Salaried Individuals:*  
- Age: 20 to 65 years*  
- Minimum Monthly Income: Rs 30,000  
- Minimum Residence Duration: 1 year  

*Self-employed and Self-employed Professionals:*  
- Minimum Age: 23 years  
- Business Vintage: Current business of 2 years and total business experience of 3 years or more  
- Minimum Turnover: Rs 50 lakh  
- Maximum Loan Amount: Up to Rs 1 crore  
- Tenure: Up to 5 years  

*NRIs:*  
- Personal Loan up to Rs 10 lakh available to meet personal requirements  

**Application Process for Personal Loan:**  
Apply online in 5 easy steps via ICICI Bank’s platform.

For more information or assistance, customers can contact ICICI Bank’s Toll-Free Customer Care Number: 1860-120-7777 or visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch.
