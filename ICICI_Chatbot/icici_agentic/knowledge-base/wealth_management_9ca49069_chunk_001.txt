CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Wealth Management Savings Account designed for individuals and families with significant banking and investment relationships. This account provides personalized banking and investment solutions tailored to high-net-worth customers.

**Programme Eligibility for Individuals (Any one of the below):**  
- Relationship value of ₹50 Lakh (includes Deposits + Account balance + Mutual Fund Value + Demat holdings)  
- Deposits + Account balance of ₹10 Lakh  
- Net monthly salary credit of ₹3 Lakh in ICICI Bank Salary Account  

**Programme Eligibility for Family Banking (Any one of the below):**  
- Family relationship value of ₹75 Lakh (includes Deposits + Account balance + Mutual Fund Value + Demat holdings)  
- Deposits + Account balance of ₹15 Lakh  

**Account Re-classification:**  
If the customer does not meet the average programme eligibility criteria for 12 months, the account will be re-classified accordingly.

**Minimum Balance and Charges:**  
- If programme eligibility criteria are not met, the customer must maintain a monthly average balance of ₹15,000 in the Savings Account to avoid non-maintenance charges.  
- Non-maintenance charges are waived for:  
  - Family Savings Account linked to ICICI Bank Salary Account  
  - Students of select institutes  

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility based on relationship value or deposits and account balance.  
- Separate criteria for individuals and family banking.  
- Re-classification after 12 months of non-eligibility.  
- Minimum balance requirement of ₹15,000 if eligibility criteria are not met.  
- Waiver of non-maintenance charges for linked family salary accounts and select students.
