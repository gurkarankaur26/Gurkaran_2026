TITLE:  
Bandhan Mutual Fund Schemes Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: Bandhan Mutual Fund, Dynamic Bond Fund, Liquid Fund, Gilt Fund, Investment Options, Dividend, Growth, IDCW, Fund Codes  

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Bandhan Mutual Fund schemes, including their types, dividend options, and unique identification codes. The schemes cover a range of investment options such as gilt funds, dynamic bond funds, and liquid funds, available in both direct and regular plans with different dividend payout frequencies and growth options.

Key Schemes and Features:

1. Bandhan CRISIL IBX Gilt Index Funds  
   - Available for April 2028 and June 2027 maturities  
   - Options: Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend options: IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) and Growth  
   - Fund codes vary by plan and option (e.g., 148793 for Reg - IDCW April 2028)

2. Bandhan Dynamic Bond Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend payout frequencies: Annual, Half-Yearly, Quarterly, Periodic IDCW, and Growth option  
   - Fund codes provided for each variant (e.g., 118415 for Dir - Ann IDCW)

3. Bandhan Liquid Fund  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans  
   - Dividend payout frequencies: Daily, Weekly, Monthly IDCW, and Growth option  
   - Special categories for unclaimed IDCW and Redemption amounts categorized by duration (less than or more than 3 years)  
   - Fund codes provided for each variant (e.g., 118366 for Dir - Dly IDCW)

The document lists unique fund codes for each scheme variant, which are essential for transactions and identification.

KEY POINTS:  
- Bandhan Mutual Fund offers a variety of schemes including Gilt, Dynamic Bond, and Liquid Funds.  
- Each scheme is available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend options include IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) with frequencies such as Annual, Half-Yearly, Quarterly, Monthly, Weekly, and Daily, as well as Growth options.  
- Unique fund codes are assigned to each scheme variant for identification and transaction purposes.  
- Special provisions exist for unclaimed IDCW and Redemption amounts in the Liquid Fund category, categorized by duration (less than or more than 3 years).
