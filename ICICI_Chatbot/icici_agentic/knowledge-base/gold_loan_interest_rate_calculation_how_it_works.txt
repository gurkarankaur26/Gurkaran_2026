CONTENT:  
Gold loans allow customers to quickly access funds by pledging their gold assets. Understanding how the interest rate on a gold loan is calculated is crucial for managing repayment and overall loan cost.

**Types of Interest Rates:**  
- ICICI Bank offers a fixed interest rate throughout the loan tenure.  
- Gold loans typically use simple interest for calculation.

**Interest Calculation Formula:**  
Interest = Principal (P) × Rate of Interest (R) × Time (T)  
Example: For a loan of ₹10,000 at 10% annual interest for 1 year, interest = 10,000 × 0.10 × 1 = ₹1,000.

**Additional Charges:**  
- Processing Fees  
- Valuation Charges  
- Penal Charges  
These charges affect the total repayment amount.

**Repayment Options:**  
- ICICI Bank provides flexible repayment options allowing payment of principal and interest at any point during the tenure.  
- Some lenders offer part-payment or prepayment options to help manage the loan effectively.

**Tools and Advice:**  
- Use the ICICI Bank Gold Loan Calculator to estimate the principal amount based on gold loan per gram.  
- It is advisable to compare interest rates, fees, and terms across lenders before finalizing a loan.  
- Consulting a financial advisor or using online calculators can provide personalized and accurate loan cost estimates.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank gold loans have fixed interest rates and use simple interest calculation.

- Additional fees like processing and valuation charges apply.
