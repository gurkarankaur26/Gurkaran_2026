TITLE: NRI Account Opening Process and Status Enquiry  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: NRI Banking, Account Opening  
TAGS: NRI Account, Account Opening, Document Submission, Status Enquiry, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
To open an NRI account with ICICI Bank, you can apply through an online application or by downloading the NRI account opening form from the official website. After filling the form and attaching all required documents, send the completed application to the following address:

ICICI Bank Limited  
NRI Remote RM Channel  
1st Floor, A wing  
"Autumn Estate"  
Chandivali Farm Road  
Chandivali  
Andheri (East)  
Mumbai - 400072  

Once your documents are submitted and verified, the account will be opened within 7 working days. If the account is being opened through an overseas branch, you need to contact that branch directly to know the status of your application.

To check the status of your account opening, you can call ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Phone Banking Services.

KEY POINTS:  
- NRI account can be opened via online application or by submitting a downloaded form with required documents.  
- Completed forms and documents must be sent to the specified ICICI Bank NRI Remote RM Channel address in Mumbai.  
- Account opening takes up to 7 working days after document submission and verification.  
- For accounts opened through overseas branches, status updates must be obtained from the respective branch.  
- 24-hour Phone Banking Services are available to check the status of your account opening application.
