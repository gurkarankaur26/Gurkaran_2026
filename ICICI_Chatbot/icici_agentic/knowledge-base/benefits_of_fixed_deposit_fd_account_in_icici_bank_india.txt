TITLE: Benefits of Fixed Deposit (FD) Account in ICICI Bank, India  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Fixed Deposit, NRI Banking, Investment  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, NRE FD, NRO FD, Tax Saver FD, Tenure, Minimum Deposit, Interest Rates  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Fixed Deposit (FD) accounts tailored for NRI customers with various benefits and flexible options. The key features of ICICI Bank’s FD accounts include:  

- **Minimum Deposit Amount:** A minimum deposit of Rs. 25,000 is required to open an NRE or NRO Fixed Deposit.  
- **Tenure:**  
  - NRO FD: Minimum tenure of 7 days up to a maximum of 10 years.  
  - NRE FD: Minimum tenure of 1 year up to a maximum of 10 years.  
- **Tax Benefits:**  
  - Tax Saver NRO FDs are available which provide tax-saving benefits under applicable sections of the Income Tax Act.  
- **Interest Rates:** Interest rates vary based on the tenure and type of FD (NRE/NRO). Customers can choose the tenure that best suits their investment goals.  
- **Safety and Security:** Deposits are secure and offer guaranteed returns.  
- **Premature Withdrawal:** Premature withdrawal is allowed as per the bank’s terms and conditions, subject to applicable penalties.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum deposit: Rs. 25,000  
- Tenure range: 7 days (NRO FD) / 1 year (NRE FD) to 10 years  
- Tax Saver NRO FD available for tax benefits  
- Flexible tenure and interest rates  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with penalties
