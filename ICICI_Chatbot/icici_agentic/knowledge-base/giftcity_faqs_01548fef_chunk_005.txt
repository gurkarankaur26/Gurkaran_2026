TITLE:  
ICICI Bank IFSC GIFT City Banking Services and Credit Facilities

ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Corporate Banking, Foreign Currency Loans, IFSC Banking, Treasury Services

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of banking and credit facilities through its International Banking Unit (IBU) at IFSC GIFT City, designed to cater to Indian corporates, their overseas subsidiaries, joint ventures, and eligible resident Indian entities under FEMA guidelines.

**Account Services:**  
- Customers can open Current and Deposit accounts at GIFT City if they are corporates incorporated in IFSC GIFT City, overseas entities linked with India and ICICI Bank, or resident Indian entities permitted under FEMA guidelines.  
- Account statements can be viewed or downloaded via net banking at https://www.giftcity.icicibank.com/.  
- User ID generation requires only the account number and registered mobile number. Passwords can be generated online with OTP verification sent to the registered mobile number.  
- Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) cannot transfer funds from GIFT City accounts to Resident Accounts.

**Credit Facilities:**  
- ICICI Bank IBU offers credit products including External Commercial Borrowings (ECB), Foreign Currency Term Loans (FCTL), and Foreign Currency Working Capital facilities.  
- Eligible applicants include Indian corporates, their wholly owned subsidiaries (WOS), and joint ventures (JV) incorporated overseas.  
- Applications are submitted through Relationship Managers and undergo evaluation for corporate credentials and compliance with ECB and foreign currency lending guidelines.  
- Facilities are available in multiple foreign currencies with dedicated Relationship Managers supporting both INR and foreign currency requirements.  
- Foreign Currency Working Capital facilities are available in fund or non-fund-based forms for overseas corporates, WOS, and JVs of Indian companies.

**Treasury and Custodial Services:**  
- Full-fledged treasury services including foreign exchange conversion, Non-Deliverable Forwards (NDF), and Non-Deliverable Options and Interest Swaps (NDOIS) are provided.  
- ICICI Bank IBU is a registered custodian with the International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA), offering custodial services to Alternative Investment Funds (AIF), Portfolio Management Services (PMS) entities, and Foreign Portfolio Investors (FPI) in India.  
- The bank facilitates global investments with safekeeping and settlement services through international tie-ups.

**Depository Services:**  
- ICICI Bank IBU acts as a Depository Participant and can onboard clients for opening Demat accounts.

**Additional Information:**  
- Nostro account details for different currencies are subject to change and can be accessed via the official ICICI Bank GIFT City website.  
- Customers are advised to consult ICICI Bank communications, official IFSCA notifications, or their Relationship Manager for updates on account services, investment options, and regulatory changes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility: Indian corporates, overseas subsidiaries, joint ventures, and resident Indian entities under FEMA guidelines.  
- Credit Facilities: ECB, FCTL, Foreign Currency Working Capital.  
- Account Access: Net banking with user ID and password generation via registered mobile number.  
- NRI Restrictions: No fund transfer from GIFT City to Resident Accounts.  
- Treasury Services: FX conversion, NDF, NDOIS.  
- Custodial Services: Registered custodian for AIF/PMS and FPIs.  
- Depository Services: Demat account onboarding.  
- Regulatory Compliance: Adherence to ECB guidelines and IFSCA regulations.  
- Contact: Relationship Managers for application and support.  
- Website for Account Statements and Nostro details: https://www.giftcity.icicibank.com/
