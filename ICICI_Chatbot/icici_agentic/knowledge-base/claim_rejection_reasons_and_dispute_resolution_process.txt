TITLE: Claim Rejection Reasons and Dispute Resolution Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Insurance Claims  
TAGS: Claims, Rejection Reasons, Dispute Resolution, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance  

CONTENT:  
A claim may be declined due to the following reasons:  

- **Non-disclosure of information:** For example, hiding pre-existing medical conditions.  
- **Policy lapse:** Non-payment of premiums leading to policy invalidation.  
- **Fraudulent claims:** Misrepresentation of facts or providing false information.  
- **Exclusions in policy:** Death or events occurring due to reasons excluded in the policy, such as suicide within the exclusion period.  

If you disagree with the claim decision, you can raise your concerns by:  
- Contacting the Claims Department via email or phone.  
- Visiting the nearest ICICI Prudential Life branch.  
- Writing to the Grievance Redressal Team.  

For written intimation related to claims, send your communication to the address provided on the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance website under the Claims Section.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Common reasons for claim rejection include non-disclosure, policy lapse, fraud, and policy exclusions.  
- Multiple channels are available to dispute a claim decision: phone, email, branch visit, or grievance redressal.  
- Written claim communications must be sent to the official address listed on the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance website.
