**Payee Registration Process for Fund Transfers:**  
- When you register a payee, the status will initially be "pending confirmation." No transfers can be made to the payee until confirmation.  
- You will receive an email alert with a Unique Registration Number (URN).  
- Log in to Net Banking and confirm the payee registration by entering the URN in the “confirm payee” section.  
- Upon successful authentication, the payee status updates to "registration confirmed," allowing fund transfers to that payee for the specified service.  
- Alternatively, payee confirmation can be done by calling ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care.

**Tax Credit and Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA):**  
- Tax credit statements can be viewed by logging into Net Banking.  
- Interest earned on NRO accounts can benefit from DTAA to avoid double taxation.  
- Double taxation occurs when income is taxed both in the home country (based on tax residency) and the source country (where income is earned).  
- DTAA is a tax treaty between India and other countries to reduce or eliminate double taxation, often by taxing income at a lower or nil rate in the source country.  
- To avail DTAA benefits in India, the following documents must be furnished:  
  - Tax Residency Certificate (TRC)  
  - Form 10F  
  - Permanent Account Number (PAN)  
- If PAN is not furnished, concessional DTAA rates will not apply.  
- Income tax rates will follow the DTAA agreement between India and the respective country.
