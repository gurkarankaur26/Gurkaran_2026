CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Credit Cards offer a range of exclusive benefits designed to enhance your spending experience and provide convenience, security, and rewards. Key features include complimentary airport lounge access, reward points, fuel surcharge waivers, discount coupons, and flexible payment options such as EMI conversion.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- **Contactless Payment:** Pay instantly by tapping your card on a POS terminal that supports contactless payments, enabling faster and secure transactions.  
- **UPI Integration:** Link your credit card with any UPI-enabled app to make payments by scanning QR codes easily.  
- **Auto-Pay Facility:** Set up auto-pay via ICICI Bank Net Banking or the iMobile app to ensure timely bill payments and avoid penalties.  
- **Digital Management:**  
  - **Net Banking:** Log in to manage your card, modify limits, block/unblock cards, convert transactions into EMIs, request new cards, view/download statements, and pay bills.  
  - **iMobile App:** Pay bills, view transaction history, check available credit, monitor reward points, manage EMI conversions, and enable/disable card features for security.  
- **Instant OTP (IOTP):** For secure transactions, send an SMS with the last 4 digits of your credit card to 5676766 to receive an instant OTP.  
- **EMI Conversion:** Convert purchases above a certain value into easy monthly installments, providing flexible payment options.  
- **Credit Building:** Use your credit card to build a strong credit history while enjoying short-term credit benefits.  
- **Lounge Access:** Premium credit cards offer complimentary access to domestic airport lounges, enhancing your travel experience.  

**How to Maximize Your Credit Card Benefits:**  
- Use contactless payments for faster checkouts.  
- Link your card with UPI apps for seamless digital payments.  
- Enable auto-pay to avoid late fees and maintain a good credit score.  
- Track and manage your card usage through ICICI Bank’s digital platforms.  
- Convert large purchases into EMIs to manage your finances better.  
- Redeem reward points and avail fuel surcharge waivers and discount coupons regularly.
