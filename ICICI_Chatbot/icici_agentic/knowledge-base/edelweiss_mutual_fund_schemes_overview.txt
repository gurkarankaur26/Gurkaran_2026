TITLE: Edelweiss Mutual Fund Schemes Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Edelweiss, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Small Cap, Multi Cap, IPO Fund, Technology Fund, Growth, IDCW, Direct Plan, Regular Plan  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Edelweiss Mutual Fund schemes available for investment through ICICI Bank. The schemes cover various categories including Large & Mid Cap, Large Cap, Mid Cap, Multi Asset Allocation, Multi Cap, Recently Listed IPO, Small Cap, and Technology funds. Each scheme is available in different plans such as Direct and Regular, and in options like Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

Key Schemes and Their Variants:  
- Edelweiss Large & Mid Cap Fund: Available in Direct Plan with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Large Cap Fund: Offered in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options, including Plan B and Plan C variants.  
- Edelweiss Mid Cap Fund: Available in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Multi Asset Allocation Fund: Offered in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Multi Cap Fund: Available in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Recently Listed IPO Fund: Offered in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Small Cap Fund: Available in Direct and Regular Plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- Edelweiss Technology Fund: Available in Direct Plan with Growth option.  

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction processing.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Edelweiss Mutual Fund schemes are available through ICICI Bank.  
- Schemes cover various market capitalizations and investment strategies.  
- Options include Growth (reinvestment of earnings) and IDCW (periodic income distribution).  
- Plans include Direct (investor directly with fund house) and Regular (through intermediaries).  
- Unique scheme codes are provided for each variant to facilitate transactions.
