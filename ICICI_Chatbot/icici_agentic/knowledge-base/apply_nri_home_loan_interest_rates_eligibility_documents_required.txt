TITLE: Apply NRI Home Loan - Interest Rates, Eligibility & Documents Required  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Home Loans specifically designed for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to purchase new or resale properties in India. The loan comes with attractive interest rates and flexible repayment tenures of up to 30 years. NRIs can conveniently apply online anytime and from anywhere without the need to visit India. The bank’s Express Home Loan platform enables quick online sanction in five easy steps, subject to document verification.

**Key Features:**  
- Home Loans available for purchase of new and resale properties  
- Attractive interest rates (specific rates to be checked on the bank’s website or by contacting the bank)  
- Flexible loan tenure up to 30 years  
- Online application and sanction process for convenience  
- Quick sanction through Express Home Loan platform (subject to document verification)  

**Eligibility:**  
- Applicant must be a Non-Resident Indian (NRI)  
- Must meet the bank’s income and credit criteria (details available on request)  

**Documents Required:**  
- Proof of NRI status (passport, visa, work permit, etc.)  
- Income proof (salary slips, bank statements, tax returns)  
- Property documents (sale agreement, title deed)  
- Identity and address proof  
- Other documents as specified by the bank  

**Process:**  
1. Apply online through ICICI Bank’s NRI Home Loan portal  
2. Submit required documents for verification  
3. Bank processes the application and verifies documents  
4. Quick sanction through Express Home Loan platform (if eligible)  
5. Disbursement of loan upon approval  

KEY POINTS:  
- NRI Home Loans available for new and resale property purchase  
- Loan tenure up to 30 years  
- Attractive interest rates (check current rates with the bank)  
- Online application and quick sanction process  
- Required documents include proof of NRI status, income, and property papers  
- Express Home Loan platform enables sanction in five easy steps, subject to verification
