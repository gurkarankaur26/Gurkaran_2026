TITLE: Credit Card Bill Payment Online  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers multiple convenient and secure options to pay your credit card bills online. Customers can choose from various payment modes including UPI, iMobile app, Net Banking, Auto Debit, and NEFT to ensure timely and hassle-free bill payments. The payment process is designed to be fast and secure, providing flexibility and ease of use. Auto Debit facility allows automatic deduction of the credit card bill amount from your savings or current account, helping you avoid missed payments and late fees.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple payment modes available: UPI, iMobile, Net Banking, Auto Debit, NEFT  
- Secure and fast payment process  
- Auto Debit facility for automatic bill payments  
- Designed for ease and convenience of ICICI Bank credit cardholders
