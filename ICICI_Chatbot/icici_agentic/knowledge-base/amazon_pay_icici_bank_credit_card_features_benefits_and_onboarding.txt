TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card - Features, Benefits, and Onboarding  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Co-branded Card, Visa, Rewards, Video KYC, Digital Onboarding  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card, powered by Visa, is a co-branded credit card launched in October 2018. It has rapidly grown to over two million customers, making it the fastest co-branded credit card in India to reach this milestone. The card is available to any registered customer of Amazon India, including those who are not existing ICICI Bank customers.

Key features of the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card include:  
- Always-on, unlimited rewards program with reward points credited directly to the Amazon Pay balance.  
- Instant issuance of the card for select customers in less than 60 seconds.  
- Contactless payment feature for safe and convenient transactions.  
- Fully digital and contactless onboarding process, including Video KYC facility introduced in June 2020, allowing customers to complete KYC remotely via video call.  
- The card is popular among millennials and sees maximum spends on online marketplaces, departmental stores, and insurance premium payments.  

The card has witnessed strong acceptance across major Indian cities such as Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Pune, Bengaluru, and Hyderabad. Over 80% of new customers onboard digitally without any physical interaction.

Eligibility & Application:  
- Any registered Amazon.in customer can apply digitally from anywhere in India.  
- Video KYC enables safe, contactless verification for new customers, including those new to banking.  

Benefits:  
- Unlimited reward points credited instantly to Amazon Pay balance.  
- Contactless payments for enhanced safety.  
- Quick card issuance for select customers.  
- Seamless digital onboarding with Video KYC.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Launched: October 2018  
- Card Type: Co-branded Visa credit card  
- Customer Base: Over 2 million customers  
- Onboarding: Fully digital with Video KYC since June 2020  
- Rewards: Unlimited, instant credit to Amazon Pay balance  
- Popular Usage: Online marketplaces, departmental stores, insurance premium payments  
- Contactless Payment: Enabled  
- Eligibility: Any registered Amazon.in customer, including non-ICICI Bank customers  
- Instant issuance available for select customers (under 60 seconds)
