CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive suite of banking products and services tailored for Larsen and Toubro Limited employees and their families, including salary accounts, loans, investment solutions, and exclusive benefits.

**Salary Account Benefits:**  
- Personalized financial profile assessment and corpus deployment solutions.  
- Portfolio rebalancing across debt and equity based on risk appetite.  
- Realigned investment strategies to meet financial goals.  
- Share account benefits with privacy maintained; linked accounts remain independent with private balances and transactions.  
- Titanium/Platinum Debit Card with unlimited ATM access and airport lounge access on spends of Rs. 10,000 per quarter.  
- Minimum balance requirement waiver for the primary account holder and up to 5 family members.  
- Complimentary insurance cover equivalent to the primary member’s salary credits.  
- Waiver of charges for online fund transfers via NEFT, RTGS, and IMPS.  
- Extend salary account benefits to up to five family members through Family Banking, which can be enrolled via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app or at branches.

**Loan Facilities:**  
- **Loan Against Shares:**  
  - Borrow up to 50% of the share value, with a maximum limit of Rs. 20 Lakhs.  
  - Interest is charged only on the amount utilized.  
- **Loan Against Mutual Funds:**  
  - Loan up to Rs. 20 Lakhs on equity mutual funds.  
  - Loan up to Rs. 1 crore on debt mutual funds.  
- **Home Loan, Personal Loan, Auto Loan, Education Loan:**  
  - Available with personalized offers (details available on request).

**NRI Banking Services:**  
- Convert Resident Savings Account to NRO Account to manage income earned in India.  
- Open NRE Account to enjoy tax-free interest and full repatriation of funds.  
- Manage NRI accounts digitally from anywhere globally, including NRE/NRO/3-in-1 Accounts, Fixed Deposits, Foreign Currency Deposits, Investments, and Loans.  
- Remittance services to transfer money abroad or to India via ICICI Bank Wire transfer, SmartWire, Money2India, and Money2World platforms with competitive exchange rates.

**Forex Prepaid Card:**  
- Range of forex prepaid cards available for international travel convenience.

**Power Pay Account:**  
- Titanium/Platinum Debit Card with unlimited ATM access.  
- Access to 3-in-1 Salary, Demat & Trading account with first-year AMC waiver.  
- Extend salary account benefits to up to five family members.  
- Waiver of charges on online transactions through NEFT/RTGS/IMPS.  
- Complimentary air and personal accident insurance with no hidden clauses.

**Select Banking:**  
- Extend benefits to up to 9 family members with Family Banking.  
- Domestic lounge access on debit card.  
- Preferential offerings on Demat accounts and loans.  
- Air and personal accidental death cover.

**Wealth Management Account:**  
- Dedicated wealth management services (details to be obtained from the bank).

- NRI banking with easy account conversion and global digital management.
