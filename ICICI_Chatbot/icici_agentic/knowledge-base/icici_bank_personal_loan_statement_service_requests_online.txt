CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides convenient online services for managing your personal loan, including accessing loan statements and raising service requests. Customers can view detailed personal loan statements, track EMI payments, and request various services related to their personal loan through the bank’s net banking platform.

**Key Services Available Online:**  
- Access and download personal loan statements  
- View EMI details and payment history  
- Raise service requests related to personal loans such as loan closure, statement requests, and changes in loan account details  
- Track the status of service requests  

**EMI Banking Related Information:**  
- Customers can view their EMI schedule and payment status online  
- Details of upcoming EMIs and past payments are available for easy tracking  

**Other Services:**  
- Request for loan statement issuance  
- Request for loan closure or foreclosure  
- Update personal details linked to the loan account  

**Contact Details for Assistance:**  
- For any queries or assistance related to personal loans, customers can contact ICICI Bank customer care through the official helpline numbers or visit the nearest branch.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal loan statements and EMI details accessible online via net banking  
- Service requests such as loan closure, statement issuance, and account updates can be raised online  
- Easy tracking of EMI payments and service request status  
- Customer support available through official helpline and branches
