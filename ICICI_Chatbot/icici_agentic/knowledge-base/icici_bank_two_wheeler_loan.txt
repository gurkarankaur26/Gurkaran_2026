TITLE: ICICI Bank Two-Wheeler Loan  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Two-Wheeler Loan, Vehicle Loan, Interest Rates, Loan Processing, Digital Banking, Loan Eligibility  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Two-Wheeler Loans with attractive features designed to provide convenience and affordability to customers. The loan comes with low EMIs starting at Rs 29 per Rs 1,000 of the loan amount and offers up to 100% funding of the on-road price of the two-wheeler, subject to eligibility.

Key features include:  
- **Digital Convenience:** Applicants can apply online from anywhere. Loan calculations and document verification are also available digitally, making the process seamless.  
- **Minimal Documentation:** Only basic documents are required for application. Pre-approved customers can avail of paperless disbursement through an online application process.  
- **Processing Fees:** The processing fee ranges between 2.5% to 4.75% of the loan amount, depending on the vehicle segment.  
- **Low Interest Rates:** Pre-approved customers benefit from exceptional interest rates, resulting in affordable EMIs.  
- **Full Funding:** Eligible customers may receive 100% financing of the on-road price of the two-wheeler.  

Customers are encouraged to use the ICICI Bank Two-Wheeler Loan Calculator to estimate EMIs and select a repayment tenure that best fits their financial planning.

KEY POINTS:  
- EMI as low as Rs 29 per Rs 1,000 of loan amount  
- Processing fee: 2.5% to 4.75% of loan amount  
- 100% funding of on-road price possible for eligible customers  
- Minimal documentation required  
- Fully digital application and verification process  
- Pre-approved customers can avail paperless disbursement
