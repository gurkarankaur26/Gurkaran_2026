CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank iMobile Banking app offers a comprehensive suite of banking services tailored for NRIs, enabling convenient management of accounts and financial transactions anytime, anywhere. The app consolidates multiple banking functions into a single platform, facilitating seamless banking experiences.

Key features include:

- **Accounts & Deposits:** Manage existing ICICI Bank accounts and open additional NRE/NRO accounts, savings accounts, loans, and credit cards. Users can also open Fixed Deposits (FDs) and Recurring Deposits (RDs) directly through the app.

- **Send Money:** Transfer funds instantly to any ICICI Bank account. The recipient must be registered on the iMobile app to receive money.

- **Global UPI Payments:** Make payments via UPI from overseas mobile numbers registered in countries such as Australia, Malaysia, Canada, France, Hong Kong, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, UAE, UK, and USA.

- **Recharge:** Recharge mobile phones, DTH, or data connections directly through the app.

- **Bill Payments:** Pay utility bills quickly after registering the biller once via Internet Banking.

- **Cards & Loans:** Monitor credit card and loan accounts in real-time.

- **Investments & Insurance:** Manage insurance policies and Demat accounts conveniently.

- **Other Services:** Access additional features such as Smartlock, ReKYC updates, communication address updates, FATCA/CRS declarations, opening additional NRE/NRO accounts, card blocking, cheque stopping, and tracking of deliverables.

Terms and conditions apply.
