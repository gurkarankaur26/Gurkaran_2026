CONTENT:  
A Pre-owned Car Loan from ICICI Bank enables customers to purchase a used or second-hand car with attractive interest rates and flexible repayment tenures. This loan is designed to make buying a pre-owned vehicle affordable and convenient.

**What is a Pre-owned Car Loan?**  
It is a financial product that helps individuals buy used cars by providing loan amounts at competitive interest rates with repayment tenures of up to 7 years.

**Key Features:**  
- **Quick and Easy Process:** Application requires submission of a filled form along with KYC documents, income proof, bank statements, and other relevant documents.  
- **Attractive Interest Rates:** Interest rates typically range between 12% and 14.50% per annum, offering low EMIs.  
- **Flexible Tenure:** Loan repayment tenure can extend up to 7 years.  
- **Eligibility Check:** Applicants can check eligibility online based on age, income, employment status, and relationship with ICICI Bank.  
- **EMI Calculator:** An online EMI calculator is available to estimate monthly payments.  
- **Benefits for Existing Customers:** Existing ICICI Bank customers may avail instant loans with minimal documentation, faster disbursal, and preferential interest rates.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  

| Parameter           | Self-employed Individuals           | Salaried Individuals               |  
|---------------------|-----------------------------------|----------------------------------|  
| Age                 | 23 to 60 years                    | 21 to 54 years                   |  
| Work Experience     | Minimum 4 years of business continuity | Minimum 2 years of work experience |  
| Minimum Income       | ₹1.75 lakh per annum              | ₹2 lakh per annum                 |  
| Loan Tenure          | Up to 7 years                    | Up to 7 years                    |  
| Residency            | Resident Indians only             | Resident Indians only             |  

**How to Apply:**  
- **Existing ICICI Bank Customers:** Can apply via Internet Banking or the iMobile app for a seamless experience.  
- **New Customers:** Can apply online by submitting the required documents and application form.

KEY POINTS:  
- Pre-owned Car Loan helps finance used car purchases with tenures up to 7 years.  
- Interest rates range from 12% to 14.50% per annum.  
- Eligibility depends on age, income, employment type, and experience.  
- Existing customers benefit from faster processing and better rates.  
- Online EMI calculator available for easy monthly payment estimation.  
- Loan available only to resident Indians.
