TITLE: Pre-Approved Two Wheeler Loan at ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Two Wheeler Loan, Pre-Approved Loan, Insta Loan, Loan Processing, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a streamlined process for Two Wheeler Loans through its Insta Two Wheeler Loan facility. This initiative leverages technology to enable faster loan processing for both existing and new customers. Customers can generate an approval letter online and avail the loan by submitting minimal documents, making the process quick and convenient.

**Key Features:**  
- Instant approval letter generation online  
- Minimal documentation required  
- Faster loan processing for existing and new customers  

**Eligibility & Process:**  
- Existing ICICI Bank customers can check their pre-approved offer by sending an SMS with the text "TW" to 5676766 from their mobile number registered with the bank.  
- New customers can also apply online and avail the benefits of the Insta Two Wheeler Loan.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Insta Two Wheeler Loan enables quick approval and disbursal  
- Minimal documentation required for loan processing  
- Existing customers can check pre-approved offers via SMS (Send "TW" to 5676766)  
- Available for both existing and new ICICI Bank customers
