CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides a comprehensive set of downloadable forms and documents for NRI customers to manage their accounts and related services. These forms are essential for various account-related requests including account opening, modifications, fixed deposits, repatriation, and KYC updates. Customers are required to send the completed forms along with a self-attested copy of a valid passport to the addresses mentioned on the respective forms. Modification requests must be couriered to the specified addresses.

Available Forms and Services include:

- **Account Opening & Related Forms:**  
  - New Relationship Account Opening Form  
  - Online Account Opening (Non face-to-face mode)  
  - Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA) Forms (to be submitted with account opening)  
  - Joint Holder / Guardian Details Form  
  - Signature Mismatch Verification  

- **Account Modification & Updates:**  
  - Request for Change of Existing Resident Account to NRO Account  
  - Request for Addition/Deletion of Applicant in Existing Account  
  - Request for Personal Detail Updation  
  - Name, Signature, and Mode of Operation (MOP) Change  
  - Updation of KYC Documents  
  - Request for FATCA/CRS Updation  
  - CKYC Form  

- **Nomination & Legal Forms:**  
  - Nomination Form  
  - Application for Nomination (Form E)  
  - Application for Cancellation or Change of Nomination (Form F)  
  - Application for Closing the Account (Form G)  
  - Application for Closing the Account by Nominee/Legal Heir of Deceased Depositor (Form H)  

- **Fixed Deposits & Recurring Deposits:**  
  - Closure of Term Deposits (NRE FD / NRO FD / NRE RD / NRO RD / FCNR(B) FD)  
  - Request for Duplicate Fixed Deposit Receipt  
  - Request for Renewal of Term Deposits  

- **Repatriation & Fund Transfer:**  
  - Request for Outward Repatriation of Funds  
  - Repatriation from FCNR/NRE Fixed Deposits and NRE Savings Bank Account  
  - Repatriation from NRO Fixed Deposits and Savings Bank Account  
  - Request Letter for Transfer of Funds from NRO Account to NRE Account  
  - Request Letter for Transfer of Funds from Non-ICICI Bank NRO Account to ICICI Bank NRE Account  

- **Other Services:**  
  - Mandate Holder Applications  
  - Request for Additional Savings / Non-Interest Bearing Account / Fixed Deposit  
  - Types of Forms under NRI Capital Gains Account Scheme (CGAS):  
    - Form A: Application for Opening CGAS Account  
    - Form B: Application for Conversion of Account (Type A and B)  
    - Form C: Application for Withdrawal from Account A  
    - Form D: Application for Subsequent Withdrawals from Account A  
  - Request for Demand Draft / Duplicate Demand Draft  
  - Request for Internet Banking / Mobile Banking Facility  
  - Request for Reissue of Internet Banking User ID/Password, ATM/Debit Card and PIN, or New Cheque Book  
  - Request for Stop Payment / Hot Listing Card  
  - Transfer of Accounts for NRI Customers  
  - Request for Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA) - NRO Accounts  
  - PIS (Portfolio Investment Scheme) Application Form and Cancellation Form  
  - Non Solicitation Declaration  
  - DEAF Refund Form  
  - Conversion from Resident to NRO for Expats (Foreign Nationals)  
  - ODFD (Outward Demand Draft) Application and Closure Forms (including closure through Phone Banking)  

KEY POINTS:  
- All forms must be submitted with a self-attested copy of a valid passport.  
- Modification requests require courier submission to specified addresses.  
- Wide range of forms available covering account opening, modification, fixed deposits, repatriation, nomination, and KYC updates.  
- Specific forms for NRI Capital Gains Account Scheme (CGAS) are provided.  
- Services include internet banking requests, demand drafts, and account transfers.  
- Repatriation requests cover funds from FCNR, NRE, and NRO accounts.  
- Legal heir and nominee forms are available for account closure in case of depositor’s demise.  
- FATCA and CRS compliance forms are integrated with account opening and updation processes.
