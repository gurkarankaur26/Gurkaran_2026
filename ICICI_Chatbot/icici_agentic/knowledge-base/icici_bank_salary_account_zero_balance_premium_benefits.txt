CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Salary Account is designed specifically for working professionals, offering zero balance requirements along with premium benefits. The account facilitates seamless salary credit and provides easy access to a range of banking and investment services.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- Zero balance requirement: No minimum balance needed to maintain the account.  
- Premium benefits tailored for salaried individuals.  
- Integrated investment options: Invest in stocks, mutual funds, and IPOs directly from your ICICI Bank Salary Account using the ICICI Bank iMobile app.  
- Linked Demat and Trading accounts: Manage your investments and trading activities conveniently through the same platform.  
- Quick and easy salary credit process ensuring timely salary deposits.  

Eligibility:  
- Designed for salaried professionals receiving regular salary credits.  

Fees and Charges:  
- No minimum balance fees applicable due to zero balance facility.  
- Other applicable charges as per ICICI Bank’s standard tariff for additional services.  

Process to Open:  
- Can be opened online or at any ICICI Bank branch by submitting necessary KYC documents and salary proof.  

Additional Services:  
- Access to service requests and customer support for quick assistance related to the account.

- Linked Demat and Trading accounts for seamless investment management.
