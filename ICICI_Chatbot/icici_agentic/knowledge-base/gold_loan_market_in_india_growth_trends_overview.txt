CONTENT:  
A Gold Loan is a secured loan where the borrower pledges gold as collateral with the lender. The gold is retained by the lender during the loan tenure, which typically ranges from 6 months to 1 year. Gold Loans can be used for various purposes including personal expenses, business needs, and emergencies.

ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans starting at an interest rate of 10% per annum. The application process is user-friendly and can be completed online.

The Gold Loan market in India is growing steadily due to factors such as easy loan availability, attractive interest rates, flexible repayment options, and minimal documentation requirements. The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) supports this growth through initiatives like the Gold Monetization Scheme (GMS) and the Gold Deposit Scheme (GDS), which encourage households to deposit idle gold and avail loans against it.

India holds a significant portion of the world’s gold reserves, with an estimated 24,000 to 25,000 metric tons of gold in Indian households, valued at up to 40% of the nation’s GDP as of 2019. Approximately 65% of this gold is held in rural India, representing a large untapped financial resource.

The credit extended against gold jewellery has seen rapid growth, increasing from around Rs 34,000 crore in March 2020 to Rs 61,000 crore in 2020-21, and further to Rs 74,000 crore by March 2022. This growth rate is significantly higher than that of personal loans, indicating the rising popularity and trust in Gold Loans as a financial product.
