CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Regular Salary Account is designed to provide employees with a convenient and benefit-rich banking experience. This account requires no minimum balance, allowing salary earners to manage their finances without the worry of maintaining a minimum amount. Account holders enjoy unlimited ICICI Bank ATM transactions, enabling easy access to cash without additional charges. The account also comes with complimentary insurance coverage, adding a layer of financial security. Digital banking facilities are integrated, offering seamless online and mobile banking services for managing the account anytime, anywhere.  

KEY POINTS:  
- No minimum balance requirement  
- Unlimited ICICI Bank ATM transactions  
- Complimentary insurance coverage included  
- Access to digital banking services for easy account management  
- Ideal for salaried individuals seeking hassle-free salary account benefits
