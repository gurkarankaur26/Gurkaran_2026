CONTENT:  
This document provides a list of mutual fund schemes along with their respective scheme codes. The schemes include various fund types such as Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options under different fund houses like HDFC and HSBC. Each scheme is identified by a unique code which is essential for transactions and investments.

KEY POINTS:  
- Each mutual fund scheme is associated with a unique code used for identification and transactions.  
- Schemes are available in different options such as Growth and IDCW (dividend payout).  
- Both Direct and Regular plans are available for most schemes.  
- Fund houses included are primarily HDFC and HSBC in this list.  
- IDCW options may have different payout frequencies such as monthly, quarterly, or annual.
