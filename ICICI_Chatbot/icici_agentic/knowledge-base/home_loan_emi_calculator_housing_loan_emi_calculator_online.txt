ICICI Bank’s Home Loan EMI Calculator is an online tool designed to help customers estimate their monthly Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for a home loan. By inputting the loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure, users can quickly calculate their EMI, enabling better financial planning and budgeting before applying for a home loan.

The calculator uses the following formula to compute EMI:  
EMI = [P x R x (1+R)^N] / [(1+R)^N - 1]  
Where:  
- P = Principal loan amount  
- R = Monthly interest rate (annual interest rate divided by 12 and then by 100)  
- N = Loan tenure in months  

For example, for a loan amount of ₹10,00,000 at an annual interest rate of 7.25% for 120 months, the EMI would be approximately ₹11,740.

Key features and benefits of the ICICI Bank Home Loan EMI Calculator include:  
- Easy to use with a simple interface requiring only loan amount, interest rate, and tenure  
- Provides accurate EMI estimates to aid in financial planning  
- Allows adjustment of loan parameters to see their impact on EMI  
- Helps customers manage monthly budgets effectively by knowing exact EMI amounts  
- Saves time by providing instant results without visiting a bank branch  
- Assists in pre-planning the loan amount and tenure based on individual financial situations
