TITLE: Two-Wheeler Loan from ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Two-Wheeler Loan, Loan Eligibility, Interest Rates, Fees, Loan Application, APR  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Two-Wheeler Loans to help customers finance the purchase of two-wheelers. The loan includes an Annual Percentage Rate (APR) which represents the annualised credit cost, encompassing both the interest rate and the loan origination fee.  

**Documents Required for Applying:**  
- Identity Proof  
- Address Proof  
- Income Proof  
- Two-Wheeler Quotation/Invoice  
- Passport size photographs  

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- The APR includes the interest rate and loan origination fee.  
- Penal charges apply in case of delayed payments (specific charges to be confirmed with the bank).  

**Loan Application Process:**  
- Submit the required documents.  
- The bank evaluates eligibility based on income and credit profile.  
- Loan amount and tenure are decided accordingly.  

**Additional Information:**  
- To calculate the APR for your loan, ICICI Bank provides an APR calculator available for download on their website.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Two-Wheeler Loan available with competitive interest rates.  
- APR includes interest rate and loan origination fee.  
- Required documents include identity, address, income proof, and vehicle quotation.  
- Penal charges apply for delayed payments.  
- APR calculator available for precise cost estimation.
