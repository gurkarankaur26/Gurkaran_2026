TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Features, Benefits, and Application Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Rewards, No Annual Fee, Visa, Digital Card, Cashback, EMI, Fuel Surcharge Waiver  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank credit card is a co-branded Visa credit card designed to offer convenient, secure, and rewarding payment experiences for Amazon shoppers and other users. It is available through a fully digital, contactless, and paperless application process via the Amazon India app or website. Upon approval, customers receive a digital card instantly and a physical card is delivered within a few days.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- No joining fee or annual fee for the card.  
- Reward points on spends with no upper limit and no expiry.  
- Reward points earn rate:  
  - 5% for Amazon Prime members on Amazon.in purchases.  
  - 3% for non-Prime customers on Amazon.in purchases.  
  - 2% on digital categories on Amazon.in such as bill payments, recharges, adding money to Amazon Pay balance, and movie bookings.  
  - 2% on spends at Amazon Pay partner merchants including Swiggy, BookMyShow, Yatra, and others.  
  - 1% on all other spends at any merchant location in India where Visa cards are accepted.  
- Fuel surcharge waiver on fuel transactions.  
- No reward points on fuel, EMI transactions, rent payments, and gold purchases.  
- Reward points are credited monthly after the billing cycle to the customer’s Amazon Pay balance.  
- Reward points can be redeemed against over 160 million products on Amazon.in and services from Amazon Pay partner merchants such as flight tickets, hotel bookings, food delivery, and movie tickets.  
- The card is accepted at over 4 million merchant locations across India.  
- No cost EMI offers available on a large selection of products.  

Application Process:  
- Customers can apply via the Amazon India app or website.  
- The card issuance process is 100% digital and paperless.  
- Digital card is issued instantly upon approval; physical card is delivered within a few days.  

KEY POINTS:  
- No joining or annual fees.  
- High reward rates for Amazon Prime members (5%) and other customers (3%) on Amazon.in.  
- Rewards on digital spends and Amazon Pay merchants.  
- Monthly reward credit to Amazon Pay balance.  
- Redeem rewards on a wide range of products and services.  
- Accepted at millions of merchant locations across India.  
- Fuel surcharge waiver and no cost EMI options.  
- Digital and physical card issuance.
