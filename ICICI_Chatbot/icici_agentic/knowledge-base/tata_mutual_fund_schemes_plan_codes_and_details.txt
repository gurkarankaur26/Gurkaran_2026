TITLE: Tata Mutual Fund Schemes - Plan Codes and Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Tata Mutual Fund, Fund Codes, Growth Plan, IDCW Plan, Investment Options  

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of Tata Mutual Fund schemes along with their respective plan types and unique identification codes. The schemes are categorized under different plans such as Regular (Reg) and Direct (Dir), and further divided into Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options. Each scheme is associated with a unique code for easy identification and transaction processing.

The schemes include various fund types such as Ethical Fund, Flexi Cap Fund, Focused Fund, India Consumer Fund, India Innovation Fund, India Pharma and Healthcare Fund, Large & Mid Cap Fund, Large Cap Fund, Mid Cap Fund, Multi Asset Allocation Fund, and Multicap Fund.

KEY POINTS:  
- Each Tata Mutual Fund scheme is available in Regular and Direct plans.  
- Plans are offered in Growth and IDCW options.  
- Unique identification codes are assigned to each scheme-plan combination for transaction and record-keeping purposes.  
- Fund types cover a wide range of investment strategies including sector-specific funds (e.g., Pharma and Healthcare), market capitalization-based funds (Large Cap, Mid Cap), and diversified funds (Multicap, Multi Asset Allocation).  
- Investors can select schemes based on their investment goals and preferred plan type.
