This document consolidates key banking information from ICICI Bank, including account types, eligibility criteria, interest rates, fees, loan products, credit card details, processes, benefits, and frequently asked questions.

1. Account Types and Eligibility:  
- Savings Account: Minimum age 18 years; valid KYC documents required.  
- Salary Account: For salaried individuals; employer tie-up required.  
- Fixed Deposit: Minimum deposit amount as specified; tenure options from 7 days to 10 years.  
- NRI Accounts: Eligibility for Non-Resident Indians with valid NRI documentation.

2. Interest Rates:  
- Savings Account: Interest rates vary; typically around 3.5% per annum (subject to change).  
- Fixed Deposits: Interest rates depend on tenure and amount; ranges from 5.5% to 7.5% per annum.  
- Loans: Home loan interest rates start from 6.75% per annum; personal loan rates vary based on credit profile.

3. Fees and Charges:  
- Account Maintenance: Some accounts may have minimum balance requirements; non-maintenance attracts charges.  
- Transaction Charges: Applicable for certain transactions beyond free limits; varies by account type.  
- Loan Processing Fees: Applicable as per loan product; varies by loan amount and tenure.  
- Credit Card Annual Fees: Varies by card type; some cards offer fee waivers on spending thresholds.

4. Loan Products:  
- Home Loans: Competitive interest rates, flexible tenure up to 30 years, balance transfer facility available.  
- Personal Loans: Quick processing, minimal documentation, flexible repayment options.  
- Car Loans: Attractive interest rates, easy documentation, loan tenure up to 7 years.  
- Education Loans: Covers tuition fees, living expenses; competitive interest rates.

5. Credit Cards:  
- Multiple variants catering to lifestyle, travel, cashback, and rewards.  
- Benefits include reward points, fuel surcharge waiver, airport lounge access, and EMI options.  
- Eligibility depends on income, credit score, and employment status.

6. Processes:  
- Account Opening: Online and branch-based; requires KYC documents such as ID proof, address proof, and photograph.  
- Loan Application: Online application with document upload; eligibility check and sanction process detailed on website.  
- Credit Card Application: Online application with income proof; instant approval on select cards.  
- Fund Transfer: NEFT, RTGS, IMPS available; UPI payments supported.

7. Benefits:  
- 24/7 Customer Support via phone, email, and chat.  
- Mobile Banking App with features like bill payments, fund transfers, and investment tracking.  
- Secure banking with two-factor authentication and fraud monitoring.  
- Personalized offers and discounts through partner merchants.

8. FAQs:  
- How to reset internet banking password? Through online portal or customer care.  
- What documents are required for loan application? ID proof, address proof, income proof, and property documents (for home loans).  
- How to check account balance? Via mobile app, SMS banking, or ATM.  
- What is the process for fixed deposit premature withdrawal? Visit branch or online portal; applicable penalties may apply.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank offers a wide range of banking products including savings, salary, fixed deposit, and NRI accounts.  
- Interest rates vary by product and tenure; loans start from competitive rates as low as 6.75% p.a.  
- Fees apply for non-maintenance of minimum balance, certain transactions, and loan processing.  
- Multiple loan products with flexible tenures and quick processing.  
- Credit cards with diverse benefits and eligibility criteria.  
- Convenient online and branch-based processes for account opening, loans, and fund transfers.  
- Robust customer support and secure digital banking platforms.  
- Comprehensive FAQs to assist customers with common queries.
