CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides car loan eligibility solutions tailored to your financial profile, helping you understand how much you qualify for based on income, credit score, and existing debts. The bank offers car loans for new and used cars, catering to salaried individuals, self-employed professionals, and self-employed non-professionals.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- **Credit Score:** Minimum CIBIL score generally starts at 750 or above, though final eligibility depends on the overall credit profile.  
- **Income Requirements:**  
  - Salaried individuals typically need a minimum monthly income of ₹30,000 to qualify. However, there is no fixed minimum salary; eligibility depends on net monthly income, existing financial obligations, credit score, and repayment capacity.  
  - Self-employed applicants must demonstrate stable income and financial capacity through business stability, financial records, tax returns, and repayment capability.  
- **Employment Status:** Permanent employment status is preferred for salaried applicants.  
- **Existing Debts:** Existing EMIs and Debt-to-Income (DTI) ratio are considered to assess repayment capacity.  

**Loan Amount and Repayment:**  
- The loan amount depends on income, credit score, existing EMIs, and repayment capacity.  
- For example, a salaried applicant with a monthly income of ₹70,000 will have their loan amount determined after evaluating their DTI ratio and credit profile.  
- Income proof is mandatory for eligibility assessment.  
- Eligible existing customers may receive pre-approved car loan offers with simplified documentation.  

**Additional Information:**  
- The salary required for a ₹10 lakh car loan varies based on loan tenure, EMI affordability, credit score, and existing obligations.  
- ICICI Bank offers flexible repayment options and competitive interest rates tailored to the applicant’s profile.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum CIBIL score: 750+ (varies by profile)  
- Minimum monthly income for salaried: ₹30,000 (no fixed minimum salary)  
- Self-employed applicants must provide financial records and tax returns  
- Permanent employment preferred for salaried applicants  
- Loan amount depends on income, credit score, existing debts, and repayment capacity  
- Income proof required for eligibility check  
- Pre-approved offers available for eligible existing customers  
- Flexible repayment options and competitive interest rates
