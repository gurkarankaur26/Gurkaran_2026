ICICI Bank’s Select Banking Salary Account is designed to offer exclusive privileges and benefits to salaried individuals. This account comes with no minimum balance requirement, making it convenient and accessible. Key features include complimentary lounge access on your debit card and preferential pricing on loans and Demat accounts, enhancing your banking and investment experience.

**Eligibility:**  
- Salaried individuals receiving salary credits through ICICI Bank.

**Key Benefits:**  
- No minimum balance requirement.  
- Complimentary lounge access with the debit card.  
- Exclusive pricing on personal loans, home loans, and other loan products.  
- Preferential rates and offers on Demat accounts and investment services.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- No charges for maintaining the salary account due to zero minimum balance criteria.  
- Standard charges apply for additional services as per ICICI Bank’s tariff schedule.

**Process to Open Account:**  
- Salary account is typically opened through employer tie-ups or by submitting salary proof and KYC documents at ICICI Bank branches or online.  

**FAQs:**  
- *Is there a minimum balance requirement?*  
  No, the Select Banking Salary Account has no minimum balance requirement.  
- *What are the loan benefits?*  
  Account holders get exclusive pricing and preferential interest rates on various loan products.  
- *How to avail lounge access?*  
  Lounge access is provided on the debit card issued with the salary account.
