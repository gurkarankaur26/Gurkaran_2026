TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card Important Information and Terms  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Reward Points, Lounge Access, Card Loss, Fraud, Disclosure, Fees  

CONTENT:  
1. **Loss, Theft, or Misuse of Card**  
- Card members must immediately notify the 24-hour Call Centre if the card is lost, stolen, or misused.  
- The Bank is not liable for any transactions incurred before the loss is reported.  
- Card members are responsible for any losses arising from fraudulent or careless actions on their part.  

2. **Reward Points**  
- Reward points are earned on transactions as per the applicable terms and conditions.  
- Points have a validity period after which they expire.  
- Certain transactions may lead to reversal of reward points as per the Bank’s policies.  

3. **Disclosure of Information**  
- ICICI Bank may disclose credit card-related information to credit bureaus without prior notice to the card member.  
- Card members are responsible for staying updated with regulatory guidelines and changes.  

4. **Lounge Access**  
- Complimentary lounge access is available depending on the card variant.  
- Charges apply if lounge access exceeds the complimentary limits.  
- Priority Pass cards are non-transferable and must be used safely by the card member.  
- Terms and conditions related to lounge access and charges are subject to change at the Bank’s discretion.  
- For detailed charges and lounge access information, refer to the Schedule of Charges available from ICICI Bank.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Immediate reporting of lost/stolen cards to 24-hour Call Centre is mandatory.  
- Bank is not liable for transactions before loss reporting.  
- Reward points earned on transactions have expiry and may be reversed.  
- Bank can share credit card information with credit bureaus without notice.  
- Lounge access benefits vary by card and may incur charges beyond limits.  
- Priority Pass cards are non-transferable and must be used responsibly.  
- Terms and charges are subject to change; refer to official Schedule of Charges.
