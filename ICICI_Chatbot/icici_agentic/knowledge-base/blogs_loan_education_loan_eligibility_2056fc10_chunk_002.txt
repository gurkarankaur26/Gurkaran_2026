TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Education Loans and Financial Products Overview

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Education Loans, Personal Finance, Banking Products

TAGS:  
Education Loan, Collateral-Free Loan, International Studies, Personal Loan, Home Loan, Car Loan, Credit Card, Investment, Insurance, Eligibility, Interest Rates

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers comprehensive financial support for education through Education Loans tailored for both domestic and international studies. The bank provides loans up to Rs 3 crore for international education and up to Rs 1 crore for domestic education. Notably, collateral-free loans are available for amounts up to Rs 1 crore, making it easier for students aiming to attend premium institutes to access funds without pledging security.

Specialized loan products are available for students planning to study in countries such as Canada, the UK, and Germany, offering customized financial solutions to meet their specific needs.

Eligibility for Education Loans depends on the student’s course and institution. Eligible courses include graduate, postgraduate, diploma, professional, technical, and job-oriented programs recognized by authoritative bodies such as UGC, AICTE, NAAC, NBA, Government authorities, AIBMS, or ICMR. The institution must be recognized either in India or abroad. The category of the institute and the type of course influence the loan amount, interest rate, and availability of collateral-free options.

Beyond Education Loans, ICICI Bank offers a wide range of financial products and services including Personal Loans, Home Loans, Car Loans, Credit Cards, Investments, and Insurance. These products support various financial needs such as funding personal aspirations, purchasing homes or vehicles, managing daily expenses, wealth growth, and financial protection.

KEY POINTS:  
- Education Loan amount: Up to Rs 3 crore for international studies; up to Rs 1 crore for domestic studies.  
- Collateral-free loans available up to Rs 1 crore.  
- Special loan products for studies in Canada, UK, and Germany.  
- Eligible courses: Graduate, postgraduate, diploma, professional, technical, and job-oriented programs recognized by UGC, AICTE, NAAC, NBA, Government authorities, AIBMS, or ICMR.  
- Institution must be recognized in India or abroad.  
- Loan amount, interest rate, and collateral requirements depend on course and institution category.  
- Additional financial products: Personal Loans, Home Loans, Car Loans, Credit Cards, Investments, Insurance.  
- ICICI Bank supports comprehensive financial needs from education to wealth management.
