TITLE:  
ICICI Bank FASTag Automatic Recharge and Standing Instructions (SI) Guide

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
FASTag, Payments, Standing Instructions, Auto Recharge

TAGS:  
FASTag, Standing Instructions, Auto Recharge, NEFT, IMPS, RTGS, UPI Mandate, CUG Wallet, Toll Payments

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to manage your FASTag wallet balance through Standing Instructions (SI) and automatic recharge options to ensure seamless toll payments without any disruption.

**Key Features and Processes:**

1. **Payment Modes for FASTag Recharge:**  
   - Only NEFT, IMPS, or RTGS payment modes are accepted for FASTag wallet recharge. Payments initiated through any other mode will be declined.

2. **Transfer from CUG Wallet to FASTag:**  
   - Amounts can be transferred from the ‘CUG Wallet’ to the FASTag wallet (referred to as ‘Tag’) either automatically or manually.  
   - Transactions through your FASTag are possible only when funds are transferred from the CUG Wallet to the Tag.

3. **Standing Instructions (SI) Setup:**  
   - SI allows automatic top-up of your FASTag wallet when the balance falls below a defined threshold.  
   - You can set SI via:  
     - Retail Internet Banking (RIB)  
     - Corporate Internet Banking (CIB)  
   - Example: If you set a threshold amount of ₹500 and a top-up amount of ₹1,000 with a start date, the system will automatically recharge ₹1,000 whenever your wallet balance falls below ₹500 starting from the specified date.

4. **How to Set SI:**  
   - Log in to Retail Internet Banking or Corporate Internet Banking.  
   - Navigate to ‘Payments’ > ‘Standing Instructions’ or ‘Statement and Activity’ > ‘Recent Activity’.  
   - Enter your Tag Account/Vehicle Number and CUG Wallet details.  
   - Define the threshold amount, top-up amount, and start date.  
   - Confirm and activate the SI.

5. **SI Execution and Failure Handling:**  
   - If there are insufficient funds in your linked Savings Account or if debit is barred, the system will retry the transaction after 8 hours.  
   - It will attempt 3 retries; if all fail, the SI will be automatically disabled.  
   - To re-enable SI, log in again to RIB and follow the activation steps.

6. **Disabling SI:**  
   - To disable SI, log in to RIB with the same User ID used to activate the SI and follow the disable instructions.

7. **Avoiding Toll Payment Hassles:**  
   - Insufficient funds in your FASTag wallet can cause delays and double toll charges at toll plazas.  
   - Automatic recharge via SI or UPI Mandate helps avoid such issues by ensuring your wallet is always funded.

8. **Auto Recharge via UPI Mandate:**  
   - ICICI Bank offers an auto recharge facility through UPI Mandate with frequency options as per your preference.  
   - This ensures your FASTag wallet is recharged automatically without manual intervention.

9. **Customer Support:**  
   - For any queries, customers can reach out to ICICI Bank’s toll-free number: 1800-2100-104.

**Important Notes:**  
- Ensure you use the correct Tag Account/Vehicle Number and CUG Wallet details while setting up SI.  
- Only payments through NEFT, IMPS, or RTGS are accepted for FASTag wallet top-ups.  
- Standing Instructions are linked to your internet banking credentials; use the same User ID for enabling or disabling SI.

KEY POINTS:  
- Accepted payment modes for FASTag recharge: NEFT, IMPS, RTGS only.  
- Automatic and manual transfer options from CUG Wallet to FASTag wallet.  
- Standing Instructions enable automatic top-up based on threshold and top-up amounts.  
- SI retries 3 times on failure before disabling automatically.  
- SI can be enabled/disabled via Retail or Corporate Internet Banking.  
- Auto recharge via UPI Mandate available for hassle-free wallet funding.  
- Toll-free customer support: 1800-2100-104.  
- Avoid double toll charges by maintaining sufficient FASTag wallet balance.
