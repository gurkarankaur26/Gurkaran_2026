TITLE: ICICI Bank Car Loan - Interest Rates, Eligibility, Tenure, and Benefits  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, Interest Rates, Eligibility, Tenure, EMI, Used Car Loan, New Car Loan, Guarantor, Down Payment  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Car Loans with flexible tenures ranging from 12 months to 84 months, allowing customers to choose a repayment period that suits their budget and repayment capacity. The interest rates on Car Loans vary depending on several factors including the applicant’s credit profile, type of vehicle (new or used), loan tenure, income profile, and prevailing market conditions.  

A good Annual Percentage Rate (APR) for a 72-month Car Loan depends on:  
- **Credit Profile:** Borrowers with a strong credit history and repayment track record may qualify for more competitive interest rates.  
- **Vehicle Type:** Interest rates for used cars are generally higher than for new cars due to lower resale value and higher risk.  
- **Loan Tenure:** Longer tenures reduce monthly EMIs but increase the total interest payable over the loan period.  
- **Market Interest Rates:** Prevailing market conditions and bank policies influence the APR.  

Applicants do not require a guarantor to apply for an ICICI Bank Car Loan. However, those with limited credit history or low income may be asked to provide a guarantor to facilitate quicker loan approval.  

Making a higher down payment reduces the loan amount, which can improve the chances of securing a lower interest rate and also reduces the EMI burden. ICICI Bank provides a Car Loan EMI Calculator to help customers estimate their monthly payments based on loan amount, tenure, and interest rate.  

In addition to Car Loans, ICICI Bank offers other financing options such as Personal Loans, Two-Wheeler Loans for bikes and scooters, and Home Loans for purchasing or constructing residential properties. These loans come with flexible tenures and competitive interest rates tailored to meet diverse customer needs.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan tenure: 12 to 84 months  
- Interest rates vary based on credit score, vehicle type, tenure, income, and market conditions  
- Used car loans have higher interest rates than new car loans  
- No guarantor required unless applicant has limited credit history or low income  
- Higher down payment can reduce interest rate and EMI  
- EMI Calculator available for loan planning  
- Other loan products: Personal Loans, Two-Wheeler Loans, Home Loans
