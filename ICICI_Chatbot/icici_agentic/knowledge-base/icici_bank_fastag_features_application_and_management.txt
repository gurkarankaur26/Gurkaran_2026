TITLE: ICICI Bank FASTag - Features, Application, and Management  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Services / FASTag  
TAGS: FASTag, Toll Payment, Electronic Toll Collection, ICICI Bank, Vehicle KYC, Toll Plaza, FASTag Wallet  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers FASTag services to enable automatic electronic toll payments at NETC-enabled toll plazas across India. Customers can apply for a FASTag through the ICICI Bank website, the FASTag Customer Portal, or authorized channels by submitting vehicle and KYC details.  

If your FASTag status shows as “HEXTAGCLOSED,” “CLOSED,” “PENDING CLOSED,” “REFUND REQUESTED,” “SUSPENDED,” or “WRITE OFF,” you must create a new FASTag account. Existing accounts can be closed by following the FASTag wallet closure process.  

To use FASTag, simply drive through the designated FASTag lane at toll plazas, where toll charges will be automatically deducted from your linked wallet or account.  

In case of loss or theft, customers should immediately log in to the FASTag Customer Portal or contact the toll-free number to block the FASTag to prevent misuse.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag enables automatic electronic toll deduction at NETC-enabled toll plazas across India.  
- Application can be made via ICICI Bank website, FASTag Customer Portal, or authorized channels with vehicle and KYC documents.  
- FASTag statuses requiring new account creation: HEXTAGCLOSED, CLOSED, PENDING CLOSED, REFUND REQUESTED, SUSPENDED, WRITE OFF.  
- Existing FASTag accounts can be closed through the FASTag wallet closure process.  
- Use designated FASTag lanes at toll plazas for seamless toll payments.  
- Immediate blocking of FASTag is possible via Customer Portal or toll-free number in case of loss or theft.
