TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card – Features, Benefits, and Rewards  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: ICICI Bank, Amazon Pay, Credit Card, Rewards, Cashback, Visa, No-cost EMI, Fuel Surcharge Waiver  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank, in partnership with Amazon Pay, has launched the ‘Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card’, a co-branded credit card designed for digitally savvy customers. This card offers an industry-leading rewards program and exclusive benefits for Amazon Prime members and other users.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- **Reward Points:**  
  - Amazon Prime members earn 5% reward points on purchases made on Amazon.in.  
  - Non-Prime customers earn 3% reward points on Amazon.in purchases.  
  - 2% reward points on spends at Amazon Pay partner merchants across various categories such as food delivery, utility payments, movie ticketing, and more.  
  - Reward points are uncapped and do not expire.  
  - Each reward point is equivalent to 1 Indian Rupee and can be redeemed on Amazon.in against a catalogue of over 160 million items or with merchants accepting Amazon Pay.  

- **Card Network:**  
  - The card is available exclusively on the Visa platform, allowing customers to earn rewards at millions of merchant locations across India where Visa is accepted.  

- **Additional Benefits:**  
  - Fuel surcharge waiver on fuel transactions.  
  - No-cost EMI options on purchases.  
  - Surprise bonus points during Amazon sale events and at Amazon Pay partner merchants.  

- **Target Audience:**  
  - Customers who frequently shop online, especially on Amazon.in.  
  - Amazon Prime members seeking higher rewards.  
  - Users looking for flexible redemption options and rich reward propositions.  

- **Customer Insights:**  
  - The card addresses the growing trend of online shopping in India and the demand for flexible, valuable reward programs.  

KEY POINTS:  
- 5% rewards for Amazon Prime members on Amazon.in purchases.  
- 3% rewards for other customers on Amazon.in.  
- 2% rewards on Amazon Pay partner merchants.  
- Reward points equal to 1 INR each, redeemable on Amazon.in or Amazon Pay merchants.  
- Reward points are uncapped and never expire.  
- Fuel surcharge waiver and no-cost EMI offers available.  
- Card is Visa-based, accepted widely across India.  
- Designed for digitally savvy customers and online shoppers.
