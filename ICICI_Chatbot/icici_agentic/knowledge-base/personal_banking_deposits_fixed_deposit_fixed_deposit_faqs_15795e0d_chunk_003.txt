TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit (FD) - Features, Interest Rates, Overdraft Facility, Nomination, and Taxation

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Fixed Deposits, Banking Products, Personal Finance

TAGS:  
Fixed Deposit, FD Interest Rates, Overdraft Facility, Nomination, Taxation, Form 15G, Form 15H, Premature Closure, Sweep-in Facility, Loan Collateral

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits (FDs) offer a secure investment option with competitive interest rates that vary based on deposit amount, tenure, and market conditions. The bank periodically updates FD interest rates on its official website.

**Interest Rates and Tenure:**  
- FD interest rates differ depending on the deposit amount and tenure.  
- Certain long-term FD tenures offer enhanced interest rates based on market conditions.  
- Rates are subject to change and should be verified on the ICICI Bank website before booking.  
- FD interest rates are uniform across all booking channels.

**Taxation and TDS:**  
- Interest earned on FDs is fully taxable as per the applicable income tax slab.  
- Investment in FDs qualifies for deduction under Section 80C, but interest income is taxable.  
- TDS is deducted if total interest earned across all FDs in a financial year exceeds the prescribed threshold.  
- To avoid TDS deduction, eligible individuals can submit Form 15G (below 60 years) or Form 15H (senior citizens) via:  
  - ICICI Bank iMobile app  
  - Net Banking  
  - Branch visit

**Premature Closure:**  
- No service charges for closing an FD.  
- Premature closure may attract interest rate adjustments or penalties as per bank policy.

**Overdraft Facility Against FD:**  
- ICICI Bank offers an overdraft facility against your FD, allowing withdrawal up to 90% of the deposit amount, subject to internal policies and FD type.  
- Interest on overdraft is linked to the FD interest rate and charged only on the utilized amount.  
- The FD continues to earn interest at the contracted rate even when an overdraft is availed.  
- Overdraft facility can be requested via:  
  - iMobile app  
  - Net Banking  
  - Branch  
- The facility is activated instantly for eligible customers.  
- Overdraft must be settled before or at FD maturity; the facility closes automatically on maturity.

**Nomination Facility:**  
- Nominee can be added at the time of FD creation or anytime later.  
- Nomination ensures easy claim of deposit amount by the nominee in case of unforeseen events.  
- Nominee details can be modified or replaced anytime by submitting a nomination update form through:  
  - iMobile app  
  - Net Banking  
  - Branch

**Sweep-in Facility:**  
- Allows linking FD to Savings or Current Account.  
- When linked account balance falls below a threshold, funds are automatically swept in from the FD, ensuring liquidity without breaking the entire deposit.

**Pledging FD as Loan Collateral:**  
- FDs can be pledged as collateral for loans and overdrafts.  
- Bank issues lien letter or certificate upon request.

**FD Calculator:**  
- Customers can enter deposit amount, tenure, and interest rate to estimate maturity amount and total interest earned.

KEY POINTS:  
- FD interest rates vary by amount and tenure; check latest rates on ICICI Bank website.  
- Interest earned is taxable; submit Form 15G/15H to avoid TDS if eligible.  
- No service charge for FD closure; premature closure may incur penalties.  
- Overdraft facility up to 90% of FD amount available with interest charged only on utilized amount.  
- Nomination can be added or updated anytime.  
- Sweep-in facility links FD to savings/current account for liquidity.  
- FDs can be pledged as collateral for loans.  
- FD calculator available for maturity and interest estimation.
