TITLE: Recurring Deposits (RD) - ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Deposits  
TAGS: Recurring Deposit, RD, Savings, Interest Rates, Tenure, Online Banking  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s Recurring Deposits (RD) allow customers to save regularly with flexible deposit options and attractive interest rates. The RD account can be opened and managed online, providing convenience and ease of access. This product is designed to help customers achieve their financial goals through disciplined savings.

Key Features:  
- Flexible tenure options to suit different savings goals  
- Attractive interest rates on deposits  
- Easy online account opening and management  
- Suitable for individuals looking to save regularly with fixed monthly deposits  

Eligibility:  
- Resident individuals can open an RD account  
- Minors can open an RD account with a guardian  

Interest Rates:  
- Interest rates are competitive and vary based on the tenure chosen  
- Interest is compounded quarterly and paid at maturity  

Deposit Amount:  
- Minimum monthly deposit amount is as per bank’s prevailing norms  
- Customers can choose the deposit amount as per their convenience  

Tenure:  
- Flexible tenure options ranging from 6 months to 10 years  

Premature Withdrawal:  
- Allowed subject to applicable penalties and terms  

Process to Open RD Account Online:  
1. Visit ICICI Bank’s official website or mobile app  
2. Select Recurring Deposit under deposits section  
3. Fill in the required personal and financial details  
4. Choose deposit amount and tenure  
5. Complete KYC verification if not already done  
6. Make the first deposit online to activate the account  

Benefits:  
- Helps inculcate a habit of regular savings  
- Earns higher interest compared to regular savings accounts  
- Flexible tenure and deposit amount options  
- Convenient online management and tracking of deposits  

KEY POINTS:  
- Flexible tenure: 6 months to 10 years  
- Interest compounded quarterly  
- Minimum monthly deposit as per bank norms  
- Premature withdrawal allowed with penalties  
- Online account opening and management  
- Suitable for resident individuals and minors with guardian
