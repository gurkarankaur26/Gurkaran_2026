TITLE: Benefits of Fixed Deposit (FD) Account in ICICI Bank, India  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Fixed Deposit  
TAGS: Fixed Deposit, FD, Interest Rates, Senior Citizens, Tax Saver FD, Tenure, Minimum Deposit  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Fixed Deposit (FD) offers a secure investment option with flexible tenure and attractive interest rates. The minimum amount required to open an FD is ₹10,000. Customers can choose a tenure ranging from 7 days up to 10 years, allowing for short-term and long-term investment planning.  

Senior citizens benefit from an additional 0.50% higher interest rate on their FD investments, enhancing their returns. ICICI Bank also provides Tax-Saver Fixed Deposits, which allow investors to save on taxes under applicable sections of the Income Tax Act.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum deposit amount: ₹10,000  
- Tenure options: 7 days to 10 years  
- Additional 0.50% interest rate for senior citizens  
- Tax-Saver FD available for tax deductions
