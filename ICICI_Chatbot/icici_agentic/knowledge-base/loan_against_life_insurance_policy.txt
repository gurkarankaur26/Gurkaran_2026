TITLE: Loan Against Life Insurance Policy  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Loan Against Insurance, Life Insurance Loan, Secured Loan, Loan Application, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Loan Against Life Insurance Policy, allowing policyholders to avail funds by leveraging their life insurance policies as collateral. This secured loan provides quick access to funds without affecting the validity of the insurance policy, provided the loan is repaid as per terms.

How To Apply:  
- Visit any ICICI Bank branch with your ID proof, income proof, and life insurance policy details.  
- Complete the loan application form with accurate personal and policy information.  
- Submit the form along with required documents to the bank representative for verification and processing.  
- Upon approval, the loan amount is disbursed promptly, often within a few working hours.

Documents Required:  
- Original life insurance policy documents  
- Address proof and ID proof  
- Income proof  
- ‘Deed of Assignment’ to assign the policy to ICICI Bank as the lender  

FAQs:  
1. How long does it take to get the loan amount disbursed?  
   - Typically, the loan amount is released within a few working hours after approval, provided all paperwork is complete and eligibility criteria are met.  

2. Does taking a loan against my life insurance policy affect my credit score?  
   - No, since this is a secured loan, your credit score is not affected by taking the loan. However, failure to repay the loan can lead to the bank surrendering the policy and may negatively impact your credit score. The policy’s validity remains unaffected if the loan is repaid properly.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan against life insurance is a secured loan with quick disbursal.  
- Requires original policy documents, ID, address proof, income proof, and deed of assignment.  
- Loan disbursal usually within a few working hours after approval.  
- No impact on credit score if loan is repaid timely.  
- Non-repayment may lead to policy surrender and credit score impact.
