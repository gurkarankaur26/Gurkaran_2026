ICICI Bank Business Current Account Features and Services

SOURCE:

CATEGORY:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive Business Current Account designed to streamline business transactions and cash flow management with integrated payment and collection solutions.

**Payment and Collection Integration:**  
- Onboard ICICI Bank’s payment gateway and integrate it with your website or mobile app for real-time settlement and consolidated reporting.  
- Supports multiple payment acceptance modes including UPI, QR codes, POS, and card payments. Funds from these channels settle into your current account as per the agreed settlement schedule and tariffs.  
- Export receipts can be routed through ICICI Bank’s trade and foreign-exchange services, allowing foreign currency collections to be credited to EEFC or RFC accounts where eligible, or converted to INR at competitive forex rates.

**Fund Transfer and Settlement Timelines:**  
- IMPS credits are immediate.  
- NEFT settlements are processed in scheduled batches.  
- RTGS transactions are real-time during operating hours.  
- Incoming funds are posted to your current account as per standard clearing timelines.

**Payment Automation and Cashflow Management:**  
- Standing instructions and mandates for repeat payments such as vendor payments, subscriptions, or rent can be configured via corporate netbanking or through your Relationship Manager.  
- Merchant refunds and chargebacks for card and gateway transactions are facilitated with reconciliation and dispute-resolution support.  
- Supports multiple file formats (CSV, Excel, MT940, etc.) and APIs compatible with leading ERP and accounting platforms for direct upload and automated reconciliation.

**Sweep Facility:**  
- Sweep-in automatically transfers surplus funds from the current account into term deposits to earn interest.  
- Sweep-out converts term deposits back to current funds when transactional thresholds are crossed, maintaining liquidity and optimizing returns.  
- Sweep features may be part of a packaged current-account solution; applicable charges are detailed in the Schedule of Charges or negotiated as part of the corporate relationship.

**Payment Scheduling and Bulk Payouts:**  
- Enables payment scheduling, bulk payouts, and approval workflows to control timing of large debits and coordinate liquidity planning, helping avoid overdraft triggers.

**Overdraft Facility:**  
- Repayment and reuse of sanctioned overdraft limits are generally immediate once funds are credited to the current account and in compliance with covenants.  
- Updated facility availability is reflected in real time on ICICI Bank’s systems.

**Account Closure:**  
- Submit closure requests via your Relationship Manager or branch after settling all dues and clearing pending transactions.  
- ICICI Bank will guide you on closure formalities, final settlement, and return of unused cheque leaves.  
- Closure is processed after clearing obligations and may require settling outstanding fees or overdrafts. Large corporate closures follow standard exit procedures as per the Schedule of Charges.

KEY POINTS:  
- Real-time settlement and consolidated reporting via integrated payment gateway.  
- Supports UPI, QR, POS, card acceptance with scheduled fund settlements.  
- Foreign currency collections credited to EEFC/RFC accounts or converted at competitive rates.  
- IMPS immediate, NEFT batch-wise, RTGS real-time during operating hours.  
- Standing instructions for repeat payments via netbanking or Relationship Manager.  
- Merchant refunds and chargeback support with reconciliation.  
- Multiple file formats and APIs for automated reconciliation with ERP systems.  
- Sweep-in/out facility to optimize liquidity and earn interest on surplus funds.  
- Payment scheduling, bulk payouts, and approval workflows to manage cash flow.  
- Overdraft limits are reusable immediately upon fund credit, reflected in real time.  
- Account closure requires settling dues; process guided by Relationship Manager and Schedule of Charges.
