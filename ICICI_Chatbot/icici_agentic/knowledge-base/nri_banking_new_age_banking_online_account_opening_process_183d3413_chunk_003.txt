TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Savings Account Inactive and Dormant Account Policies and Activation Process

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Savings Account Management

TAGS:  
Inactive Account, Dormant Account, Account Activation, KYC, FATCA, Funds Transfer, Chequebook, ATM Card, Account Restrictions

CONTENT:  
**Definitions:**  
- **Inactive Account:** A savings account with no transactions for more than 12 months is classified as inactive.  
- **Dormant Account:** A savings account with no transactions for more than 24 months is classified as dormant.  

**Purpose:**  
These classifications safeguard your interest by alerting you to inactivity. A notification letter will be sent to your registered communication address.

**Restrictions:**  
- **Inactive Account:**  
  - Chequebook issuance requests are not permitted.  

- **Dormant Account:**  
  - Requests for chequebook issuance are not permitted.  
  - Renewal of ATM/Debit Card is not allowed.  
  - Requests for change of address, addition or deletion of joint holders, and signature modifications are not allowed.  
  - Withdrawals through ATM, Net Banking, or Branch are not permitted.  

**Activation Process:**  
- **Inactive Account Activation:**  
  To reactivate an inactive account, conduct any transaction via cheque, ATM, or Infinity banking. Alternatively, visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch in India and submit an account activation letter.  

- **Dormant Account Activation:**  
  To normalize a dormant account, use one of the following methods:  

  **Direct Courier Method:**  
  1. Fill the Dormancy Activation Request Form.  
  2. Attach KYC documents:  
     - Proof of address where you want to receive communications.  
     - First two and last two pages of your valid passport.  
     - Documents must be signed and attested by a local Bank, Notary Authority, or Indian Embassy if residing outside India.  
  3. Submit FATCA form if applicable. If your country of residence has changed in the last two years, submit the FATCA/CRS form along with a copy of your visa.  
  4. Send documents via:  
     - PO Box services available in USA, Singapore, United Kingdom, UAE, Australia, and Canada, OR  
     - Courier to:  
       ICICI Bank Limited,  
       RPC Mumbai, 5th Floor, A-Wing,  
       Autumn Estates, Chandivali Farm Road,  
       Chandivali, Next to Chandivali Studio,  
       Opp MHADA,  
       Andheri-East, Mumbai-400072, INDIA.  

**Funds Transfer Registration:**  
- To transfer funds online, register a payee under the relevant section (Other ICICI Bank Account, Non-ICICI Bank Account, or Card-2-Card Funds Transfer).  
- After entering payee details, registration will be pending confirmation. No transfers are allowed until confirmation.  
- You will receive an email alert with a Unique Registration Number (URN).  
- Login to Net Banking and confirm the payee registration by entering the URN in the “confirm payee” section.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Inactive account: no transactions for 12+ months; dormant account: no transactions for 24+ months.  
- Inactive accounts cannot request chequebooks; dormant accounts have multiple restrictions including no ATM withdrawals or address changes.  
- Activation requires transaction or submission of documents depending on account status.  
- Dormant account activation requires KYC documents, FATCA form (if applicable), and attestation if residing abroad.  
- Funds transfer requires payee registration and confirmation via URN.
