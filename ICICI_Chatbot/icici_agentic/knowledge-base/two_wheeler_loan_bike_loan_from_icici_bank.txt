CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers competitive interest rates on two wheeler loans, making it easier for customers to finance their dream bike. The loan is designed to provide quick and convenient financing options for purchasing new or used two wheelers.  

Key features of the Two Wheeler Loan include:  
- Attractive interest rates tailored to customer profiles  
- Flexible loan tenure options  
- Quick processing and disbursal  
- Minimal documentation requirements  
- Option to apply online or through ICICI Bank branches  

Eligibility Criteria:  
- Applicant should be an Indian resident  
- Minimum age: 21 years  
- Maximum age at loan maturity: 60 years  
- Stable source of income or employment  

Loan Amount and Tenure:  
- Loan amount varies based on the value of the two wheeler and applicant’s repayment capacity  
- Tenure options typically range from 12 to 60 months  

Interest Rates and Fees:  
- Interest rates are competitive and vary based on credit profile and loan amount  
- Processing fees and other charges may apply as per bank’s policy  

Application Process:  
- Contact ICICI Bank through their official website or visit the nearest branch  
- Submit required documents including identity proof, address proof, income proof, and two wheeler details  
- Loan application is processed and approved based on eligibility and credit assessment  
- Upon approval, loan amount is disbursed directly to the dealer or customer’s account  

Benefits:  
- Easy financing for new and used two wheelers  
- Flexible repayment options to suit customer needs  
- Quick and hassle-free loan approval  
- Competitive interest rates to reduce EMI burden  

FAQs:  
Q: Can I apply for a two wheeler loan online?  
A: Yes, ICICI Bank provides an online application facility for two wheeler loans.  

Q: What documents are required for the loan application?  
A: Identity proof, address proof, income proof, and details of the two wheeler are required.  

Q: Is there a prepayment option available?  
A: Yes, prepayment options are available as per the bank’s terms and conditions.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Competitive interest rates on two wheeler loans
