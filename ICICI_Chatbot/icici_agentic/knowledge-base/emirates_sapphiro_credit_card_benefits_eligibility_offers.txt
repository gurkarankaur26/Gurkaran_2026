TITLE: Emirates Sapphiro Credit Card - Benefits, Eligibility & Offers  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Emirates Sapphiro, Credit Card, Offers, Eligibility, BookMyShow, Complimentary Tickets, Add-on Card  

CONTENT:  
The Emirates Sapphiro Credit Card from ICICI Bank offers exclusive benefits including complimentary movie and event tickets via BookMyShow, subject to specific spending criteria.  

**Complimentary Tickets Offer:**  
- To be eligible for complimentary movie/event tickets, cardholders must spend ₹25,000 or more on their Emirates Sapphiro Credit Card within the preceding quarter.  
- The offer is valid in the calendar quarter following the spends quarter.  

**Spends and Offer Eligibility Period:**  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Dec 26, 2025, and Mar 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie offers between Apr 1, 2026, and June 30, 2026.  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Mar 26, 2026, and June 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie offers between July 1, 2026, and Sep 30, 2026.  

**Additional Details:**  
- Customers holding dual network credit cards (Mastercard & Amex or Visa & RuPay) can avail one complimentary movie offer per month, per card variant.  
- Any additional bookings beyond the complimentary benefit will be charged as per the rates set by the service provider.  
- Add-on/Plus card members must individually meet the ₹25,000 spend requirement in the preceding quarter to be eligible for the offer in the subsequent quarter.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum spend of ₹25,000 per quarter required to avail complimentary movie/event tickets.  
- Complimentary ticket offers are valid only in the calendar quarter following the spend quarter.  
- Dual network cardholders can avail one complimentary ticket per month per variant.  
- Add-on cardholders must meet spend criteria individually.  
- Additional bookings beyond complimentary tickets are chargeable.
