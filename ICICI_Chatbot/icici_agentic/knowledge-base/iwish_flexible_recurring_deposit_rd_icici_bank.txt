CONTENT:  
A Recurring Deposit (RD) is a savings instrument that allows you to save small amounts at fixed intervals, making it easier to build savings without a large initial investment. Unlike Fixed Deposits (FDs), RDs require monthly contributions. However, traditional RDs require a fixed monthly deposit, which may not suit individuals with variable income or expenses.

ICICI Bank offers a flexible RD product called **iWish Flexible Recurring Deposit**, designed to provide greater convenience and flexibility for savers. This product is available exclusively to ICICI Bank Savings Account holders with Internet Banking activated.

**Key Features of iWish Flexible RD:**  
- **Flexible Deposit Amount:** You can deposit any amount at any time, with no fixed monthly commitment.  
- **Multiple Deposits:** You can make multiple deposits in a month, unlike regular RDs which allow only one fixed deposit per month.  
- **No Penalty for Missed Payments:** There is no penalty if you miss a deposit, unlike regular RDs which impose penalties for missed payments.  
- **Minimum Initial Deposit:** Rs. 50  
- **Goal-Based Savings:** You can set specific savings goals such as Vacation, Education, Electronics, Shopping, Down payment, etc., specifying the target amount and time frame.  
- **Interest Rates:** Interest rates are similar to regular ICICI Bank RDs for the same tenure. Interest is calculated on the amount and tenure of each deposit.  

**How to Open iWish Flexible RD:**  
1. Log in to ICICI Bank Internet Banking.  
2. Navigate to My Accounts > Deposits > iWish-Flexible RD.  
3. Click on ‘Start iWish’.  
4. Enter details such as the type of goal, goal amount, time period, and initial deposit amount.  
5. Transfer the initial deposit from your Savings Account to activate the iWish RD.  

**Benefits of iWish Flexible RD:**  
- Flexibility to save according to your income and expenditure patterns.  
- Ability to contribute more when income is high and less when income is low.  
- No penalties for missed or irregular deposits.  
- Helps in disciplined savings towards specific financial goals.
