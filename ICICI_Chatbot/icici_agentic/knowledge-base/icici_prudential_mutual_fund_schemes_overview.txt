TITLE: ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund Schemes Overview  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds, Investment Products  
TAGS: ICICI Prudential, Mutual Funds, Bond Funds, Debt Funds, Growth, IDCW, Investment Options  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential offers a wide range of mutual fund schemes primarily focused on debt and bond funds. These schemes cater to different investor needs with options for growth and income distribution cum capital withdrawal (IDCW) plans. The funds are available in direct and regular plans with various payout frequencies such as weekly, monthly, quarterly, and half-yearly IDCW.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes include:  
- ICICI Prudential All Seasons Bond Fund (Growth, Weekly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Banking & PSU Debt Fund (Direct Growth, Quarterly IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Bond Fund (Direct Growth, Monthly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW, Half-Yearly IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential BSE Liquid Rate ETF (IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Constant Maturity Gilt Fund (Direct and Regular Growth, Quarterly IDCW)  
- ICICI Prudential Corporate Bond Fund (Direct and Regular Growth, Monthly and Quarterly IDCW)  

These funds invest in a diversified portfolio of debt and money market instruments, government securities, corporate bonds, and PSU bonds, aiming to provide regular income and capital appreciation with relatively lower risk compared to equity funds.

Investors can choose between:  
- Growth Option: Earnings are reinvested to generate capital appreciation.  
- IDCW Option: Periodic income distribution in the form of dividends.  

Plans:  
- Direct Plan: Lower expense ratio as there is no distributor commission.  
- Regular Plan: Includes distributor commission.  

Payout Frequencies for IDCW:  
- Weekly  
- Monthly  
- Quarterly  
- Half-Yearly  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple bond and debt fund schemes available under ICICI Prudential Mutual Funds.  
- Options for Growth and IDCW (income distribution) with various payout frequencies.  
- Direct and Regular plans available to suit investor preferences.  
- Investment in government securities, PSU bonds, corporate bonds, and money market instruments.  
- Suitable for investors seeking regular income or capital appreciation with moderate risk.  
- Fund codes are available for each scheme and plan variant for easy identification.
