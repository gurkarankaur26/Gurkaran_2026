ICICI Bank offers convenient, flexible, and quick Car Loans with minimal documentation to ensure a hassle-free application process. The required documents vary based on the applicant’s employment type: Salaried, Self Employed Professional, or Self Employed Non-Professional.

**Documents Required:**

| Document                          | Salaried                     | Self Employed Professional                      | Self Employed Non Professional                  |
|----------------------------------|------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|
| Application Form                 | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| KYC Documents                   | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Photographs                     | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Proof of Identity               | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Proof of Address                | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Age Proof                      | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Bank Statements                | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Signature Verification         | Required                     | Required                                       | Required                                        |
| Income Proof                   | Latest Salary Slip or Form 16 | Income Tax Returns of last 2 financial years with complete financial/audit report | Income Tax Returns of last 2 financial years    |
| Other Documentation            | N/A                          | Business Stability or Ownership Proof          | Partnership deed and letter signed by all partners authorizing one partner (if applicable) |
| Companies and Societies        | N/A                          | Resolution by Board of Directors and Memorandum & Articles of Association | N/A                                             |
| Udyam Registration Certificate (URC) | Mandatory for Micro & Small Enterprises (MSE) classification (subject to norms) | Mandatory for Micro & Small Enterprises (MSE) classification (subject to norms) | Mandatory for Micro & Small Enterprises (MSE) classification (subject to norms) |
