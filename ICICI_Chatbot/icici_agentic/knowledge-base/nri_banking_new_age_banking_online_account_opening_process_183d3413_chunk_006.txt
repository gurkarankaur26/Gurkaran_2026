TITLE:  
ICICI Bank - Linking Multiple Savings Accounts and Document Submission for NRIs

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Banking Services, NRI Banking, Net Banking

TAGS:  
ICICI Bank, NRI, Savings Account, Net Banking, Account Linking, Document Submission, Customer Care

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank customers can link all their savings accounts under one user ID for easier management. This can be done online through the "Service Request" option available in Net Banking. Alternatively, customers can download the request form and mail it to ICICI Bank’s India address or submit it at any ICICI Bank branch in India.

For NRIs residing in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE, documents can be posted to the local P.O. Box in their respective countries to save on courier charges.
