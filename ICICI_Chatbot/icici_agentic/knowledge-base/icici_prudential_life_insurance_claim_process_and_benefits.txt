SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Insurance Claims  
TAGS: ICICI Prudential, Life Insurance, Claim Process, Claim Documents, Nominee, Legal Heir, Claim Payment  

CONTENT:  
This document outlines the process for submitting claim documents for ICICI Prudential Life Insurance, eligibility for claim benefits, and the modes of claim payment.

**Where to Submit Claim Documents:**  
- **Branch:** Submit documents at the nearest ICICI Prudential Life Insurance branch. Locate branches at www.iciciprulife.com/branchlocator  
- **Website:** Upload documents online via www.iciciprulife.com > Claims > File a Claim  
- **WhatsApp:** Type ‘Claim’ on WhatsApp number 9920667766 to check claim status and receive assistance  
- **Mail:** Courier documents to ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Limited, 9th Floor, B Wing, Office No. 906, BSEL Tech Park, Opp. Vashi Station, Section 30, Vashi, Navi Mumbai 400706  

**Entitlement to Claim Benefit:**  
- The claim benefit is paid to the nominee mentioned in the policy.  
- If no nominee is available, the legal heir is entitled to receive the benefit.  

**In Case of No Nominee or Predeceased Nominee:**  
- The claim benefit is paid to the legal heir as per succession laws.  
- The claimant must provide legal proof of relationship with the deceased policyholder.  

**Mode of Claim Payment:**  
- Electronic transfer to the registered bank account of the beneficiary.  
- Cheque payment if electronic transfer is not feasible.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple channels available for claim submission: branch, online, WhatsApp, and mail.  
- Nominee is the primary beneficiary; legal heir is secondary if no nominee exists.  
- Legal proof required if claiming as a legal heir.  
- Claim payments are primarily made via electronic transfer; cheque is an alternative.  
- WhatsApp number for claim status and assistance: 9920667766
