ICICI Prudential Mutual Fund Schemes Overview

SOURCE:

CATEGORY:

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Prudential offers a wide range of mutual fund schemes catering to different investment needs and preferences. These schemes include equity funds, debt funds, hybrid funds, and sector-specific funds. Each scheme is available in different plans such as Direct and Regular, and options like Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

Key Mutual Fund Schemes include:

1. **Equity Funds**  
   - ICICI Prudential BSE Sensex Index Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Business Cycle Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Dividend Yield Equity Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Energy Opportunities Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Equity - Arbitrage Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Equity & Debt Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Equity Minimum Variance Fund  
   - ICICI Prudential Children’s Fund  

2. **Plan Types**  
   - Direct Plan: Investors invest directly without distributor commission, resulting in lower expense ratio.  
   - Regular Plan: Investments routed through distributors, includes commission fees.

3. **Options**  
   - Growth Option: Earnings are reinvested, increasing NAV.  
   - IDCW Option: Periodic income distribution to investors.

4. **Dividend Frequency**  
   - Annual  
   - Half-Yearly  
   - Monthly

Investors can choose plans and options based on their investment horizon, risk appetite, and income requirements.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple schemes available under ICICI Prudential Mutual Funds covering various sectors and investment styles.  
- Plans available in Direct and Regular modes.  
- Options include Growth and IDCW with different dividend payout frequencies.  
- Suitable for investors seeking capital appreciation, regular income, or balanced investment.  
- Enables diversification across equity, debt, and hybrid funds.
