CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank acts as a Corporate Agent registered with the Insurance Regulatory & Development Authority of India (IRDAI) under Registration No. CA0112 (valid till 31st March 2026) for distribution of insurance products. The bank has corporate agency agreements with ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company Limited for life insurance and ICICI Lombard General Insurance Company Limited for general insurance.

Insurance products offered through ICICI Bank are underwritten solely by the respective insurance companies. ICICI Bank does not underwrite risks or act as an insurer. The insurance contract exists only between the insured and the insurance company. ICICI Bank is not liable for any obligations or performance under the insurance contract.

Insurance products are sold as standalone products and are not linked to any banking products or services. Purchasing insurance is voluntary and not a precondition for availing any banking services.

NRIs interested in life insurance can access a range of products through ICICI Prudential Life Insurance via ICICI Bank. Customers are advised to carefully read the product brochure for details on risk factors, terms, conditions, and exclusions before purchasing.

For grievances related to insurance, customers can approach the IRDAI grievance management system at igms.irda.gov.in.
