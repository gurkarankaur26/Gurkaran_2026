SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers the iMobile app tailored for NRI customers to facilitate secure and convenient mobile banking. The app incorporates enhanced security features including mandatory SIM Binding authentication for NRIs.

**How to Download iMobile App:**  
- **App Store / Play Store:** Search and install ‘iMobile’  
- **SMS:** Send SMS with text "iMobile" to 5676766 to receive the download link  
- **Browser:** Visit mobile.icicibank.com/dl from your mobile browser  

**Activation Process for NRI Customers:**  
1. Install the iMobile app from App Store or Play Store.  
2. Open the app and tap on "Let's Get Started".  
3. Tap on "Proceed" to verify your mobile number.  
4. Select the SIM card of the registered mobile number and click "Continue".  
5. Create your MPIN (Mobile Personal Identification Number).  
6. Verify your identity using either:  
   - **Debit Card:** Authenticate using your debit card grid number, then enable fingerprint or face ID for login.  
   - **Net Banking ID:** Authenticate using your Internet banking user ID and password, then enable fingerprint or face ID for login.  

**Important Notes:**  
- SIM Binding authentication is mandatory for NRI customers to enhance security.  
- Fingerprint or face ID login is available after initial authentication.  

KEY POINTS:  
- iMobile app is available on App Store, Play Store, via SMS link, or browser download.  
- Mandatory SIM Binding authentication for NRIs ensures enhanced security.  
- MPIN creation is required for app activation.  
- Two verification methods: Debit Card grid number or Net Banking ID.  
- Biometric login (fingerprint/face ID) can be enabled post-authentication.
