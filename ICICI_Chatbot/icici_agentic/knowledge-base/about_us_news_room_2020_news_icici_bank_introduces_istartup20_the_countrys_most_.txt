TITLE:  
ICICI Bank introduces ‘iStartup2.0’, the country’s most comprehensive programme for startups

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Startup Banking, Business Accounts, Banking Services

TAGS:  
iStartup2.0, Startup Banking, Current Account, Relationship Manager, Digital Concierge, Startup Assist, Business Banking, ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank has launched ‘iStartup2.0’, a comprehensive programme designed to cater to the banking and beyond-banking needs of startups. The programme offers an exclusive Current Account available in three variants—Platinum, Gold, and Silver—providing the widest range of startup accounts in the country. Eligible startups include new businesses up to 10 years old, such as partnerships, private and public limited companies, and limited liability partnerships.

Key features of the iStartup2.0 Current Account include:  
- Instant account opening and account number issuance at the time of company incorporation through API integration with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) website, eliminating the need to re-enter details.  
- On-demand physical verification of original KYC documents by an ICICI Bank official at a time convenient to the startup.  
- Dedicated Relationship Manager for personalized banking support.  
- Premium savings accounts for promoters and salary accounts for employees.  
- Waiver of quarterly average balance requirement for one year.  
- Customizable account facilities tailored to startup needs.  
- Single account usage for both domestic and international trade transactions with preferential pricing, an industry-first service.

Beyond banking, the programme offers ‘Startup Assist’, a suite of services in partnership with external providers to support startups at various stages:  
- ‘Set-up’ Concierge: Access to experts at discounted rates for company registration, taxation, compliance, logistics, facility management, and other complex services.  
- ‘Digital Concierge’: One-stop digital solutions including website development and hosting, social media marketing, SEO, and SEM to facilitate digital transformation.  

These services are accessible via a dedicated concierge helpline, app, or email, providing startups with a single point of contact for multiple business needs.
