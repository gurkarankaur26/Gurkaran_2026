TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Net Banking and Mobile Banking Registration and Password Generation

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Digital Banking Services

TAGS:  
Net Banking, Mobile Banking, User ID, Password, Registration, Customer Care, NRI Services

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers convenient options for customers to register for Net Banking and Mobile Banking services, generate or recover passwords, and manage their accounts under one user ID.

**Net Banking Registration and Password Generation:**  
- Customers can apply online through the "Service Request" option available in Net Banking to link multiple accounts under one user ID.  
- Alternatively, customers can download the request form, fill it, and mail it to the following address:  
  ICICI Bank Limited  
  Net Banking Department  
  6th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali  
  Landmark: Next to Chandivali Studio, Opp MHADA  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai-400072, INDIA  

- NRIs residing in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can post their documents to the local P.O. Box in their respective countries to save on courier charges.  
- Customers may also visit any ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the request form in person. Valid identity proof is required.  
- To generate or activate a user ID, customers can call the 24-hour Customer Care service or send a duly signed request letter to:  
  ICICI Bank Limited,  
  RPC Mumbai,  
  5th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates,  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali,  
  Landmark: Next to Chandivali Studio, Opp MHADA,  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai-400072, INDIA  

- Forgotten user IDs or passwords can be retrieved by calling the 24-hour Customer Care Centre after authentication. Passwords are sent by courier to the registered address for security reasons and are not provided over the phone.  

**Mobile Banking Registration:**  
- Customers can subscribe to Mobile Banking by downloading the registration form from the ICICI Bank website, filling it out, and mailing it with relevant documents to the address mentioned above.  
- NRIs can post documents to the local P.O. Box in their respective countries.  
- Alternatively, customers can visit any ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the Mobile Banking registration form with valid identity proof.  

**Customer Support:**  
- For any clarifications or more information, customers can contact the 24-hour Customer Care or write through the "Email us" option on the ICICI Bank website.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Net Banking and Mobile Banking registration can be done online, by mail, or at any ICICI Bank branch.  
- NRIs have dedicated P.O. Boxes in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE for document submission.  
- Passwords are sent by courier and not disclosed over the phone for security.  
- 24-hour Customer Care is available for assistance with user ID and password issues.  
- Valid identity proof is required for branch submissions.
