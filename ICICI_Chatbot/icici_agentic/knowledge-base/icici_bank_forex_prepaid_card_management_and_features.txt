CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Forex Prepaid Card allows customers to manage their card conveniently and securely through multiple digital platforms. Users can log in to view their card balance, reset PINs, block or unblock the card, and make wallet transfers anytime, anywhere.  

Management Options:  
- **Net Banking:** Access your Forex Prepaid Card by logging into ICICI Bank’s Net Banking platform. Link your card to your account for easy and seamless management.  
- **iMobile App:** Use the ICICI Bank iMobile app to manage your Forex Prepaid Card on the go. After logging in, link your card to start managing your card features.  
- **Self Care Portal:** Register and log in to the exclusive Self Care Portal dedicated to Forex Prepaid Cardholders for 24/7 card management.  

Key Features:  
- View card balance and transaction history  
- Reset or change card PIN securely  
- Block or unblock the card instantly in case of loss or theft  
- Transfer funds to and from the Forex Prepaid Card wallet  
- Link card to bank account for easy reload and management  

The digital platforms provide secure and seamless access to all card-related services, ensuring convenience and control for the cardholder.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Manage Forex Prepaid Card via Net Banking, iMobile app, or Self Care Portal  
- Features include balance check, PIN reset, card block/unblock, and wallet transfers  
- Secure digital access anytime, anywhere  
- Linking card to bank account simplifies reload and management
