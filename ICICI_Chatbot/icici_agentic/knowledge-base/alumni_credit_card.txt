CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Alumni Credit Card is an exclusive credit card designed specifically for college alumni. It offers lifelong privileges with zero cost, making it an attractive option for former students who want to enjoy banking benefits without any joining or annual fees.

Key Features:  
- No joining fee  
- No annual fee  
- Exclusive privileges tailored for college alumni  

Eligibility:  
- Must be an alumnus of the selected college  

Benefits:  
- Enjoy lifelong privileges associated with the card  
- Access to various offers and discounts exclusive to alumni cardholders  

Fees and Charges:  
- Joining Fee: NIL  
- Annual Fee: NIL  

Process to Apply:  
- Select your college from the list provided by ICICI Bank  
- Complete the application process online or at the nearest ICICI Bank branch  

FAQs:  
Q: Is there any fee to get the Alumni Credit Card?  
A: No, there is no joining or annual fee for this card.  

Q: Who is eligible to apply for this card?  
A: Only alumni of the selected colleges are eligible to apply.  

Q: What are the benefits of this card?  
A: The card offers lifelong privileges and exclusive offers for alumni.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Exclusive credit card for college alumni  
- No joining fee and no annual fee  
- Lifelong privileges and exclusive offers  
- Eligibility limited to alumni of selected colleges
