TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Savings Account Features and Overdraft Facility

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Banking Products

TAGS:  
Savings Account, Overdraft Facility, Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit, Interest Rates, Biometric Verification, Debit Card, Rewards, Online Banking

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank savings accounts offer a range of features designed to provide convenience, security, and financial flexibility to customers. These accounts come with biometric verification through the mobile application, ensuring that only registered users can access their funds securely.

Most savings accounts include a debit card that can be used for ATM withdrawals, online shopping, and in-store purchases. Each debit card transaction earns reward points, adding value to everyday expenses.

Savings accounts can be linked to Fixed Deposits (FDs) and Recurring Deposits (RDs), allowing customers to earn higher interest rates while maintaining liquidity. This integration helps customers manage their finances efficiently by combining savings with investment options.

A key benefit of ICICI Bank savings accounts is the unsecured overdraft facility. This facility provides a financial safety net by allowing customers to access funds up to Rs. 5 lakhs without collateral. The overdraft is pre-approved for eligible customers and can be activated instantly through online loan processing, with no paperwork required.

Interest is charged only on the amount utilized, which can reduce borrowing costs compared to traditional loans where interest is charged on the entire loan amount. There are no commitment charges, prepayment fees, or foreclosure charges, offering customers greater financial flexibility.

The savings account features competitive interest rates, easy accessibility, robust security measures, and favourable minimum balance requirements. Customers are encouraged to evaluate their banking needs and select the account that best aligns with their financial goals.

KEY POINTS:  
- Biometric verification via ICICI Bank mobile app for secure access  
- Debit card included with rewards points on transactions  
- Link savings account with Fixed Deposits (FDs) and Recurring Deposits (RDs) for higher interest earnings  
- Unsecured overdraft facility up to Rs. 5 lakhs, pre-approved for eligible customers  
- Instant online activation of overdraft facility with no paperwork  
- Interest charged only on the utilized overdraft amount  
- No commitment charges, prepayment fees, or foreclosure charges on overdraft  
- Competitive interest rates and favourable minimum balance requirements  
- Additional benefits include enhanced security and seamless integration with other banking products
