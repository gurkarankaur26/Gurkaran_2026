**Key Terms:**  
- **Direct Plan:** Investment made directly with the fund house without intermediaries, usually with lower expense ratios.  
- **Regular Plan:** Investment made through intermediaries or distributors.  
- **Growth Option:** Earnings are reinvested in the scheme, increasing NAV.  
- **IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal):** Periodic dividend payouts to investors.  
- **Flexi IDCW:** Dividend payout frequency is flexible as per scheme terms.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple UTI Mutual Fund schemes are available with various dividend payout options and plan types.  
- Each scheme and option has a unique code for identification and investment purposes.  
- Investors can choose between Direct and Regular plans based on their preference for expense ratio and intermediary involvement.  
- Dividend options include Growth, Annual, Half-Yearly, Quarterly, Monthly, Weekly, Fortnightly, and Flexi IDCW.  
- Some schemes like UTI Liquid Fund have discontinued certain plans/options.
