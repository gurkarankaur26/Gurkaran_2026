TITLE: What is a Salary Account? Know its Benefits and Features  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Products  
TAGS: Salary Account, Savings Account, Benefits, Features, Loans, Minimum Balance, Interest Rate  

CONTENT:  
A Salary Account is a type of Savings Account where an employee receives their monthly salary directly from their employer. These accounts are typically offered through tie-ups between employers and banks. ICICI Bank Salary Accounts are designed to provide salaried employees with convenient banking features and benefits.

**Key Features of ICICI Bank Salary Account:**  
- **No Minimum Balance Requirement:** There is no minimum balance requirement as long as the salary is credited monthly. If salary is not credited for 3 consecutive months, the account will be converted to a regular savings account with applicable minimum balance requirements.  
- **Interest on Balance:** The account earns interest on the balance maintained. Interest is calculated daily and credited quarterly.  
- **Online Fund Transfers and Phone Banking:** Account holders can transfer funds online globally and use phone banking services for transactions.  
- **Debit Card and Personalised Cheque Book:** Salary Account holders receive a debit card for ATM withdrawals and payments, along with personalised cheque books.  
- **Easy Access to Loans:** Having a Salary Account with ICICI Bank facilitates quick access to various loans such as Personal Loans, Car Loans, and Home Loans.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Offered exclusively to salaried employees through employer tie-ups.  
- Employees need to fill an application form submitted by the employer to open the account.  

**Process to Open a Salary Account:**  
1. Employee fills out the Salary Account application form.  
2. Employer submits the form to ICICI Bank.  
3. Bank opens the Salary Account and salary is credited monthly.  

**Benefits:**  
- Zero balance account providing flexibility in money management.  
- Convenient salary credit and easy access to funds.  
- Enhanced banking facilities including online and phone banking.  
- Preferential loan offers and faster loan processing.  

KEY POINTS:  
- No minimum balance required if salary is credited monthly.  
- Interest is calculated daily and credited quarterly.  
- Debit card and cheque book provided.  
- Online fund transfer and phone banking available.  
- Easy access to personal, car, and home loans.  
- Salary Account converts to regular savings account if salary not credited for 3 months.
