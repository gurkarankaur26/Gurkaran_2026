ICICI Bank offers comprehensive NRI banking services designed to meet the financial needs of Non-Resident Indians. The bank provides secure login access for NRI customers to manage their accounts and avail various banking products and services seamlessly.

Key NRI Banking Services:  
- **NRI Accounts:** Savings and deposit accounts tailored for NRIs with attractive interest rates.  
- **FCNR Deposits:** Fixed deposits in foreign currency offering stable and competitive returns, helping NRIs grow their savings without currency risk.  
- **Money Transfer:** Easy and quick inward and outward remittances to and from India.  
- **Loans and Cards:** Home loans and credit cards designed specifically for NRIs to support their financial goals.  
- **KYC Update:** Facilities to update Know Your Customer details online to ensure compliance and uninterrupted banking services.  
- **Repatriation Services:** Outward repatriation of funds from India to abroad with proper documentation and compliance.  
- **Investment Options:** Access to mutual funds and other investment schemes suitable for NRIs.  

The bank also provides dedicated services such as e-Deposit Confirmation Advice (e-DCA), TDS certificates, and iTrack for transaction tracking, ensuring transparency and ease of banking for NRIs.

- Secure NRI login for easy access to banking services.

- Quick and reliable inward and outward remittance services.  
- Home loans and credit cards available for NRIs.

- Access to a wide range of investment schemes including mutual funds.
