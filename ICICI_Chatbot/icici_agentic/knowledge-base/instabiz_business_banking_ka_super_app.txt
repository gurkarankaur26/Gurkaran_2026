TITLE: InstaBIZ – ‘Business Banking ka Super App’  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Business Banking, Mobile Banking, Digital Banking  
TAGS: InstaBIZ, Business Banking, Overdraft, Eazypay, POS, QR, Trade Emerge, Discounts, Mobile App  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s InstaBIZ app is India’s first comprehensive business banking app designed to provide advanced mobile banking services to customers of all banks. The app offers quick access to multiple banking services with industry-first solutions, enabling businesses to operate efficiently and grow.

Key features of the InstaBIZ app include:

- **Link Any Bank Account with Eazypay Merchant Solutions:** Customers can link any bank account to access instant overdraft, quick payment settlements, and tap-to-pay card payments.
  
- **InstaOD Plus Facility:** New users can avail an instant, paperless overdraft facility up to Rs. 25 lakh. This facility is available to clients of any bank and can be activated within seconds through the app.
  
- **Business Management Tools:** The app provides 24/7 updates on business transactions and offers services such as accounting, tax filing, and business networking to help scale businesses.
  
- **Instant POS and QR Solutions:** Users can link their accounts to enjoy Eazypay features like instant settlements, collections, and ordering of POS machines and QR codes directly from the app.
  
- **Trade Emerge Platform:** A one-stop solution offering regulatory and policy information, market intelligence for partner discovery, logistical booking, and transaction banking services.
  
- **Exclusive Discounts and Offers:** Access special discounts and offers on partner platforms such as IndiaMART, ClearOne, and IndiaFilings to support business growth.

ICICI Bank’s InstaBIZ app is designed to simplify business banking and support customers in their growth journey with innovative digital solutions.

KEY POINTS:  
- Instant and paperless overdraft facility up to Rs. 25 lakh (InstaOD Plus) available to all bank customers.  
- Link any bank account to access Eazypay Merchant Solutions including instant settlements and tap-to-pay.  
- 24/7 business transaction updates and services like accounting, tax filing, and networking.  
- Instant POS and QR code ordering and management through the app.  
- Trade Emerge platform for regulatory info, market intelligence, and logistics.  
- Exclusive partner discounts on IndiaMART, ClearOne, IndiaFilings, etc.
