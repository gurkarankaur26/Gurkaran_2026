CONTENT:  
Investing in a Fixed Deposit (FD) with ICICI Bank offers financial security and steady growth with minimal risk. One of the key benefits is the option to receive monthly interest payouts, providing a consistent income stream that can supplement other earnings or cover regular expenses, making it especially beneficial for retirees and conservative investors.

**Interest Rates:**  
- General Citizens: Up to 6.60% p.a.  
- Senior Citizens (60+ years): Up to 7.10% p.a. (additional 0.50% interest rate over general rates)  

**Monthly Interest Payout Examples:**  

| Deposit Amount | Tenure | Interest Rate | Monthly Interest Payout (₹) |  
|----------------|--------|---------------|-----------------------------|  
| ₹1,00,000      | 1 year | 6.25%         | ₹518                        |  
| ₹1,00,000      | 5 years| 6.60%         | ₹547                        |  
| ₹10,00,000     | 1 year | 6.25%         | ₹5,181                      |  
| ₹10,00,000     | 5 years| 6.60%         | ₹5,470                      |  

**Note:** Senior citizens receive an additional 0.50% interest rate on the above figures.

**Additional Monthly Interest Examples:**  
- 3 months tenure: ₹7,885 monthly at 4.75% (for higher deposit amounts)  
- 1 year tenure: ₹11,105 monthly at 6.7%  

For personalized calculations based on your preferred deposit amount and tenure, ICICI Bank provides an FD Calculator on their website to estimate potential monthly interest payouts instantly.

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Fixed Deposits:**  
- Flexible tenures to suit various financial goals  
- Attractive interest rates with additional benefits for senior citizens  
- Option for monthly interest payouts to ensure steady income  
- Safe and secure investment with minimal risk
