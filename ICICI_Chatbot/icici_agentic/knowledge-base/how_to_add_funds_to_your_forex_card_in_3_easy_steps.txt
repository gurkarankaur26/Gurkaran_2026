CONTENT:  
A Forex Card enables customers to carry out transactions in foreign currencies while travelling abroad. It offers ease, security, and better exchange rates compared to cash or credit cards. Loading money onto the Forex Card before travelling is essential to cover trip expenses. ICICI Bank provides multiple convenient ways to reload your Forex Card with a daily online transaction limit of INR 150,000.

**How to reload a Forex Card?**  
You can reload your Forex Card anytime before or during your travel using the following methods:

1. **Internet Banking**  
   - Log in to Internet Banking  
   - Navigate to Cards & Loans > Forex & Prepaid Cards  
   - Select your linked Forex Prepaid Card  
   - Click on Reload  
   - Enter the required details and confirm  
   - The Forex Card will be reloaded immediately  

2. **iMobile App**  
   - Log in to the iMobile app  
   - Go to Cards & Forex  
   - Select Forex Prepaid Card and choose your linked card  
   - Click on ‘Reload’  
   - Enter the required details and confirm  
   - The Forex Card will be reloaded immediately  

3. **Forex Branches**  
   - Visit the ICICI Bank website to locate the nearest Forex Branch by entering your city  
   - Complete the Reload Form  
   - Submit copies of your Passport, Visa, and Forex Card; carry originals for verification  
   - Submit the form at the branch  
   - After processing, you will receive a confirmation message or email once funds are loaded

- Forex Card allows transactions in multiple foreign currencies with better exchange rates and security.  
- Reload limit: INR 150,000 per day for online transactions.  
- Reload options: Internet Banking, iMobile app, and Forex Branches.
