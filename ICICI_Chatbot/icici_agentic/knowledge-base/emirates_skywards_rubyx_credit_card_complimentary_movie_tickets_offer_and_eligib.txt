TITLE: Emirates Skywards Rubyx Credit Card - Complimentary Movie Tickets Offer and Eligibility  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Emirates Skywards Rubyx Credit Card, Complimentary Tickets, BookMyShow, Eligibility, Spend Criteria, Add-on Cards  

CONTENT:  
Starting April 1, 2026, to be eligible for complimentary movie or event tickets via BookMyShow using the Emirates Skywards Rubyx Credit Card, cardholders must spend a minimum of ₹25,000 within the preceding quarter. This qualifying spend entitles the cardholder to avail the complimentary ticket offer in the following calendar quarter.

**Spends and Offer Eligibility Period:**  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Dec 26, 2025 and Mar 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets between Apr 1, 2026 and June 30, 2026  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Mar 26, 2026 and June 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets between July 1, 2026 and Sep 30, 2026  

**Additional Details:**  
- Customers holding dual network credit cards (Mastercard & Amex or Visa & RuPay) can avail one complimentary movie offer per month, per card variant.  
- Any additional bookings beyond the complimentary benefit will be charged as per rates set by the service provider.  
- Add-on or Plus card members must individually meet the ₹25,000 spend criteria in the preceding quarter to be eligible for the complimentary ticket offer in the subsequent quarter.  

For booking and detailed terms and conditions, customers are advised to refer to the official ICICI Bank website.

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum spend of ₹25,000 in preceding quarter required to avail complimentary movie/event tickets.  
- Complimentary ticket offer valid only in the calendar quarter following the qualifying spend period.  
- Dual network cardholders can avail one complimentary ticket per month per variant.  
- Add-on cardholders must meet spend criteria individually.  
- Additional bookings beyond complimentary tickets are chargeable.
