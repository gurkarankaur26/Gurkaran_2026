CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to create Recurring Deposits (RD) through its Net Banking platform. Recurring Deposits are a disciplined savings instrument where you can deposit a fixed amount every month and earn interest on the accumulated amount. This facility allows customers to open and manage RDs online without visiting the branch.

**Eligibility:**  
- ICICI Bank savings account holders with Net Banking access.  
- Resident individuals including salaried and self-employed customers.

**Process to Create Recurring Deposit on Net Banking:**  
1. Log in to your ICICI Bank Net Banking account using your User ID and Password.  
2. Navigate to the 'Deposits' section and select 'Recurring Deposit'.  
3. Choose the savings account from which the monthly installment will be debited.  
4. Enter the monthly installment amount, tenure (minimum 6 months to maximum 120 months), and preferred interest payout option (cumulative or monthly).  
5. Review the details and confirm the RD creation.  
6. The monthly installment will be automatically debited from your savings account on the chosen date.

**Interest Rates:**  
- Interest rates on Recurring Deposits vary based on the tenure and prevailing bank rates.  
- Rates are compounded quarterly and credited at maturity or monthly as per the chosen payout option.  
- For the latest interest rates, customers should refer to the official ICICI Bank website or Net Banking portal.

**Benefits of Creating RD via Net Banking:**  
- Convenience of opening and managing RD anytime, anywhere.  
- Automatic monthly debit from savings account ensures disciplined savings.  
- Flexible tenure options from 6 months to 10 years.  
- Competitive interest rates with quarterly compounding.  
- Option to choose interest payout frequency.  
- No need to visit the branch, saving time and effort.

**Fees and Charges:**  
- No charges for opening or maintaining Recurring Deposits via Net Banking.  
- Premature withdrawal may attract penalties as per bank policy.

**FAQs:**  
- *Can I open multiple RDs through Net Banking?*  
  Yes, you can open multiple Recurring Deposits with different amounts and tenures.  
- *Is it possible to change the installment amount after RD creation?*  
  No, the installment amount is fixed at the time of RD creation. For changes, you need to close the existing RD and open a new one.  
- *What happens if the monthly installment is not debited?*  
  If the installment is not debited due to insufficient balance, the RD may be considered as discontinued or premature closure may apply.  
- *Can NRIs open Recurring Deposits via Net Banking?*  
  NRIs can open FCNR or NRE term deposits but should check eligibility for RDs specifically.
