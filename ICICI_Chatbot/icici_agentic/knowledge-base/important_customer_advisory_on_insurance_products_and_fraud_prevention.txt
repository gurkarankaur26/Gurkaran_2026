TITLE: Important Customer Advisory on Insurance Products and Fraud Prevention  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Customer Advisory / Fraud Prevention  
TAGS: Insurance, Customer Advisory, Fraud Prevention, Security, ICICI Bank  
CONTENT:  
- Purchasing any insurance product from ICICI Bank is completely voluntary.  
- Availing insurance products is not mandatory for obtaining any other banking facility from ICICI Bank.  
- Customers are advised to be vigilant against spurious phone calls and fictitious or fraudulent offers.  
- ICICI Bank does not solicit personal information or payments through unsolicited calls or messages.  
- Customers should verify the authenticity of any communication claiming to be from ICICI Bank by contacting official bank channels.  
- In case of suspicious activity or fraud attempts, customers should immediately report to ICICI Bank’s official helpline or visit the nearest branch.  
KEY POINTS:  
- Insurance purchase is voluntary and not linked to other banking services.  
- Beware of fraudulent calls and offers.  
- Verify communications through official ICICI Bank channels.  
- Report suspicious activities promptly.
