CONTENT:  
A Current Account is a specialized bank account designed for businesses and individuals engaged in frequent financial transactions. It facilitates seamless payment and collection services, easy access to funds, and supports import-export operations. This account is essential for maintaining liquidity and managing daily business transactions efficiently.

ICICI Bank’s Current Account offers:  
- High transaction limits suitable for business needs.  
- Multiple payment options including NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, UPI, POS, and QR payments for easy receipt and disbursement of funds.  
- Business-friendly tools such as Corporate Net Banking, InstaBIZ mobile banking app, bulk payment facilities, and detailed transaction reporting for effective cash flow management and account reconciliation.  
- Overdraft and working capital support (subject to eligibility) to provide quick access to funds during urgent business needs.  
- Integration with merchant services solutions for enhanced convenience.

**Eligibility:**  
Current Accounts can be opened by:  
- Individuals or Hindu Undivided Families (HUF)  
- Companies (Private or Public Limited)  
- Partnership Firms  
- Proprietorships  

**Documents Required:**  
- Filled application form with photograph(s)  
- PAN Card  
- Address Proof  
- Certificate of Incorporation and Memorandum of Association (MoA) for Companies & Trusts  
- Partnership Agreement for Partnerships  
- Identity and residence proof of all partners (for Partnerships)  
- Cheque from an existing Savings Account for account opening  
- Contact details including email ID and mobile number  

**Benefits:**  
- Efficient handling of business finances with streamlined payment solutions and quick fund transfers.  
- Multiple payment options ensuring smooth transactions for businesses and customers.  
- Access to overdraft facilities for urgent fund requirements without disrupting business operations.
