ICICI Bank GIFT City USD Debit Card – Features, Eligibility, Fees, and Services

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank GIFT City USD Debit Card is a debit card linked directly to the USD-denominated GIFT City Global Savings Account. It allows customers to perform transactions in US Dollars without currency conversion, facilitating seamless international usage.

**Eligibility:**  
- Available exclusively to Non-Resident Indian (NRI) customers who open or hold a USD-denominated GIFT City Global Savings Account with ICICI Bank, IBU GIFT City.

**Application Process:**  
1. **New NRI Customers:** Can opt for the Debit Card at the time of opening the GIFT City Global Savings Account; the card is issued simultaneously.  
2. **Existing Customers:** Can apply by submitting a Debit Card request form through their Relationship Manager or Branch Manager.

**Key Benefits:**  
- Globally accepted in USD.  
- Enables hassle-free transactions via Point of Sale (POS), e-commerce, and contactless platforms.  
- Easy cash withdrawals at ATMs worldwide.

**Fees:**  
- Replacement fee for a new Debit Card is USD 20.

**Card Services via ICICI Bank IBU GIFT City Net Banking:**  
- Change transaction limits of the Debit Card.  
- Block or unblock the Debit Card.  
- Change the Debit Card PIN anytime.  
- Monitor transaction controls.

**Card Renewal and Replacement:**  
- The Debit Card is automatically renewed and delivered by the 25th of the month in which the existing card expires; no application is required.  
- For replacement or new card requests, customers can contact their Relationship Manager or email contactgiftcity@icici.bank.in.  
- Replacement cards are delivered within 7 to 14 working days to the registered communication address. The existing card is blocked upon issuance of the new card.

KEY POINTS:  
- The GIFT City USD Debit Card is linked to the USD Global Savings Account for NRIs.  
- Enables transactions directly in USD without currency conversion.  
- Available to all NRI customers with a USD GIFT City account.  
- Card issuance at account opening or via request for existing customers.  
- Replacement fee: USD 20.  
- Card management (limit changes, blocking/unblocking, PIN changes) available via Net Banking.  
- Automatic renewal of cards; replacement within 7-14 working days upon request.  
- Customer support email: contactgiftcity@icici.bank.in.
