KEY POINTS:  
- The list is effective from June 2026.  
- Only mutual fund schemes listed by ICICI Bank are eligible for Loan Against Securities.  
- The schemes include both Direct (Dir) and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options.  
- Each scheme is identified by a unique AMFI code for verification.  
- Customers can pledge units of these approved mutual funds as collateral to avail loans.  
- Loan Against Securities allows customers to borrow funds by pledging their mutual fund units without redeeming them.  
- This facility helps in liquidity without disturbing the investment portfolio.
