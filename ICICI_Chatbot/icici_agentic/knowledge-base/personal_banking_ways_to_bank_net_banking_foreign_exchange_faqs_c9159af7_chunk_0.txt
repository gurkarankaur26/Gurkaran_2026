TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Forex Services – Online Purchase, Refund, and Usage of Forex Prepaid Card and Foreign Currency Notes

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Forex Services, Prepaid Cards, Foreign Currency

TAGS:  
Forex Prepaid Card, Foreign Currency Notes, Online Forex Purchase, Forex Refund, ICICI Bank Net Banking, iMobile App, Forex Documentation, Forex Delivery, NRI Forex Rules

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers convenient online services for purchasing foreign exchange products such as Forex Prepaid Cards and Foreign Currency Notes through its Net Banking and iMobile application platforms. These services are designed to assist customers traveling overseas for various purposes.

**Key Features and Services:**  
- **Products Available:**  
  - ICICI Bank Forex Prepaid Card  
  - Foreign Currency Notes  

- **Eligibility:**  
  - NRI customers are not eligible to purchase Forex Prepaid Cards, Traveller’s Cheques, or Foreign Currency Notes within India.

- **Online Purchase Process:**  
  - Customers can place orders for foreign currency notes and Forex Prepaid Cards online via ICICI Bank Net Banking or iMobile app.  
  - Requests for online purchase and home delivery must be made at least 3 working days before the travel date. For urgent requirements, customers should visit the nearest branch offering forex services.  
  - Required details for placing an order include passport number, passport expiry date, and debit card details.  
  - When abroad, customers need their debit card to complete online reload transactions on the Forex Prepaid Card via Net Banking or iMobile app.

- **Refund of Unspent Forex:**  
  - Customers can refund the unspent balance on Forex Prepaid Cards and Currency Notes at any ICICI Bank Forex servicing branch.  
  - Refunds can also be processed online through Net Banking or iMobile app, with the refund amount credited to the linked savings account.  
  - Refund transactions are completed within one working day, and the amount is credited in Indian Rupees at the prevailing exchange rate.  
  - Refunds are subject to compliance with RBI regulations.

- **Order Tracking:**  
  - Customers can track their forex order status using the ‘Track your Order’ option available on ICICI Bank Net Banking and iMobile app.  
  - Alternatively, customers may contact ICICI Bank Customer Care for order status.

- **Documentation and Delivery:**  
  - To receive Forex delivery, customers must:  
    - Fill and sign the required forms (Form A2 cum application and LRS declaration).  
    - Submit a copy of their passport.  
    - Present the original passport for physical verification.  
    - For business travel, submit a copy of a valid business visa, which will be verified against the original.  
    - Be physically present at the delivery location to hand over documents and receive the Forex.  

- **Service Availability:**  
  - Forex purchase and management services are available round the clock on ICICI Bank Net Banking and iMobile app.  
  - ICICI Bank reserves the right to have service downtime due to technical issues without prior notice.

KEY POINTS:  
- Forex Prepaid Cards and Foreign Currency Notes can be purchased online via ICICI Bank Net Banking and iMobile app.  
- Orders must be placed at least 3 working days before travel; urgent needs require branch visit.  
- NRI customers cannot purchase Forex products in India.  
- Refunds of unspent Forex balances can be done online or at branches, credited to linked savings accounts within one working day.  
- Required documents include passport copy, original passport for verification, and business visa copy if applicable.  
- Customers must be physically present to receive Forex delivery.  
- ‘Track your Order’ feature available for monitoring order status.  
- Services are subject to RBI regulations and possible technical downtimes.
