TITLE: Car Loan EMI Calculator - Calculate EMI for New Car Loan  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Car Loan, EMI Calculator, Loan Tenure, Interest Rate, Monthly Instalment  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a Car Loan EMI Calculator to help customers estimate their monthly instalments and total interest payable over the loan tenure. Customers can input the loan amount starting from ₹1,00,000, select an approximate interest rate, and choose a loan tenure between 12 and 84 months. The calculator provides an indicative monthly EMI amount and total interest cost to assist in financial planning.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount starting from ₹1,00,000  
- Loan tenure options between 12 and 84 months  
- Approximate interest rate input for EMI estimation  
- Calculates monthly EMI and total interest payable  
- Results are indicative and subject to ICICI Bank’s final policies
