TITLE: How Long Does Credit Card Processing Take in India?  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card Processing, Application Timeline, Eligibility, Credit Score, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
Credit Cards are a popular financial tool offering benefits such as Cashback, Reward Points, and other incentives. Applying for a Credit Card in India can be done online or in person. A common query among applicants is the duration it takes to receive the card after submitting an application.

**Credit Card Application Process at ICICI Bank:**  
- Submit the application with all required documents.  
- The bank reviews eligibility criteria and documentation.  
- If approved, the Credit Card is dispatched to the registered address within a few days.  
- The cardholder must activate the card and set a PIN before use.

**Eligibility and Documentation:**  
- Applicants must meet the bank’s eligibility criteria to ensure hassle-free approval.  
- Required documents typically include identity proof, address proof, income proof, and other KYC documents.  
- A good credit score (700 or higher) significantly improves chances of approval.  
- Existing Credit Card holders may experience longer processing times as the bank reviews usage and repayment history.

**Processing Time:**  
- Approval timelines vary based on application completeness, accuracy, and internal processing.  
- Typically, decisions are made within a few days to a few weeks.  
- Instant approval is possible in some cases.

**Checking Application Status:**  
- Applicants can check their Credit Card application status by logging into the ICICI Bank website or calling 1800 1080.  
- It is advised to avoid applying for multiple Credit Cards simultaneously to prevent negative impacts on credit score.

KEY POINTS:  
- Credit Card dispatch occurs within a few days post-approval.  
- Eligibility includes meeting documentation requirements and having a credit score of 700+.  
- Processing time ranges from a few days to a few weeks.  
- Application status can be checked online or via phone (1800 1080).  
- Avoid multiple simultaneous Credit Card applications to protect credit score.
