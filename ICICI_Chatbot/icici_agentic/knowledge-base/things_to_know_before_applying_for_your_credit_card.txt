TITLE: Things To Know Before Applying For Your Credit Card  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Eligibility, Fees, Interest Rates, Rewards, EMI, Benefits  

CONTENT:  
Credit Cards offer several advantages over Debit Cards, including building a good credit history, easier access to loans with favorable interest rates, cheaper insurance, and new cellular plans. Regular use of a Credit Card can also help you earn reward points or cashback and protect your purchases from theft or damage.

Before applying for a Credit Card, consider the following important factors:

1. **Eligibility Criteria**  
   Eligibility varies by bank but generally includes basic requirements such as age, income, and credit score. Meeting these criteria is essential to apply for a Credit Card.

2. **Choosing the Right Credit Card**  
   Understand the features of the credit card before applying. Different cards offer different benefits and fees.

3. **EMI Tenure and Interest Rates**  
   Credit Cards allow you to borrow money and repay in easy installments (EMIs). Check the interest rates and choose an EMI tenure that suits your affordability. Most credit cards offer an interest-free period if payment is made within the due date.

4. **Online Accessibility**  
   Your credit card should support online registration, allowing you to track daily spending, available balance, and ongoing EMIs. E-statements should be sent to your registered email ID.

5. **Cash Withdrawal Facilities**  
   Look for cards that allow cash withdrawal worldwide, usually with a minimal charge on the withdrawal amount.

6. **Joining and Annual Fees**  
   Some cards have a joining fee and/or annual fee. You can also opt for lifetime free credit cards with no annual fees. Review these charges before applying.

7. **Additional Features**  
   - SMS alerts for new offers or discounts  
   - 24x7 customer service for complaints such as lost or stolen cards  
   - Insurance cover providing comprehensive protection  

8. **Reward Points and Cashback**  
   Credit Cards offer reward points that can be redeemed for discounts on shopping, dining, flight bookings, and cashback on fuel spends.

KEY POINTS:  
- Credit Cards help build credit history and offer financial benefits.  
- Eligibility criteria must be met before applying.  
- Choose a card based on interest rates, EMI tenure, fees, and features.  
- Most cards offer an interest-free period if payments are timely.  
- Online access and e-statements are important for tracking expenses.  
- Cash withdrawal is available globally with minimal charges.  
- Consider joining and annual fees; lifetime free cards are available.  
- Additional benefits include SMS alerts, 24x7 customer service, and insurance cover.  
- Earn reward points and cashback on various spends.
