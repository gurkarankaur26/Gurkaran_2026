TITLE:  
Overview of UTI Short Duration and Ultra Short Duration Funds

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: UTI Funds, Short Duration Fund, Ultra Short Duration Fund, IDCW, Growth, Investment, Dividends  

CONTENT:  
This document provides detailed information about various UTI Short Duration and Ultra Short Duration mutual fund schemes, including their types, dividend options, and plan codes.

**UTI Short Duration Fund**  
- Available in multiple variants: Growth, Dividend (IDCW) with different payout frequencies such as Annual, Half-Yearly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Flexible IDCW options.  
- Plans include Direct and Regular options.  
- Some schemes have been discontinued but are listed for reference.  
- Dividend options: IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) with payout frequencies: Annual, Half-Yearly, Monthly, Quarterly, Flexible.  
- Growth option available for capital appreciation without dividend payouts.

**UTI Ultra Short Duration Fund**  
- Available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Dividend options include Monthly, Annual, Daily, Flexible, Fortnightly IDCW, and Growth options.  
- Designed for investors seeking short-term investment with relatively lower risk and better liquidity.  

**Key Features:**  
- Multiple IDCW payout options to suit investor preferences.  
- Growth options for capital appreciation.  
- Direct and Regular plans to cater to different investor needs.  
- Suitable for investors looking for short-term investment horizons with moderate risk.  

KEY POINTS:  
- UTI Short Duration Fund and UTI Ultra Short Duration Fund offer various IDCW payout frequencies: Annual, Half-Yearly, Monthly, Quarterly, Daily, Flexible, and Fortnightly.  
- Both funds have Direct and Regular plan options.  
- Growth options are available for investors preferring capital appreciation over periodic income.  
- Some schemes have been discontinued but are listed for historical reference.  
- These funds are suitable for investors seeking short-term investment with moderate risk and liquidity.
