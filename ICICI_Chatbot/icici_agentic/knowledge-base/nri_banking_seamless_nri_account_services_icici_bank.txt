CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a comprehensive range of banking services tailored specifically for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to manage their finances seamlessly across borders. The bank provides various account types, deposits, loans, investment options, and payment services designed to meet the unique needs of NRIs.

**NRI Accounts and Services:**  
- **NRI Savings Account:** Manage income earned in India with ease. Suitable for NRIs looking to maintain and operate accounts in India.  
- **FCNR Deposits:** Fixed deposits in foreign currency offering attractive interest rates, helping NRIs grow their savings without currency risk.  
- **NRE and NRO Accounts:** Facilitate easy repatriation of funds and management of income earned abroad or in India.  
- **Money Transfer:** Seamless transfer of funds to and from India, supporting multiple currencies and quick processing.  
- **Loans and Cards:** Access to home loans, personal loans, and credit cards designed for NRIs to support their financial needs in India and abroad.  

**Investment Services:**  
- **Mutual Funds (SIP):** Choose from thousands of schemes to invest systematically in India.  
- **Savings + Demat + Trading Account:** Integrated accounts to be investment ready and trade in Indian markets.  
- **Real Estate Investment:** Facilities to invest in Indian real estate with ease.  

**Special Features and Benefits:**  
- Attractive interest rates on FCNR deposits and NRE savings accounts.  
- Ability to hold and manage funds in multiple foreign currencies such as USD, GBP, and others.  
- Dedicated services for NRIs relocating to countries like the UK and Canada, including account security and fund transfer options.  
- Nominee facility available (only one nominee allowed per account).  
- Digital services including Scan and Pay, Smart Lock, Block Card, and Fraud Reporting for enhanced security and convenience.  

**Eligibility and Process:**  
- Open to Non-Resident Indians holding valid passports and visas.  
- Account opening can be initiated online or at ICICI Bank branches.  
- Required documents typically include passport, visa, proof of overseas address, and Indian address proof if applicable.  
- Nominee details must be provided at the time of account opening.  

**Fees and Charges:**  
- Specific fees and charges vary by account type and service; customers are advised to refer to the latest fee schedule provided by ICICI Bank.  
- No charges for basic account opening and maintenance for certain NRI accounts; charges may apply for additional services like fund transfers and card issuance.  

**Interest Rates:**  
- FCNR deposits offer competitive interest rates depending on the currency and tenure.  
- NRE and NRO savings accounts provide attractive interest rates as per prevailing RBI guidelines.  

**FAQs:**  
- Can NRIs open accounts online? Yes, ICICI Bank facilitates online account opening for NRIs.  
- What currencies are supported for FCNR deposits? Multiple foreign currencies including USD, GBP, EUR, and others.  
- Is repatriation of funds allowed? Yes, funds in NRE and FCNR accounts are fully repatriable.  
- Can NRIs invest in mutual funds? Yes, NRIs can invest in mutual funds through ICICI Bank’s platform.
