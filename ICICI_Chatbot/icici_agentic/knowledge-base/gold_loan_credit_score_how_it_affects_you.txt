TITLE: Gold Loan & Credit Score: How It Affects You  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Gold Loan, Credit Score, Loan Eligibility, Loan Repayment, Secured Loan, Interest Rates  

CONTENT:  
A gold loan is a secured loan offered by banks and financial institutions against gold jewellery and ornaments as collateral. It is a convenient financing option with simple documentation, attractive interest rates, and quick disbursal. Gold loans can be availed for amounts up to Rs. 1 crore depending on factors such as gold rate, loan tenure, number of ornaments, their carat rating, and weight.  

To apply for a gold loan, submission of KYC documents is mandatory, but no income proof is required since it is a secured loan. This makes gold loans accessible even without regular income proof.  

Regarding credit scores, gold loans do not heavily depend on creditworthiness for approval because they are backed by collateral. However, timely repayment of the gold loan positively impacts your credit score. Conversely, defaulting or delaying repayment can significantly damage your credit score, similar to other types of loans. It is advisable to take a gold loan only if you are confident about repaying it on time without affecting other financial obligations.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Gold loan is a secured loan against gold jewellery and ornaments.  
- Loan amount can be up to Rs. 1 crore based on gold value, tenure, carat, and weight.  
- Only KYC documents are required; no income proof needed.  
- Timely repayment improves credit score.  
- Default or delayed payment negatively affects credit score.  
- Suitable for emergency funds, education, weddings, and other crucial expenses.
