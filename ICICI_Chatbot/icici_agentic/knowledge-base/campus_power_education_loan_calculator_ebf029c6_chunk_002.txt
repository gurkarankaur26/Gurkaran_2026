CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers an Education Loan EMI Calculator to help students and their families estimate the monthly EMI, total interest payable, and overall borrowing cost before applying for an education loan. This tool allows borrowers to plan their finances by comparing different loan amounts and repayment tenures. It also provides a comparison between financing education through a loan versus self-funding, highlighting the opportunity cost and potential investment returns if savings are used instead of borrowing.

The education loan repayment tenure can extend up to 15 years, including the moratorium period, with repayment typically starting after course completion. Early repayment planning is encouraged to align the repayment structure with the borrower’s expected future income and reduce financial burden.

The calculator also estimates potential tax savings under Section 80E on the interest paid on the education loan, helping borrowers understand the effective cost of borrowing.

Eligibility and Documentation Requirements:  
- Identity proof of applicant and co-applicant  
- Address proof (for NRIs, both Indian and overseas address proofs are required)  
- Academic documents such as mark sheets, degree certificates, and entrance test scores  
- Admission proof including university admission letter and fee structure (for USA - i-20 form, for UK - CAS letter)  
- Registration certificate for professional courses (e.g., CA, Doctor)  
- Income proof of co-applicant:  
  - Salaried: Salary slips and bank statements for the last 3 months  
  - Self-employed: Audited financials, Income Tax Returns (ITR), etc.  
- Additional banking and financial documents as requested by the bank  
- PAN Card of applicant and co-applicant  
- Recent photographs of applicant and co-applicant  
- Completed application form  

Process to Use the Education Loan EMI Calculator:  
1. Enter the loan details such as amount, interest rate, and repayment tenure in the Education Loan EMI Calculator.  
2. Review the estimated EMI and total payable amount.  
3. Click on ‘Apply Now’ to proceed with the loan application.

Benefits of Using ICICI Bank Education Loan and Calculator:  
- Helps estimate monthly EMI and total cost of borrowing before applying.  
- Compares loan financing with self-funding to evaluate cost-effectiveness.  
- Provides insights on tax benefits under Section 80E on interest paid.  
- Enables planning for repayment tenure up to 15 years including moratorium.  
- Supports students without collateral by helping estimate affordable loan amounts.  
- Facilitates early repayment planning to reduce financial burden.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Education loan repayment tenure: Up to 15 years including moratorium period.  
- Repayment typically starts after course completion.  
- Tax benefits available under Section 80E on interest paid.  
- Required documents include identity, address, academic, admission proof, income proof, PAN cards, photographs, and completed application form.
