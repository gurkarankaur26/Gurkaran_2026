CONTENT:  
**Mutual Funds**  
- Invest anytime, anywhere using iMobile app and Internet Banking.  
- Choose from mutual fund schemes offered by leading fund houses.  
- Use the ‘Import Fund’ feature to consolidate all your mutual fund folios held elsewhere.  
- Access capital gain/loss statements under the “Reports” section in iMobile.  
- Perform non-financial transactions such as updating email address, mobile number, nominee name, and folio consolidation via iMobile.  

**National Pension System (NPS)**  
- Market-linked retirement plan offering multiple tax benefits.  
- Portability and flexibility across retail, corporate, multiple fund managers, and asset allocation options.  
- Tax exemptions:  
  - Up to ₹2 lakh tax exemption.  
  - Additional tax benefit under Section 80CCD(1B) of up to ₹50,000 over and above Section 80CCD(1) deduction of ₹1.5 lakh.  
  - Corporate model offers tax benefits up to 10% of basic pay in old tax regime and up to 14% in new tax regime.  
- On maturity, up to 60% of the corpus can be withdrawn as a tax-free lump sum.  

**Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY)**  
- Accidental insurance scheme providing life cover.  
- Coverage: ₹2 lakh for death or permanent disability, ₹1 lakh for partial disability.  
- Annual premium: ₹20.  
- Eligibility: Individuals aged 18 to 70 years.  
- Application available online.  

**Digital Banking Services**  
- Mobile Banking: Download and activate the iMobile app for convenient banking.  
- Net Banking: Authenticate via Customer Care, generate User ID and password online instantly.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual Funds can be managed fully via iMobile and Internet Banking with features like folio consolidation and capital gain reports.  
- NPS offers significant tax benefits and flexible investment options with up to 60% tax-free withdrawal on maturity.  
- PMSBY provides affordable accidental insurance with ₹2 lakh coverage at just ₹20 annual premium for eligible customers aged 18-70.  
- Digital banking services include easy activation and login for iMobile app and Net Banking for seamless banking experience.
