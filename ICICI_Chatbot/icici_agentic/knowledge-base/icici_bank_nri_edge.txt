CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank NRI Edge is a comprehensive knowledge platform designed to assist Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) at various stages of their overseas journey. The platform allows users to select their current life stage from four options: Moving Abroad, Study Abroad, Settled Overseas, and Relocating to India. Based on the selected life stage, the platform provides tailored information and articles relevant to the user’s needs.

The knowledge hub covers a wide range of topics including:  
- NRI Banking: Information on NRI accounts, deposits, and remittances.  
- Investments: Guidance on investment planning in India, including immovable property, gold investments, mutual funds, Public Provident Fund (PPF), and Foreign Direct Investment (FDI).  
- Taxation and Inheritance: Details on taxation rules applicable to NRIs, legal aspects, and inheritance planning.  
- Real Estate: Insights into investing in Indian real estate for NRIs.  
- Beyond Banking: Additional financial solutions and services relevant to NRIs.

Users can further filter articles based on the financial solution they seek, such as NRI Banking, Investments, Taxation and Inheritance, Real Estate, and Beyond Banking, enabling a personalized and focused knowledge experience.

KEY POINTS:  
- Four life stages for personalized content: Moving Abroad, Study Abroad, Settled Overseas, Relocating to India.  
- Covers key NRI topics: banking, remittances, investments, taxation, legal, inheritance, and real estate.  
- Investment options include immovable property, gold, mutual funds, PPF, and FDI.  
- Provides detailed information on taxation and inheritance laws for NRIs.  
- Enables filtering by financial solutions for targeted information.
