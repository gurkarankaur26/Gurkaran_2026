Education loans provide financial assistance to students and professionals pursuing higher education, helping cover tuition fees and other related expenses without depleting family savings. ICICI Bank offers various types of education loans tailored to different needs, including domestic and study abroad education loans.

**Key Expenses Covered Under an Education Loan:**  
- Tuition fees  
- Examination, library, and lab charges  
- Books, study materials, uniforms, and laptops  
- Hostel or accommodation fees  
- Travel expenses (for study abroad loans)  
- Other necessary expenses such as project work, study tours, and thesis costs  

**Types of Education Loans Offered by ICICI Bank:**  

1. **Domestic Education Loan**  
- For students pursuing courses in recognized Indian institutions (undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, or professional courses).  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹3 crore, subject to eligibility.  

2. **Study Abroad Education Loan**  
- For students enrolling in international universities in countries like the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, etc.  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹3 crore, subject to eligibility.  

**Benefits of ICICI Bank Education Loans:**  
- Covers a wide range of education-related expenses beyond tuition fees.  
- Flexible repayment options available after course completion.  
- Helps avoid financial stress on family savings.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Education loans cover tuition, accommodation, travel, and other academic expenses.  
- Loan amounts up to ₹3 crore available for both domestic and international studies.  
- Flexible repayment options post-course completion.  
- Applicable for undergraduate, postgraduate, diploma, and professional courses.
