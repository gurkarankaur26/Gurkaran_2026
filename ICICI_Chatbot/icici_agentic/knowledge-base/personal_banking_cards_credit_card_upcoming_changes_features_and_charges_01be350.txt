TITLE:  
Upcoming Changes to ICICI Bank Credit Card Features and Charges

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Cards, Lounge Access, Charges, Dynamic Currency Conversion, Benefits, Eligibility, Fees  

CONTENT:  
Starting 01 January 2025, ICICI Bank is introducing updated criteria for complimentary domestic airport lounge access on certain credit cards. Cardholders who spend a minimum of ₹75,000 in the previous calendar quarter will be eligible for complimentary lounge access in the subsequent calendar quarter. The number of complimentary accesses varies by card type.

**Lounge Access Eligibility Illustration:**  
| Spend Period | Lounge Eligibility Period |  
|--------------|---------------------------|  
| 26 Sep 2024 – 25 Dec 2024 | 01 Jan 2025 – 31 Mar 2025 |  
| 26 Dec 2024 – 25 Mar 2025 | 01 Apr 2025 – 30 Jun 2025 |  
| 26 Mar 2025 – 25 Jun 2025 | 01 Jul 2025 – 30 Sep 2025 |  
| 26 Jun 2025 – 25 Sep 2025 | 01 Oct 2025 – 31 Dec 2025 |  

**Exclusions:**  
The updated lounge access criteria do NOT apply to the following cards:  
- ICICI Bank Diamant Credit Cards  
- ICICI Bank Emeralde Private Metal Credit Cards  
- ICICI Bank Emeralde Private Credit Cards  
- ICICI Bank Emeralde Credit Cards  
- ICICI Bank Emirates Emeralde Credit Cards  
- MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Credit Cards  
- Times Black ICICI Bank Credit Cards  

**BookMyShow Benefits Eligibility:**  
Spending ₹25,000 in specified periods will unlock BookMyShow benefits in the subsequent quarter as follows:  
| Spend Period | BookMyShow Benefits Period |  
|--------------|----------------------------|  
| 26 Dec 2025 – 25 Mar 2026 | 01 Apr 2026 – 30 Jun 2026 |  
| 26 Mar 2026 – 25 Jun 2026 | 01 Jul 2026 – 30 Sep 2026 |  
| 26 Jun 2026 – 25 Sep 2026 | 01 Oct 2026 – 31 Dec 2026 |  
| 26 Sep 2026 – 25 Dec 2026 | 01 Jan 2027 – 31 Mar 2027 |  

**Dynamic Currency Conversion (DCC) Charges:**  
DCC fees vary by card type as follows:  
| Credit Card Type | DCC Charges |  
|------------------|-------------|  
| Times Black ICICI Bank Credit Card | 1.49% |  
| ICICI Bank Emeralde Metal, Emeralde, Emeralde Private Credit Cards | 2.00% |  
| MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Travel Credit Card | 0.99% |  
| Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card | 1.99% |  
| All other credit cards including MakeMyTrip ICICI Bank Signature and Platinum Credit Cards | 3.50% |  

These changes are effective from 05 February 2024 for DCC charges and from 01 January 2025 for lounge access benefits.

KEY POINTS:  
- Complimentary domestic airport lounge access requires ₹75,000 spend in the previous quarter.  
- Lounge access eligibility is quarterly and linked to spend in the prior quarter.  
- Certain premium and co-branded cards are excluded from the lounge access update.  
- BookMyShow benefits require ₹25,000 spend in the previous quarter to unlock benefits in the next quarter.  
- Dynamic Currency Conversion fees range from 0.99% to 3.50% depending on the credit card.  
- Changes to DCC charges effective 05 February 2024; lounge access changes effective 01 January 2025.
