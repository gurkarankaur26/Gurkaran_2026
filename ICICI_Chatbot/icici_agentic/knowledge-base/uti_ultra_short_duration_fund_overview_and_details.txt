TITLE: UTI Ultra Short Duration Fund - Overview and Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: UTI Ultra Short Duration Fund, Mutual Funds, Investment, IDCW, Growth, Quarterly Dividend, Weekly Dividend, Annual Dividend  

CONTENT:  
The UTI Ultra Short Duration Fund is a mutual fund scheme offering various options for investors seeking short-term investment opportunities with regular income or growth potential. The fund has multiple plans and dividend payout frequencies to suit different investor preferences.

Available Options and Dividend Frequencies:  
- Direct Plan - Quarterly IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal)  
- Direct Plan - Weekly IDCW  
- Regular Plan - Annual IDCW  
- Regular Plan - Fortnightly IDCW  
- Regular Plan - Quarterly IDCW  
- Flexi IDCW  
- Growth Option  
- Discontinued IP Growth Option (2011)  

Key Features:  
- The fund primarily invests in short duration debt instruments, aiming to provide regular income with relatively low risk.  
- IDCW options allow investors to receive periodic dividends, while the Growth option reinvests earnings to increase the NAV.  
- Multiple dividend payout frequencies (weekly, fortnightly, quarterly, annual) provide flexibility in income realization.  
- The fund has been available since at least 2009, with some options discontinued over time.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Fund Type: Ultra Short Duration Debt Fund  
- Investment Options: Direct and Regular Plans  
- Dividend Frequencies: Weekly, Fortnightly, Quarterly, Annual  
- Growth Option Available  
- Discontinued Options: IP Growth (2011)  
- Suitable for investors seeking short-term income or growth with low risk
