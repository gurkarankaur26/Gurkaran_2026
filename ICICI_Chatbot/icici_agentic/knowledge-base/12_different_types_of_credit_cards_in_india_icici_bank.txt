CONTENT:  
Credit Cards are versatile financial tools designed to meet diverse spending needs and financial goals. ICICI Bank offers a variety of credit cards tailored to different lifestyles and preferences. Understanding these types can help you select the card that best suits your requirements.

**Types of Credit Cards offered by ICICI Bank:**

1. **Regular Credit Cards**  
   - Ideal for routine spending and beginners.  
   - Benefits include cashback, reward points, and fuel surcharge waivers.  
   - Example: ICICI Bank Coral Credit Card – offers discounts on dining and movie tickets.

2. **Premium Credit Cards**  
   - Designed for users seeking enhanced benefits such as higher rewards and complimentary airport lounge access.  
   - Example: ICICI Bank Rubyx Credit Card – provides exclusive points across shopping, dining, and travel categories.

3. **Super Premium Credit Cards**  
   - Targeted at high-spending individuals.  
   - Benefits include concierge services, international lounge access, wellness benefits, unlimited lounge access, and no over-limit charges.  
   - Example: ICICI Bank Emeralde Credit Card – ideal for frequent flyers and luxury lifestyle seekers.

4. **Co-branded Credit Cards**  
   - Launched in partnership with leading brands, offering brand-specific benefits like discounts and accelerated rewards.  
   - Example: ICICI Bank Amazon Pay Credit Card – cashback on Amazon for Prime users and attractive rewards on other spends.

5. **Business Credit Cards**  
   - Help manage corporate expenses with higher credit limits, employee card options, and detailed expense tracking.  
   - Example: ICICI Bank Business Advantage Black Credit Card – offers fuel and travel benefits, cashback on business spends, and integration with expense management tools. Suitable for SMEs and entrepreneurs.

**Benefits Across ICICI Bank Credit Cards:**  
- Reward points and cashback on various categories.  
- Fuel surcharge waivers.  
- Airport lounge access (varies by card type).  
- Discounts on dining, shopping, travel, and entertainment.  
- Concierge and wellness services (premium cards).  
- Business expense management features (business cards).

**Eligibility and Fees:**  
- Eligibility criteria vary by card type and include income requirements and credit score assessments.  
- Fees and interest rates differ based on the card category and benefits offered.  
- Specific details on fees, interest rates, and eligibility can be obtained during the application process or from the ICICI Bank website.

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank offers 12 types of credit cards tailored to different customer needs.  
- Cards range from Regular to Super Premium, including Co-branded and Business cards.  
- Benefits include cashback, reward points, lounge access, concierge services, and business expense management.  
- Examples include Coral (Regular), Rubyx (Premium), Emeralde (Super Premium), Amazon Pay (Co-branded), and Business Advantage Black (Business).  
- Eligibility and fees vary; detailed information is available on the ICICI Bank website.
