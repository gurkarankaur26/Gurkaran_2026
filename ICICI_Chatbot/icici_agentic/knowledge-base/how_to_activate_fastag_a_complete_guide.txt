CONTENT:  
FASTag is a mandatory electronic toll collection system introduced in 2014 to enable cashless toll payments and reduce congestion at toll plazas. It is a magnetic sticker affixed to the front windscreen of four-wheelers. When a vehicle passes through a toll plaza, the FASTag is scanned, and the toll charges are automatically deducted from the linked account or wallet.

**What is FASTag?**  
- A sticker with RFID technology affixed on the vehicle’s front windscreen.  
- Enables automatic toll fee deduction without cash transactions.  
- Helps reduce waiting time and traffic congestion at toll plazas.  
- Records all toll payments and FASTag recharges for easy tracking.

**How to Activate FASTag**  

1. **Self Activation via ICICI Bank iMobile App**  
   - Download and log in to the ICICI Bank iMobile app.  
   - Enter the 4-digit PIN received on your registered mobile number.  
   - Select the 'Shop' tab and tap on the ‘FASTag’ icon.  
   - Enter your vehicle registration number, make, and model.  
   - Agree to the Terms and Conditions.  
   - Provide a valid email ID and delivery address.  
   - Select the bank account for payment debit.  
   - Tap on proceed to complete the purchase and activation.  
   - The FASTag is automatically activated once linked to your account.  

2. **Activation at a Certified Bank Branch**  
   - Visit a certified bank branch that supports FASTag activation.  
   - Submit your vehicle Registration Certificate (RC), KYC documents, and other required details.  
   - Request to link the FASTag to your bank account.  
   - Confirm activation with the bank representative.  
   - Note: Not all banks provide FASTag activation services; verify with your bank beforehand.

**Benefits of FASTag**  
- Faster toll plaza transactions through dedicated FASTag lanes.  
- Eliminates the need for cash payments and waiting for change.  
- Reduces traffic congestion and waiting times at toll plazas.  
- Provides a digital record of toll payments and recharges.  
- Convenient recharge options through multiple digital channels.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag is mandatory for all four-wheelers for toll payments.  
- Activation can be done online via ICICI Bank iMobile app or at certified bank branches.  
- Requires vehicle registration number, RC, and KYC documents for activation.  
- Multiple recharge options available including digital payments and cheque.  
- Enables faster, cashless toll payments and reduces congestion.
