CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Fixed Deposits (FDs) for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) under two main categories: NRE (Non-Resident External) and NRO (Non-Resident Ordinary) Fixed Deposits. These deposits come with specific interest rates, tenures, and terms governed by RBI guidelines and ICICI Bank policies.

**Interest Rates and Tenure:**  
- NRE FD interest rates apply for tenures of 1 year or more.  
- NRO FD interest rates apply for tenures starting from 7 days.  
- Senior Citizen rates do not apply to NRI FDs.  
- Interest rates are subject to change without prior notice and apply to both new and renewed FDs.  
- If an account holder changes status from Non-Resident to Resident, Resident Deposit rates will apply.

**Taxation:**  
- Interest earned on NRO FDs is subject to Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) as per Income Tax laws.  
- No TDS is applicable on interest earned on NRE FDs.

**Premature Withdrawal Penalty:**  
- Interest on premature withdrawal is paid only for the period the deposit was held, at the rate prevailing at booking for the actual tenure, minus applicable penalties.  
- No interest is paid if the entire NRE FD is withdrawn before 1 year or if the NRO FD is withdrawn before 7 days.  
- Any excess interest paid at the contracted rate (monthly/quarterly payouts) will be recovered from the principal upon premature withdrawal.

**Penalty Rates on Premature/Partial Closure:**  

| Tenure of Deposit           | Less than Rs. 25 Cr | Rs. 25 Cr and above |  
|----------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|  
| Less than 1 year            | 0.50%               | 0.50%               |  
| 1 year and above but < 5 years | 1.00%           | 1.00%               |  
| 5 years and above           | 1.00%               | 1.50%               |  

**FDs Without Premature Withdrawal Facility:**  
- Cannot be closed before the predetermined tenure except in special cases (e.g., regulatory directions, deceased claims).  
- No interest is paid on early closure; any credited interest is recovered from the principal.  
- Overdraft against Fixed Deposit (ODFD) is not available for these deposits.  
- Auto-renewal facility is not available.  
- Minimum and maximum terms:  
  - Traditional NRO FDs: 3 months to 10 years  
  - Cumulative NRO FDs: 6 months to 10 years  
  - NRE FDs: 1 year to 10 years

**Additional Notes:**  
- All terms are subject to RBI guidelines and ICICI Bank’s policies.  
- In case of conflict, RBI guidelines and these terms prevail.

KEY POINTS:  
- NRE FD interest rates apply for 1 year or more; NRO FD rates apply from 7 days tenure.  
- No TDS on NRE FD interest; TDS applicable on NRO FD interest.  
- Senior Citizen rates do not apply to NRI FDs.  
- Premature withdrawal penalties range from 0.50% to 1.50% depending on tenure and deposit amount.  
- No interest paid if NRE FD withdrawn before 1 year or NRO FD before 7 days.  
- FDs without premature withdrawal facility have strict closure rules and no overdraft or auto-renewal.  
- Minimum and maximum tenures vary by FD type and scheme.  
- Interest rates and terms are subject to change without notice.
