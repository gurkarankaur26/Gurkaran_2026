TITLE: Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card - Features, Benefits, and Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Amazon Pay, ICICI Bank, Credit Card, Cashback, Fuel Surcharge Waiver, Digital Onboarding  

CONTENT:  
The Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card offers a range of benefits designed to enhance the shopping and payment experience for customers, especially on Amazon.in. The card provides attractive cashback offers and a seamless digital onboarding process, ensuring convenience throughout the card lifecycle—from application to usage and renewal.

**Key Features and Benefits:**  
- **Cashback Offers:**  
  - 5% cashback on Amazon.in spends (excluding rent, tax, and education payments).  
  - 1% unlimited cashback on all other spends outside Amazon (excluding fuel, rent, tax, education, utilities, and international spends).  
- **Fuel Surcharge Waiver:** 1% fuel surcharge waiver on fuel transactions.  
- **Digital Onboarding:** Fully digital application and approval process for quick and convenient card issuance and renewal.  

**About Amazon Pay:**  
Amazon Pay is a digital payment platform that facilitates safe, fast, and frictionless payments both on and off Amazon.in. It supports multiple payment methods including Amazon Pay UPI, Amazon Pay Balance, Amazon Pay Later, and the Amazon Pay ICICI Bank Credit Card. Amazon Pay aims to simplify payments with features like “one-click” payments and a cash-load facility in the Amazon Pay Wallet, supporting the government’s vision of a less-cash society in India.

**About ICICI Bank:**  
ICICI Bank Ltd is a leading private sector bank in India with total assets of ₹21,23,839 crore as of June 30, 2025. The bank is listed on BSE (ICICIBANK), NSE (ICICIBANK), and NYSE (IBN).
