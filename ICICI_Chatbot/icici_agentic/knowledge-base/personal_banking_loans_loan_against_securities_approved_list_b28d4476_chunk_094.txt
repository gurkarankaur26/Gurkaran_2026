TITLE:  
UTI Mutual Fund Schemes Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds / Investment Products  
TAGS: UTI Mutual Fund, Low Duration Fund, Money Market Fund, IDCW, Growth, Investment Options, Dividend Frequency

CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of UTI Mutual Fund schemes, detailing various fund options, their plan types, and dividend payout frequencies. The schemes include Low Duration Funds, Money Market Funds, and Overnight Funds, available under different plans such as Regular, Direct, Retail, and Retirement. Dividend payout options include Daily (Dly), Weekly (Wkly), Fortnightly (Fortntly), Monthly (Mthly), Quarterly (Qtly), Half-Yearly (half Yly), Annual (Ann), Flexible (Flexi), and Periodic (Perd) Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW). Growth options are also available for investors seeking capital appreciation.

KEY POINTS:  
- Fund Types:  
  - UTI Low Duration Fund  
  - UTI Money Market Fund  
  - UTI Overnight Fund  

- Plan Types:  
  - Regular (Reg)  
  - Direct (Dir)  
  - Retail (Retail)  
  - Retirement (Ret)  

- Dividend Frequency Options:  
  - Daily (Dly IDCW)  
  - Weekly (Wkly IDCW)  
  - Fortnightly (Fortntly IDCW)  
  - Monthly (Mthly IDCW)  
  - Quarterly (Qtly IDCW)  
  - Half-Yearly (half Yly IDCW)  
  - Annual (Ann IDCW)  
  - Flexible (Flexi IDCW)  
  - Periodic (Perd IDCW)  

- Growth Option available for capital appreciation in most funds.  

- Some schemes are discontinued but listed for reference.  

- Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference.

- Investors can choose dividend payout frequency based on their income needs.

- Suitable for investors looking for low-risk, short-term investment options with regular income or growth.
