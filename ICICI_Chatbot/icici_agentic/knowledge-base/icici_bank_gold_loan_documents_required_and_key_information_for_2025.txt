CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers quick and easy Gold Loans to meet your financial needs with minimal documentation. You can avail an instant Gold Loan by submitting basic documents such as proof of identity, proof of address, and two passport size photographs. The loan amount can go up to ₹2 crore depending on the value of your gold.

**Documents Required:**  
| Category of Customers | Documents Required |  
|----------------------|--------------------|  
| All Customers        | 1. Two passport size photographs  
                      | 2. Any one proof of identity: Driving License, Valid Passport, Voter ID Card, or Aadhaar Card  
                      | 3. Any one proof of address: Driving License, Voter ID Card, Aadhaar Card, Valid Passport, or Job Card issued by NREGA  
| Agriculture Loans    | Proof of land holding (if loan amount is more than ₹1 lakh)  

**Loan Process:**  
- Visit any ICICI Bank branch offering Gold Loans with your gold jewellery.  
- Your gold will be valued in your presence, sealed, and stored securely in the bank’s vault.  
- The vault is a highly secure facility ensuring transparency and safety.  
- You can avail the loan instantly across the counter with the submitted documents.  
- When you wish to redeem your gold, it will be returned in the same condition as submitted.  

**Additional Information:**  
- Loan amount: Up to ₹2 crore  
- For assistance or to initiate the process, give a missed call on 84448 84448.  
- By contacting ICICI Bank, you authorize them to call or SMS you regarding Gold Loan assistance, overriding any DNC/NDNC registrations.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimal documentation required: ID proof, address proof, and photos  
- Loan amount up to ₹2 crore based on gold valuation
