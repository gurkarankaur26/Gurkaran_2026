CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers education loans ranging from Rs 50 lakh to Rs 1 crore for studies in India or abroad. The loan is designed to support students pursuing professional higher education with minimal documentation, attractive interest rates, and quick disbursement.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Applicant must be a Resident Indian.  
- Age between 18 and 35 years.  
- Admission secured in a recognized institution in India or abroad through entrance tests.  
- Co-applicant (parent/sibling/guarantor) must provide proof of regular income.  
- Academic record should be good and proven.  
- Qualification pursued: Graduate, Postgraduate degree, or PG diploma.  
- Admission status must be confirmed.  
- Security: Tangible collateral or guarantor required depending on loan amount and income source.  

**Loan Features and Benefits:**  
- Loan amount: Rs 50 lakh to Rs 1 crore.  
- Collateral-free loan up to Rs 40 lakh for select institutes.  
- Interest rate starting at 10.50% per annum (minimum interest rate 8.30% p.a.).  
- Pre-visa disbursal facility for students going abroad.  
- Covers other student expenses such as laptops and exchange travel.  
- Seamless remittance to international institutes.  
- Hassle-free and timely disbursement process.  
- Interest deduction available under Section 80E of the Income Tax Act, 1961.  
- Enables students to take financial responsibility without depending solely on parents’ savings.  

**Documents Required:**  
- Proof of Identity: Passport, Aadhaar card, PAN card, or Voter ID.  
- Proof of Residence: Utility bills or rental agreement.  
- Academic Records: Admission letter, mark sheets, and other relevant documents.  
- Proof of income of co-applicant (salary slips, income tax returns, etc.).  

KEY POINTS:  
- Education loan amount ranges from Rs 50 lakh to Rs 1 crore.  
- Applicant age must be between 18 and 35 years.  
- Admission must be confirmed in a recognized institution in India or abroad.  
- Collateral-free loans available up to Rs 40 lakh for select institutes.  
- Interest rates start at 10.50% p.a., with a minimum of 8.30% p.a.  
- Pre-visa disbursal available for foreign education loans.  
- Interest deduction under Section 80E of Income Tax Act applicable.  
- Minimal documentation and quick loan disbursal.
