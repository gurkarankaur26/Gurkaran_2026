ICICI Bank provides dedicated customer care services for NRI customers, available 24/7 from anywhere in the world. NRI customers can reach out for assistance related to their banking needs, including general queries and specific services like trading and demat accounts.

Key contact numbers for NRI customers are:  
- Toll-Free Numbers (within India): 1800 1080, 1800 102 5600  
- Global Contact Number (Non-Toll-Free): +91 40 3099 8025  
- For i-Direct (Trading/Demat Account) queries: +91 22 3914 0422  
- Email support for i-Direct: nri@icicidirect.com  

Customers can also raise service requests online to get guidance on their queries.

KEY POINTS:  
- 24/7 customer care support for NRI customers  
- Toll-free helpline numbers available within India  
- Dedicated global contact number for overseas callers  
- Separate helpline and email for trading and demat account queries  
- Option to raise service requests online for personalized assistance
