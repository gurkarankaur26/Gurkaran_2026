TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Personal Loan Eligibility and Assessment Criteria

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Personal Loans

TAGS:  
Personal Loan, Eligibility, Income, Credit Score, Repayment Capacity, Self-Employed, Salary Account, Loan Assessment

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Personal Loans with eligibility and loan amount determined by multiple factors including monthly income, repayment capacity, employer profile, credit profile, and job stability. Having a Salary Account with ICICI Bank can facilitate a smoother eligibility assessment as income patterns are clearly visible, but eligibility is ultimately based on a comprehensive internal evaluation.

Key eligibility factors include:  
- Age: Minimum 23 years, maximum 65 years at loan tenure end  
- Income: Minimum monthly income of ₹30,000 for salaried individuals; self-employed applicants are assessed based on business continuity, income consistency, and financial documentation  
- Credit Profile: A healthy credit score (CIBIL score) and good credit history are essential  
- Existing Financial Obligations: Outstanding loans and credit card dues impact repayment capacity and loan amount eligibility  
- Employment Type: Both salaried and self-employed individuals are eligible, with assessment focusing on repayment capacity rather than employment type alone  

ICICI Bank provides a Personal Loan eligibility calculator to help applicants estimate their eligible loan amount based on income, existing obligations, and repayment capacity. This tool aids in quick eligibility checks and financial planning before applying.

To improve eligibility chances, applicants should:  
- Maintain a healthy credit profile  
- Reduce existing debts  
- Ensure regular and stable income  
- Avoid missed repayments  
- Keep financial records organized  

The maximum loan amount, such as a ₹10 lakh Personal Loan, depends on income level, credit score, and job stability. The bank conducts a detailed review to finalize eligibility and loan amount.

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum age: 23 years; maximum age at loan end: 65 years  
- Minimum monthly income for salaried: ₹30,000  
- Self-employed eligibility based on business income and financial records  
- Credit score and repayment capacity are critical factors  
- Existing loans and credit card dues affect loan amount eligibility  
- Salary Account with ICICI Bank can aid smoother assessment but is not sole eligibility criteria  
- Personal Loan eligibility calculator available for quick estimation  
- Maintain good credit health and reduce debts to improve eligibility
