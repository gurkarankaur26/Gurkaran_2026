CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank Corporate Net Banking (CNB) is a comprehensive online banking platform designed for corporate and business customers, including Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs), large enterprises, institutions, and business entities. It enables seamless 24/7 access to essential banking features through a fast and intuitive interface, allowing businesses to manage multiple accounts and transactions efficiently.

Key Features and Benefits:  
- **Multiple Account Management:** Manage several business accounts from a single platform.  
- **Payments and Collections:** Initiate single or bulk payments instantly with real-time tracking and instant reports. Supports tax payments and bill payments.  
- **Customized Approvals and Access Control:** Create personalized payment rules, reports, and access roles tailored to different business types.  
- **API and Host-to-Host (H2H) Integration:** Connect your ERP system with CNB via APIs or H2H for smarter, faster, and safer banking backed by enterprise-grade security.  
- **Instant Notifications:** Receive SMS and email alerts to track every stage of financial operations.  
- **Bulk File Upload:** Transfer funds to multiple beneficiaries simultaneously using bulk upload.  
- **Fund Transfers:** One-to-one fund transfers to ICICI Bank and non-ICICI Bank accounts within India.  

Fund Transfer Limits and Timings:  
| Time and Payment Mode | NEFT | RTGS | IMPS |  
|----------------------|------|------|------|  
| 1:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. | Corporate workflow limit | Corporate workflow limit | ₹20 Lakhs |  
| 12:00 a.m. to 1:00 a.m. and 7:00 p.m. onwards | Not available | Not available | Not available |  

Note: International fund transfers are not supported via CNB. Customers must visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch for such requests.

Login and Security:  
- Users can generate their Login ID and Password via the ICICI Bank website.  
- In case of incorrect password or locked account, users can reset their password online through the password reset process.  
- User ID activation is done by resetting the password.  

Additional Services:  
- Manage Fixed Deposits through CNB.  
- Generate Inquiry Card/Debit Card PIN online.  
- Register billers and track bill payments easily.  
- Instant transfers to own accounts.  

CNB is designed to simplify business banking by integrating payments, loans, trade, tax, collections, treasury, and accounts management into one powerful platform.

KEY POINTS:  
- Corporate Net Banking is available 24/7 with real-time transaction tracking.  
- Supports single and bulk payments with customizable access controls.  
- API and H2H integration for ERP connectivity.  
- Fund transfer limits: IMPS up to ₹20 Lakhs; NEFT and RTGS as per corporate workflow limits.  
- Only domestic fund transfers supported; international transfers require branch visit.  
- Password reset available online for login issues.  
- SMS and email notifications for transaction updates.  
- Additional features include Fixed Deposit management and biller registration.
