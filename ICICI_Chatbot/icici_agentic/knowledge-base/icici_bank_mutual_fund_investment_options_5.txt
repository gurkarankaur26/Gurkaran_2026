CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a variety of mutual fund investment options in partnership with Aditya Birla Sun Life Mutual Fund. These funds cater to different investment preferences including growth plans, dividend payout options (IDCW - Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal), and frequency of dividend payouts (daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly). The funds are available under different categories such as Dynamic Bond Fund, Floating Rate Fund, GSec Fund, Income Fund, and Liquid Fund. Each fund is available in Direct (Dir), Regular (Reg), and Retail (Ret) plans with specific fund codes for easy identification.

Key Mutual Fund Options:  
- **Dynamic Bond Fund**: Available in Growth, Monthly IDCW, and Quarterly IDCW options.  
- **Floating Rate Fund**: Offers Daily, Weekly, and Growth options under Direct, Regular, and Retail plans.  
- **GSec Fund**: Available in Growth and Quarterly IDCW options under Direct and Regular plans.  
- **Income Fund**: Offers Growth and Quarterly IDCW options under Direct and Regular plans.  
- **Liquid Fund**: Available in Daily, Weekly IDCW, and Growth options under Direct, Regular, and Institutional Plans.

Each fund option is identified by a unique code for transactions and tracking.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple mutual fund schemes available with Aditya Birla Sun Life via ICICI Bank.  
- Options include Growth and Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) plans.  
- Dividend payout frequencies include daily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly.  
- Funds available under Direct, Regular, and Retail plans.  
- Unique fund codes provided for each scheme and plan variant.  
- Fund categories include Dynamic Bond, Floating Rate, GSec, Income, and Liquid Funds.
