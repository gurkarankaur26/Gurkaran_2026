TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Mutual Fund Investment Options and Dividend Plans

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY:  
Mutual Funds, Investment, Banking Products

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
This document provides a detailed list of mutual fund schemes available through ICICI Bank, focusing on various dividend payout options and growth plans. The schemes include multiple fund categories such as Short Duration Funds, Strategic Bond Funds, Treasury Advantage Funds, Ultra Short Duration Funds, and Banking and PSU Funds. Each scheme is available in different plans including Direct and Regular options, with dividend payout frequencies such as Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, and Annual IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) options, as well as Growth options.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:

1. **Axis Short Duration Fund**  
   - Weekly IDCW option available.

2. **Axis Strategic Bond Fund**  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans.  
   - Dividend options: Growth, Half-Yearly IDCW, Quarterly IDCW.

3. **Axis Treasury Advantage Fund**  
   - Available in Direct, Regular, and Retail plans.  
   - Dividend options: Daily IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Growth.

4. **Axis Ultra Short Duration Fund**  
   - Available in Direct and Regular plans.  
   - Dividend options: Daily IDCW, Weekly IDCW, Monthly IDCW, Growth.

5. **Bandhan Banking and PSU Fund**  
   - Direct plan options with Annual IDCW, Daily IDCW, and Fortnightly IDCW.

**Definitions:**  
- **IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal):** Dividend payout option where investors receive periodic income from the fund.  
- **Growth Option:** Earnings are reinvested in the fund, leading to capital appreciation.

**Investment Process:**  
- Investors can select the preferred mutual fund scheme and plan (Direct or Regular).  
- Choose the dividend payout frequency or growth option as per investment goals.  
- Investments can be made through ICICI Bank’s platform or authorized distributors.

**Benefits:**  
- Flexibility in choosing dividend payout frequency to suit income needs.  
- Wide range of fund categories catering to different risk profiles and investment horizons.  
- Direct plans offer lower expense ratios compared to regular plans.  
- Growth options suitable for long-term capital appreciation.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple mutual fund schemes available with various dividend payout options (Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, Half-Yearly, Annual).  
- Both Direct and Regular plans offered for most funds.  
- IDCW options provide periodic income; Growth options reinvest earnings.  
- Fund categories include Short Duration, Strategic Bond, Treasury Advantage, Ultra Short Duration, and Banking & PSU Funds.  
- Suitable for investors seeking income or growth through mutual fund investments.  
- Investment can be done via ICICI Bank’s platform.
