CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s Mutual Fund Calculator is an online tool designed to help investors estimate potential returns on their mutual fund investments, whether through a one-time lump sum or a Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). The calculator is user-friendly, requires no technical expertise, and is available 24/7 for convenient access.

Key features include:  
- Estimation of maturity value based on investment amount, duration, and expected rate of return.  
- Support for both lump sum and SIP investment modes.  
- Ability to compare returns between different investment methods.  
- Calculation of various return types such as absolute, annualised, total, trailing, point-to-point, and rolling returns.  
- Helps investors plan financial goals by projecting returns over fixed tenures.  

How to use the calculator:  
1. Select the investment mode: ‘One-Time Investment’ or ‘SIP’.  
2. Enter the investment amount or target amount.  
3. Input the investment duration.  
4. Enter the expected rate of return.  
5. Click ‘Calculate’ to view the estimated maturity value or required investment amount.  

Important Information:  
- The calculator provides estimated returns based on the inputs provided and does not guarantee actual returns.  
- The expected rate of return is a critical input as small differences can significantly impact the maturity value over time.  

Example illustrating the impact of a 1% difference in return rate over 10 years for a SIP of ₹10,000/month:  
| SIP Amount     | Tenure  | Return Rate | Expected Value  |  
|---------------|---------|-------------|-----------------|  
| ₹10,000/month | 10 years| 10%         | ₹20,14,576      |  
| ₹10,000/month | 10 years| 11%         | ₹21,24,298      |  

Minimum Investment:  
- Investors can start mutual fund investments with as little as ₹500 per instalment through SIP, making it accessible and flexible for all types of investors.
