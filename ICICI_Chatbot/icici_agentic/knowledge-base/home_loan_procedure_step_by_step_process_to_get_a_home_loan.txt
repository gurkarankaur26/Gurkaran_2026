CONTENT:  
A Home Loan enables you to purchase or build a house by borrowing money from a bank or financial institution. The process to get a home loan in India is streamlined and convenient, requiring applicants to meet eligibility criteria, provide necessary documents, and maintain a good credit score.

**Step-by-Step Guide to Availing a Home Loan**

1. **Check your Eligibility**  
   Use the Home Loan eligibility calculator on the ICICI Bank website by entering your income, ongoing EMIs, illustrative interest rate, and desired loan duration. This provides an instant estimate of the loan amount you are eligible for.

2. **Select the Loan Amount and Tenure**  
   Choose the loan amount and repayment tenure, which can be up to 20 years. This decision affects your monthly EMI and total interest payable.

3. **Submit the Application and Documents**  
   Complete the loan application with accurate details and submit required documents:  
   - KYC documents (Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, utility bills) for identity and address proof  
   - Bank statements  
   - Relevant property documents  
   Documents can be submitted online via ICICI Bank Net Banking or the iMobile app.

4. **Loan Sanction**  
   The bank reviews your application and, upon approval, issues a Sanction Letter detailing the loan amount, interest rate, and other terms.

5. **Loan Disbursement**  
   After document verification, the loan amount is disbursed directly to the property seller or builder, completing the home loan process.

**Interest Rate Options**  
- **Fixed Interest Rate:** The interest rate remains constant throughout the loan tenure, ensuring fixed EMI payments.  
- **Floating Interest Rate:** The interest rate varies based on market conditions, which can lead to fluctuating EMI amounts.

KEY POINTS:  
- Eligibility can be checked online using ICICI Bank’s Home Loan eligibility calculator.  
- Loan tenure can be up to 20 years.  
- Required documents include KYC proofs, bank statements, and property documents.  
- Application and document submission can be done online via Net Banking or iMobile app.  
- Sanction Letter contains loan amount, interest rate, and terms.  
- Loan disbursed directly to property seller/builder after verification.  
- Choose between fixed and floating interest rates based on preference for EMI stability or flexibility.
