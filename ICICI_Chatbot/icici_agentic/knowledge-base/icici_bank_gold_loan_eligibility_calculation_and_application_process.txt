CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans that allow customers to borrow money against the value of their gold. To determine the loan amount, it is important to understand the market value of your gold, which depends on its weight, purity, and current market price.

**Key Steps to Calculate and Apply for a Gold Loan:**  
1. **Determine Gold Weight and Purity:** Obtain accurate information about the weight and purity of your gold from a trusted jeweller.  
2. **Monitor Market Price:** Keep track of the current market price of gold, available on financial news websites or the ICICI Bank website. This helps in identifying the best time to apply for a loan.  
3. **Calculate Market Value:** Multiply the weight and purity of your gold by the current market price to get the market value.  
4. **Apply Loan-to-Value (LTV) Ratio:** Multiply the market value by the LTV percentage offered by ICICI Bank to find out the maximum loan amount you can borrow.  
5. **Assess Financial Situation:** Before applying, evaluate your monthly income, existing debts, and expenses to ensure you can meet repayment obligations.  
6. **Use Online Tools:** ICICI Bank provides a Gold Loan eligibility calculator on its website, which factors in the weight, purity, and market price of gold to give an accurate loan estimate.

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount depends on gold weight, purity, and current market price.  
- LTV ratio is applied to the market value to determine borrowing limit.  
- Financial assessment is crucial before applying for a loan.  
- ICICI Bank offers an online Gold Loan eligibility calculator for convenience.
