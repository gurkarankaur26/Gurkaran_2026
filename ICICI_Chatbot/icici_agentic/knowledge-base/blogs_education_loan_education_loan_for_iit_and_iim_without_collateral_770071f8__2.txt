CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers an Education Loan specifically designed for students aspiring to study at premier institutions like IITs and IIMs. This loan covers tuition fees, accommodation, and other academic expenses, making quality education accessible even for students without collateral.

**Loan Features:**  
- **Loan Amount:** The maximum loan amount is based on tuition fees and other related educational expenses, subject to institutional eligibility and ICICI Bank’s prevailing loan guidelines.  
- **Moratorium Period:** The loan includes a moratorium period equal to the course duration plus an additional 6 to 12 months, during which the borrower is required to pay only simple interest.  
- **Interest Rates:** Interest rates vary; applicants should verify the current rates before applying.  
- **Fees and Charges:** Additional costs may include processing fees, pre-payment penalties, and late payment charges. It is important to clarify all such fees with ICICI Bank before finalizing the loan.  
- **Repayment:** Repayment terms vary and should be reviewed carefully. ICICI Bank provides an Education Loan EMI calculator on its website to help estimate monthly installments.

**Benefits:**  
- No collateral required, making it accessible for students without assets to pledge.  
- Covers a wide range of educational expenses including tuition and accommodation.  
- Flexible moratorium period aligned with course duration plus extra months.  
- Support for students attending premier institutions like IITs and IIMs.
