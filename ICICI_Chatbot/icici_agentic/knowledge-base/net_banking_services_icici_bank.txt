CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank’s Net Banking service allows customers to perform a wide range of banking transactions and manage their finances conveniently from anywhere in the world. The platform provides real-time access to all accounts and offers over 350 services through a user-friendly dashboard designed for ease of use.

Key features of ICICI Bank Net Banking include:  
- **Account Management:** View account details, track assets, liabilities, loans, investments, and credit cards on a single platform.  
- **Deposits:** Open and manage fixed deposits (FD) and recurring deposits (RD) online.  
- **Fund Transfer:** Transfer funds within ICICI Bank accounts or to other banks using NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, and UPI.  
- **Bill Payments:** Pay utility bills, credit card bills, and other payments seamlessly.  
- **Credit Cards:** Access credit card details, make payments, and raise service requests.  
- **Loans:** Apply for loans, view loan details, and raise service requests.  
- **Investments:** Manage investments including mutual funds, PPF, and other instruments.  
- **Demat Services:** Access and manage Demat accounts and related service requests.  
- **Insurance:** View and manage insurance policies and service requests.  
- **Forex and Remittance:** Access foreign exchange services and remit funds internationally.  
- **Additional Services:** Recharge mobile phones, access tax services, use the shopping mall, and manage travel cards.  
- **Personalization:** Customize your profile and dashboard for a tailored banking experience.  
- **Chatbot Support:** Get instant assistance through the integrated chatbot for queries and support.  

The platform is designed to provide a seamless and comprehensive banking experience, enabling customers to carry out most banking activities without visiting a branch.
