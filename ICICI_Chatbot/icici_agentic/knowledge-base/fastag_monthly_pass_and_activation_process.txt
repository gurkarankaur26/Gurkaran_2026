TITLE: FASTag Monthly Pass and Activation Process  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Digital Payments, FASTag, Toll Payments  
TAGS: FASTag, Monthly Pass, Toll Payment, NHAI, Activation, Prepaid, Electronic Toll Collection  

CONTENT:  
The FASTag monthly pass is a prepaid toll payment system designed for frequent highway travelers. It enables electronic toll payments without the need to stop at toll plazas, facilitating a faster and more convenient journey. This service is offered by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) and other toll operators across the country.

The monthly pass allows users to pay toll charges in advance, making it ideal for those who regularly travel on highways. It eliminates the need to remember to recharge the FASTag frequently, as the pass covers toll payments for the entire month.

FASTag activation depends on the completion of verification and linking processes. Once the FASTag is approved and correctly affixed to the vehicle’s windshield, activation is typically completed shortly after successful validation. This ensures that the FASTag is ready for use at toll plazas without delay.

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag monthly pass is a prepaid toll payment system for frequent highway travelers.  
- Offered by NHAI and other toll operators across India.  
- Enables electronic toll payments without stopping at toll plazas.  
- Monthly pass covers toll payments for an entire month, reducing the need for frequent recharges.  
- Activation depends on verification and linking completion.  
- Activation is typically completed shortly after successful validation and correct affixing of the FASTag.
