TITLE: iStartup 2.0 – Comprehensive Banking and Beyond Banking Solutions for Startups  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
iStartup 2.0 is ICICI Bank’s holistic and comprehensive banking programme designed specifically for startups and MSMEs, offering a combination of banking and beyond banking services across various stages of a startup’s lifecycle. The programme aims to provide startups with seamless digital onboarding, integrated business and personal banking, and access to a wide range of allied services to support their growth and operational efficiency.

Key features of iStartup 2.0 include:  
- **Digital Onboarding:** Startups can apply for the iStartup Current Account digitally during company incorporation via the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) website or through ICICI Bank’s digital platforms.  
- **Eligibility:** Any startup can apply for the programme, regardless of whether they are existing ICICI Bank customers.  
- **One-Stop Shop for Startup Needs:** The programme offers services beyond banking such as website development, digital marketing, staffing solutions, regulatory and tax advisory, and accounting support through partnerships with leading service providers under the ‘Startup Assist’ initiative.  
- **Integrated Banking Services:** The iStartup Current Account caters to all business, trade, and personal banking requirements within a single relationship, accessible via ICICI Bank’s mobile and internet banking platforms.  
- **Convenience and Efficiency:** The programme reduces paperwork and manual processes, enabling startups to focus on business growth by improving productivity and operational efficiency.  

Application Process:  
- Apply digitally during company incorporation on the MCA website.  
- Visit https://www.icici.bank.in/business-banking/accounts/current-account  
- SMS ‘STARTUP’ to 5676766  
- Visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch for assistance.  

Benefits:  
- Instant digital onboarding during company incorporation.  
- Comprehensive banking and allied business services in one place.  
- Access to expert services for early and growth-stage startup needs.  
- Robust digital platforms for on-the-go banking and transactions.  
- Support designed to free up bandwidth from administrative tasks.  

KEY POINTS:  
- iStartup 2.0 is open to all startups, ICICI Bank customers or not.  
- Digital onboarding available via MCA website and ICICI Bank digital channels.  
- Offers current account tailored for startups with integrated business and personal banking.  
- ‘Startup Assist’ provides allied services like marketing, staffing, regulatory, and accounting support.  
- Application via MCA incorporation, ICICI Bank website, SMS, or branch visit.  
- Focus on reducing paperwork and enhancing startup productivity.
