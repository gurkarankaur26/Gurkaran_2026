CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Foreign Currency Non-Resident (Bank) [FCNR (B)] Fixed Deposits tailored for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to help them efficiently grow their foreign currency earnings. These deposits are maintained in foreign currency and protect the principal and interest from exchange rate fluctuations.

**Key Features of FCNR (B) Deposits:**  
- Deposits can be made in multiple foreign currencies such as USD, GBP, EUR, CAD, AUD, JPY, and others.  
- Tenure options typically range from 1 year to 5 years.  
- Interest is compounded quarterly and credited either to the deposit or to a linked account as per customer preference.  
- FCNR (B) deposits are fully repatriable, meaning both principal and interest can be transferred abroad without restrictions.  
- Interest earned is exempt from Indian income tax and capital gains tax.  
- No risk of exchange rate fluctuation on principal and interest as deposits are maintained in foreign currency.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) and Persons of Indian Origin (PIOs) are eligible to open FCNR (B) deposits.  
- Deposits can be opened singly or jointly with another NRI or resident Indian close relative.  

**Interest Rates:**  
- ICICI Bank offers competitive interest rates on FCNR (B) deposits which vary based on currency and tenure.  
- Interest rates are subject to change and are updated periodically. Customers are advised to check the latest rates before investing.  

**Process to Open FCNR (B) Deposit:**  
1. Visit ICICI Bank’s NRI banking portal or branch.  
2. Submit the required documents including NRI status proof, passport, visa, and overseas address proof.  
3. Choose the currency and tenure for the deposit.  
4. Fund the deposit from an NRE/FCNR account or by inward remittance from abroad.  
5. Receive deposit confirmation and interest payment details.  

**Benefits:**  
- Protection against exchange rate risk.  
- Tax benefits on interest income.  
- Flexible tenure and currency options.  
- Easy repatriation of funds.  
- Quarterly interest payouts or reinvestment options.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FCNR (B) deposits are foreign currency fixed deposits for NRIs.  
- Available in multiple currencies with tenures from 1 to 5 years.  
- Interest compounded quarterly, fully repatriable principal and interest.  
- Interest income is tax-free in India.  
- Eligibility limited to NRIs and PIOs.  
- Competitive interest rates subject to periodic revision.  
- Easy online and branch-based application process.
