TITLE:  
Invesco India Mutual Fund Schemes Overview

SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mutual Funds  
TAGS: Mutual Funds, Invesco India, Investment, Fund Schemes, IDCW, Growth, Duration Funds

CONTENT:  
This document provides an overview of various Invesco India Mutual Fund schemes available for investment. The schemes include different fund types such as Low Duration Fund, Money Market Fund, Overnight Fund, Short Duration Fund, and Ultra Short Duration Fund. Each scheme offers multiple plan options including Direct and Regular plans, with different dividend payout frequencies such as Daily (Dly), Weekly (Wkly), Monthly (Mthly), Quarterly (Qtly), and Annual (Ann) Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal (IDCW) options, as well as Growth options.

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:  
- **Invesco India Low Duration Fund**: Available with Weekly IDCW options.  
- **Invesco India Money Market Fund**: Offers Direct and Regular plans with Daily, Monthly, Weekly IDCW, and Growth options.  
- **Invesco India Overnight Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Daily, Monthly, Weekly IDCW, and Growth options.  
- **Invesco India Short Duration Fund**: Available in Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options.  
- **Invesco India Ultra Short Duration Fund**: Offers Annual, Daily, Monthly, Quarterly IDCW, and Growth options in Direct plans.

Each scheme is identified by a unique code for easy reference and transaction purposes.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple Invesco India Mutual Fund schemes are available catering to different investment durations and preferences.  
- Plans are offered in both Direct and Regular options.  
- Dividend payout frequencies include Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Quarterly, and Annual IDCW.  
- Growth options are available for capital appreciation.  
- Suitable for investors seeking short to ultra-short duration investment options with varying dividend payout preferences.  
- Unique scheme codes are assigned for each plan and option to facilitate transactions.
