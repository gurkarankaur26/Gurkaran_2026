TITLE: Current Account and Linked Sweep-in Fixed Deposit Facilities  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Business Banking  
TAGS: Current Account, Fixed Deposit, Sweep-in Facility, Business Banking, Interest Rates, Surplus Funds  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Current Accounts designed for businesses to manage their day-to-day transactions efficiently. Since Current Accounts do not earn interest, businesses with surplus funds can benefit from the linked sweep-in Fixed Deposit facilities. This facility allows surplus funds to be automatically transferred into a Fixed Deposit, enabling the funds to earn interest while remaining accessible for business transactions.  

The sweep-in Fixed Deposit facility ensures that businesses maximize returns on idle funds without compromising liquidity. The interest earned on the Fixed Deposit is credited to the Current Account, providing seamless access to funds when needed.  

Terms and conditions apply to the use of the sweep-in Fixed Deposit facility.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Current Accounts do not earn interest.  
- Linked sweep-in Fixed Deposit facility allows surplus funds to earn interest.  
- Surplus funds are automatically transferred to Fixed Deposit and remain accessible.  
- Interest earned is credited back to the Current Account.  
- Suitable for businesses with fluctuating surplus funds.  
- Terms and conditions apply.
