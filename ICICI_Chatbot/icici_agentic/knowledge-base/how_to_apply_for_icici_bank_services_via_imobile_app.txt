TITLE: How to Apply for ICICI Bank Services via iMobile App  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Mobile Banking, Account Services  
TAGS: iMobile, Mobile Banking, Application Process, ICICI Bank, Account Opening  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to apply for various banking services through its iMobile app. To complete your application, follow these steps:

1. Select your plan details and preferences, then click 'Next'.  
2. Fill out the questionnaire that appears and proceed to the next step.  
3. Click on 'Confirm the application' to finalize your request.

Using the iMobile app enhances your banking experience by allowing you to manage your banking needs anytime and anywhere.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Application process is fully digital via iMobile app.  
- Stepwise guidance: select plan, fill questionnaire, confirm application.  
- Enables easy and quick access to banking services.  
- Designed to improve user convenience and banking experience.
