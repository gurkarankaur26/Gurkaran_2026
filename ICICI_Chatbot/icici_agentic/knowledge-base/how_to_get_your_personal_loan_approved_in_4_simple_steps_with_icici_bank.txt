TITLE: How to Get Your Personal Loan Approved in 4 Simple Steps with ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans

CONTENT:  
A Personal Loan from ICICI Bank is an unsecured loan designed to meet your financial emergencies with minimal documentation and fast processing. You can avail a loan amount ranging from Rs 50,000 up to Rs 50 lakh, with interest rates starting at 10.85% per annum. The repayment tenure is flexible, from 12 months up to 72 months.  

Whether you hold a Savings Account with ICICI Bank or not, you can apply through the Unified Customer Journey (UCJ) platform. Non-ICICI Bank customers need to upload scanned KYC documents to facilitate the loan process and future banking needs.  

The loan is processed quickly, with disbursal possible within 20 minutes after application verification. There are no restrictions on the usage of funds except that the loan cannot be used for speculative purposes.  

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Must be a resident of India  
- Age between 23 and 65 years  
- Minimum professional experience of 3 years  

**Application Process:**  
1. New applicants visit https://bit.ly/39QSmpP  
2. Enter mobile number, PAN card details, and email ID  
3. Fill in additional required information  
4. Upon verification, the loan amount is disbursed within 20 minutes  

**Benefits:**  
- Unsecured loan with no collateral required  
- Minimal paperwork and fast processing  
- Flexible loan amount and tenure options  
- Instant loan disbursal for eligible applicants  
- Suitable for various financial needs such as marriage, education, medical emergencies, travel, etc.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount: Rs 50,000 to Rs 50 lakh  
- Interest rate: Starting at 10.85% p.a.  
- Tenure: 12 to 72 months  
- Eligibility: Resident Indian, age 23-65, minimum 3 years professional experience  
- Instant loan disbursal within 20 minutes after verification  
- No collateral required  
- Funds can be used for any purpose except speculative activities
