TITLE: Reloading Your ICICI Bank Forex Card in a Foreign Country  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Forex Card Services  
TAGS: Forex Card, Reload, International Travel, Online Banking, Authorization, Foreign Currency  

CONTENT:  
Reloading your ICICI Bank Forex Card while abroad is a simple and convenient process that enhances your international travel experience by reducing the need to carry cash and manage currency fluctuations. You can reload your Forex Card multiple times as needed, ensuring continuous access to foreign currency funds.

**Reloading Process:**  
- **Online Reload:** Reload your Forex Card instantly through the iMobile app or Internet Banking, anytime and anywhere.  
- **Authorization Letter:** If you are unable to reload the card yourself, you can authorize someone else to do it on your behalf by providing an authorization letter with your signature.  
- **Required Documents for Authorized Reload:**  
  - Photocopy of your Passport  
  - Self-attested Form A2  
  - Signed reload form  
  - Identity card of the person submitting the reload request  

**Benefits:**  
- Quick and easy top-up within minutes  
- Multiple reloads allowed without restrictions  
- Timely transaction alerts for every reload and usage  
- Enhanced security and convenience during international travel  

KEY POINTS:  
- Forex Card can be reloaded online via iMobile app and Internet Banking.  
- Authorization letter required if someone else reloads the card.  
- Required documents include passport copy, self-attested Form A2, signed reload form, and identity proof of the authorized person.  
- Multiple reloads allowed with instant processing.  
- Transaction alerts sent for every reload and usage.
