SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
Specialised Investment Funds (SIFs) are investment vehicles managed by experienced fund managers who actively adjust strategies based on thorough research and prevailing market outlook. These funds offer greater flexibility in portfolio design, allowing adaptability in asset allocation and strategy selection to suit evolving market conditions.

SIFs are particularly suitable for High Net-Worth Individuals (HNIs) and institutional investors seeking more flexible investment options compared to traditional mutual funds. They provide higher return potential through diverse investment strategies and better risk management by implementing structured exposure limits.

SIFs are regulated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), ensuring investor protection and compliance with regulatory standards. They are launched under mutual fund regulations, and taxation is applied at the investor level following the same broad rules as mutual funds.

ICICI Bank Limited is an AMFI Registered Mutual Fund Distributor and SIF Distributor, facilitating access to these investment products.
