TITLE: ICICI Bank Sapphiro Credit Card: Travel and Lifestyle Rewards  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Travel Rewards, Lifestyle Benefits, Movie Tickets, Emirates Sapphiro, Spend Eligibility  

CONTENT:  
The ICICI Bank Emirates Sapphiro Credit Card offers exclusive travel and lifestyle rewards, including complimentary movie and event tickets via BookMyShow. To avail the complimentary tickets offer starting April 1, 2026, cardholders must spend ₹25,000 or more on their Emirates Sapphiro Credit Card within the preceding quarter. This spend qualifies the cardholder for the complimentary ticket offer in the following calendar quarter.

**Spends and Offer Eligibility Period:**  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Dec 26, 2025, and Mar 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets from Apr 1, 2026, to June 30, 2026.  
- Spend ₹25,000 between Mar 26, 2026, and June 25, 2026 → Eligible for complimentary movie tickets from July 1, 2026, to Sep 30, 2026.

**Additional Details:**  
- Customers holding dual network credit cards (Mastercard & Amex or Visa & RuPay) can avail one complimentary movie offer per month, per card variant.  
- Any additional bookings beyond the complimentary benefit will be charged as per the rates set by the service provider.  
- Add-on or Plus card members must individually meet the ₹25,000 spend requirement in the preceding quarter to be eligible for the complimentary ticket offer in the subsequent quarter.

KEY POINTS:  
- Complimentary movie/event tickets via BookMyShow available on spending ₹25,000 per quarter.  
- Eligibility for complimentary tickets applies in the calendar quarter following the spend quarter.  
- Dual network cardholders can avail one complimentary ticket per month per variant.  
- Add-on cardholders must meet spend criteria individually.  
- Additional bookings beyond complimentary tickets are chargeable.
