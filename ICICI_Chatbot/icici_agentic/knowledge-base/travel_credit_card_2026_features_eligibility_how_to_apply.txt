**Additional Benefits:**  
- Reward points earned on all purchases can be redeemed for travel bookings, merchandise, or statement credit.  
- Travel insurance coverage is often included with select cards, providing protection against trip cancellations, lost baggage, and medical emergencies.  
- Fuel surcharge waiver on transactions between ₹ 400 and ₹ 4,000 at all fuel pumps for select cards.  

**Eligibility:**  
- Indian residents aged 18 years and above can apply.  
- Income criteria vary by card variant; higher-tier cards require higher income proof.  
- Applicants must have a good credit score and a stable income source.  

**How to Apply:**  
- Visit the ICICI Bank official website or branch.  
- Choose the preferred Travel Credit Card variant.  
- Fill out the application form with required KYC documents and income proof.  
- Submit the application online or at the branch.  
- Upon approval, the card is delivered to the registered address.  

KEY POINTS:  
- ICICI Bank offers multiple Travel Credit Cards tailored to different travel needs and spending patterns.  
- Fees vary from ₹750 to ₹12,000 plus GST for joining and annual charges depending on the card.  
- Benefits include airport lounge access, air miles, milestone rewards, fuel surcharge waivers, and travel insurance.  
- Reward points can be earned on domestic and international spends, with accelerated points on select categories.  
- Eligibility requires Indian residency, minimum age 18, income proof, and good credit history.  
- Application can be completed online or offline with necessary documentation.
