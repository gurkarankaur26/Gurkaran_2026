Owning a car often requires financial planning, especially when opting for a Car Loan. Determining the Equated Monthly Instalment (EMI) you can afford is crucial to ensure your loan repayments fit comfortably within your budget without causing financial strain.

**Understanding Car Loan EMI**  
The EMI for a car loan depends on three main factors:  
- **Loan Amount:** Total amount borrowed, including the car’s on-road price, insurance, and other associated costs.  
- **Interest Rate:** The cost of borrowing, which varies based on creditworthiness and market conditions.  
- **Loan Tenure:** The repayment period. Shorter tenures mean higher EMIs but less interest paid overall; longer tenures reduce EMI amounts but increase total interest paid.
