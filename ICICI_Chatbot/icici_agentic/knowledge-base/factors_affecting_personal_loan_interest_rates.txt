TITLE: Factors Affecting Personal Loan Interest Rates  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Interest Rates, Eligibility, Credit Score, Income, Employment, Loan Repayment  

CONTENT:  
A Personal Loan from ICICI Bank is an unsecured loan designed to meet diverse financial needs such as buying a car, funding higher education, or covering wedding expenses. It does not require collateral and offers quick disbursal. However, interest rates on Personal Loans are typically higher than secured loans like Home Loans and vary based on several factors related to the borrower’s profile.

Key factors affecting Personal Loan interest rates include:

- **Credit History:** The lender evaluates your CIBIL score, a three-digit number reflecting your credit repayment history. A good credit score indicates reliability and can help secure a lower interest rate.  
- **Income:** Candidates with a fixed and guaranteed income are offered lower interest rates as they are considered more reliable. Higher income and job security positively influence the rate.  
- **Employment Type:** Salaried individuals, especially those in government or private sector jobs with job security, are eligible for attractive interest rates. Self-employed and business owners can also apply but may face different rates.  
- **Relationship with the Lender:** Existing customers with a loyal banking relationship can negotiate better interest rates on Personal Loans.  
- **Repayment and Loan Defaults:** A history of timely repayments improves credit score and loan eligibility. Defaults or bad repayment history lead to higher interest rates or loan rejection.

ICICI Bank offers Personal Loan interest rates starting from **10.60% per annum**.

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal Loans are unsecured and can be used for any financial need.  
- Interest rates depend on credit score, income, employment type, relationship with the bank, and repayment history.  
- Good credit score and stable income lead to lower interest rates.  
- Government and private sector employees generally get better rates due to job security.  
- ICICI Bank Personal Loan interest rates start at 10.60% p.a.
