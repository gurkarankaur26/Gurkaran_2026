TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card - Application, Eligibility, and FAQs  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Eligibility, Application Process, Fees, Interest Rates, CIBIL Score, PAN, Aadhaar, NRI, Student Cards, Secured Cards  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of Credit Cards with varying benefits and eligibility criteria. Customers can apply online via the ICICI Bank website or mobile app by submitting basic personal and income details. The Bank verifies eligibility and communicates the application status.

**Eligibility:**  
- Primary Credit Card applicants must be at least 21 years old.  
- Add-on (Plus) Cards can be issued to individuals aged 12 years and above, depending on the card type and Bank policy.  
- Applicants do not need to hold an ICICI Bank account to apply.  
- Final-year students from select top colleges may apply under specific student card programs, subject to Bank verification.  
- NRI customers can apply for eligible Credit Cards, subject to regulatory guidelines and documentation.  
- Retired individuals can apply based on pension income, savings, or by opting for secured cards against Fixed Deposits.  
- Applicants without a credit score can apply for secured Credit Cards against Fixed Deposits or entry-level cards to build credit history.  

**Application Process:**  
- Apply online via the ICICI Bank website or iMobile app.  
- Provide PAN card details (mandatory for identity verification and regulatory compliance).  
- Link Aadhaar and PAN as per regulatory guidelines to complete KYC and enable smooth processing.  
- Track application status online using the “Track My Application” feature with registered mobile number and application details.  

**Approval Factors:**  
- Credit history and CIBIL score significantly impact approval chances and credit limit.  
- Poor or inconsistent credit behavior may reduce eligibility.  
- Applications may be declined due to insufficient credit history, low income, incomplete documentation, multiple recent applications, or failure to meet internal criteria.  
- Having an active loan does not disqualify applicants; approval depends on overall credit profile and repayment history.  

**Credit Limit Determination:**  
- Based on declared income, existing repayment history, credit score, and other internal assessments.  

**Communication:**  
- Applicants receive communication regarding approval or rejection.  
- Rejection reasons are broadly shared (e.g., eligibility or documentation issues), but detailed internal credit assessment parameters are not disclosed.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Minimum age for primary card: 21 years; add-on card: 12 years and above.  
- PAN card mandatory; Aadhaar-PAN linking required.  
- No ICICI Bank account required to apply.  
- Student, NRI, retired, and secured card options available.  
- Application can be tracked online.  
- Approval depends on credit score, income, documentation, and internal criteria.  
- Secured cards available against Fixed Deposits for those without credit history.
