TITLE: ICICI Bank FASTag Now Available on Google Pay  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Digital Payments, FASTag, Toll Payments  
TAGS: FASTag, ICICI Bank, Google Pay, UPI, Digital Toll Payments, NPCI, IHMCL, NHAI  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank has partnered with Google Pay to enable users to order, track, and recharge ICICI Bank FASTag digitally through UPI on the Google Pay app. This collaboration allows customers to obtain FASTag without visiting merchants or toll plazas, ensuring a safe and contactless experience. ICICI Bank is the first bank to offer FASTag issuance via Google Pay.

FASTag is a product owned by Indian Highways Management Company Ltd. (IHMCL) and is part of the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) and National Highway Authorities of India (NHAI) initiative to digitize toll payments on national and state highways.

ICICI Bank has integrated FASTag at Mumbai toll plazas and the parking zone at GMR Hyderabad International Airport to promote contactless and hassle-free transactions. The bank was the first to launch FASTag nationally on the Mumbai – Vadodara corridor in 2013.

Key benefits of this collaboration include doorstep delivery of FASTag free of cost, seamless application through UPI, and interoperability between banks on a digital payments platform. This initiative supports increased adoption of FASTag for toll payments, especially useful during the pandemic.

Statements from key officials highlight the importance of this partnership in enhancing digital payment efficiency in transit and making interstate travel frictionless.

- ICICI Bank FASTag can now be ordered, tracked, and recharged via Google Pay using UPI.
