CONTENT:  
FASTag is a reloadable tag affixed to your vehicle's windscreen that enables automatic deduction of toll charges using RFID technology, allowing seamless passage through toll plazas without manual payment. It is linked to a prepaid account from which toll charges are deducted.

**KYV (Know Your Vehicle) Verification Process:**  
- Mandatory for all FASTags issued.  
- Requires submission of clear images:  
  - Front and side views of the vehicle:  
    - Front image must clearly show the Licence Plate Number, FASTag sticker affixed on the vehicle, and edges of the vehicle.  
    - Side image must show edges of the vehicle from both front and back sides.  
  - FASTag sticker image:  
    - Taken from inside the vehicle after affixing the FASTag on the windshield. The Tag Serial Number must be clearly visible.  
  - Registration Certificate (RC):  
    - Upload front and back images of the RC. Digital RC copies from DigiLocker or mParivahan apps are acceptable.  

- All images must be clear and correct for verification.  
- If the KYV verification fails or is declined by the bank, the FASTag will be hotlisted, disabling its use at toll plazas. Penalties imposed by regulatory authorities must be paid manually.  
- To remove the FASTag from the hotlist, upload correct documents again for re-verification.  

**FASTag Account Recharge Limits:**  
- For customers with complete KYC: ₹1,00,000 limit.  
- For customers without KYC documents: ₹10,000 limit.  
- Recharge options include cheque payment, Internet Banking, or iMobile app.  

**KYC Update for Amazon Pay ICICI Bank FASTag:**  
- Customers can update their FASTag KYC details directly through the ICICI Bank platform by logging into their account and accessing the FASTag section.
