TITLE: Gold Loan - Impact of Fluctuating Gold Rates on Loan Amount  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Loans  
TAGS: Gold Loan, Loan Amount, Gold Rates, Interest Rates, Eligibility, Fees  

CONTENT:  
Gold loans from ICICI Bank are influenced by the prevailing gold rates, which directly impact the loan amount per gram of gold pledged. When gold prices are higher, customers can avail a larger loan amount for the same quantity of gold. It is advisable to apply for a gold loan when gold prices are favorable to maximize the loan amount.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Loan amount is directly proportional to the current gold price per gram.  
- Higher gold prices result in a higher loan amount for the same quantity of gold.  
- Customers should consider gold price fluctuations before applying to optimize loan benefits.
