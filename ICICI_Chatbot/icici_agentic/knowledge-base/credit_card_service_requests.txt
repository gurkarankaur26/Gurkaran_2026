TITLE: Credit Card Service Requests  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards, Customer Service  
TAGS: Credit Card, Service Requests, PIN, Statements, Customer Support, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides online support for all credit card service requests, allowing customers to raise requests related to statements, PIN generation or reset, dispatch issues, and other credit card-related concerns quickly and securely.  

To access these services, customers must log in using their Net Banking User ID and Password.  

If you do not have login credentials, you can:  
- Generate your User ID and Password online via the ICICI Bank website  
- Contact Customer Care at 1800 1080 for assistance  

KEY POINTS:  
- Online service requests available for credit card statements, PIN, dispatch issues, and more  
- Requires Net Banking login credentials for access  
- Option to generate User ID and Password online if not available  
- Customer Care helpline: 1800 1080
