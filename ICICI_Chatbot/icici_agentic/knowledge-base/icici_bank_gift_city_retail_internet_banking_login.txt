TITLE: ICICI Bank GIFT City Retail Internet Banking Login  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Internet Banking  
TAGS: ICICI Bank, GIFT City, Internet Banking, Login, Security  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides a secure internet banking login portal specifically for its GIFT City retail customers. Customers can access their accounts through the dedicated URL: https://giftcity.icici.bank.in. The portal uses a secure virtual keyboard to enhance security during login, preventing keylogging and unauthorized access.

Important security measures to follow before logging in:  
- Ensure the URL in the browser address bar begins with https://giftcity.icici.bank.in  
- Do not enter login or sensitive information in any pop-up windows  
- Verify the security certificate by clicking on the padlock icon in the browser to confirm the site is verified by Entrust  

The secure virtual keyboard includes all alphanumeric keys and special characters, allowing users to enter their credentials safely.

KEY POINTS:  
- Dedicated internet banking portal for ICICI Bank GIFT City retail customers  
- Secure URL: https://giftcity.icici.bank.in  
- Use of secure virtual keyboard for login  
- Verify security certificate (Entrust verified) before entering credentials  
- Avoid entering sensitive information in pop-up windows
