CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a wide range of mutual fund investment options from reputed fund houses such as LIC Mutual Fund and Mirae Asset Mutual Fund. These funds cater to various investment preferences including large cap, multi cap, hybrid, arbitrage, balanced advantage, banking and financial services, equity savings, flexi cap, and focused funds.  

Key Mutual Fund Schemes and Options:  

1. LIC Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- LIC MF Large Cap Fund (Direct IDCW and Growth options)  
- LIC MF Multi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  

2. Mirae Asset Mutual Fund Schemes:  
- Aggressive Hybrid Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Arbitrage Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Balanced Advantage Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Banking and Financial Services Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Equity Savings Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Flexi Cap Fund (Direct and Regular plans with Growth and IDCW options)  
- Focused Fund (Direct Growth option)  

Each scheme is available in Direct and Regular plans, with options for Growth (capital appreciation) and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal) for investors seeking periodic income.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple mutual fund schemes available from LIC and Mirae Asset via ICICI Bank.  
- Options include Direct and Regular plans.  
- Investment styles include Large Cap, Multi Cap, Hybrid, Arbitrage, Balanced Advantage, Banking & Financial Services, Equity Savings, Flexi Cap, and Focused funds.  
- Growth option for capital appreciation.  
- IDCW option for periodic income distribution.  
- Suitable for investors with varying risk appetites and investment horizons.
