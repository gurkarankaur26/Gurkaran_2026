TITLE:  
ICICI Bank FASTag - Features, Activation, Closure, and FAQs

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

FASTag Services

TAGS:

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank FASTag enables automatic toll payments by deducting toll charges electronically when the vehicle approaches the toll plaza, eliminating the need to stop for cash transactions.

**Key Services Offered:**  
- Buy new FASTag  
- Recharge FASTag  
- Raise a query  
- Update Vehicle Number  
- Close FASTag  
- View FASTag details  
- Request tag replacement  
- Download statement  
- Auto Recharge when balance falls below threshold  

**FASTag Closure Process:**  
- Submit closure request with required documents.  
- Bank verifies the request within 3 business days.  
- Upon approval, the FASTag is put under exception code 03 (low balance status) in NPCI and IHMCL records to block transactions.  
- After 15 days of approval, the balance refund is credited to your wallet, and the FASTag status changes to exception code 06 (closed status).  
- If any transactions occur on the blocked tag, you can raise a dispute for a refund from the toll plaza.  
- You can check the activity history and closure status on the FASTag web portal: https://fastaglogin.icicibank.com/CUSTLOGIN/Default.aspx  
- For urgent permanent blocking (e.g., vehicle sold), mark the tag as Lost/Stolen via the portal to immediately add it to exception code 06 (closed/permanent block).  

**Reactivation and Replacement:**  
- If FASTag is hotlisted due to negative balance, recharge with sufficient funds to reactivate.  
- If hotlisted due to incorrect vehicle number or missing registration certificate, upload the registration certificate and contact customer care at 1800 2100 104.  
- FASTags with status “Closed” in NPCI and IHMCL records cannot be reactivated.  
- If your FASTag status is “Returndamage,” “LOST,” or “STOLEN,” you can get a replacement under the existing FASTag wallet.  
- For statuses like “HEXTAGCLOSED,” “CLOSED,” “PENDING CLOSED,” “REFUND REQUESTED,” “SUSPENDED,” or “WRITE OFF,” a new FASTag account must be created. Existing accounts can be closed following the FASTag wallet closure process.  

**Customer Support:**  
- For any issues related to FASTag, vehicle number updates, or tag replacement, contact ICICI Bank customer care at 1800 2100 104.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag enables cashless toll payments with automatic deduction.  
- Closure requests take 3 business days for verification and 15 days for refund processing.  
- Tags blocked due to negative balance or incorrect details can be reactivated after rectification.  
- Closed FASTags cannot be reactivated; new tags/accounts may be required depending on status.  
- Urgent blocking of FASTag is possible by marking it Lost/Stolen via the portal.  
- Customer care helpline: 1800 2100 104  
- FASTag portal for managing tags and checking status: https://fastaglogin.icicibank.com/CUSTLOGIN/Default.aspx
