CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Gold Loans secured against gold jewellery with loan amounts up to ₹2 crore*. The interest rate on Gold Loans varies based on factors such as loan amount, tenure, purity and weight of gold, and borrower’s creditworthiness. The loan process involves minimal documentation and quick approvals.

**Gold Loan Interest Rates (Q4: Jan 2026 - Mar 2026):**  
- Minimum Interest Rate: 8.55% p.a.  
- Maximum Interest Rate: 16.50% p.a.  
- Mean Interest Rate: 10.69% p.a.  

*Note: Interest rates are subject to change based on market conditions and bank policies.*

**Loan Features and Benefits:**  
- Loan amount up to ₹2 crore*  
- Minimal documentation required  
- Quick approval process  
- Flexible tenure options  
- Competitive interest rates based on borrower profile and gold valuation  
- No foreclosure charges if foreclosed after specified period  
- Transparent fee structure  

**Eligibility:**  
- Individuals providing gold jewellery as collateral  
- Subject to bank’s credit assessment and gold valuation  

**Application Process:**  
- Apply online or at ICICI Bank branches  
- Submit gold jewellery for valuation  
- Provide necessary KYC and income documents  
- Loan disbursed after approval  

KEY POINTS:  
- Gold Loan interest rates range from 8.55% to 16.50% p.a. with a mean of 10.69% p.a.  
- Processing fee up to 2% of loan amount  
- Foreclosure charges vary based on timing of prepayment, nil after 120/180 days depending on tenure  
- Valuation charges depend on loan amount slab (₹350 to ₹1,500)  
- Overdue charges include penal interest of 5% p.a. on overdue amount plus legal notice fees  
- Loan amount up to ₹2 crore with minimal documentation and quick approvals
