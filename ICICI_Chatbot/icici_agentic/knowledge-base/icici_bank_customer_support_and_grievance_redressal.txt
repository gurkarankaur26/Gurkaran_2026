TITLE: ICICI Bank Customer Support and Grievance Redressal  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Customer Service  
TAGS: Customer Care, Grievance Redressal, Contact, Support, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides multiple channels for customer support and grievance redressal to ensure a seamless banking experience. Customers can access services and resolve issues through the following methods:

1. **Customer Care Contact:**  
   - Phone: Call 1800 1080 for assistance with banking services.  
   - Email: Write to customer.care@icicibank.com from your registered email ID for support.  
   - Branch Visit: Visit the nearest ICICI Bank branch for in-person assistance.

2. **Digital Access:**  
   - Net Banking and iMobile App: Customers can manage their accounts and raise service requests through ICICI Bank’s Net Banking portal or the iMobile app.

3. **Grievance Redressal:**  
   - If issues remain unresolved, customers can escalate their concerns through the Grievance Redressal Matrix available on the ICICI Bank website.  
   - Contact the Nodal Officer for further escalation.

KEY POINTS:  
- Customer Care Toll-Free Number: 1800 1080  
- Email Support: customer.care@icicibank.com (from registered email)  
- Multiple channels available: Phone, Email, Branch, Net Banking, iMobile app  
- Grievance escalation through the Grievance Redressal Matrix and Nodal Officer contact  
- Support available for all banking-related queries and complaints
