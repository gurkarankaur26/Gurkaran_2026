TITLE: Differences Between Current Account and Savings Account  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Banking Accounts  
TAGS: Current Account, Savings Account, Interest Rates, Overdraft, Transaction Limits, Business Banking, Personal Banking  

CONTENT:  
Current Account and Savings Account are two primary types of bank accounts offered by ICICI Bank, catering to different financial needs and customer profiles.

**Current Account:**  
- Designed primarily for businesses, entrepreneurs, companies, and firms.  
- Facilitates frequent and large transactions without restrictions.  
- Does not earn interest.  
- Provides benefits such as overdraft facilities, cheque books, and easy online banking.  
- Suitable for managing daily business expenses, supplier payments, and collections from clients.  
- Allows overdrawing (overdraft facility).  
- Supports multiple transactions with higher limits.  

**Savings Account:**  
- Intended for individuals such as housewives, students, retired professionals, HUFs, and salaried professionals.  
- Helps in saving money safely while earning interest on deposits.  
- Encourages the habit of saving with secure storage and steady growth of funds.  
- Offers features like easy deposits, ATM withdrawals, debit cards, and online banking.  
- Ideal for building emergency funds, saving for future goals, or keeping extra money safe.  
- Does not permit overdrawing.  
- Has limited transaction limits compared to current accounts.  
- Interest rates are applicable and updated periodically (refer to ICICI Bank’s official site for current rates).  

**Comparison Table:**  

| Criteria           | Current Account                          | Savings Account                          |  
|--------------------|----------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------|  
| Purpose            | Spending and Frequent Transactions     | Savings and Wealth Building              |  
| Suitability        | Entrepreneurs, Businessmen, Companies  | Housewives, Students, Retired Professionals, HUFs, Salaried Professionals |  
| Interest Rate      | Nil                                    | Applicable (varies, check latest rates) |  
| Transaction Limit  | Multiple, higher limits                 | Limited                                 |  
| Overdrawing        | Permitted (Overdraft facility)         | Not Permitted                           |  

KEY POINTS:  
- Current Accounts are best suited for business needs with frequent transactions and overdraft facilities but do not earn interest.  
- Savings Accounts are ideal for individuals aiming to save money and earn interest with limited transaction capabilities and no overdraft.  
- Interest rates on Savings Accounts are subject to change; customers should check the latest rates on the ICICI Bank website.  
- Overdraft facility is exclusive to Current Accounts.  
- Both accounts offer online banking and debit card facilities for convenience.
