TITLE: How to replace FASTag, update vehicle number and close FASTag account using ICICI Bank iMobile app  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides convenient digital solutions for managing your FASTag account through the iMobile app. Customers can replace their FASTag, update vehicle registration details, and close their FASTag account seamlessly without visiting a branch.

**How to Replace FASTag using ICICI Bank iMobile app:**  
1. Log in to the ICICI Bank iMobile app.  
2. Navigate to the FASTag section.  
3. Select the option to replace FASTag.  
4. Provide the required details and submit the request.  
5. The bank will process the replacement and dispatch the new FASTag to your registered address.

**How to Update Vehicle Number:**  
1. Access the FASTag section in the iMobile app.  
2. Choose the option to update vehicle number.  
3. Enter the new vehicle registration number.  
4. Submit the update request.  
5. The updated vehicle number will be linked to your existing FASTag account.

**How to Close FASTag Account:**  
1. Open the iMobile app and go to the FASTag section.  
2. Select the option to close the FASTag account.  
3. Confirm your request.  
4. The bank will process the closure and refund any remaining balance as per the terms and conditions.

**Benefits of Managing FASTag via iMobile app:**  
- 24/7 access to FASTag services.  
- Quick and hassle-free updates and replacements.  
- No need to visit the branch physically.  
- Secure and user-friendly interface.  

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag replacement, vehicle number update, and account closure can be done via ICICI Bank iMobile app.  
- No branch visit required; all processes are digital and convenient.  
- New FASTag is delivered to the registered address after replacement.  
- Vehicle number updates link the new registration to the existing FASTag.  
- FASTag account closure refunds balance as per bank policy.  
- Services available 24/7 through the iMobile app.
