TITLE:  
ICICI Bank Cheque Book Requests, Stop Payment, and Mandate Registration Services

SOURCE:  
ICICI Bank

CATEGORY:  
Banking Services

TAGS:  
Cheque Book, Stop Payment, Mandate Registration, Mandate Cancellation, Customer Care, Service Charges, NRE Savings Account

CONTENT:  
**Cheque Book Request:**  
- Customers can request a cheque book online via the "Service Request" option in Net Banking.  
- Requests can also be made by calling ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care.  
- Alternatively, customers can download the cheque book request form, fill it, and mail it to:  
  ICICI Bank Limited,  
  RPC Mumbai,  
  5th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates,  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali,  
  Landmark: Next to Chandivali Studio, Opp MHADA,  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai - 400072, INDIA.  
- NRIs in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can post documents to local P.O. Boxes to save on courier charges.  
- Customers may also visit any ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the cheque book request form.  
- Applicable service charges will be levied as per the bank’s schedule.

**Stop Payment Request:**  
- Stop payment requests can be made online through the "Service Request" option in Net Banking.  
- Alternatively, customers can call the 24-hour Customer Care or visit any ICICI Bank branch to submit a stop payment request letter.  
- Applicable service charges apply.

**Mandate Registration:**  
- To register a mandate holder, customers must download the 'Add a Mandate' request form, fill it, and send it to the proposed mandate holder.  
- The form must be signed by both the account holder and the mandate holder.  
- The mandate holder must visit an ICICI Bank branch with the signed form and relevant documents for submission.  
- Required documents include a recent photograph, proof of identity, and proof of address. Photocopies must be self-attested, and original documents must be presented for verification.  
- For any clarifications, customers can contact the 24-hour Customer Care or use the "Email us" option on the ICICI Bank website.

**Mandate Cancellation:**  
- To cancel a mandate, the account holder must fill out the 'Cancel Mandate from my account' request form, signed by both the account holder and the mandate holder.  
- The mandate holder can submit the cancellation form by:  
  - Mailing it to the India address mentioned above, or  
  - Visiting any ICICI Bank branch in India.  
- Applicable service charges apply.

**Service Charges:**  
- Charges for cheque book requests, stop payment requests, and mandate services are as per the applicable service charges.  
- Customers can view service charges for NRE Savings Accounts on the ICICI Bank website.

KEY POINTS:  
- Multiple channels available for cheque book requests: online, phone, mail, or branch visit.  
- Stop payment requests can be made online, via phone, or branch visit.  
- Mandate registration requires signed forms from both parties and submission of identity and address proofs.  
- Mandate cancellation requires signed forms from both parties and can be submitted by mail or branch visit.  
- Service charges apply to all these services.  
- Dedicated 24-hour Customer Care support is available for assistance.  
- NRIs have local P.O. Box options in select countries to reduce courier costs.
