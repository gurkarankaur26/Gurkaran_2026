ICICI Bank Travel Credit Cards – Features, Eligibility, Application Process, and Benefits

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of Travel Credit Cards designed to provide exclusive travel-related benefits, rewards, and privileges. These cards cater to different customer needs and spending patterns, offering rewards in the form of flight vouchers, hotel vouchers, airport lounge access, premium upgrades, valet and car parking services, porter services, and reward points on spends.

**Key Travel Credit Cards and Benefits:**

1. **Adani One ICICI Bank Platinum Credit Card**  
   - Flight voucher on ₹10,000 spent within 60 days + Hotel vouchers  
   - 2 Domestic Airport Lounge visits annually  
   - 8 Premium upgrades  
   - 2 valet + 2 car parking services per year  
   - 4 porter services per year for every ₹1 lakh spent  
   - Reward points:  
     - 3% in the Adani ecosystem  
     - 1.5% on international spends  
     - 1% on domestic spends  
   - 1% waiver up to ₹4,000 at all fuel outlets  

2. **Adani One ICICI Bank Signature Credit Card**  
   - Flight voucher on spends of ₹25,000 in 60 days + Hotel vouchers  
   - 4 Domestic Airport Lounge visits + 2 International Airport Lounge visits annually  
   - 16 Premium upgrades  
   - 4 valet + 4 car parking services per year  
   - 8 porter services per year for every ₹1 lakh spent  
   - 2 Pranaam services (1 on activation, 1 on spends of ₹3 lakh)  
   - Reward points:  
     - 7% in the Adani ecosystem  
     - 2% on international spends  
     - 1.5% on domestic spends  
   - 1% waiver up to ₹4,000 at all fuel outlets  

**Eligibility Criteria for Travel Credit Cards:**  
- Age: Applicant must be between 21 and 65 years.  
- Income: Minimum annual income varies depending on the specific card.  
- Credit Score: Must have a good or excellent credit record and credit score.  
- Employment and financial stability are considered during evaluation.

**Documents Required for Application:**  
- Valid identity proof: Passport, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, or Driving Licence.  
- Address proof: Utility bills, signed rental agreements, or latest bank account statement with address.  
- Income proof: Salary slips, income tax returns, or business financial statements.  
- Recent bank statements may be required.  
- Passport-sized photographs.

**Application Process:**  
1. Choose the Travel Credit Card that suits your financial and travel needs by visiting the ICICI Bank Credit Card page or logging into the iMobile app.  
2. Click on ‘Apply Now’ or ‘Get Started’ and fill in the application form with accurate personal and financial details.  
3. Upload the necessary documents including identity proof, address proof, income proof, and photographs.  
4. Submit the application for verification.  
5. ICICI Bank will review the application and verify the documents.  
6. Upon approval, the bank will send a confirmation and dispatch the Credit Card to the applicant’s registered address.

KEY POINTS:  
- Travel Credit Cards offer flight and hotel vouchers, airport lounge access, premium upgrades, valet, car parking, and porter services.  
- Reward points vary by card and spend category, with higher rewards in the Adani ecosystem and on international spends.  
- Eligibility requires age between 21-65 years, minimum income criteria, and a good credit score.  
- Required documents include identity proof, address proof, income proof, and recent bank statements.  
- Application can be completed online via ICICI Bank’s website or iMobile app.
