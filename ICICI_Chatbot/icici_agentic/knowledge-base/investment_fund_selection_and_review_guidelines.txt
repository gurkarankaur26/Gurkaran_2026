TITLE: Investment Fund Selection and Review Guidelines  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Investment Planning  
TAGS: asset allocation, fund consistency, expense ratio, financial goals, investment review, portfolio management  
CONTENT:  
When selecting investment funds, it is important to consider several key factors to ensure alignment with your financial objectives. These factors include asset allocation, fund consistency, expense ratio, and how well the fund aligns with your overall financial goals. Regular periodic reviews of your investments—ideally annually or during significant financial changes—are recommended to maintain alignment with your objectives and to make adjustments as necessary. This approach helps optimize returns while managing risk effectively.  
KEY POINTS:  
- Evaluate asset allocation to ensure diversification and risk management.  
- Assess fund consistency by reviewing past performance and management stability.  
- Consider the expense ratio to understand the cost impact on returns.  
- Align fund choices with your specific financial goals and risk tolerance.  
- Conduct periodic reviews annually or during major financial changes to keep investments on track.
