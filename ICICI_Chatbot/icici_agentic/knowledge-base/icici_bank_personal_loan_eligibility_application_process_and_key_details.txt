TITLE: ICICI Bank Personal Loan - Eligibility, Application Process, and Key Details  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Eligibility, Application Process, Foreclosure, Interest Rates, Loan Amount, Fees  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers Personal Loans with flexible eligibility criteria and loan amounts ranging from ₹50,000 up to ₹50 lakh, subject to internal policies. The loan amount and tenure depend on factors such as income, credit score, existing liabilities, employment status, and repayment capacity.

**Eligibility:**  
- Salaried applicants must have a minimum monthly salary of ₹30,000.  
- Eligibility is influenced by income, credit score (CIBIL), existing financial obligations, job stability, and employer profile.  
- Co-applicants with stable income can improve eligibility.  
- Eligibility criteria are subject to change as per RBI guidelines and ICICI Bank policies.  
- Use the Personal Loan eligibility calculator by inputting income, expenses, existing loans, and tenure to estimate the maximum loan amount.  

**Loan Amount:**  
- Minimum loan amount: ₹50,000  
- Maximum loan amount: ₹50 lakh (based on income, age, credit score, and other factors)  

**Application Process:**  
1. Download the ICICI Bank iMobile app and log in using MPIN or fingerprint.  
2. Navigate to ‘Get Instant Loan’ and select 'Apply now' under the Personal Loan section.  
3. Enter the required loan amount.  
4. Review and validate all loan details.  
5. Accept the Terms and Conditions and click ‘Disburse Now’.  
6. Authenticate using Aadhaar-based eSign and submit the application.  

**Loan Disbursement:**  
- The loan amount is disbursed after successful application and verification.  

**Foreclosure:**  
- Foreclosure of Personal Loan is allowed after payment of at least one EMI.  
- Foreclosure charges and applicable taxes will be levied as per ICICI Bank’s service charges.  

**Improving Eligibility:**  
- Maintain a good credit score by timely bill payments.  
- Reduce existing debts.  
- Demonstrate consistent income or add a co-applicant with stable income.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal Loan amount ranges from ₹50,000 to ₹50 lakh.  
- Minimum salary eligibility: ₹30,000 per month for salaried individuals.  
- Eligibility depends on income, credit score, liabilities, and employment status.  
- Application can be completed via ICICI Bank iMobile app with Aadhaar eSign authentication.  
- Foreclosure allowed after one EMI with applicable charges.  
- Eligibility calculator available to estimate loan amount.
