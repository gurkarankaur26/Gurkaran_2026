TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card Benefits, Features, and Security  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Cards, Benefits, Security, Rewards, Spending Limits, Milestone Benefits  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank credit cards offer a range of benefits designed to enhance your spending experience and provide security. Key features include access to airport lounges, both domestic and international, depending on the card type. Milestone benefits reward cardholders with vouchers, bonus points, or fee waivers upon reaching certain annual spending thresholds.  

Security is a priority with features such as OTP verification, the ability to lock or unlock your card instantly, and zero-liability fraud protection to safeguard against unauthorized transactions.  

Using multiple credit cards can help optimize rewards and benefits across different spending categories, allowing you to maximize your returns. Additionally, credit cards come with features to prevent overspending, including the option to set daily or monthly spending limits and receive alerts, helping you stay within your budget.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Access to domestic and international airport lounges depending on the credit card.  
- Milestone benefits include vouchers, bonus points, and fee waivers based on annual spend.  
- Security features: OTP verification, card lock/unlock, zero-liability fraud protection.  
- Multiple credit cards can optimize rewards across spending categories.  
- Spending control features: daily/monthly limits and alerts to prevent overspending.
