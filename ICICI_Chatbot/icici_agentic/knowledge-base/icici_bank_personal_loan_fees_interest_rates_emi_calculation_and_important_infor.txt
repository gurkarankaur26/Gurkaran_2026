CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers personal loans with interest rates starting from 9.99% per annum, subject to eligibility and internal assessment. The final interest rate offered depends on factors such as credit score, income stability, repayment history, and existing liabilities.

**EMI Calculation:**  
- Personal Loan EMIs are calculated based on the loan amount, interest rate, and repayment tenure.  
- The EMI amount depends on the principal amount, monthly interest rate, and number of installments.  
- ICICI Bank provides a Personal Loan EMI Calculator to help applicants estimate their monthly EMI and total repayment amount instantly.  
- Applicants can adjust the loan amount and tenure in the calculator to explore different repayment scenarios.

**Important Information:**  
- As per Rule 9B of the Prevention of Money Laundering (Maintenance of Records) Rules, customers must inform ICICI Bank of any changes in their KYC details (such as address, contact details, or profile) within 30 days of the change.  
- Updated documents must be submitted to enable the bank to update its records accordingly.
