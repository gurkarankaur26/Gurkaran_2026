**ATM/Debit Card Request Process:**  
- Customers can request an ATM/Debit Card and PIN online via the "Service Request" option in Net Banking.  
- Alternatively, requests can be made by contacting ICICI Bank’s 24-hour Customer Care.  
- Customers may download the request form from the ICICI Bank website, fill it, and mail it to:  
  ICICI Bank Limited,  
  RPC Mumbai,  
  5th Floor, A-Wing, Autumn Estates,  
  Chandivali Farm Road, Chandivali,  
  Andheri-East, Mumbai - 400072, INDIA  
- NRIs residing in USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Singapore, and UAE can post documents to local P.O. Boxes in their respective countries to save on courier charges.  
- Customers can also visit any ICICI Bank branch in India to submit the request form.  
- Applicable service charges will be levied as per the bank’s schedule.  

**Complaint Redressal for ATM/Debit Card Transactions:**  
- Customers may lodge complaints with the card issuing bank for any disputed ATM transactions, even if the transaction occurred at another bank’s ATM.  
- As per RBI guidelines, the issuing bank must re-credit wrongly debited amounts within 7 working days from receipt of the complaint.  
- If the bank delays beyond 7 working days and the complaint is lodged within 30 days, the bank shall pay compensation of ₹100 per day for the delay, credited automatically to the customer’s account.  
- If the bank does not respond satisfactorily, customers may escalate the complaint to the local Banking Ombudsman.
