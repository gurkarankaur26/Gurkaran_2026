TITLE: Eligibility & Requirements for Personal Loan at ICICI Bank  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Personal Loans  
TAGS: Personal Loan, Eligibility, Income Criteria, Credit Score, Employment, Documents, EMI, ICICI Bank  

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers collateral-free Personal Loans up to Rs 50 lakh for various personal needs such as weddings, holidays, medical emergencies, and other expenses. Both salaried and self-employed individuals can apply, subject to specific eligibility criteria.

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- **Age:**  
  - Salaried applicants: 20 to 58 years  
  - Self-employed applicants: Minimum 28 years (25 years for doctors), maximum 65 years  
- **Income:**  
  - Salaried: Minimum monthly income of Rs 30,000  
  - Self-employed (non-professionals): Minimum turnover of Rs 40 lakh with a minimum profit of Rs 1 lakh (audited financials)  
  - Self-employed (professionals): Minimum turnover of Rs 15 lakh with a minimum profit of Rs 2 lakh (audited financials)  
  - Income requirements may vary based on location and occupation  
- **Credit Score:** A good credit score is essential for better loan terms and approval chances  
- **Employment:**  
  - Salaried: Minimum 2 years in current profession or job  
  - Self-employed: Minimum 5 years in current business (3 years for doctors)  
- **Residence:** Must be a resident of India with a valid residential address and residing at the current address for at least 1 year  

**Documents Required:**  
- Identity proof (e.g., Aadhaar, PAN)  
- Address proof  
- Income proof (salary slips, bank statements, audited financials for self-employed)  
- Employment proof  
- Additional documents based on occupation (e.g., business registration, office address proof)  
- Existing ICICI Bank customers can check eligibility and apply for Insta Personal Loan via iMobile or Internet Banking with minimal documentation  

**EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment):**  
- EMI is the fixed monthly payment made to repay the loan principal and interest  
- ICICI Bank provides a Personal Loan EMI Calculator on its website to help applicants calculate EMIs based on loan amount and tenure  

KEY POINTS:  
- Personal Loans up to Rs 50 lakh, collateral-free  
- Age limits: 20-58 years (salaried), 28-65 years (self-employed)  
- Minimum income: Rs 30,000/month (salaried), turnover and profit criteria for self-employed  
- Minimum employment duration: 2 years (salaried), 5 years (self-employed)  
- Good credit score required  
- Valid Indian residency and minimum 1-year residence at current address  
- Required documents vary by applicant type; existing customers have simplified process  
- EMI Calculator available online for easy repayment planning
