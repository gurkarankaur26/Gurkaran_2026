TITLE: ICICI Bank Credit Card Terms and Conditions  
SOURCE: ICICI Bank  
CATEGORY: Credit Cards  
TAGS: Credit Card, Fees, Charges, Interest Rates, Limits, Billing, Payment, Termination  

CONTENT:  
1. Fees and Charges  
- **Annual Fee:** Varies by card type; communicated at application and billed annually.  
- **Renewal Fee:** May apply similarly to annual fees.  
- **Cash Advance Fee:** Charged on cash withdrawals from ATMs in India or abroad; fee amount subject to change. Finance charges apply from the date of withdrawal until full payment.  
- **Other Charges:** Fees may apply for specific services or defaults. ICICI Bank may alter or introduce fees with prior customer notification.  

2. Interest-Free Period  
- The interest-free credit period depends on the card scheme and merchant claim submission timelines.  
- No interest-free period if the previous month’s balance is unpaid or if cash advances are taken.  

3. Credit and Cash Withdrawal Limits  
- Limits are set by ICICI Bank and communicated at card issuance and via monthly statements.  
- Limits may be revised based on internal criteria with immediate notification.  
- Cardholders may request limit increases by submitting financial documents.  

4. Billing and Payment  
- Monthly finance charges apply on unpaid balances and cash advances.  
- Finance charges on cash advances accrue from the transaction date until full payment.  
- Partial payments incur finance charges on outstanding and new transactions until dues are cleared.  
- Monthly statements detail transactions, payments, and outstanding balances.  
- Payments can be made via Net Banking, ATMs, Phone Banking, NEFT, and other channels.  
- The bank sends reminders for outstanding dues and may engage third parties for collections.  

5. Right of Lien and Set Off  
- ICICI Bank may set off any monies held in any account against outstanding credit card dues if payment is not made within the prescribed time.  

6. Termination and Revocation  
- Cardholders may terminate membership by submitting a written request and cutting the card.  
- The bank may suspend or terminate card use for unusual transactions, non-payment, or exceeding credit limits.  

KEY POINTS:  
- Annual and renewal fees vary by card and are communicated upfront.  
- Cash advances incur fees and finance charges from withdrawal date.  
- Interest-free period is conditional and not applicable on unpaid balances or cash advances.  
- Credit limits are assigned and can be increased upon request with documentation.  
- Finance charges apply on unpaid balances and cash advances.  
- Multiple payment channels are available.  
- Bank has lien rights on customer accounts for unpaid dues.  
- Card membership can be terminated by customer or bank under specified conditions.
