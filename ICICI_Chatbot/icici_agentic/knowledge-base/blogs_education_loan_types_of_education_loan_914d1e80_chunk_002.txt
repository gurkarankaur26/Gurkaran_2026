CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers a range of Education Loans tailored to meet the diverse needs of students and working professionals seeking financial assistance for higher education and skill development. These loans cover expenses such as tuition fees, books, accommodation, and other academic costs.

**Types of Education Loans:**

1. **Undergraduate Education Loans**  
   Designed for students pursuing a bachelor’s degree after completing 12th grade. The loan covers tuition, books, accommodation, and other academic expenses.

2. **Postgraduate Education Loans**  
   For students pursuing higher studies such as MBA, MSc, MA, MTech, etc. Eligibility requires completion of a bachelor’s degree. These loans support career advancement and specialization.

3. **Education Loans Based on Collateral:**

   - **With Collateral:**  
     Allows borrowing higher amounts by pledging assets like property or Fixed Deposits. Offers attractive interest rates and flexible repayment options. Loan amounts can be up to ₹3 crore for both Indian and international universities.

   - **Without Collateral (Unsecured Education Loans):**  
     Provides funding without asset pledging, ideal for students lacking collateral. Loan amounts can be up to ₹3 crore based on eligibility.

4. **Third-Party Guarantee:**  
   In cases where collateral is not available, a guarantor (relative, friend, or well-wisher) may be required to co-sign the loan, taking responsibility for repayment if the borrower defaults.

5. **Career Education Loans:**  
   Targeted at working professionals with 2+ years of experience who want to upgrade skills or pursue advanced courses, including professional certifications and executive programs. These loans aim to enhance job opportunities and skill development.

6. **Instant Education Loans:**  
   Available to eligible customers through an online application via ICICI Bank Net Banking or the iMobile app. Features include attractive interest rates, minimal documentation, and quick approval.

**Key Features and Benefits:**

- Loan amounts up to ₹3 crore for both secured and unsecured loans.
- Coverage includes tuition fees, accommodation, books, and other academic expenses.
- Flexible repayment options tailored to borrower needs.
- Attractive interest rates, especially for loans with collateral.
- Quick and easy application process, including instant loans via digital platforms.
- Support for both domestic and international education.
- Career loans designed specifically for working professionals.

**Eligibility Criteria:**

- For undergraduate loans: Completion of 12th grade.
- For postgraduate loans: Completion of a bachelor’s degree.
- For career education loans: Minimum 2 years of work experience.
- Eligibility for unsecured loans depends on creditworthiness and other bank criteria.
- Guarantor may be required if collateral is not provided.

**Application Process:**

- Apply online via ICICI Bank Net Banking or iMobile app for instant loans.
- Submit required documents such as academic records, identity proof, income proof (for career loans), and collateral documents if applicable.
- Provide guarantor details if required.
- Loan approval and disbursal timelines vary based on loan type and documentation.
