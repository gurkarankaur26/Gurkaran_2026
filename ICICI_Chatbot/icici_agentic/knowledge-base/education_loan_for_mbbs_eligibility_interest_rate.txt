Pursuing an MBBS degree involves significant financial investment. ICICI Bank offers tailored Education Loans to support aspiring medical professionals in managing these expenses effectively. The loan products are designed to provide substantial financial assistance with flexible terms, competitive interest rates, and a hassle-free application process.

**Features and Benefits:**  
- **Loan Amounts:** Up to ₹3 crore for international medical studies and up to ₹1 crore for domestic medical studies.  
- **Collateral-Free Loans:** Available up to ₹1 crore for students admitted to premium medical institutions.  
- **Specialised Products:** Customised loan offerings for government employees, CXOs, and professionals with at least two years of experience.  
- **Application Process:** Fully digital application and approval process with pre-admission sanction options, reducing the need for branch visits.  
- **Interest Rates:** Competitive and flexible interest rates based on the course and loan amount.  
- **Eligibility:** Indian nationals aged 16 to 35 years with completion of 10+2 (12th Standard) or Diploma for undergraduate medical courses.  
- **Co-Borrower and Security Options:** Flexibility to include family members as co-borrowers; loans can be secured with property (house or flat), Fixed Deposit (FD), or an existing ICICI Bank Home Loan as cross-collateral.  
- **Convenient Application:** Apply online or at any ICICI Bank branch. Existing customers can check eligibility and avail instant sanction through the ICICI Bank Instant Education Loan feature.  

**Eligibility Criteria:**  
- Applicant must be an Indian national aged between 16 and 35 years.  
- Must have completed 10+2 (12th Standard) or Diploma for undergraduate medical courses.  
- Admission to a recognised medical institution (domestic or international).  

**Interest Rates and Fees:**  
- Interest rates are competitive and vary depending on the loan amount and course. Specific rates are provided during the application process.  
- Processing fees and other charges apply as per ICICI Bank’s standard loan policies.  

**Application Process:**  
1. Check eligibility online or visit an ICICI Bank branch.  
2. Submit required documents including admission proof, academic records, identity proof, and co-borrower details if applicable.  
3. Complete the digital application form.  
4. Receive pre-admission sanction (if applicable) and final approval.  
5. Disbursement of loan amount as per the course fee schedule.

  Family members can be co-borrowers to support the loan application.  
- *Can existing ICICI Bank customers avail instant sanction?*  
  Yes, existing customers can use the Instant Education Loan feature for quick eligibility checks and sanction.
