CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank provides a range of ongoing offers and promotions applicable on Credit Cards and Credit Card EMIs. These offers are designed to provide customers with benefits such as discounts, cashback, and easy payment options on purchases made using ICICI Bank Credit Cards or through EMI conversions.

**Offer Validity Periods:**  
- Multiple offers are valid from June 28, 2026, to September 30, 2026, applicable on Credit Cards and Credit Card EMIs.  
- Some offers are valid from July 1, 2026, to July 31, 2026, specifically on Credit Cards and Credit Card EMIs.  
- Certain offers extend from May 13, 2026, to July 31, 2026, valid on Credit Card EMIs.  
- Other offers run from January 21, 2026, to January 20, 2027, valid on Credit & Debit Cards and Internet Banking.  
- Additional offers are valid from May 21, 2026, to March 31, 2027.  
- Short-term offers are available from July 3 to July 9, 2026, and July 1 to July 15, 2026, valid on Credit & Debit Cards and Credit Card EMIs.  
- Some offers have extended validity from June 13, 2026, to July 31, 2027, on Credit Card EMIs.

**Applicability:**  
- Offers apply to transactions made using ICICI Bank Credit Cards.  
- EMI offers are available on Credit Card EMIs, allowing customers to convert their purchases into easy monthly installments.  
- Some offers are also valid on Debit Cards and Internet Banking transactions.

**How to Avail:**  
- Customers can apply for ICICI Bank Credit Cards through the official website to become eligible for these offers.  
- Offers are automatically applicable on eligible transactions during the offer validity period.

KEY POINTS:  
- Offers valid primarily between mid-2026 and early 2027.  
- Applicable on ICICI Bank Credit Cards, Credit Card EMIs, Debit Cards, and Internet Banking.  
- Benefits include discounts, cashback, and EMI conversion options.  
- Multiple overlapping offers with varying validity periods.  
- Customers must use ICICI Bank Credit/Debit Cards or Internet Banking to avail offers.  
- Credit Card application available online for new customers.
