CONTENT:  
This document provides a comprehensive list of mutual fund schemes available, including their types, plan options, and identification codes. The schemes cover various categories such as multi-cap funds, index funds, small cap funds, sectoral funds, hybrid funds, and arbitrage funds. Each scheme is available in different plan options like Direct (Dir) and Regular (Reg), and payout options such as Growth and IDCW (Income Distribution cum Capital Withdrawal).

KEY POINTS:  
- Mutual funds are available in Direct and Regular plans.  
- Payout options include Growth (reinvestment of earnings) and IDCW (periodic income distribution).  
- Various fund categories are offered: Multi Cap, Index, Small Cap, Sectoral, Hybrid, and Arbitrage.  
- Each scheme has a unique identification code for easy reference.  
- Investors can choose funds based on their risk appetite and investment goals.
