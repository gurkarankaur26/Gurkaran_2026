ICICI Bank NRI Account Services and Fund Transfer Guidelines

CONTENT:  
ICICI Bank offers comprehensive services for Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) including account opening, fund transfers, and various payment options.

**Account Opening for NRIs:**  
- NRI accounts can be opened by visiting any ICICI Bank branch in India or representative offices in Singapore, United Kingdom, Bahrain, Hong Kong, or UAE.  
- Documents required vary depending on whether the account is opened in India or overseas. All documents must be self-attested by the respective account holders.  
- For first-time travelers abroad, specific documentation is required (details available at the branch).  
- Account opening status can be checked by contacting the branch where documents were submitted. If documents are in order, the account is opened within 2 working days.

**Adding a Joint Holder:**  
- To add a joint holder, visit the nearest branch with the required self-attested documents or post them to:

**Fund Transfer Services:**  
- **From NRE Account:**  
  - Funds can be transferred overseas without restrictions.  
  - Transfer methods include Net Banking and branch requests in India.  
- **From NRO Account:**  
  - Overseas transfers are limited to USD 1 million per financial year.  
  - Submission of Form 15CA (an undertaking by the account holder) and other required documents at the branch is mandatory.

**Payment and Transfer Options:**  
- **Net Banking:**  
  - Available 24x7 for convenient account operation and fund transfer requests.  
- **Phone Banking:**  
  - 24-hour toll-free customer care lines are available. If toll-free lines are not accessible in your country, a click-to-call facility is provided on the website.  
- **Standing Instructions:**  
  - Can be set up by calling 24-hour Customer Care or visiting the nearest branch in India.  
- **Cheque / Demand Draft:**  
  - Issuable from NRE/NRO accounts.  
- **NEFT/RTGS:**  
  - Requests can be placed at any ICICI Bank branch in India.

KEY POINTS:  
- NRI accounts can be opened in India or at ICICI Bank’s overseas representative offices.  
- Account opening requires self-attested documents; processing time is typically 2 working days.  
- Joint account holders can be added by submitting required documents at the branch or by post.  
- NRE account holders can transfer funds overseas without limits; NRO account holders have a USD 1 million annual limit.  
- Form 15CA is mandatory for overseas transfers from NRO accounts.  
- Multiple fund transfer options: Net Banking, Phone Banking, Standing Instructions, Cheque/Demand Draft, NEFT, RTGS.  
- 24x7 customer support available via toll-free numbers or click-to-call facility.
