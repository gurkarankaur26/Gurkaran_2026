TITLE:  
ICICI Bank FASTag – Easy Online and Offline Application for Cashless Toll Payments

SOURCE:

TAGS:  
FASTag, Toll Payments, ICICI Bank, Online Application, Offline Application, Cashless Toll, Vehicle Sticker, Activation, Recharge

CONTENT:  
FASTag is an electronic toll collection system that enables cashless payments at toll plazas, allowing you to pass through without stopping for cash transactions. ICICI Bank offers a convenient way to obtain a FASTag either online or offline.

**Application Process:**  
- **Online Application:** Apply via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app or Internet Banking by uploading the required documents digitally. The FASTag is then delivered to your doorstep. This method is faster and more convenient.  
- **Offline Application:** Visit a FASTag booth at a toll plaza or the nearest ICICI Bank branch offering this service. Submit the required documents physically and wait for verification before receiving your FASTag.

**FASTag Activation:**  
- Affix the magnetic FASTag sticker on the inner side of the front windscreen, centrally below the rear-view mirror.  
- Activate the FASTag by visiting the provider’s website, entering the FASTag ID or scanning the QR code.  
- Link the FASTag to a prepaid wallet or directly to your bank account for automatic toll deductions.

**Recharge and Payment:**  
- Recharge your FASTag using Internet Banking, prepaid e-wallets, Credit Card, or Debit Card.  
- Linking FASTag directly to your bank account enables automatic toll tax deduction, eliminating the need for manual recharges.

**Vehicle Compatibility and Sticker Colours:**  
FASTag is vehicle-specific and available in different colour-coded stickers based on vehicle categories such as cars, buses, trucks, and heavy construction vehicles.

KEY POINTS:  
- FASTag enables cashless, hassle-free toll payments.  
- Online application via ICICI Bank’s iMobile app or Internet Banking is quick and convenient.  
- Offline application requires visiting a toll plaza booth or bank branch.  
- FASTag sticker must be affixed inside the front windscreen below the rear-view mirror.  
- Activation involves entering FASTag ID or scanning QR code and linking to wallet or bank account.  
- Recharge options include Internet Banking, prepaid wallets, Credit/Debit Cards.

- Different sticker colours correspond to different vehicle categories.
