TITLE: iMobile Banking Services App Terms & Conditions for NRI Banking | ICICI Bank
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/mobile-banking/imobile/services-offered-by-imobile

Access all your banking services in one app—manage accounts, transfer funds, pay bills, recharge, invest, and more. Enjoy the convenience of iMobile anytime, anywhere.
 
- Accounts & Deposits Manage your ICICI Bank accounts, open additional NRE/NRO accounts, savings, loans, and Credit Cards easily from one place. You can also open Fixed Deposits (FDs) and Recurring Deposits (RDs).
 
 
- Send Money: Send money to any ICICI Bank account anytime. Just ensure the person you want to send money to, is registered on the iMobile.
 
 
- Global UPI Payments: Payments via overseas numbers from countries including Australia, Malaysia, Canada, France, Hong Kong, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, Singapore, UAE, UK and USA.
 
 
- Recharge: Recharge your mobile, DTH, or data connection directly using the iMobile.
 
 
- Bill Payments: Pay your utility bills quickly using the iMobile. You just need to register the biller once through Internet Banking before paying.
 
 
- Cards & Loans: Keep track of your Credit Card and loan accounts directly on the iMobile.
 
 
- Investments & Insurance: Manage your insurance policies and Demat account easily through the iMobile.
 
 
- Other Services: Access various features directly from iMobile like Smartlock, ReKYC update, communication address update, FATCA/CRS Declaration, open additional NRE/NRO Accounts, block your card, stop a cheque & track your deliverables.
