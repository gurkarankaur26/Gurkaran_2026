TITLE: Education Loan interest rates & charges (2026) | ICICI Bank
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/loans/education-loan/interest-rates

ICICI Bank offers Education Loans with competitive education loan interest rates to help students manage higher education expenses in India and abroad. Student loan interest rates may vary depending on factors such as course type, institution, loan amount, repayment tenure and applicant eligibility. Education Loans may cover tuition fees and other eligible academic expenses with flexible repayment options and structured EMI plans. Customers can apply online with simple documentation and choose suitable repayment tenure based on financial requirements and repayment capacity.
As of the quarter ending March 2026, ICICI Bank Education Loan interest rates range from 9.00% to 13.00% per annum, with a mean rate of 10.54% per annum. Secured Education Loans for select domestic institutes start as low as 8.50% per annum, while Unsecured Education Loans for abroad studies start at 10.25% per annum. The applicable rate depends on the course, institution category, loan security, applicant profile and co-applicant credit history.
