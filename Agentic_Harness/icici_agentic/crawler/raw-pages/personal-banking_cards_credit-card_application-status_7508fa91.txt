TITLE: Track ICICI Bank Credit Card Application Status Online
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/cards/credit-card/application-status

You can easily check your Credit Card application status with ICICI Bank through a simple online process. Enter your application reference number or registered mobile details on the official status tracking page to view real-time updates. This helps you stay informed about approval stages, document verification, and final decision, ensuring a smooth and transparent credit card application experience.
Once you have applied for an ICICI Bank Credit Card, you will receive a message from the bank acknowledging your application. This message will contain a reference number and application number, which act as essential identifiers for tracking the status of your application. You will receive a confirmation SMS indicating your application is being processed if you meet the eligibility criteria.
ICICI Bank's iMobile app > go to Services > go to Card Services > Track application
Once you are on the ICICI Bank's iMobile app and click on ‘Track application’, the stages below help you see which stage your application is at:
1) Application initiated - Once you apply, the Credit Card application is initiated
2) Appointment - Following the application, there is an appointment step
3) Document verification - Once the appointment has been made, the documents required from the applicant are verified
4) Application processing - The next step is the processing of the particular application
5) Card delivery – Once the processing of the application is successful, the card is delivered to the applicant.
