A Gold Loan Calculator is an online tool that helps you estimate the loan amount you can receive by pledging gold as collateral. It factors in gold weight, purity, number of ornaments and the prevailing market rate to calculate the loan value accurately.
To calculate eligibility, enter the gold’s purity (18 to 24 carats), its weight (in grams) and the loan tenure. The calculator uses these inputs, along with the Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio and the gold’s market price, to determine your maximum loan amount.
Simply enter the weight, purity of your gold and the number of ornaments, select the desired tenure (6 or 12 months), and view your results. ICICI Bank’s Gold Loan Calculator provides an instant and accurate estimate of your eligible loan amount.
