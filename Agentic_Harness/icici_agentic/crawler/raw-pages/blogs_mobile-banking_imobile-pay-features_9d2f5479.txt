What are the key features of ‘iMobile’
Customers can experience the speed, ease and safety of the Mobile Banking app using iMobile, now accessible to Non-ICICI Bank customers as well. You can use it just like any other UPI apps. Let’s look at iMobile features and iMobile benefits.
ICICI Bank mobile application, iMobile app that was limited only to the current customers, now offers payments and banking services for all. The newly rolled out Mobile Banking app, ‘iMobile‘ can be used by customers of any bank. It is similar to major UPI apps, allowing you to implement financial tasks efficiently with proper GPRS connection. Basically, it eliminates the need for downloading multiple apps for payments or banking, as it empowers mobile users to carry out transactions from a single source. Let’s look at some of the iMobile.
Here are some of the top iMobile features and iMobile benefits for all customers (including ICICI Bank and Non-ICICI Bank customers):
- Pay to Contact: One of the prominent features of the Mobile Banking app is ‘pay to contacts’ that allows users to see UPI IDs of those in their contact list and transfer money across payment app and digital wallet. This functionality offers users the convenience of inter-operability.
- Accessibility for all: The Mobile Banking app is open for all customers holding bank accounts with any bank. You can do all types of transactions as it is a completely secured option.
- Scan to pay: It lets you make payment in an instant by scanning the QR code, as well as it enables you to send money to any UPI ID, bank account or self, without incurring any expense or fee. Be it hospitals, grocery shops, restaurants, petrol pumps, pharmacies or multiplexes; you can make payments or transfer money with convenience and ease.
- Banking at fingertips: One of the highlights of the iMobile app is you can explore and start a bank relationship with ICICI Bank. If you wish to open a Savings Account or apply for a new Credit Card or loan of any type, it is easy to do so using the Mobile Banking application. Get the privilege of instant approval on loans and avail of Credit Cards at zero joining fees.
- Link bank accounts: Simply link your bank account with the iMobile UPI ID at the time of creating the login. Once linked with a UPI-enabled bank, it allows you to send and receive money from any bank account without sharing the account number or personal other banking details.
- Additional features: Along with the above iMobile features, the forthcoming benefits added include paying utility bills, recharge mobile phones, buy movie tickets, book travel tickets, buy gift cards, make investments in mutual funds, FD, RD and others. The Mobile Banking app will also allow you to keep a check on your balances by letting you track expenses.
It’s time to access all our payments or banking needs from a single platform and download the iMobile app on your mobile devices. Once you download the app, you can instantly link your bank account, generate a UPI ID and start transactions with ease and speed.
