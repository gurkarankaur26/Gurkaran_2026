Essential Considerations
Here are some things to remember when you decide to buy Forex online:
- Purposes and Limit: Understand the purposes and limit associated with your Forex transaction. For instance, there is a limit of USD 2,50,000 or its equivalent per financial year for various purposes such as travel, education, medical treatment, etc. Make sure your transaction complies with the limit and purposes.
- Documentation: Ensure you have all the necessary documentation before initiating a Forex transaction. This may include ID proof, proof of travel, employment, education, etc. Be prepared to provide these documents as per the requirements.
- Regulatory Guidelines: Familiarise yourself with the regulatory guidelines regarding Forex transactions, especially if your remittance exceeds the limit of USD 2,50,000. For detailed information, contact any ICICI Bank Forex Servicing Branch.
- GST: Be aware of the Goods and Services Tax (GST) slab applicable to Forex transactions. Understand the GST implications on your transactions and ensure compliance with the tax regulations.
- Branch Locator: Use the Branch locator tool on the ICICI Bank website or app to locate the nearest Forex Servicing Branch.
