Invest in Indian Stocks with an NRE PIS
Portfolio Investment Scheme (PIS) allows Non-Resident Indians (NRIs) to invest in equity shares of an Indian company in the secondary market on repatriation basis. This can be done using an NRE PIS account through a registered stockbroker in India.
