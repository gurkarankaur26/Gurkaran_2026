The ICICI Bank Compare Credit Card tool allows you to compare multiple Credit Cards on the website to help you evaluate their features and benefits. Here is how to compare cards:
1. Visit the Compare Credit Cards page on the ICICI Bank website
2. Select the Credit Cards you want to compare
3. Review the cards based on key parameters such as best usage categories, reward points, benefits and redemption options
4. Check additional details like how to maximise benefits, important information about the card and application requirements
This comparison helps you evaluate different cards and choose the one that suits your spending preferences and requirements.
