TITLE: How to Earn Assured Monthly Interest on Fixed Deposit (FD)
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/blogs/fixed-deposits/how-to-open-fixed-deposit-account

Fixed Deposits (FDs) are considered one of the most secure and reliable investment options available today, offering assured returns. They provide a steady income stream through interest payments. FDs operate on a straightforward principle: deposit a certain amount for a specific tenure and receive interest at predetermined rates. The interest is usually compounded on a short-term, monthly, quarterly or cumulative basis.
A monthly interest payout will enable customers to create a recurring stream of income. Without further delay, let’s understand how you can earn assured returns through monthly earnings with Fixed Deposits.
The initial investment (principal) directly affects the interest earned. A higher principal amount significantly impacts the overall returns from the FD.
Interest rates offered by the bank significantly impact monthly earnings. Higher interest rates result in greater monthly interest earnings. Even a slight increase in the interest rate can notably enhance the monthly returns on the FD.
Opting for a longer tenure can maximise monthly earnings due to the compounding effect but balancing this with liquidity needs is essential. Premature withdrawals can cost you penalties.
Here's a step-by-step guide on how one can open a Fixed Deposit (FD) with monthly interest payout with ICICI Bank in just 60 seconds:
Begin by logging into your Bank account using Internet Banking or iMobile app. Once logged in, navigate to the section that pertains to Fixed Deposits. This can usually be found under the 'Investments' or 'Savings' tab.
Fill in the required details, such as the investment amount you wish to deposit and the tenure or duration of the FD. The term can vary, typically ranging from seven (7) days to ten (10) years, so choose the duration according to your financial goals.
After determining the investment amount and tenure, you will have to choose the payout plan. You can choose how you want to receive the interest accumulated on your FD - whether monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or annual.
Before submitting your FD application, take a moment to review all the details you've entered. Ensure the investment amount, tenure and payout plan are as per your preferences and financial requirements.
Before investing in an FD, consider these strategies:
Researching and comparing FD interest rates among various banks or financial institutions is very important, as it will ensure that you secure the best possible returns for your investment. Banks offer different rates based on factors like tenure, age of the investor and deposit amount.
Diversification is a crucial principle in investment. Laddering deposits involves spreading your investments across multiple FDs with varying tenures. This strategy balances liquidity and maximises returns.
Choose the right interest payout frequency that aligns with your financial goals and needs. Banks often provide options for monthly, quarterly, semi-annual or annual interest payouts. Therefore, opting for monthly payouts can supplement regular income or fulfil immediate financial requirements.
While FDs offer stability and predictable returns, it's crucial to maintain realistic expectations. External factors like inflation and taxes can impact the actual purchasing power of your earnings over time. Inflation erodes the value of money, potentially impacting the worth of your FD returns.
