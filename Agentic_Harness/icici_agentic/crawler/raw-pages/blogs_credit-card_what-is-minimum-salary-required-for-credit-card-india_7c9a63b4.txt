TITLE: What is the Minimum Salary Required for Credit Card in India?
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/blogs/credit-card/what-is-minimum-salary-required-for-credit-card-india

Credit Cards are widely accessible in India, but eligibility often depends on your income level. Banks assess minimum salary requirements to determine repayment capacity and creditworthiness. Understanding these criteria can help you choose the right card and improve approval chances before applying across different card categories offered by various banks.
The minimum salary required for Credit Card approval in India varies depending on the card type and applicant profile. Entry-level cards may require a lower income, while premium cards need higher income levels. Apart from minimum salary for Credit Card eligibility, banks also assess credit score, repayment history and employment stability.
Apart from the basic eligibility criteria, there are other factors that play a significant role in determining your eligibility for a Credit Card, including:
Age: ICICI Bank usually requires applicants to be at least 21 years of age. However, some cards may have different age requirements, so checking the specific card's eligibility criteria is important.
Income: Having a regular source of income is vital for Credit Card approval. While the income requirements may vary, ICICI Bank typically considers a net annual income of Rs 2.5 lakh for Credit Card applications. However, certain cards may have stricter income criteria.
Nationality: ICICI Bank Credit Cards are available to both Indian residents and non-residents. However, confirming the eligibility criteria with the bank is advisable before applying.
