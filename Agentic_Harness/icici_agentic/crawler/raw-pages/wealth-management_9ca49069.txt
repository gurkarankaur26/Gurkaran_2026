TITLE: ICICI Bank Wealth Management Savings Account – Personalized Banking and Investment Solutions
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/wealth-management

Programme Eligibility for individuals (Any one of the below):
 
- Relationship value of ₹50 L
 (Deposits + Account balance + MF Value + Demat holdings)
- Deposits + Account balance = ₹10 L
 
*Net monthly salary credit of ₹3 L in ICICI Bank Salary Account
 
Programme Eligibility for family banking (Any one of the below):
 
- Family relationship value of ₹75 L
 (Deposits + Account balance + MF Value + Demat holdings)
- Deposits + Account balance = ₹15 L
 
If the customer does not meet average programme criteria for 12 months, then the account will be re-classified.
 
If programme eligibility criteria are not met, customer needs to maintain monthly average balance of ₹15,000 in Savings Account to avoid non-maintenance charge.
 
Non-maintenance charges are waived off for:
 
- Family Savings Account linked to ICICI Bank Salary Account
- Students of select institutes
