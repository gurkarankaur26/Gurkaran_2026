A Bike Loan/Two Wheeler Loan is a financial product that allows individuals to purchase a bike and repay the loan amount through Equated Monthly Instalments (EMIs).
Buying a bike on EMI offers several benefits. Firstly, it allows you to own a bike without needing a large upfront payment, making it more affordable. Secondly, it enables you to choose a repayment tenure that aligns with your financial goals and capabilities. Additionally, Bike Loans often come with competitive interest rates, allowing you to save money in the long run. Finally, you can build a positive credit history by making regular and timely EMI payments.
EMI is the fixed amount you pay each month towards your bike loan. It includes both the principal amount and the interest charged by the lender. The EMI remains the same throughout the repayment tenure, making it easier to plan your finances. As you pay your EMIs, the outstanding loan amount gradually decreases until it is fully repaid.
