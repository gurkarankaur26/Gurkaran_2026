TITLE: Benefits of Fixed Deposit (FD) Account in ICICI, India
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/deposits/fixed-deposit/benefits

Things you should know
-  
                                        Minimum AmountYou require a minimum ₹ 10,000 to open an FD. 
-  
                                        Minimum and Maximum TenureYou can invest in FD from 7 days to 10 years. 
-  
                                        Additional Interest ratesSenior citizens can avail of a 0.50% higher interest rate on FD. 
-  
                                        Save on TaxesInvest in Tax-Saver FDs to get tax deductions.
