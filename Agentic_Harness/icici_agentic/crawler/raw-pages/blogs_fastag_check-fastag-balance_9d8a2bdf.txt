TITLE: How to Check FASTag Balance & Status Using Vehicle Number
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/blogs/fastag/check-fastag-balance

FASTag helps you zip through toll plazas and makes your road trips a breeze. However, this is possible only if your FASTag has enough balance. So, here’s a step-by-step guide to checking your FASTag balance.
Every vehicle has to pay toll charges at toll stations before getting onto a national highway. One of the biggest problems toll plazas would create was traffic congestion as people used cash for paying the toll. Thanks to the announcement by the government to make FASTag a mandatory toll payment method, toll payment is now fast and easy.
FASTag is an online toll payment facility that uses Radio-Frequency Identification (RFID) that enables automatic deduction of toll when a vehicle passes through the plaza. You don't have to wait in a long queue; you can pass through the FASTag lane, where the the tag is read and toll is deducted automatically. However, this is possible only if there’s adequate balance in the FASTag account. Hence, it’s important to know your FASTag balance and recharge it if necessary. Let's get to know how you can check your FASTag balance in this blog post.
