Points to Consider Before Applying
Before taking an Education Loan, keep these important factors in mind:
- Course credibility: Always select a programme that adds real value to your professional journey. Banks consider the credibility of the course and the institute before approving Education Loans, as this increases the chances of career growth and consequently, of timely repayment. 
- Repayment capacity: Before applying, calculate your estimated EMIs and check if they are manageable within your monthly income. A clear understanding of your repayment ability avoids financial stress and helps you stay disciplined in paying off your loan comfortably. 
- Moratorium period: You can get a moratorium period, which allows you to start repaying the loan after completing your course. Check if this option is available, as it provides flexibility and financial relief while pursuing higher studies. 
- Collateral requirements: Some Education Loans require collateral, especially if the loan amount is higher. Understand if any collateral, like property, fixed deposits or insurance is needed. Being aware in advance reduces surprises during the application process and helps you be prepared.
