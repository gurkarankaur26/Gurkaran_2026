Specific eligibility criteria for customers to qualify for a Travel Credit Card are mentioned below:
The applicant’s age should be between 21 and 65 years.
Most Travel Credit Cards from ICICI Bank have a certain minimum annual income, depending on the specific Card.
To be eligible, one needs to have a good credit record and credit score. Usually, it should be in the ‘good’ or ‘excellent' range.
The following documents are required to apply for a Travel Credit Card from ICICI Bank:
Valid documents that are commonly acceptable include: Passport, Aadhaar Card, PAN Card or Driving licence.
Documents like utility bills, signed rental agreements or the latest bank account statement with the address.
Proofs of income may include a salary slip, an income tax return and a business financial statement, among others.
Fill in the application form provided by ICICI Bank and ensure that the correct details are entered in each field.
You might need to submit recent bank statements while applying for the Credit Card.
It is worth mentioning here that the above criteria, in terms of document submissions, might vary depending on the particular Travel Credit Card being applied for.
Ensure that you meet the age, income, employment status and credit score criteria required.
Choose a Travel Credit Card that best suits your needs, both financially and in terms of travel. Every Card has unique benefits and features.
Go to the Credit Card page on the Bank’s website or log in to the iMobile app and choose your Card. Click on ‘Apply Now’ or ‘Get Started’ to submit your financial and personal information.
Upload the necessary documents, including passport-sized photographs, proof of identity, proof of address and proof of income.
The Bank will examine your application. Make sure you have provided the correct information for your application to be accepted.
The Bank will send you a confirmation once your application is approved after verification.
The Credit Card will be sent to your registered address. On receiving the Card, you are required to activate it.
When selecting a Travel Credit Card, look for features like lounge access deals, low foreign transaction fees and travel insurance. Moreover, you should consider annual fees, partner airlines and reward redemption options to ensure that the Card matches your travel habits and offers value for money.
