TITLE: ICICI Bank FASTag FAQs - Know Eligibility, Charges, Usage & More

ICICI Bank FASTag is a device that uses Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) technology for making toll payments directly from the prepaid account linked to it. It is affixed on the windscreen of your vehicle and enables you to drive through toll plazas without stopping for cash transactions. The tag is read by the antennae at the toll plaza, and the toll amount is deducted automatically.
Yes, the Government of India has mandated FASTag for all M and N category vehicles (four-wheelers and above). Vehicles without a functional FASTag or with insufficient balance are liable to pay double the applicable toll amount in cash or through other electronic modes at National Highway toll plazas.
ICICI Bank FASTag offers several advantages, including cashless transactions, which save time and fuel by reducing wait times at toll plazas. Users receive instant SMS alerts for all transactions, making it easy to track expenses. Additionally, it provides access to a dedicated customer portal for statements and online recharges, ensuring a seamless journey.
ICICI Bank issues FASTags in different colors based on the vehicle class as defined by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI). For example, Violet tags are for cars/jeeps/vans (Class 4), while other colors like Orange, Yellow, and Pink are assigned to various categories of trucks, buses, and construction machinery based on their axle count and size.
ICICI Bank FASTag is part of the National Electronic Toll Collection (NETC) program. It is accepted at all National Highway toll plazas across the country. Furthermore, it is increasingly being accepted at various State Highway toll plazas, select city parking lots, and for fuel payments at participating petrol pumps.
You can purchase an ICICI Bank FASTag offline by visiting any ICICI Bank branch. Additionally, the bank has set up Point of Sale (POS) terminals at various National Highway toll plazas, select petrol pumps, and Regional Transport Offices (RTOs) where you can provide documentation and get a tag issued instantly.

Any individual or corporate entity owning a vehicle of Class 4 or above is eligible to apply for an ICICI Bank FASTag. This includes owners of private cars, commercial fleets, buses, trucks, and earth-moving machinery. The applicant must have a valid Registration Certificate (RC) for the vehicle.
Individual customers need to provide a copy of the vehicle’s Registration Certificate (RC), a passport-sized photograph, and a valid KYC document. Accepted KYC documents include a PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving License. The address on the KYC document should ideally match the delivery address.
Corporate entities must provide the firm's PAN Card, the Registration Certificate/Partnership Deed/Certificate of Incorporation, and the ID proof of the authorized signatory. For public or private limited companies, a Board Resolution or Power of Attorney authorizing the person to operate the account is also required.
While it is standard practice for the vehicle owner to apply for the FASTag, you can apply for one if you have the RC copy. However, the KYC documents of the applicant must be provided. In certain cases, like leased vehicles, additional documentation proving authorization may be requested by the bank.
A PAN Card is generally required as part of the KYC process to comply with financial regulations. If an individual does not have a PAN Card, they may submit Form 60 along with other valid Identity and Address proofs as per the bank's internal policy and RBI guidelines.

The FASTag Annual Pass is a toll payment scheme for non-commercial vehicles (cars, jeeps, vans) that lets you travel on the NHAI (National Highways Authority of India) managed national highways and expressways without worrying about FASTag balance or recharge. The pass is valid for one year or up to 200 trips, whichever is reached first and must be linked to your existing FASTag. So, if you have an active ICICI Bank FASTag, your Annual Pass will be automatically linked to it.
The Annual Pass is priced at ₹3,000 (including taxes). Once purchased and activated, it remains valid for 365 days from the activation date or 200 toll trips, whichever is reached first. After the validity expires, your FASTag will automatically revert to regular per-trip toll billing.< /p>
You can purchase or recharge the Annual Pass online through the Rajmargyatra app or the official NHAI portal. The pass is linked to your existing FASTag. Yes, it is safe. The FASTag uses passive RFID technology, meaning it does not have a power source and only transmits data when it is within range of a powered toll scanner. It cannot be used to track your vehicle's location remotely or to steal personal financial data.
The RFID readers used for FASTag have a specific frequency and encryption protocol. Standard handheld devices cannot easily "skim" or deduct money from your tag. Additionally, toll deductions only occur through authorized NETC-linked merchant terminals, ensuring a high level of security.
