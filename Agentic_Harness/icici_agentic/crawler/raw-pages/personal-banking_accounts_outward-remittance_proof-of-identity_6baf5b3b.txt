TITLE: Documents - Overseas Money Transfer | ICICI Bank
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/accounts/outward-remittance/proof-of-identity

Resident India, who do not hold an ICICI Bank Account, can submit the transaction request by visiting ICICI Bank branch in India. Customer can complete a one time registration by submitting basic KYC documents for proof of identity and proof of address. Post registration is done, transaction request can be given at the branch.
