                            How do I report a claim? 
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            To report your claim you can:
 - Visit the claims section of ICICI Prudential Life website – www.iciciprulife.com for online claim intimation or Report claim by filling online form
- Call ICICI Prudential Life’s 24x7 Claim Care number on 1800 2660 or
- Visit your nearest ICICI Prudential Life Insurance branch
- E-mail at claimsupport@iciciprulife.com
- SMS to 56767 from your registered mobile number. Your claim will be formally registered only after we receive a written claim intimation at our Branch/Claims Cell. For calls within India, call Claim Care number 1800 2660 (10:00 a.m. – 7:00 p.m., Monday to Saturday, except national holidays. Overseas customers can call on +91 80693 85555.
 
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            What documents are required to file a claim? 
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            The required documents vary based on the type of claim (death, maturity, or health). Generally, you will need:
 Mandatory documents required for claim registration:
 - Duly filled and signed Claimant Statement Form
- Recent photograph of the claimant
- Copy of Death Certificate of the Life Assured, issued by government authority
- Claimant photo identity proof
- Claimant address proof (Any one of the following: Aadhar Card, Valid Passport or Driver's License, Voters ID are considered as proofs)
- Pan card / Form 60 of the claimant
- Copy of cancelled cheque / bank statement / bank passbook with printed account number and name of the claimant
- Duly filled and signed Payout mandate form with bank account details of the claimant
For a detailed list, refer to the Claim Documents Checklist on the website.

                            Who is entitled to receive the claim benefit? 
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            The benefit is paid to:
 - The nominee mentioned in the policy
- The legal heir if no nominee is available
 
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            What happens if there is no nominee or the nominee has predeceased the policyholder? 
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            In such cases, the claim benefit is paid to the legal heir as per succession laws. The claimant must provide legal proof of relationship with the deceased policyholder.
                           
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            How will I receive the claim amount?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            The claim amount is transferred via:
 - Electronic transfer to the registered bank account of the beneficiary
- Cheque payment, if electronic transfer is not possible
 
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            What is the time frame to report a claim?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            Claims should be reported as soon as possible after the event. Delays may require additional documentation
                           
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            What is a claim form, and where can I get one?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            A claim form is a mandatory document required to process the claim. It can be downloaded from the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance website or collected from the nearest branch
                           
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            What factors determine the need for additional documents?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            The insurer may request additional documents: Based on the documents submitted by you, the claim was reviewed by the claim assessor. Requirement for additional documents have been raised for the claim assessor to evaluate and decide the claim. Submitting these documents will only speed up the claim process.
 - Cause of death
- Duration of the policy
- Involvement in medical treatment
- Legal and regulatory requirements
 
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            Why is it necessary to submit all required records and documents?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            Providing complete and accurate documentation ensures faster processing of the claim. Missing documents may cause delays or rejection.
                           
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            Under what circumstances can a claim be rejected?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            A claim may be declined due to:
 | Reason | Example | 
|---|
| Non-disclosure of information | Hiding pre-existing medical conditions | 
| Policy lapse | Non-payment of premiums | 
| Fraudulent claims | Misrepresentation of facts | 
| Exclusions in policy | Death due to excluded reasons like suicide within policy exclusions | 
                           
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            How do I raise concerns about a claim decision?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            If you disagree with the claim decision, you can:
 - Contact the Claims Department via email or phone
- Visit the nearest ICICI Prudential Life branch
- Write to the Grievance Redressal Team
 
 
                         
                       
 
                    
 
                     
                      
 
                        
 
                       
                          
                            Where can I send a written intimation to the claims department?
                          
 
                          
                        
 
                       
                        
 
                          
                            You can send claim-related written communication to the address provided on the ICICI Prudential Life Insurance website under the Claims Section.
