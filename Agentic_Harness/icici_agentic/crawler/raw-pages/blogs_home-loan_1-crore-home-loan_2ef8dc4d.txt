FAQs on 1 crore Home Loan EMI
Q1: What is the impact of changing interest rates on my ₹ 1 crore Home Loan EMI?
A1: Fluctuating interest rates can significantly impact your EMI. A rise in rates increases the EMI and the total interest payable and vice versa. ICICI Bank's flexible rate options allow borrowers to choose according to their comfort and market trends.
Q2: How can I manage a sudden increase in EMI due to rate changes?
A2: ICICI Bank provides counselling and restructuring options in case of difficulty in managing increased EMIs due to interest rate hikes.
Q3: What are the implications of a joint Home Loan on EMI and eligibility?
A3: A joint Home Loan can enhance loan eligibility and may offer tax benefits to both co-borrowers. It also implies shared responsibility for EMIs.
Q4: How can I align my Home Loan with my long-term financial goals?
A4: It's essential to align your Home Loan with your financial goals. ICICI Bank's financial advisors can help you integrate your Home Loan into your broader financial plan, ensuring that it complements your long-term objectives.
Q5: Can I switch my loan tenure or amount after a few years?
A5: Yes, ICICI Bank offers flexibility in changing the loan tenure or amount, subject to certain terms and conditions, to adapt to your changing financial circumstances.
Q6: Is there a possibility for prepayment of the Home Loan and are there any charges for it?
A6: ICICI Bank allows prepayment of Home Loan, which can significantly reduce the total interest payable. However, there may be prepayment charges, depending on the type of interest rate (fixed or floating) and the terms of your loan agreement.
Q7: What kind of insurance options are available with a ₹ 1 crore Home Loan?
A7: ICICI Bank often recommends loan protection insurance plans that cover the outstanding loan in case of unforeseen circumstances like the borrower's death. This ensures that the loan burden does not fall on the borrower's family.
Q8: How does my existing debt and credit utilisation affect my Home Loan eligibility and EMI?
A8: Existing debt and credit utilisation play a critical role in determining your loan eligibility and EMI. High existing debts may lead to a lower loan amount approval or higher interest rates, as these indicate a higher repayment burden.
Q9: Can I avail of any tax benefits on a ₹ 1 crore Home Loan?
A9: Yes, borrowers can avail of tax benefits under various sections of the Income Tax Act on both the principal repayment and the interest payment of the Home Loan. These benefits can reduce the overall cost of borrowing.
Q10: What happens in case of default in Home Loan EMI payments?
A10: In case of EMI payment defaults, borrowers may need to pay penalty charges and it may also affect their credit score negatively. The bank usually offers a grace period and options for restructuring the loan in case of genuine financial difficulties.
