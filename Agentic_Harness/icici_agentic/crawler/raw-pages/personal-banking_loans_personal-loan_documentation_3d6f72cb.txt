TITLE: Documents Required for Personal Loan in India | ICICI Bank
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/loans/personal-loan/documentation

ICICI Bank makes your loan process seamless with the minimal documents required for a Personal Loan. and a bank statement or a salary slip. For self-employed applicants, the documents required may differ. Be sure to check the documents needed before applying for Personal Loan.
As part of the ‘Go Green’ initiative - an endeavor to reduce use of paper and make our contribution to this noble cause, the below mentioned documents related to your Personal Loans will be sent on your e-mail ID registered with us, with effect from 05 February 2017.
Also, we wish to inform you that if your e-mail ID is not available, communication will be sent through physical letters.
If your e-mail ID is not registered with us, we request you to visit your nearest loan servicing branch or call our Customer Care and update your e-mail ID.
For Personal Loan, Aadhaar and PAN details are mandatory for applicants and co-applicants (wherever applicable).
| Document Category | Details | 
|---|---|
| Identity & Residence Proof | PAN is mandatory. ID is verified automatically using the Aadhaar number provided by the customer. | 
| Income Proof | Income details may be fetched securely through Net Banking or an account aggregator. For customers who do not hold an ICICI Bank account, salary slips may be required as income proof. | 
| Office Address Proof | Salaried customers may enter their office address during the application process. For self-employed applicants, office address proof may be required. | 
| Business Continuity Proof | Not applicable as part of the digital journey; continuity of business is verified via account information, if needed. | 
*Conditions apply
*ICICI Bank may request additional documents, if required, during verification.
