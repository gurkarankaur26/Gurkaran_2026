A Current Account is a bank account designed for businesses that require frequent and high-volume transactions. A Current Account does not earn interest on the balance, but it offers higher transaction flexibility, multiple daily transactions and access to overdraft facilities. It is ideal for entrepreneurs, traders, firms and companies looking to manage day-to-day business payments efficiently. ICICI Bank's Current Account comes with a QAB requirement of ₹50,000 and offers access to the ICICI Bank InstaBIZ app, Corporate Net Banking and a comprehensive suite of business banking solutions.
It is crucial for business owners in India to know the features and benefits of a Current Account. Due to features like higher limits on number and value of transactions, access to overdraft and easy ways to handle everyday business operations, a Current Account is of great help to professionals, entrepreneurs and business owners.
To open a Current Account, choose a trusted bank like ICICI Bank, which can fulfil your business’s financial needs through easy access, reasonable maintenance expenses and efficient customer service. The process of opening a Current Account with ICICI Bank is simple, takes just a few minutes and you can do it from any place and at any time.
ICICI Bank offers an online process to apply for a Current Account and the Bank’s representatives provide assistance in completing the account opening process. Here is how you can apply for a Current Account online with ICICI Bank.
Visit the ICICI Bank website, i.e. www.icici.bank.in
Go to: Business > Accounts > Current Account
Click on ‘Apply’ and fill out the online application form
Enter your details and agree to the disclaimer.
An ICICI Bank representative will contact you and assist you in completing your application for the Current Account. Be prepared by keeping your documents handy, such as identity proof, address proof and business proofs. The online application form serves as an easy way to initiate the account opening process – the process is completed offline.
It is easy to open an ICICI Bank Current Account using the traditional offline process, i.e., by visiting a Branch. Here are the steps:
Visit any ICICI Bank Branch
Consult our team at the Branch about the various types of Current Accounts that are offered and select the Current Account that best meets your company's needs
Get the Current Account application form and fill in the necessary information such as your name, business type, contact details and business details
Make sure you have carried the necessary documents like PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, proof of address, proof of company registration and other required documents based on the type of business and submit their copies as required
After reviewing your application, the Bank will check your documentation. After successful verification, your Current Account will be opened & activated and you will receive your Account details.
In order to become a Current Account holder, the following documents must be submitted to the bank of your choice:
Proof of identity of the individual opening the Current Account, such as a PAN Card, Voter ID, valid Passport and / or Driving Licence
Proof of address for future communication. An individual may show a telephone or electricity bill in order to establish the same
A cheque from an existing Savings Account
Colour photograph(s) of the applicant(s)
Certificate of Incorporation and Memorandum of Association are required in the case of companies and trusts
Companies should provide an address at which they can be reached
Partnership Agreement is required in the case of partnership firms
Identity proof and residence proof of all partners.
