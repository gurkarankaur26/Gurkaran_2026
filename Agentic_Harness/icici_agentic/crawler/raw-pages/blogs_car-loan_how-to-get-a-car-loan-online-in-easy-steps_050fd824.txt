eKYC enables car purchases across PAN India
ICICI Bank uses its Car Loan Digital Disbursement process to simplify the car buying journey. Eligible customers can go through an eKYC flow, which enables the customer to enter their current address from where they wish to buy a car, in case their permanent address in not similar. It requires verification through an Aadhaar OTP, post entering the address details. The process ensures that the customer can get their car registered as per their wish, faster processing and reduces the need for physical documentation, making the entire experience quick and hassle-free.
