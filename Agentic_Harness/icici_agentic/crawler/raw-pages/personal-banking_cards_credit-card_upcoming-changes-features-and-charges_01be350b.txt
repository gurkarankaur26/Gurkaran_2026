TITLE: Credit Cards - Upcoming changes to Credit Card features and charges
URL: https://www.icicibank.com/personal-banking/cards/credit-card/upcoming-changes-features-and-charges

Starting 01 January 2025, you can enjoy complimentary lounge access at domestic airports by spending ₹75,000 on certain ICICI Bank Credit Cards. Spends made in the previous calendar quarter will unlock access for the subsequent calendar quarter. For example, to be eligible for complimentary lounge access in the quarter of January 2025 to March 2025, you need to spend a minimum of ₹75,000 in the quarter of October 2024 to December 2024 and similarly for subsequent quarters. The number of complimentary access varies as per the Card.
Please go through the below illustration to understand the Spend period along with the Lounge eligibility period
| S. No. | Spend period | Lounge eligibility period | 
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Spend ₹75,000 between 26 September 2024 and 25 December 2024 | Get complimentary lounge access between 01 January 2025 and 31 March 2025 | 
| 2 | Spend ₹75,000 between 26 December 2024 and 25 March 2025 | Get complimentary lounge access between 01 April 2025 and 30 June 2025 | 
| 3 | Spend ₹75,000 between 26 March 2025 and 25 June 2025 | Get complimentary lounge access between 01 July 2025 and 30 September 2025 | 
| 4 | Spend ₹75,000 between 26 June 2025 and 25 September 2025 | Get complimentary lounge access between 01 October 2025 and 31 December 2025 | 
This updated criterion for availing complimentary domestic airport lounge access will be applicable for all eligible ICICI Bank Credit Cards except:
