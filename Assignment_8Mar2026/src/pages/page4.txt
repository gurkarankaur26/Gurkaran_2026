Culinary Arts & Techniques
The Art of French Cooking
Classical French cuisine is renowned for its emphasis on foundational techniques and high-quality ingredients. Techniques such as braising, roasting, and sautéing are perfected over years, demanding precision and patience. The foundational sauces—mother sauces—form the basis of countless, complex dishes.
Modernizing Traditional Recipes
While respecting tradition, modern chefs are incorporating global flavors and healthier techniques into classic recipes. Reduced fat alternatives and, plant-based ingredients are becoming commonplace in fine dining. This evolution ensures that traditional cooking methods remain relevant in a contemporary context.
